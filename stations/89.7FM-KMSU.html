KMSU is a public radio station operated by Minnesota State University in Mankato, Minnesota that carries a mixed news, talk, and music format. A repeater station, KMSK, serves the city of Austin. A translator station, K220AQ, serves the city of Fairmont, and another, K220AR, serves the city of Albert Lea. It is part of Minnesota's AMPERS network.
