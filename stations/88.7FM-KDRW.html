KDRW is a non-commercial FM radio station licensed to and serving Santa Barbara, California. The station is owned by Santa Monica College and serves as a repeater of KCRW and its public radio format. However, KDRW also originates some programming locally from its studios on the campus of Antioch University Santa Barbara.
