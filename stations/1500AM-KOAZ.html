KOAZ is an AM broadcast station licensed to Vanguard Media LLC. at Isleta Pueblo, New Mexico. It is operating at 1510 kHz at \"Isleta\" with 5,000 watts daytime and 25 watts nighttime, from 34°58′46.2″N 106°44′15.1″W. The station serves the Albuquerque Metropolitan area. Its studios are located in Northeast Albuquerque.
