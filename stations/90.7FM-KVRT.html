KEDT-FM 90.3 MHz is an FM public broadcasting radio station that serves the Corpus Christi, Texas area. It is under ownership of The South Texas Public Broadcasting System. Originally signing on in 1982 as KKED-FM in 1993 it changed its call letters to KEDT to match that of KEDT-TV.
