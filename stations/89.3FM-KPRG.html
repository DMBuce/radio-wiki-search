KPRG is Guam's only public radio station. The station is owned and operated by the Guam Educational Radio Foundation and is licensed to Hagåtña. The station signed on the air on January 28, 1994 and is a member of NPR, APM, PRI and BBC. Its studio is located on the campus of the University of Guam in Mangilao, Guam.
