WEKU, is a National Public Radio-member station licensed to Richmond, Kentucky serving the eastern half of Kentucky, including nearby Lexington. Owned by Eastern Kentucky University, it primarily features NPR news and talk programming during the week and folk/Kentucky Music on weekends. WEKU operates nine frequencies in Central and Eastern Kentucky.
