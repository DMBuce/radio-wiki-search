KVNF, is a National Public Radio-affiliated community radio station in Paonia, Colorado. It features locally hosted music programming, independently produced public radio programs, local news and interviews, as well as National Public Radio programming. KVNF serves Western Colorado via two stations with full-power licenses and five translators. All transmitters carry the same programming and are needed to cover KVNF's large and mountainous listening area.
