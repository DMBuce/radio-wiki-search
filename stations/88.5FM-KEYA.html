Turtle Mountain Chippewa Reservation\nKEYA, is a National Public Radio member public radio station in Belcourt, North Dakota. Studios are located at the Turtle Mountain Indian Reservation in Belcourt and has been through many renovations and changes, they have moved three buildings and the format has changed from vinyl, to Fidelipac, and most recently Cassette tape and Compact disc. \"KEYA\" has also employed highschool workers to work the board as Disc jockey's.
