WBAS is an AM radio station licensed to West Yarmouth, Massachusetts, United States, which transmits at a power of 1,000 watts on 1240 kHz. Established in 1940 as WOCB, its format most recently consisted of Portuguese language programming, however the station is currently silent. WBAS also operates FM translator station W268CP on 106.5 MHz.
