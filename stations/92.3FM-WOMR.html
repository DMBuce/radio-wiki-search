WOMR is a public broadcasting community radio station based in Provincetown, Massachusetts. Its call-sign stands for \"OuterMost Radio\". It went into operation in 1982 on 91.9 MHz, switching to 92.1 in 1995 to gain a power boost from 1 kilowatt to 6 and allowing it to reach as far away as Plymouth. In 2010, WOMR began to broadcast up the Cape from its repeater in Orleans, which goes under the callsign WFMR and transmits on 91.3 MHz.
