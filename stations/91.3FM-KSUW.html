KSUW is a radio station licensed to Sheridan, Wyoming. The station is owned by the University of Wyoming, and is an affiliate of Wyoming Public Radio (WPR), airing a format consisting of news, jazz, adult album alternative and classical music originating from KUWR in Laramie, Wyoming. The station's tower is located south of Sheridan near the town of Story.
