KWMU, is the flagship National Public Radio station in St. Louis, Missouri, United States. Known on-air as St. Louis Public Radio, it is licensed to the Curators of the University of Missouri System and operates as part of the University of Missouri-St. Louis. Its studios are located in Grand Center in midtown St. Louis.
