WMEX is a commercial AM radio station licensed to serve the Boston media market. The station was silent from late June 2017 to November 7, 2019, when it returned to the air with the new city of license of Quincy, Massachusetts. The station first began broadcasting in 1934 as WMEX, and after using various call signs since 1978, regained the original WMEX call sign on November 17, 2014.
