WUKY is a National Public Radio (NPR) station in Lexington, Kentucky. Owned by the University of Kentucky (UK), it is an Adult Album Alternative station that airs more than 100 hours of music per week, in addition to programming from NPR, Public Radio International, the BBC, and American Public Media. The station broadcasts from state of the art studios located in the northwestern suburbs of Lexington at the intersection of Greendale Road and Spurr Road.
