Minnesota Public Radio broadcasts on 43 stations that serve Minnesota and its neighboring communities and 42 translators providing additional local coverage. (40 + 41 = 81 total.) Stations are located in Minnesota, Wisconsin, North Dakota, South Dakota, Michigan (Houghton), Iowa (Decorah), and Idaho. MPR also operates KPCC in Pasadena, California.
