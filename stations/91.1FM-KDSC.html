KDSC is a radio station licensed to serve Thousand Oaks, California. The station is owned by the University of Southern California, and is a repeater of KUSC and their classical music format. KDSC formerly competed for the K216FM signal on 91.1 on Los Angeles' west-side, which rebroadcast KKLQ (FM), the Los Angeles affiliate for Contemporary Christian K-LOVE, until the translator's license was cancelled on May 8, 2020.
