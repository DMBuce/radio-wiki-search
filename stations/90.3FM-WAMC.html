WAMC is a public radio network headquartered in Albany, New York. The network has 12 broadcast radio stations (transmitters) and 16 broadcast relay stations. The two flagship stations of the WAMC network are WAMC-FM 90.3 MHz and its AM repeater station WAMC AM 1400 in Albany. The organization's legal name is \"WAMC\" and it is also known as \"WAMC Public Radio\" or \"WAMC Northeast Public Radio.\"
