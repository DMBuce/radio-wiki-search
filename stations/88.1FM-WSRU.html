WSRU is the college radio station of Slippery Rock University in Slippery Rock, Pennsylvania. It is owned by the Student Government Association and the Board of Trustees of SRU. It is operated at a \"rocking\" 100 watts of power, serving SRU and the surrounding community. WSRU is run entirely by SRU students.
