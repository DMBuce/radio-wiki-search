KWRR, is a public radio station in Ethete, Wyoming, serving residents of the Wind River Indian Reservation. Programming on KWRR consists of local programming, plus programs from National Public Radio and Native Voice One. It was established in 1996. As of June, 2018 the station has been off the air.
