WVXU (91.7 FM) is a public radio station located in Cincinnati, Ohio. It is owned by Cincinnati Public Radio, which also operates station WGUC and WMUB. It airs public radio news/talk syndicated programming from National Public Radio, American Public Media and Public Radio International.
