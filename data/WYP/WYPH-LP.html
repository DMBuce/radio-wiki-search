WYPH-LP (102.5 FM, "Y102.5") is a radio station licensed to serve the community of Manchester, Connecticut. The station is owned by New River Community Church. It airs a contemporary Christian music format.

The station was assigned the WYPH-LP call letters by the Federal Communications Commission on February 14, 2014.
