WYPC (1330 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a sports format. Licensed to Wellston, Ohio, United States. The station serves the Wellston/Jackson area, is currently owned by Jackson County Broadcasting, Inc., the owner of the station since 1970, and features programming from CNN Radio and Westwood One.
