WMVL (101.7 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a classic hits format. Licensed to Linesville, Pennsylvania, United States, the station serves the Meadville, Pennsylvania area. The station is currently owned by Vilkie Communications and features programing from ABC Radio, Jones Radio Network and Premiere Radio Networks.

Before being purchased by Vilkie Communications, WMVL was based in Linesville, and was known as WVCC ("The Wonderful Voice of Crawford County").
