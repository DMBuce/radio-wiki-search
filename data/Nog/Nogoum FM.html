Nogoum FM is the first private radio station in Egypt, started in 2003, based in the Egyptian Media Production City. Nugoom FM broadcasts on frequency FM 100.6 in Cairo and airs a variety of daily social, artistic, cultural, political, sports, women, and children's programs. It won best radio station in the sixth Arab Satellite Festival in 2015; it also started Nogoum FM TV channel in May 2015.
