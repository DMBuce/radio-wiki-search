XHCAQ-FM 92.3/XECAQ-AM 740 is a combo radio station in Cancún, Quintana Roo. It is owned by and carries the Radio Fórmula network.

740 AM is a Canadian clear-channel frequency, on which CFZM in Toronto, Ontario is the dominant Class A station. XHCAQ-FM must reduce nighttime power in order to prevent interference to the skywave signal of CFZM.
