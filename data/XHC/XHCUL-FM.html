XHCUL-FM is a noncommercial social radio station broadcasting on 104.9 MHz in Culiacán, Sinaloa, Mexico. The station is owned by a non-profit foundation associated with Capital Media and leased to El Heraldo de México as part of its El Heraldo Radio news/talk network. The station shares a transmitter with Radio Sinaloa in the center of Culiacán.
