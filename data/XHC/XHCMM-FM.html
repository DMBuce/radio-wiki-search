XHCMM-FM is a radio station on 95.5 FM in Coalcomán, Michoacán, broadcasting from atop Cerro El Mono. It serves as a rimshot into the Colima, Colima, radio market. It is owned by Homero Bautista Duarte and is known as Adictiva.
