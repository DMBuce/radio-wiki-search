XHFG or Pulsar 107.3 is a radio station in Tijuana, broadcasting in Spanish and English languages. The station broadcasts and operates at 107.3 MHz with an ERP of 20 kW, XHFG is owned by Uniradio, which owns radio stations in Baja California and Sonora. XHFG has a US sales office in National City.

The station is primarily a Spanish Hot AC station, although it also plays English-language songs on occasion.

XHFG-FM broadcasts in HD.
