XHFL-FM/XEFL-AM is a radio station in Guanajuato, Guanajuato. Broadcasting on 90.7 FM and 1500 AM from Cerro Aldana, XHFL is known as Radio Santa Fe de Guanajuato. 1500 AM is a United States clear-channel frequency.
