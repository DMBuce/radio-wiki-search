CKDX-FM (88.5 FM, Jewel 88.5) is a radio station licensed to Newmarket, Ontario, Canada. Owned by Evanov Radio Group (but with a 30% stake held by outside investors under numbered companies), it broadcasts a soft adult contemporary format. CKDX's studios are located on Dundas Street West in the Eatonville neighbourhood of Etobicoke, Toronto, while its transmitter is located near Aurora
