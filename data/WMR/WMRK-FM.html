WMRK-FM (107.9 FM) is a radio station licensed to serve the Shorter, Alabama. Alexander Broadcasting Company is the licensee. The station is operated under a local marketing agreement by EMF Broadcasting (K-LOVE).

WMRK-FM broadcasts a Contemporary Christian format serving the Montgomery, Alabama, market.
