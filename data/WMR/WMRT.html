WMRT (88.3 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a classical format. WMRT also plays jazz weekday afternoons. Licensed to Marietta, Ohio, United States, it serves the Parkersburg-Marietta area. The station is currently owned and by Marietta College and is run almost entirely by students.
