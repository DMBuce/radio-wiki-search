WADI (95.3 FM, "95.3 The Bee") is a radio station licensed to serve the community of Corinth, Mississippi, United States. The station is owned by The Eagle Radio Network, Inc. It broadcasts a country music format.

The station was assigned the WADI call letters by the Federal Communications Commission.

The Programming is a simulcast arrangement with WXWX, but the two stations have different owners and have coverage areas which overlap.
