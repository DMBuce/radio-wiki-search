Mix FM Lebanon (104.4 - 104.7 FM) is a radio station in Lebanon, broadcasting on the FM band at the frequencies of 104.4 to 104.7. The station first broadcast on 23 February 1996.

Mix FM mainly broadcasts new and trending music. The station also broadcasts exclusive national and international shows in the morning, afternoon, and weekend nights.
