Mix 102.3 (call sign: 5ADD) is a commercial radio station in Adelaide, Australia, owned by the Australian Radio Network.

Mix 102.3 plays current hits and a variety of 1980s and 1990s music (Hot Adult Contemporary), primarily targeted at the 25-54 age group. Adelaide's Mix 102.3 is part of the KIIS Network with sister stations in other major Australian cities - KIIS 106.5 Sydney, KIIS 101.1 Melbourne, 97.3 FM Brisbane, 96FM Perth and Mix 106.3 Canberra.
