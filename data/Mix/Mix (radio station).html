Mix is a greatest hits radio station in New Zealand, broadcasting music from the 70s, 80s and 90s. Mix is owned and operated by New Zealand Media and Entertainment. Mix is targeted at 35 to 54-year-olds. Its head office and studios are located in central Auckland, alongside New Zealand Media and Entertainment's seven other radio networks.

Mix has just one presenter, Breffni O'Rourke in Nelson. Mix plays a wide variety of music from superstar acts such as Madonna, Prince, Bruce Springsteen, Bryan Adams, Bon Jovi, Crowded House, Queen, Talking Heads, Tom Petty and more.
