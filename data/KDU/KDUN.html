KDUN (1030 AM, "K-Dune") is an American Heritage radio station licensed to serve the community of Reedsport, Oregon. The station, which began broadcasting in June 1961, is currently owned by KDUN, LLC.

KDUN broadcasts at 50,000 watts in the daytime, the maximum power output permitted by the FCC for AM stations. At night, the station must reduce power to 633 watts to protect WBZ in Boston.
