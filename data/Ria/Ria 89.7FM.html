Ria 89.7FM is a Malay radio station of Mediacorp in Singapore. This radio station operates on a 24-hour basis which plays hottest Malay hits predominantly from the 90s to present, targets forward-thinking professionals, appealing to both genders. The station's DJs include veteran Azlin Ali, who has more than 20 years of experience in the radio industry.
