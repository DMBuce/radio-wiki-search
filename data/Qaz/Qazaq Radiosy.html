Qazaq Radiosy is the largest broadcasting network in Kazakhstan. It began broadcasting in September 29, 1921, and is included in the Qazaqstan Radio and Television Corporation. On October 1, 2012, production translated into a new broadcasting media center Qazmediacenter in Astana.
