CFCP-FM is a Canadian radio station that broadcasts an active rock format at 98.9 FM in Courtenay, British Columbia. The station is branded as World Class Rock - 98.9 Jet FM and is owned by Vista Broadcast Group.

The station originally began broadcasting in 1959 at 1440 AM until November 11, 1999, when the station moved to the FM dial as Magic 98.9 with an adult contemporary format. In October 2005, the station changed formats to active rock with the branding Jet FM. On May 30, 2014, CFCP changed its name to "98.9 The Goat". On November 1, 2019 The Goat changed all their logos on their Facebook and website and re branded as Jet FM once again, welcoming back former host Pete Montana and Robyn in the morning.
