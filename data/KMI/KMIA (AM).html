KMIA (1210 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a regional Mexican format. Licensed to Auburn–Federal Way, Washington, United States, it serves the Seattle metropolitan area. The station is currently owned by Amador and Rosalie Bustos, through licensee Bustos Media Holdings, LLC.
