KLJT (102.3 FM; Fuzíon 102.3) is a terrestrial American radio station, airing a Spanish language Christian format branded as "Fuzíon 102.3 & 103.1". Licensed to Jacksonville, Texas, United States, the station is owned by the Educational Radio Foundation of East Texas. KLJT simulcasts programming with its sister station KMPA Pittsburg. The combined signals serve the majority of the Tyler-Longview area.
