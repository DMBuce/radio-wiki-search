KLJA (107.7 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a Spanish AC format. Licensed to Univision Radio in Georgetown, Texas, United States, it serves the Austin area. The station has a transmitter in Georgetown and has studios along MoPac Expressway in Northwest Austin.
