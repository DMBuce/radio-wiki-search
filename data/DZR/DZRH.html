DZRH (666 AM) is a commercial radio station owned and operated by Manila Broadcasting Company. The station's studio is located at Design Center of the Philippines, Vicente Sotto Street, CCP Complex, Pasay City (beside Star City); while its transmitter is located at I. Marcelo Street, Brgy. Malanday, Valenzuela City. The station has nationwide coverage via relay stations located within the Philippines.

DZRH is the oldest radio station in the Philippines. The station celebrated its 80th anniversary last July 15, 2019.
