90.1 Spirit FM (DWRV 90.1 MHz) is an FM station owned and operated by the Roman Catholic Diocese of Bayombong in the Philippines. Its studios and transmitter are located at Maharlika Hi-way, Brgy. Luyang, Bayombong.
