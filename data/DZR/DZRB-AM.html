DZRB (738 AM), on-air as Radyo Pilipinas (RP1), is a radio station owned and operated by the Philippine Broadcasting Service, an agency under the Presidential Communications Operations Office. It serves as the flagship station of the Radyo Pilipinas network. The station's studios are located in 4/F, PIA/Media Center Building, Visayas Avenue, Brgy. Vasra, Diliman, Quezon City, and its transmitter is located at Brgy. Marulas, Valenzuela City. DZRB is the first radio station in the Philippines before the launch of DZRH. It operates from 4am to 10pm on weekdays and 4am to 8pm on weekends.
