XHLCE-FM is a noncommercial (social) radio station on 90.1 FM in La Cruz, Sinaloa under the name "Óscar FM". It is owned by Radio Agricultores del Valle de Sinaloa, A.C. and operated by Grupo Promomedios Mazatlán, which also owns the local cable system in La Cruz.
