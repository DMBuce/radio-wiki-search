XHLTZ-FM is a radio station in Aguascalientes, Aguascalientes. It broadcasts regional Mexican music on 106.1 FM as "La Ranchera".

XHLTZ formerly broadcast on AM as XELTZ-AM 740. It moved from Loreto in two stages, first to El Puertecito de la Virgen, and then to Aguascalientes proper in December 2002.
