XHLTN-FM (known on-air as XLTN) is a commercial Spanish AC radio station in Tijuana, Baja California, Mexico, broadcasting on 104.5 MHz. It is also heard in the U.S. city of San Diego and maintains offices in Chula Vista. The station came on air in 1970.
