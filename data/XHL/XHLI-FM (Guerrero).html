XHLI-FM/XELI-AM is a radio station in Chilpancingo, Guerrero. Broadcasting on 94.7 FM and 1580 AM, XHLI is owned by Radio S.A. and carries a pop format known as Máxima (not to be confused with XHCHH-FM "Capital Máxima").
