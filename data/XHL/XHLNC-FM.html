XHLNC-FM is a noncommercial radio station in Tecate, Baja California, Mexico, on 104.9 MHz. It broadcasts to the San Diego-Tijuana metropolitan area from a its transmitter on Cerro Bola. XHLNC-FM airs a Spanish variety format and is branded as Click XM.

From 2000 to 2018, XHLNC was a classical music station known as XLNC1, broadcasting from studios in Chula Vista, California. Since then, it has been operated by several Mexican and United States groups.
