CFPG-FM (99.1 FM, Peggy @ 99.1) is a radio station in Winnipeg. Owned by Corus Entertainment, it broadcasts a mainstream adult contemporary format. Its transmitter is located in Duff Roblin Provincial Park, while its studios are located alongside its sister stations at 201 Portage.
