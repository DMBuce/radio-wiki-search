CFPL is a radio station owned by Corus Entertainment and based in London, Ontario, Canada. Transmitter power is 10,000 watts daytime, 5,000 watts nighttime. Broadcast frequency is 980 kHz (AM). CFPL uses a four-tower directional antenna with differing patterns during the day and night. The station primarily airs news and talk programming. The studios are located in downtown London while its transmitter is located south of London at Wellington Road and Scotland Drive.
