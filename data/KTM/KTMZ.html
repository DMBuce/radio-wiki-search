KTMZ (1220 AM) is an AM radio station licensed to Pomona, California. The station is owned by Lotus Communications. KTMZ is part of a simulcast with KWKW in Los Angeles, California and airs a Spanish-language sports format as an affiliate of TUDN Radio.
