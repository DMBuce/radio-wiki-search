WQWK (1450 AM) is a sports radio station broadcasting in State College, Pennsylvania. It is currently an affiliate of ESPN Radio. It had a former affiliation with the NBC Radio Network.

It is the flagship station of the Penn State Nittany Lions radio network, and also airs local State College sports.
