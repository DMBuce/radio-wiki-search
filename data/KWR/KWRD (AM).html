KWRD (1470 AM, 98.5 FM; The Heartbeat of East Texas) is a terrestrial American radio station, relayed by an FM translator, broadcasting a Classic Country music format, including news programming from Westwood One and the Texas State Network. Licensed to Henderson, Texas, United States, the station serves the Henderson-Longview-Marshall area. The station is owned by Wanda J. Hanszen and Jerry T. Hanszen, through licensee Hanszen Broadcasting, Inc.
