KWRK (FM 96.1 MHz) is a radio station with a city of license of Window Rock, Arizona, United States. The station is owned by The Navajo Nation. It airs a country music format.

The station was assigned the KWRK call letters by the Federal Communications Commission on February 1, 1991.
