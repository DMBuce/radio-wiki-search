WQCC (106.3 FM, “Kicks 106.3”) is a radio station broadcasting a country music format. Licensed to La Crosse, Wisconsin, United States, the station serves the La Crosse area. The station is currently owned by Magnum Communications.

All the stations of the "La Crosse Radio Group" are housed at 1407 2nd Avenue North in Onalaska, Wisconsin.
