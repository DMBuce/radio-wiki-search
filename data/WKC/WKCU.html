WKCU is a Country formatted broadcast radio station licensed to Corinth, Mississippi. WKCU serves Corinth, Burnsville, and Booneville in Mississippi and Guys in Tennessee. WKCU is owned by TeleSouth Media and operated under their licensee TeleSouth Communications Inc. The station is a member of the Mississippi Association of Broadcasters.
