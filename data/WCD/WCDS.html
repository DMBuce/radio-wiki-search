WCDS (1230 AM) is a radio station that broadcasts a sports radio format. The station is licensed to Glasgow, Kentucky, and serves the Glasgow/Bowling Green radio market, including the Cave region of west-central Kentucky. It is owned by Commonwealth Broadcasting through licensee Newberry Broadcasting, Inc, and is affiliated with the ESPN Radio Network, following the same format as WWKU-AM 1450 in Bowling Green.
