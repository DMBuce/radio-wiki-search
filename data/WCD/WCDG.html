WCDG (88.7 FM) is a radio station licensed to Dahlonega, Georgia. The station broadcasts a Christian Talk and Teaching format and is owned by Silver Dove Broadcasting, Inc.

In October 2020, a deal was reached to sell WCDG to Legacy Broadcasting, owners of KYMS in Idaho.
