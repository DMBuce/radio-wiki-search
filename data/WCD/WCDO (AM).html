WCDO (1490 AM) is a radio station broadcasting an Adult Contemporary format licensed to Sidney, New York, United States. The station is owned by Dave Mance under the name of CDO Broadcasting, Inc., and features programming from Westwood One.

The station simulcasts its sister station WCDO-FM 100.9.
