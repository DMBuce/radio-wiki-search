WCDA (106.3 FM, "Hits 106.3") is a radio station broadcasting in Lexington, Kentucky, United States. It plays adult top 40 music, as it is a Mediabase Hot AC reporter. It is owned by L.M. Communications. The station's studios are located at Triangle Center in downtown Lexington, and its transmitter is located atop Lexington Financial Center, also in downtown.
