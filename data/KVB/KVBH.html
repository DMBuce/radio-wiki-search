KVBH (107.5 MHz) is a commercial FM radio station in San Antonio, Texas. The station is owned by Univision and it airs a Rhythmic Adult Contemporary radio format. Studios and offices are located on Network Boulevard in Northwest San Antonio. The transmitter site is in Elmendorf, on Elmendorf-Lavonia Road.
