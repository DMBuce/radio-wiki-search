ZSS-FM is a radio station in Nassau, Bahamas, broadcasting a gospel music format. The station has its primary transmitter (ZSS-FM 89.9 MHz) in Nassau, with a repeater (ZSS-FM-2 95.5 MHz) in Marsh Harbour.
