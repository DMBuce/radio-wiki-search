WOGO (680 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a News Talk Information format. Licensed to Hallie, Wisconsin, United States, the station serves the Eau Claire area. The station is currently owned by Stewards of Sound, Inc. WOGO was originally licensed to Cornell, Wisconsin.
