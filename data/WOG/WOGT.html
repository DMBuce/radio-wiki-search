WOGT (107.9 MHz) is a commercial FM radio station licensed to East Ridge, Tennessee. It is owned by Cumulus Media and serves the Chattanooga metropolitan area. As of November 5, 2020 WOGT is stunting with a Christmas music format.

Studios and offices are on Pineville Road in Chattanooga. The transmitter is off Pinehurst Avenue in Red Bank.
