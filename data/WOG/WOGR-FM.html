WOGR-FM (93.3 FM) is an American radio station broadcasting a Gospel music format, licensed to serve the community of Salisbury, North Carolina. The station is currently owned by Victory Christian Center, Inc., and features programming from Salem Communications which is also heard on WOGR in Charlotte.

The station was originally launched in 1977 as WNDN-FM, the college radio station of Salisbury's Catawba College. It was subsequently acquired by Victory Christian Center in 1996 to expand the broadcast area of Charlotte's WOGR.
