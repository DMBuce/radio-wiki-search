Realitatea FM or RFM is a radio station based in Bucharest. It is a talk-show/news radio station, part of Realitatea-Catavencu media group. They are broadcasting on:

  • Bucureşti 90.2 FM
  • Cluj-Napoca 92.8 FM
  • Sibiu 90.4 FM
  • Sulina 107 FM
  • Constanța 98.9 FM
