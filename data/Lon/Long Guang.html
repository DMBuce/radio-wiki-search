Long Guang (Chinese: 龙广; pinyin: Lóngguǎng), which stands for Dragon Broadcast, is the radio station group that was once the Harbin Ren Min Guangbo Dian Tai (simplified Chinese: 黑龙江人民广播电台; pinyin: Hēilóngjiāng Rénmín Guǎngbō Diàntái; Korean: 흑룡강인민방송), translated as Heilongjiang People's Broadcasting Station, which consists of several radio stations broadcasting from Harbin and the greater Heilongjiang Province area. (Evidence of Harbin Ren Min Guangbo Dian Tai is in the BBS of this radio station).
