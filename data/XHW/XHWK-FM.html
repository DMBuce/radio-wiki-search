XHWK-FM / XEWK-AM is a clear-channel AM station near Guadalajara, Jalisco, Mexico. It is licensed for 50 kilowatts during the day and 10 kilowatts at night on 1190 kHz and has its transmitter at Atemajac del Valle, Zapopan, Jalisco. It began in 1954 as a rebroadcaster of XEW-AM.
