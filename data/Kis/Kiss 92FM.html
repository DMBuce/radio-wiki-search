Kiss 92FM is an English-language radio station based in Singapore which plays current hits and music from past decades (primarily the 1990s to today) primarily competing with Class 95. It is a 24-hour radio station of SPH Radio Pte Ltd, which also owns One FM 91.3, Money FM 89.3, 96.3 Hao FM and UFM100.3.

The station works closely with its parent company to provide information and freebies to its listeners. For example, some articles read are selected from The Straits Times, Lianhe Zaobao, My Paper, Shin Min Daily News and Lianhe Wanbao.
