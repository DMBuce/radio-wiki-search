KIIS Extra 95.8 is a radio station broadcasting on 95.8 MHz Stereo based in Corfu. It serves Corfu, the Ionian Islands, Epirus and the western portion of Greece. The station is a mixture of variety eighties (80's) till today's hits. The station was originally launched in 1994 as TOP FM Corfu. On 25 November 1997, it was relaunched and renamed as Energy (NRG) Corfu.

On 22 January 2014, relaunched again and renamed as KISS FM 95,8 Corfu making it one of newest stations in the country. On 8 January 2016, renamed as KIIS Extra FM 95,8.

Anastasios Mexas is the program director from January 2014 and the owner of this station from November 2017.

The station's logo plagiarises the logos of Australian radio stations KIIS 106.5 and KIIS 101.1.
