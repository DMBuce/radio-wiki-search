Kiss (styled as KISS) is a British National Digital radio station owned & operated by Bauer as part of the Kiss Network. It broadcasts nationally to the UK on DAB Digital Radio, as well as on FM in London, Bristol and the Severn Estuary, and East Anglia. The station started as Kiss FM - a 1980s pirate radio station that was to become the UK's first legal radio station specialising in black and dance music.
