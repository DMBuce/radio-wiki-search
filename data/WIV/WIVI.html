WIVI (102.1 FM, "Pirate Radio") is a radio station licensed to serve Cruz Bay, U.S. Virgin Islands. The station is licensed to Ackley Caribbean Enterprises, Inc. which is wholly owned by Gordon P. Ackley as part of the Ackley Media Group. It airs a classic rock format.

The Federal Communications Commission assigned this station the call sign WDCM on August 24, 1988, which it held until switching to WWKS call letters on February 26, 1996.

The station swapped call signs and formats with sister station WIVI on July 1, 2017.
