WIVG (96.1 FM, "Sports 56/96.1") is a radio station licensed to Tunica, Mississippi, serving the Memphis, Tennessee area with an Sports format. Owned by Flinn Broadcasting, the station's studios are located in Southeast Memphis, and the transmitter site is in Coldwater, Mississippi near Arkabutla Lake.

In June 2019, WIVG changed their format from alternative rock to a simulcast of sports-formatted WHBQ 560 AM Memphis, TN.
