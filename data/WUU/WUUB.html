WUUB (106.3 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a sports format. Licensed to serve Jupiter, Florida, United States, the station is owned by Good Karma Broadcasting, LLC. The station is also broadcast on HD radio. The stations studios are in West Palm Beach and the transmitter tower is in Riviera Beach.
