Channel 103 is an Independent Local Radio station broadcasting to the island of Jersey on 103.7 FM. The station launched on October 25, 1992. Channel 103 is owned by the Tindle Radio Group, which also owns Island FM in Guernsey and Midlands 103 in Ireland.
