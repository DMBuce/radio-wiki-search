KBGX (105.3 FM) is a radio station broadcasting an Oldies format. Licensed to Keaau, Hawaii, the station serves the Hilo area. The station is currently owned by Matthew Clapp, Jr., through licensee Mahalo Multimedia, LLC, and features programming from Citadel Media's "The True Oldies Channel" satellite feed.
