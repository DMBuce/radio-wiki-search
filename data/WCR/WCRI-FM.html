WCRI-FM (95.9 FM; "Classical 95.9") is a classical music-formatted radio station on Block Island, Rhode Island affiliated with the World Classical Network. The station is owned by Judson Group, Inc., a company that includes the son and grandsons of broadcasting pioneer Ted Jones, founder of Charles River Broadcasting and Boston classical music station WCRB.
