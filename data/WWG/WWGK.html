WWGK (1540 AM) - branded 1540 KNR2 - is a commercial daytime-only sports radio station licensed to Cleveland, Ohio, providing limited coverage to Greater Cleveland. Owned by Good Karma Brands, WWGK serves as the secondary ESPN Radio affiliate for Cleveland, and alongside sister station WKNR forms a duopoly branded ESPN Cleveland

The WWGK studios are located in Downtown Cleveland. The transmitter tower is located on Euclid Avenue at East 81st Street in the Fairfax section of Cleveland. In addition to a standard analog transmission, WWGK is available online.
