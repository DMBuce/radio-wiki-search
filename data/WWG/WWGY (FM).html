WWGY and WRQR-FM are known as "Froggy 94.3 & 99.3" (WWGY) and "Rocky 105.5" (WRQR). Froggy 94.3 & 99.3 is a country music formatted radio station licensed to Fulton, Kentucky and WRQR-FM broadcasting a classic rock format licensed to Paris, Tennessee.

WWGY serves Union City, Tennessee, Fulton, Kentucky and Martin, Tennessee, while WRQR-FM serves Paris, Tennessee, McKenzie, Tennessee and Murray, Kentucky.

The nationally syndicated Ace & TJ Show can be heard on "KF-KQ".

Both WWGY and WRQR-FM are owned and operated by Forever Media, through licensee Forever South Licenses, LLC.

On February 3, 2020 WWGY changed their format from top 40/CHR to country, branded as "Froggy 94.3 & 99.3", while WRQR-FM changed their format from top 40/CHR to classic rock, branded as "Rocky 105.5".
