WUVI (1090 AM) was a radio station licensed to serve Charlotte Amalie, U.S. Virgin Islands. The station was owned by Three Angels Corporation and leased to the University of the Virgin Islands. Its license was cancelled September 23, 2019.

WUVI aired a College radio format using student on-air talent from the university's radio program. News programming from Pacifica Radio was also broadcast.
