KHXM (1370 kHz) is a commercial AM radio station licensed to Pearl City, Hawaii, USA. The station is owned by George Hochman through licensee Hochman Hawaii Two, Inc. KHXM operates with 6,200 watts from a non-directional transmitter near Mililani Cemetery Road in Pearl City.

KHXM broadcasts Chinese language programming to the Honolulu media market, covering the island of O'ahu.
