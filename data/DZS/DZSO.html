DZSO (720 AM) Bombo Radyo is a radio station owned and operated by Bombo Radyo Philippines. The station's studio and transmitter are located at Bombo Radyo Broadcast Center, Pennsylvania Ave., Parian, San Fernando, La Union. Bombo Radyo La Union provides news, public service, drama, and music for the audiences of La Union, and the nearby provinces like Ilocos Sur, Pangasinan, and Benguet. DZSO operates daily from 4:00 AM to 9:30 PM.
