104.1 Spirit FM is an FM radio station owned and operated by the Roman Catholic Apostolic Vicariate of Calapan in the Philippines. The station's studio and transmitter are located along Durian St. near St. Benedict Chaplaincy, Brgy. Lalud, Calapan.
