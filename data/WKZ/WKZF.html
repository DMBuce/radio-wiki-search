WKZF is an FM radio station licensed for 102.3 MHz in Morton, Illinois near Peoria. It is owned by Midwest Communications, along with WXCL, WSWT, WIRL, WPBG and WMBD (AM). The stations share studios on the 12th floor of The Civic Center Plaza Building in downtown Peoria.
