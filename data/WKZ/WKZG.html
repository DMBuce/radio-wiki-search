WKZG (104.3 FM) is a radio station licensed to Seymour, Wisconsin and serving the Fox Cities and Northeast Wisconsin. Owned and operated by Woodward Communications, WKZG airs an adult contemporary format. WKZG's studios are located on College Avenue in Appleton, while its transmitter is located in Seymour.
