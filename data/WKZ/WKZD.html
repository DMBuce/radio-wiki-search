WKZD (1310 AM) is a Christian talk radio formatted radio station licensed to Priceville, Alabama, that serves Decatur, Alabama, and the western portion of the Huntsville, Alabama, market. The station is owned by Somerville Baptist Church. The station's programming is available for live streaming over the internet via its website.
