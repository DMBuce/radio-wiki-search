WKZU 104.9 FM is a radio station licensed to Iuka, Mississippi. The station has a Classic Country music format, with music from the 1960s, 1970s, 1980s, and 1990s. The station's target audience is 32- to 54-year-old adults who started listening to country music in the 1970s and 1980s.
