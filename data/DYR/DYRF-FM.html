DYRF (99.5 FM) - branded as 99.5 Star FM Iloilo - is a music and news FM radio station owned and operated by People's Broadcasting Service, Inc. and managed by Bombo Radyo Holdings, Inc.. The station's studio, offices and transmitter is located at the 3rd floor R. Florete Building, Rizal Cor. Iznart Streets, Iloilo City. Star FM Iloilo operates daily from 4:00 AM to 11:00 PM, except Holy Week of each year where it goes off the air at 3:00 PM of Good Friday and lasts until 4:00 AM of Easter Sunday.

Upon the established of Bombo Radyo's first FM radio station in 1976, DYRF serves as the network's flagship FM station of the Bombo Radyo Philippines in Iloilo City.
