DYRB (540 AM) Radyo Pilipino is a radio station owned and operated by the Radio Corporation of the Philippines. Its studio is located at 2nd Floor, Unit #4 M. Pacubas Dr., Brgy. Mambaling, Cebu City, and the transmitter located at Alumnos, Brgy. Basak San Nicolas, Cebu City. It operates daily from 4:00 AM to 9:00 PM.
