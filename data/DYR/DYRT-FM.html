DYRT (99.5 FM) - on-air as Barangay RT 99.5 - is a radio station owned and operated by GMA Network Inc.. The station's studio are located at the GMA Skyview Complex, Nivel Hills, Apas, Cebu City, while its transmitter facilities are located at Mt. Busay, Brgy. Bonbon, Cebu City. It operates 24 hours a day. DYRT is the first provincial FM station of RGMA.
