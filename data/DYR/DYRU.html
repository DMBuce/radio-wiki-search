DYRU (currently branded as Barangay RU 92.9 Super Radyo) is a news and music FM radio station owned by RGMA, a subsidiary of GMA Network Inc. The station's studio and transmitter is located at Torres-Oliva Bldg., Roxas Ave. Ext., Brgy. Andagao, Kalibo, Aklan. At present, Barangay RU 92.9 Super Radyo Kalibo is considered as one of the top stations in the FM band in Kalibo, Aklan.
