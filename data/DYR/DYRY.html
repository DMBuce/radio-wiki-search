Power 102.1 RFM (DYRY 102.1 MHz) is an FM station owned and operated by Gold Label Broadcasting System. Its studios and transmitter are located at Naranghita St., Brgy. Poblacion, Mabinay. It is managed by Municipal SB Member Beverly "Berly" Ruiz.
