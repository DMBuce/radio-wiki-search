DYRD (102.3 FM), broadcasting as 102.3 Kiss FM, is a radio station owned and operated by the Bohol Chronicle Radio Corporation. The station's studio and transmitter facilities are located at #56 Bernardino Inting St., Tagbilaran. Based on a survey conducted by Holy Name University Center for Research and Publications in 2017, it is ranked as the most listened to FM station.
