DYRD (1161 AM) is a radio station owned and operated by the Bohol Chronicle Radio Corporation. The station's studio is located at #56 Bernardino Inting St., Tagbilaran, and its transmitter facilities are located along Burgos St., Tagbilaran. Based on a survey conducted by Holy Name University Center for Research and Publications in 2016, it is ranked as the most listened to AM station.
