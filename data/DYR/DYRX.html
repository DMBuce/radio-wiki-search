DYRX (103.7 MHz Roxas City) is branded as 103.7 Star FM Roxas, a music and news FM radio station owned by Consolidated Broadcasting System, Inc. under Bombo Radyo Philippines. The station's studio and transmitter are located at the Bombo Radyo Broadcast Center, Arnaldo Boulevard, Roxas City.
