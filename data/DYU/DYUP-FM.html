DYUP 102.7 FM is a music and non-commercial educational FM college radio station operating in Miagao, Iloilo, the Philippines affiliated with the University of the Philippines Visayas. The radio format of the station is student information, entertainment, technology transfer, news and music. It also serves as a training ground for broadcasting and communication students.
