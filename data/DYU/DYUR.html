DYUR (105.1 FM), broadcasting as Halo Halo 105.1, is a radio station owned and operated by Viva Live, Inc., a subsidiary of Viva Entertainment. The station's studio and transmitter are located at the 2nd floor of AMP Building, Veterans Drive, Lower Nivel Hills, Lahug, Cebu City. Halo Halo operates daily from 4:00 AM to 1:00 AM.
