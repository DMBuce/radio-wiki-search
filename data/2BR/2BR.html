Two Boroughs Radio (known on air as 2BR) was a local radio station serving East and Central Lancashire, England. It was owned and operated by Global and broadcast from studios at Clayton-le-Moors near Accrington.

2BR was merged with Capital Manchester and relaunched as part of the Capital radio network on 8 April 2019.
