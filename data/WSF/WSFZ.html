WSFZ (930 AM) is a radio station licensed to serve Jackson, Mississippi. The station is owned by SSR Communications, Inc. As of December 1, 2020, WSFZ is broadcasting an African-American oriented all-newsformat. It is in the process of being sold to the parent company of BIN, iHeartMedia.
