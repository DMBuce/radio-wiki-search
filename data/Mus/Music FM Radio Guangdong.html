Music FM Radio Guangdong (Chinese: 广东电台音乐之声; lit. 'Radio of Guangdong, Sound of Music'), which uses the moniker "Music FM", is a music radio station on 99.3 FM in Guangzhou, Guangdong, China. It is owned by Radio Guangdong, a part of Guangdong Radio and Television.
