CJNU-FM (93.7 MHz) is a Canadian radio station in Winnipeg, Manitoba. Owned by the Nostalgia Broadcasting Cooperative, the station plays an adult standards radio format. CJNU-FM's offices and studios are in One Lombard Place. The transmitter is located on Roslyn Road in Osborne Village.
