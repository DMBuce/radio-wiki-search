KRMS (1150 AM, "NewsTalk KRMS, 1150AM - 97.5 FM") is a radio station licensed to serve Osage Beach, Missouri, United States. The station, established in December 1952, is owned by Viper Communications, Inc., and broadcasts news/talk programming to central Missouri.
