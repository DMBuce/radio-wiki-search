KRMQ-FM (101.5 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a classic hits format. Licensed to Clovis, New Mexico, United States, the station serves the Clovis area. The station is currently owned by Rooney Moon Broadcasting, Inc. and features programming from Westwood One.
