WINU (104.9 MHz, "Alt 104.9") is a commercial FM radio station licensed to Altamont and serving New York's Capital District. The station is owned by Pamal Broadcasting and airs an alternative rock radio format.

WINU has an effective radiated power (ERP) of 530 watts. The transmitter is in the Helderberg Mountain antenna farm off Pinnacle Road in the Voorheesville section of New Scotland.
