KIIS 1065 (call sign: 2WFM) is a commercial FM radio station in Sydney, Australia, on a frequency of 106.5 MHz. KIIS 1065 is one of the flagship stations on the Australian Radio Network's KIIS Network. The station's headline show is The Kyle and Jackie O Show.
