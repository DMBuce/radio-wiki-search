KXUS (97.3 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a mainstream rock format. Licensed to Springfield, Missouri, United States, it serves the Springfield area. The station is currently owned by iHeartMedia, Inc. and licensed to Clear Channel Broadcasting Licenses.
