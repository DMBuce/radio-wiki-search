KGFW (1340 AM) is an American radio station broadcasting a News Talk Information format. Licensed to Kearney, Nebraska, United States, the station serves the Grand Island-Kearney area. The station is owned by NRG Media and features programming from Fox News Radio, Premiere Radio Networks and Westwood One. KGFW is a sister station to KQKY and KRNY.
