KGFL (1110 AM, "Cruisin Oldies 94.7 & 1110") is a radio station broadcasting an oldies music format. Licensed to Clinton, Arkansas, United States, the station is currently owned by King-Sullivan Radio.

1110 AM is United States clear-channel frequency, on which KFAB in Omaha, Nebraska and WBT in Charlotte, North Carolina are the dominant Class A stations. KGFL must leave the air during nighttime hours to protect the skywave signals of those stations. The FM translator on 94.7 MHz provides 24 hour coverage of the Cruisin' Oldies format.
