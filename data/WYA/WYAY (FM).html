WYAY (106.3 FM, "Carolina Country") is a radio station broadcasting a country music format. Licensed to Bolivia, North Carolina, United States, WYAY serves the Wilmington area. The station is currently owned by Andrulonis Media as part of its "Carolina Country" network.
