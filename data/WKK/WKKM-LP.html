WKKM-LP is an American low-power FM radio station, licensed to the city of Harrison, Michigan. The station operates on a frequency of 106.5 MHz with an effective radiated power of 83 watts. The station is owned by The Country King, Incorporated; studios, offices and transmitter facilities are at 209 East Spruce Street in Harrison.
