KMOM (105.5 FM, "105.5 Maverick FM") is a radio station broadcasting a country music format. Licensed to Roscoe, South Dakota, United States, the station serves the Aberdeen area. The station is currently owned by Dakota Broadcasting.

The call sign was previously used for an AM radio station at 1070 AM licensed to Monticello, Minnesota, United States, which operated during the 1980s and early 1990s.

On May 23, 2020 KMOM rebranded as "105.5 Maverick FM".
