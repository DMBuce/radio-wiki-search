KMOA (89.7 FM) is a non-commercial educational radio station licensed to serve the community of Nu'uuli, a village on the central east coast of Tutuila Island, American Samoa. The station is owned by Global Teen Challenge and the broadcast license is held by Teen Challenge of American Samoa.
