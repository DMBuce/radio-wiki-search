NPO Radio 2 is a public-service radio station from the Netherlands, broadcasting in an adult hits format, focusing on the 1980s and 1990s, however 1960s and 1970s hits may also air. It is part of the Netherlands Public Broadcasting system, NPO.

According to 2018 statistics, Radio 2 has a 12% audience market share, a number higher than any other station in the Netherlands.
