KBCK (1400 AM, "The Buck") is a radio station licensed to serve Deer Lodge, Montana. The station is owned by Robert Cummings Toole. It airs a Classic Country music format.

The station was assigned the KBCK call letters by the Federal Communications Commission on June 20, 2002.
