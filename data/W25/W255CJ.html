W255CJ FM 98.9 is a 180-watt "broadcast translator" radio station in Atlanta, Georgia, US, and returned to the air on December 21, 2018 after being off the air due to a dispute with another station on the same frequency, in a case that was being reviewed by the FCC. It's airing an alternative rock format known as 99X.
