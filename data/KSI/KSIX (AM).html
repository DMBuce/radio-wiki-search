KSIX (1230 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a local sports talk format featuring 2 local shows as well as Dan Patrick, Jim Rome and nights and weekends from Yahoo! Sports Radio, with additional programming from Westwood One. Licensed to Corpus Christi, Texas owned by SPORTSRADIOCC, LLC. The station is also simulcast on 95.1 FM and 96.1 FM in Corpus Christi.
