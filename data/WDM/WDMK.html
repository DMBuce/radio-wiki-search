WDMK; (105.9 FM, "105.9 Kiss-FM") is a radio station in Detroit. Owned by Beasley Broadcast Group, it broadcasts an urban adult contemporary format.

The station broadcasts from an antenna 725 feet in height with an effective radiated power of 20,000 watts. The station's transmitter is located in the Detroit suburb of Oak Park at the intersection of 10 Mile Road and Greenfield. Its studios, along with those of WGPR, are on Detroit's lower eastside.
