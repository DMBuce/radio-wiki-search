WDMC (920 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a religious format. Licensed to Melbourne, Florida, United States, the station is currently owned by Divine Mercy Communications.

WDMC has been granted an FCC construction permit to move to a different transmitter site, increase day power to 8,000 watts and night power to 4,000 watts.
