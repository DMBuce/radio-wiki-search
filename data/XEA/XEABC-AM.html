XEABC-AM is a radio station in Mexico City, licensed to San Sebastián Chimalpa in the State of Mexico. Broadcasting on 760 kHz, XEABC is owned by México Radio, S.A. de C.V., concessionaire for the Estudios Tepeyac division of Organización Editorial Mexicana, and broadcasts a talk radio format as "ABC Radio 760 AM".

760 AM is a United States clear-channel frequency. WJR in Detroit, Michigan is the only Class A station on this frequency.
