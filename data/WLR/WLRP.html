WLRP (1460 AM, "Radio Raíces 1460 AM") is a radio station licensed to serve San Sebastián, Puerto Rico. The station is owned by Las Raíces Pepinianas, Inc. The station has been on the air since January 6, 1965. It airs news, talk shows, and music in Spanish (Spanish Variety). The station was assigned the WLRP call letters by the Federal Communications Commission on July 12, 1982.
