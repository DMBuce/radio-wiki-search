WLRO (1210 AM was a radio station serving the Baton Rouge area of the U.S. state of Louisiana. The station broadcast with an ERP of 10 kW-D/1 kW-N power and was licensed to Denham Springs (where the transmitter was located). Its studios were located east of downtown Baton Rouge near the I-10/I-12 interchange.
