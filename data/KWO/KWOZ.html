KWOZ is a radio station airing a country music format licensed to Mountain View, Arkansas, broadcasting on 103.3 MHz FM. The station serves the areas of Searcy, Arkansas, Mountain Home, Arkansas, Batesville, Arkansas, and Newport, Arkansas, and is owned by WRD Entertainment, Inc.
