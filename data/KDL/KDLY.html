KDLY (97.5 FM, "Classic Hits 97.5") is a radio station broadcasting a classic hits music format. Licensed to Lander, Wyoming, United States, the station serves the Riverton area and the Wind River Reservation as well as most of Central Wyoming. The station is currently owned by Fremont Broadcasting, Inc.
