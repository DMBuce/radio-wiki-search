KDLW is a commercial radio station located in Los Lunas, New Mexico, broadcasting to the Albuquerque, New Mexico area on 106.3 FM.

KDLW broadcasts at 100,000 watts from a site near Los Lunas and covers Albuquerque from the south, and also serves communities in Valencia County and Central New Mexico. KDLW is owned by Vanguard Media and airs a Regional Mexican format branded as "La Zeta 106.3".
