KIBB (97.1 FM) is a radio station broadcasting an adult hits format branded as "Bob FM". Licensed to Haven, Kansas, the station serves the Wichita area. The station is currently owned by Rocking M Media. The station's studios are located in Wellington, Kansas, and the transmitter is in Colwich, Kansas.
