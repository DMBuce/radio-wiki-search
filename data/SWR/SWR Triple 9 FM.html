SWR Triple 9 FM (Formerly SWR FM) (ACMA callsign: 2SWR) is a community radio station based in Blacktown in Sydney. The station broadcasts to parts of Greater Western Sydney, but can be received in most of the Sydney metropolitan area.

The mission of SWR Triple 9 is to offer the community a voice and an opportunity for anyone to access the airwaves regardless of age, gender, nationality or experience. SWR Triple 9 is a mostly volunteer run organisation and is funded through listener support, grants and limited commercial sponsorship.
