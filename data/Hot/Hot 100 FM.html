Hot 100 (call sign: 8HOT) is a CHR-formatted commercial radio station in Darwin, Northern Territory, Australia. The station begin broadcasting on 1 July 1991 and has a relay in Katherine and Jabiru. The positioning statement is "Darwin's Hit Music Station".

The current programming lineup consists of Dannii and Brent for breakfast, Xylon McEwan for mornings, Jax, Sarah in the afternoons, Kate, Tim & Joel, and The Random 30 Countdown. On the weekends, the station does not air networked programming.

The current programming director is Dave Daly and the general manager is Michael Harvey.
