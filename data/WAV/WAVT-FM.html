WAVT-FM (101.9 FM, "T102") is a commercial FM radio station licensed to serve Pottsville, Pennsylvania. The station is owned by Pottsville Broadcasting Company and broadcasts a Hot Adult Contemporary format. The station also broadcasts local high school sports and Penn State Nittany Lions football.
