WTLC-AM is an AM radio station owned by Radio One in Indianapolis, Indiana. The station operates on the AM radio frequency of 1310 kHz. The studios are located downtown at the corner of Meridian and St. Joseph Streets. The transmitter and antenna, according to the FCC, are located on the south side of Indianapolis between Troy and Sumner Avenues near the Indiana Railroad (the same block as WTTV's former studios).
