KSHQ 100.7 FM is a radio station licensed to Deerfield, Missouri. The station broadcasts a Sports radio format and is owned by Nancy Miller, through licensee One Media, Inc. The station is also simulcast on WMBH 1560 AM to reach the Joplin market better.
