KAAK (98.9 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a Top 40 (CHR) format. Licensed to Great Falls, Montana, United States, the station serves the Great Falls area. The station is currently owned by Cherry Creek Radio and licensed to CCR-Great Falls IV, LLC.
