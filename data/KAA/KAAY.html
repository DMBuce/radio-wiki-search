KAAY (1090 kHz) is a commercial AM radio station in Little Rock, Arkansas, owned by Cumulus Media. It airs a religious format of instruction and preaching, with most of the schedule made up of paid brokered programming, featuring local and national religious leaders, including Charles Stanley, Jim Daly, John F. MacArthur and Albert Pendarvis. Overnight, automated Contemporary Christian music is heard. The station's studios are located in West Little Rock, and the transmitter is located off McDonald Road in Wrightsville, Arkansas.

KAAY is Arkansas's primary entry point station for the Emergency Alert System.
