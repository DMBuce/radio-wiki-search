WKVM (810 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a Religious format. Licensed to San Juan, Puerto Rico, United States, it serves the Puerto Rico area. The station is currently owned by Grupo RTC, under its licensee Catholic, Apostolic & Roman Church in Puerto Rico.
