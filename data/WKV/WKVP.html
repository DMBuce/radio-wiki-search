WKVP (106.9 FM, "K-LOVE") is a non-commercial FM radio station licensed to serve Camden, New Jersey in the Philadelphia media market. The station is owned and operated by Educational Media Foundation and is an affiliate of K-LOVE, EMF's contemporary Christian music network. Its broadcast tower is located southeast of Camden at (39°54′34.0″N 75°5′59.0″W).
