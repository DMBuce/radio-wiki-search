WKVS (103.3 FM) is a radio station licensed to Lenoir, North Carolina, United States. The station is currently owned by Foothills Radio Group, LLC. The station currently carries local live programming with the "Cash in the Morning" morning show, "Middays with Rachael" and "The Drive Home with Wild Bill". WKVS is also the home of the popular Saturday morning sports talk show "The Scoreboard" with David Jones, Jeff Link and Justin Carlton. WKVS broadcasts high school football games mainly in Caldwell, Burke and Catawba Counties.
