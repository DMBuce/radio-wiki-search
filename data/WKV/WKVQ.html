WKVQ (1540 AM) is a Christian radio station broadcasting a Christian talk and teaching format. Licensed to Eatonton, Georgia, United States, the station is currently owned by Craig Baker. WKVQ is a sister station to WYTH 1250 AM in Madison, Georgia, but does not simulcast.
