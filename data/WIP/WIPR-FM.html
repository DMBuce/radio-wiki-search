WIPR-FM (91.3 FM, "Allegro 91.3") is a radio station broadcasting a classical format, but the station also broadcasts some NPR programs. Licensed to San Juan, Puerto Rico, the station is currently owned by la Corporación de Puerto Rico para la Difusión Pública and is branded as Allegro 91.3.
