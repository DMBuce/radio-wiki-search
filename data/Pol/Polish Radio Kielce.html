Polish Radio Kielce (Polskie Radio Kielce) - is the regional broadcaster based in Kielce. Radio Kielce is a part of public radio in Poland Polish Radio. It can be received in the Świętokrzyskie Voivodeship and partly in neighboring voivodeships.
