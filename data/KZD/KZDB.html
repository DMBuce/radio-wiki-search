KZDB (100.5 FM) is a radio station licensed to Roswell, New Mexico, United States. The station is currently owned Majestic Broadcasting, LLC.

On August 1, 2017, KZDB changed their format from classic rock (which moved to KSFX 1230 AM) to adult contemporary, branded as "100.5 Kool FM".
