WQXA-FM (105.7 FM, "105.7 The X") is a commercial FM radio station licensed to serve York, Pennsylvania. Owned by Cumulus Media, it broadcasts an active rock format serving South Central Pennsylvania. Its studios are located at 2300 Vartan Way in Harrisburg, Pennsylvania and the station's broadcast tower is located near York at (39°59′56.0″N 76°41′42.0″W).
