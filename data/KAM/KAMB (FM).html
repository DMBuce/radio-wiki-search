KAMB (101.5 FM, Celebration Radio) is an American radio station broadcasting a Contemporary Christian format. Licensed to Merced, California, United States, KAMB is an independent, commercial-free, listener-supported station and serves portions of Northern California including areas of the Central Valley and Bay Area.
