CICW-FM is a radio station broadcasting community radio programming and a variety of music on the frequency of 101.1 MHz/FM in Elora and Fergus located in Centre Wellington, Ontario, Canada. The station's branding is The Grand @ 101 (formerly The Grand @ 92.9).
