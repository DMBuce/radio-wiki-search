CICS-FM is a Canadian radio station, which broadcasts at 91.7 MHz in Sudbury, Ontario. The station airs a country format, branded as Pure Country 91.7. The studio and offices are located at 60 Elm Street, and the station transmits a 50,000 watt signal from the CBC tower.
