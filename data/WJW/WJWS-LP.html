WJWS-LP (93.7 FM) is a radio station licensed to serve the community of Jasper, Indiana. The station is owned by Greater Jasper Consolidated Schools. It airs a variety format.

The station was assigned the WJWS-LP call letters by the Federal Communications Commission on February 18, 2014.
