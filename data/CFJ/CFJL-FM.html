CFJL-FM (100.5 MHz, Hot 100.5) is a radio station in Winnipeg, Manitoba. Owned by Evanov Radio Group, it broadcasts a classic hits format focusing on songs from the 1990s and early-2000s.

CFJL-FM has studios and offices co-located with sister stations CKJS and CHWE-FM at 520 Corydon Avenue. The transmitter is located off St. Mary's Road at Duff Roblin Provincial Park in Saint Germain.
