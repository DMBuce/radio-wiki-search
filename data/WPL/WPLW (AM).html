WPLW (570 AM; "Star 92.9") is a hot adult contemporary radio station, licensed to Raleigh, North Carolina, which serves the Triangle region, including Durham and Chapel Hill. Its studios are located in Raleigh, and the transmitter is co-located at the WPTF transmitter site in Cary. WPLW's programming is also carried on two translator stations in Raleigh, W225DF (92.9 FM) and W262CZ (100.3 FM). From November through December, Star 92.9 plays Christmas music for the holiday season.
