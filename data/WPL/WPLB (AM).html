WPLB (1070 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a classic hip hop format. Licensed to Plattsburgh, New York, United States, the station serves the Burlington-Plattsburgh area. The station is owned by Loud Media. WPLB's programming is also heard on translator station W279DE (103.7 FM) in Plattsburgh.
