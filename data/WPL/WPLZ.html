WPLZ (95.3 FM, Big FM) is a classic hits formatted radio station licensed to Ooltewah, Tennessee, United States, and serves the Chattanooga area. The station is currently owned by J. L. Brewer Broadcasting of Cleveland, LLC.

WPLZ has their home studio at the Chattanooga offices of Brewer Broadcasting at 1305 Carter Street, along with sister stations Power 94, Groove 93 and ESPN 105.1.
