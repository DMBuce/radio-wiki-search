WPLW-FM (102.5 MHz) is a commercial FM radio station licensed to Hillsborough, North Carolina, and serving the Research Triangle, including Durham and Raleigh. It broadcasts a Top 40 - CHR radio format, which is simulcast on sister station 96.9 WWPL Goldsboro. The stations are owned by New Century Media Group. According to FCC ownership filings, New Century Media Group is 100% owned by Donald W. Curtis, Chairman and CEO of Curtis Media Group.

WPLW-FM has an effective radiated power (ERP) of 1,500 watts and is considered a Class A FM station. By contrast, some Raleigh-area stations, such as WNCB and WRDU run 100,000 watts. The transmitter is off Bivins Road north of Durham.
