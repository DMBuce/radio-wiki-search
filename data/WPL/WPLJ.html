WPLJ (95.5 FM) is a non-commercial contemporary Christian music radio station licensed to New York City. WPLJ is owned by the Educational Media Foundation (EMF) and broadcasts EMF's flagship programming service, K-Love. WPLJ's transmitter is located at the Empire State Building, and broadcasts four HD Radio digital subchannels in addition to its analog transmission.
