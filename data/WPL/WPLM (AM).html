WPLM (1390 AM) is a radio station licensed to serve Plymouth, Massachusetts, United States. WPLM broadcasts to the South Shore and Cape Cod areas. The station is owned by Plymouth Rock Broadcasting Co., Inc. WPLM is listed as silent since June 7, 2018; it most recently simulcast the adult contemporary format of sister station WPLM-FM (99.1).

On June 14, 2019, WPLM (AM) was granted the approval to resume operations and is back on the air simulcasting sister station, 99.1 FM WPLM-FM as, Easy 99.1.
