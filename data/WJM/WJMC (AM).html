WJMC (1240 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a full-service format of middle of the road music, news, and talk. Licensed to Rice Lake, Wisconsin, United States, the station serves the Rice Lake-Spooner area. The station is currently owned by TKC, Inc. and features programming from Westwood One.
