WJMP was a commercial daytime-only radio station licensed to serve Kent, Ohio at 1520 AM, and covered the Akron, Ohio radio market. The station broadcast from 1965 to 2016, ceasing operations when the owners turned in the license to the Federal Communications Commission (FCC) for cancellation.
