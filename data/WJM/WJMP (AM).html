WJMP (1120 kHz) is a commercial AM radio station licensed to Maryville, Tennessee, and serving the Knoxville radio market. It airs a Classic Hip Hop radio format and is owned by Loud Media, through licensee Mid-Century Radio LLC.

WJMP broadcasts at 1,000 watts by day. But because AM 1120 is a clear channel frequency reserved for KMOX in St. Louis, Missouri, WJMP must sign-off at sunset to avoid interference. Listeners in the Knoxville metropolitan area can hear WJMP around the clock on its FM translator station, W246DH at 97.1 MHz, and could previously be heard on W268BP at 101.5Â MHz.
