WJMO (1300 AM) – branded Praise 94.5 – is a commercial urban gospel radio station licensed to serve Cleveland, Ohio. Owned by Radio One, the station covers Greater Cleveland. Besides a standard analog transmission, the station also simulcasts over low-power FM translator W233CG (94.5 FM) and the HD digital subchannel of co-owned WENZ, and streams online. The WJMO studios are located along the Euclid Avenue Corridor in Cleveland's east side, while the station transmitter resides in the Cleveland suburb of Parma.
