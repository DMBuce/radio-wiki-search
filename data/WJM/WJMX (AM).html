WJMX (1400 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a news/talk format. Licensed to Darlington, South Carolina, United States, it serves the Florence area. The station is owned by iHeartMedia, Inc., through licensee AMFM Radio Licenses, L.L.C., and features programming from Westwood One. Its studios are located in Florence, and its transmitter is located south of Dillon.
