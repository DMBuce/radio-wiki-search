KFSW (1650 AM / 98.7 FM, "The Cross 98.7") is a radio station broadcasting a southern gospel format to the Fort Smith, Arkansas, United States, area. The station is licensed to G2 Media Group LLC.

KFSW's AM signal has one of the largest areas of coverage for AM stations in Fort Smith, while the FM signal at K254AM-FM 98.7 broadcasts in a radius from Fort Smith reaching as far away as Spiro and Muldrow in Oklahoma as well as Greenwood and Van Buren in Arkansas.
