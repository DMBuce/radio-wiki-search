KFSH-FM (95.9 FM, "95.9 The Fish") is a commercial religious radio station serving Orange County and southern Los Angeles County, California. The station is owned by Salem Media Group and has broadcast a contemporary Christian music (CCM) format since 2000. The station has studios in Glendale, California, with the transmitter based in the city of Orange. The branding refers to the ichthys, the ancient fish icon used by the Christian church since its birth during the time of the Roman Empire. The ichthys itself is part of the station logo.
