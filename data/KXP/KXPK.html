KXPK (96.5 MHz "La Tricolor 96.5") is a commercial FM radio station licensed to Evergreen, Colorado, and serving the Denver-Boulder radio market. The station is owned by Entravision and it airs a Regional Mexican radio format. Its studios are located in Denver near Sports Authority Field at Mile High, and the transmitter is on Squaw Mountain in Idaho Springs, Colorado.

KXPK's main transmitter has an effective radiated power (ERP) of 100,000 watts. Programming is also heard on a 500 watt booster station in Boulder, Colorado, KXPK-1-FM, also at 96.5 MHz.
