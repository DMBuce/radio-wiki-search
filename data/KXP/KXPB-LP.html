KXPB-LP (89.1 FM, "Your host on the Coast") is a radio station broadcasting a variety music format. Licensed to Pacific Beach, Washington, United States, the station is currently owned by the M.A.A.P. Foundation. It serves Pacific Beach and Moclips, Washington.
