KNIA (1320 AM / 95.3 FM, "Real Country AM 1320") is a full-service radio station licensed to serve Knoxville, Iowa, United States. The station is owned by M and H Broadcasting, Inc.

KNIA broadcasts a country music format with news and other public affairs programming. On Sundays, Gospel music and other religious programming is aired.
