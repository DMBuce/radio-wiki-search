WOJO (105.1 FM) "Que Buena 105.1" is a radio station broadcasting a regional Mexican format. Licensed to Evanston, Illinois, United States, the station serves the Chicago area. The station is currently owned by Tichenor License Corporation, a division of Uforia Audio Network.

WOJO's studios are located at 625 North Michigan Avenue in downtown Chicago, and its transmitter is located atop the John Hancock Building.
