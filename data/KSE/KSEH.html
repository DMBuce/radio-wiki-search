KSEH is a Spanish-language Soft AC radio station branded as La Suavecita 94.5[1]. It is owned by Entravision Communications and broadcasts a 50,000 watt signal located on the frequency of 94.5 FM to the Imperial Valley, Yuma, Mexicali and San Luis Rio Colorado areas. The city of license is Brawley, California.
