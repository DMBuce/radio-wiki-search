WYMC (1430 AM) is a radio station licensed to Mayfield, Kentucky, United States. The station airs an Oldies/Full service format and is currently owned by JDM Communications, Inc. WYMC is also heard on 93.9 FM through a translator in Mayfield, Kentucky.
