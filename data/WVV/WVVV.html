WVVV (96.9 FM) is a radio station broadcasting an Adult Contemporary format. Licensed to Williamstown, West Virginia, United States, it serves the Parkersburg-Marietta area. The station is currently owned by Seven Ranges Radio Co. of St. Marys, WV. Previously the call sign of WVVV FM 104.9 was used for an FM station on 104.9; the sister station to WJJJ, both licensed to Christiansburg, Virginia.
