3CS is a radio station broadcasting in the South-West Victoria region, but based in Colac. The station is aimed at an over 40 demographic, and its format is based on classic hits, talk, sports and news. It broadcasts local content between 1-6pm, while all other programs come from 3AW or 2GB, except "The Morning Rush" which is broadcast across ACE Radio's heritage network.
