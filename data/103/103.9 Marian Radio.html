103.9 Marian Radio (103.9 FM) is a radio station owned by and operated by Birhen Sa Kota Broadcasting. The station's studio and transmitter are located at Our Lady of Fatima Parish, Hayes St., Brgy. Camaman-an, Cagayan de Oro. It is an affiliate of Radyo Bandera since 2018.
