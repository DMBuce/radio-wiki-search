WBIG-FM (100.3 FM) – branded BIG 100 – is a commercial classic rock radio station licensed to Washington, D.C. Owned by iHeartMedia, the station serves the Washington metropolitan area. The WBIG studios are located in the Washington suburb of Rockville, Maryland, while the station transmitter resides in Falls Church, Virginia. Besides a standard analog transmission, WBIG broadcasts over two HD Radio channels, and is available online via iHeartRadio.
