Tuks FM is the radio station of the University of Pretoria and one of South Africa's community broadcasters. It was one of the first community broadcasters in South Africa to be given an FM licence. It was known for its diverse and all alternative music but of late switched to rap and pop whereby losing a great number of listeners in the Pretoria area. Tuks Fm is operated by UP's students.
