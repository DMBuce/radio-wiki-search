KSWA 1330 AM is a radio station licensed to Graham, Texas. The station airs a News Talk format in a simulcast with KROO Breckenridge, Texas. Both stations are owned by Monte Spearman and Gentry Todd Spearman, through licensee High Plains Radio Network, LLC.
