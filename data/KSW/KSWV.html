KSWV (810 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a Classic Hits format featuring music from the 1960s through the 1980s. Licensed to Santa Fe, New Mexico, United States, the station serves the Albuquerque area. The station is currently owned by Celina Gonzales, through licensee GCBendito 4 LLC.

810 AM is a United States clear-channel frequency; KSWV must reduce power during nighttime hours in order to protect the skywave signals of the Class A stations, which are KGO in San Francisco, California and WGY in Schenectady, New York.
