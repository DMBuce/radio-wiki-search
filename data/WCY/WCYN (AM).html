WCYN (1400 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a classic hits format. Licensed to Cynthiana, Kentucky, United States, the station serves the Lexington area. The station is owned by WCYN Broadcasting, Inc.

WCYN operates an FM translator, W267CW, on 101.3 MHz.
