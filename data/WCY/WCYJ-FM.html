WCYJ-FM (99.5 FM) is a student-run radio station broadcasting a Hot Adult Contemporary format. Licensed to Waynesburg, Pennsylvania, US, it serves the Waynesburg area. The station is currently owned by Waynesburg University. Streaming live at www.waynesburgsports.com

Popular shows include, The Morning Buzz with host Brandon Rainelli, The Last Call with Ryan "Stumpy" Legarsky, The Truth with Alfonso " Spice Spice" Ferrari, and Shelby "Cobra" Tabrosky. As well as a Top 20 countdown show with General Manager Steve Hullings, Brandon Reed, and Nate Regatti.

WCYJ-FM also broadcast Waynesburg sporting events.
