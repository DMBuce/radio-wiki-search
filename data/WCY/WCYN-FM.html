WCYN-FM (102.3 FM) is a radio station broadcasting an adult hits format. Licensed to Cynthiana, Kentucky, United States, the station serves the Lexington area. The station is owned by Cumulus Media.

WCYN-FM was silent from March 7, 2017 to sometime in 2019, due to the lack of an exciter. It has since returned to the air, simulcasting WVLK-FM.
