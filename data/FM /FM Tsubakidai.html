Akita Tsubakidai FM Broadcast System Co.,Ltd. (秋田椿台エフエム放送株式会社, Akita Tsubakidai FM Hoso Kabushiki-gaisha) is a Japanese FM station based in Yuwa, Akita, Japan. Their advertising rate is the lowest in Japan, 500 yen per play for 30 seconds ads.
