WKJO (102.3 FM) is a radio station licensed to Smithfield, North Carolina. The station is owned by Richard Heilmann, Sr., through licensee Triangle Marketing Associates, Inc. Its studios are located just outside of Smithfield, NC at 104 Airport Industrial Drive, Clayton, NC. Its transmitter tower is northwest of Smithfield in rural Clayton, North Carolina.
