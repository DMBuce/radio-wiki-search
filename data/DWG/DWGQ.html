93.3 Radyo Serbisyo (DWGQ 93.3 MHz) is an FM station owned and operated by the Municipal Government of Gumaca. Its studios and transmitter are located at 2nd Floor, Hernandez Bldg., Maharlika Highway, Brgy. Tabing Dagat, Gumaca.
