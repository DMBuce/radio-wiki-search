97.1 OKFM is a Legazpi City, Philippine-based radio station with OPM-CHR musical format. It is owned by PBN Broadcasting Network Incorporated. Its studios are located at 3rd Floor Bayona Building, Imperial Court Subdivision Phase 1. Its transmitter is located at Brgy. Taysan. It operates from 5AM to midnight daily.
