CKZW, formerly CJRS, is a 24-hour non-profit radio station based in Montreal, Quebec, Canada. Broadcasting a French language (and at times English language) Jewish format known as Radio Shalom, the station broadcasts at 1650 AM. At certain times, the station also broadcasts a bilingual Christian format known as La Radio Gospel.
