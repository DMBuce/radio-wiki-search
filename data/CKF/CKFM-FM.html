CKFM-FM, branded as 99.9 Virgin Radio, is a Canadian radio station, broadcasting at 99.9 FM in Toronto, Ontario. The station is owned by Bell Media. The station airs a CHR/Top 40 format (as it reports to BDS and Mediabase as a Canadian Top 40 Airplay panel Canadian CHR reporter) and is the flagship of Canada's Virgin stations. CKFM's studios are located at 250 Richmond Street West in the Entertainment District, while its transmitter is located at the top of the CN Tower.
