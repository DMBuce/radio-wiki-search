CKFI-FM is a Canadian radio station licensed to Swift Current, Saskatchewan, serving the southwestern Saskatchewan area broadcasting at 97.1 FM with a hot adult contemporary format branded as Magic 97.1.

The station is currently owned and operated by Golden West Broadcasting which also sister stations CKSW and CIMG-FM.

The station received approval by the CRTC on February 3, 2005 and began broadcasting later that year. CKFI's studios are located at 134 Central Avenue North in Swift Current, Saskatchewan.
