K297BI (107.3 MHz) is an FM radio station in St. Louis, Missouri, branded as "Classic 107.3" and owned and operated by the nonprofit Radio Arts Foundation–St. Louis. The station's studios are located in Clayton, Missouri.

The station is dedicated to the classical music format and its hosts include James (Jim) Connett, Jim Doyle, Kathy Lawton Brown, Tom Sudholt and Julie Schuster.

Classical programming on K297BI is also broadcast on the HD Radio channel 96.3 WFUN-FM-HD2, also operated by the Radio Arts Foundation.
