DXJR (1575 AM) Radyo Lumad is a community radio station owned by Rizal Memorial Colleges Broadcasting Corporation and operated by the Rural Missionaries of the Philippines-Northern Mindanao Region, Kalumbay Regional Lumad Organization and Kodao Productions. The station's studio is located at Sitio Sandugo, Brgy. Tankulan, Manolo Fortich. Radyo Lumad serves as the community station for the Lumad.

This station was formerly owned by Cagayan de Oro Media Corporation under the brand Media Higala from 2010 to 2012, when it went off the air. In 2014, RMC Broadcasting Corporation bought the station and rebranded it as Radyo ni Juan Northern Mindanao. On September 2017, the Rural Missionaries of the Philippines-Northern Mindanao Region took over the operations of the station and rebranded to Radyo Lumad. Nonetheless, it still carries news and commentaries from the Radyo ni Juan network every morning, while carrying local programming for the rest of the day.
