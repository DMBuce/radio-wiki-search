DXJC (99.0 FM), broadcasting as 99.0 Voice FM, is a community radio station owned by Prime Broadcasting Network and operated by the Al-Balagh Foundation. The station's studio is located at the 3rd Floor, Jamiat Cotabato Bldg., Bubong Rd., Brgy. Datu Balabaran, Cotabato City.
