WSBR (740 AM) is an American radio station that is currently silent. Licensed to Boca Raton, Florida, United States, the station serves the West Palm Beach/Boca Raton radio markets, with the Miami/Fort Lauderdale market as a secondary. The station is owned by Beasley Broadcast Group, Inc., doing business as Beasley Media Group, LLC. Its studios are in Boca Raton, and the transmitter was located in Parkland.
