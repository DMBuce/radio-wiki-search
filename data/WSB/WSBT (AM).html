WSBT (960 kHz Sports Radio 96.1 FM & 960 AM) is a commercial radio station in South Bend, Indiana. It is owned by Mid-West Family Broadcasting and primarily airs a sports radio format, with some talk and news programming. The studios and offices are on East Douglas Road in Mishawaka.

WSBT broadcasts at 5,000 watts, using a directional antenna to protect other stations on AM 960. The transmitter is located in the southern portion of South Bend, off Ironwood Road. With a good radio, WSBT's signal can be picked up from Lake Michigan to Fort Wayne. For listeners who prefer FM radio, WSBT is also heard in South Bend and adjacent communities on translator station W241AD at 96.1 MHz.
