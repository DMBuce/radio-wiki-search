CIBX-FM is a radio station in Fredericton, New Brunswick, Canada, broadcasting at 106.9 FM. The station is owned by Bell Media and plays a Top 40/CHR radio format branded on-air as 106.9 Capital FM.

The studios and offices are on Rookwood Avenue in Fredericton, shared with sister stations CFXY-FM and CKHJ. The transmitter is on Provincial Route 620 at Route 617, at Hamtown Corner in Douglas, northwest of Fredericton.
