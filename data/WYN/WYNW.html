WYNW (92.9 FM, "Relevant Radio 92.9 FM") is a radio station broadcasting a religious format. Licensed to Birnamwood, Wisconsin, United States, the station is currently owned by Starboard Media Foundation, Inc. and features programming from Relevant Radio.
