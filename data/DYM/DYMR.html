DYMR (576 AM) Radyo Pilipinas is a radio station owned and operated by Philippine Broadcasting Service in the Philippines. The DYMR studio is located inside the Cebu Technological University campus, M.J. Cuenco Ave. cor. R. Palma, St. Cebu City, and its transmitter is located at Brgy. Perrelos, Carcar. It operates daily from 6:00 AM to 8:00 PM.
