DYML (105.7 FM), branded as 105.7 Love Radio Roxas, is a 24-hour music radio station with Top 40 (CHR) and OPM format. It is operated by BroadReach Broadcasting Corporation, a subsidiary of Manila Broadcasting Company in the Philippines. The station's studio and transmitter are located at the 2nd Floor of the TAJ Cervales Building, Barangay Tiza, Roxas City, Capiz.
