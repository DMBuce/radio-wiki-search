WZJR (91.7 FM) is a radio station in Portland, Indiana. It was originally established as WBSJ by Ball State University It primarily featured National Public Radio programming. The transmitter for WBSJ failed in January 2019. Ball State University, the license owner, chose not to replace it due to cost. In April 2020, Ball State University announced its intention to sell WBSJ to Jayland Radio Inc. for $40,702.43.
