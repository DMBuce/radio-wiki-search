KODA (99.1 MHz, "Sunny 99.1") is a commercial FM radio station in Houston, Texas. It broadcasts an Adult Contemporary radio format and is owned by iHeartMedia, Inc. From mid-November to December 25, KODA switches to Christmas music. Its studios and offices are located along the West Loop Freeway in Uptown Houston.

KODA has an effective radiated power (ERP) of 100,000 watts. The Senior Road Tower transmitter is off Farm to Market Road 2234 near Fort Bend Parkway in Southwest Houston. KODA broadcasts in the HD Radio hybrid format, with its sister station KTRH 740 AM simulcast on the HD2 subchannel and a Spanish-language Christian radio service on HD3.
