KODZ (99.1 MHz) is a commercial FM radio station in Eugene, Oregon, broadcasting to the Eugene-Springfield radio market. The station is owned by Bicoastal Media and airs a classic hits radio format.

The studios and offices are on River Valley Road in Springfield. The transmitter is off Hill Road north of Springfield, amid towers for other Eugene-area FM and TV stations. KODZ is also heard on an FM translator station in Florence, K237EC 95.3Â MHz.
