WCMD is a sports formatted broadcast radio station affiliated with ESPN Radio.

WCMD is licensed to Cumberland, Maryland, serving the Cumberland/Frostburg area. WCMD is owned and operated by West Virginia Radio Corporation. It simulcasts on 1230 AM and 102.1 FM, a translator station, W271AT.
