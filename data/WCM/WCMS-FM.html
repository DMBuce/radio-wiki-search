WCMS-FM (94.5 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a New Country format. Licensed to Hatteras, North Carolina, United States, the station serves the Elizabeth City-Nags Head area. WCMS is an affiliate of MRN, PRN and the Indianapolis Motor Speedway Radio Network and carries the NASCAR Sprint Cup and Nationwide Series races. WCMS has used the name "Water Country" since it signed on in 1999. The original call letters were WWOC. The change to the WCMS call letters was granted on 7/28/2005. The station was owned by Max Media until 2018 after a sale from Ray-D-O Biz LLC.
