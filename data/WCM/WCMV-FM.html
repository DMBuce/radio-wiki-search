WCMV-FM (94.3 Leland, Michigan) is a Central Michigan University-owned station that is currently silent due following a purchase. Until February 29, 2020, it simulcast an Adult Hits format with WFDX branded as Music Radio, The Fox FM. WCMV-FM serves the Traverse City area with 20,500 watts.
