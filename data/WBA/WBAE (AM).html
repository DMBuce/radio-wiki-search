WBAE (1490 kHz, "107.1/93.5 The Bay") is a commercial AM radio station licensed to Portland, Maine. Owned by Saga Communications, it broadcasts a soft adult contemporary format. Its studios and offices are located on Western Avenue in South Portland, and its transmitter is off Forrest Avenue in Portland.

WBAE can also be heard in the Portland area on its FM translator, W296CZ 107.1 MHz. The station is simulcast on 1400 WVAE in Biddeford, Maine, which has its own FM translator, W228EE 93.5; the station's current branding is derived from these FM feeds.
