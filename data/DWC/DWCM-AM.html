DWCM (1161 AM) Aksyon Radyo is a radio station owned by Manila Broadcasting Company through its licensee Cebu Broadcasting Company and operated by Bagong Sibol Broadcast Marketing Corporation. The station's studio is located at the 2nd Floor, Duque Bldg., Galvan St., Brgy. Torres Bugallon, Dagupan, and its transmitter is located at Brgy. Lucao, Dagupan.
