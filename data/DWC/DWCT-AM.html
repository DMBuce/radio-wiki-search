DWCT (1557 AM) Radyo Uno is a radio station owned and operated by Capitol Broadcasting Center. Its studios and transmitter are located at Zone 5, Sol's Subd., Brgy. Bitano, Legazpi, Albay. On December 2, 2019, its transmitter was damaged by Typhoon Tisoy.
