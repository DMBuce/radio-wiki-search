DWCM, under the brand 99.5 Love Radio Legazpi, is a music FM radio station of the Manila Broadcasting Company operating in Legazpi. Its studio and transmitter is located at the 3rd Floor Morante Bldg., Imperial Court Subd. Ph.2, Rizal St., Legazpi City. It operates daily from 4:00 AM to 10:00 PM.
