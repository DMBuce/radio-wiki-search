DWCZ (94.7 FM), broadcasting as 94.7 Spirit FM, is a radio station owned and operated by Diocesan Multi-Media Services, Inc., the media arm of the Diocese of Legazpi. Its studio is located at the 2nd Floor, SEDCen Bldg., Landco Business Park, Brgy. Capantawan, Legazpi, Albay, and its transmitter is located at the former St. Jude Catholic School, Brgy. Bitano, Legazpi, Albay. It is the only FM station in Albay and the only CMN Spirit station airing a Top 40 format.
