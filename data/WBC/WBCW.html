WBCW (89.7 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a Contemporary Christian format that is licensed to Upland, Indiana, United States. The station is owned by Taylor University. It operates as a simulcast of WBCL 90.3 FM in Fort Wayne, Indiana.

WBCW was assigned the WTUR call letters by the Federal Communications Commission (FCC) on August 11, 1995. On November 28, 2014, the station's call sign changed to the current WBCW.
