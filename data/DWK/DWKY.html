DWKY (91.5 FM), under the brand 91.5 Win Radio, is a radio station owned by Mabuhay Broadcasting System, Inc. and operated by ZimZam Management, Inc. of Manuelito "Manny" Luzon, serving as the flagship station of the Win Radio Network. The station's studio and transmitter are located at 40th Floor Summit One Tower, Shaw Blvd., Mandaluyong City. DWKY-FM operates 24 hours daily, except Good Friday of the Paschal Triduum of the Holy Week annually.
