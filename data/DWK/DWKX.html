DWKX (103.5 FM), on-air as 103.5 K-Lite, is a commercial radio station owned and operated by Advanced Media Broadcasting System, and is one of the partner stations of Tiger 22 Media. Its studio and transmitter are located at Unit 906A, Paragon Plaza Building, EDSA corner Reliance St., Mandaluyong City.
