DWKV (102.3 FM), broadcasting as CitiBeat 102.3 is a music radio station in the Batangas-Lipa radio market of the Philippines owned by Kaissar Broadcasting Network. The station's studio and transmitter is located at JR Business Complex, Mataas na Lupa, Lipa City. Its transmitter antenna is heavily directional to eastern Batangas in order to protect the signal of 102.3 Radyo Natin Lemery, broadcasting 25 km southwest of Lipa.

The station has endured certain extents of notoriety due to unaddressed technical glitches and a string of failed promotional events that drew the ire of other stations in the market.
