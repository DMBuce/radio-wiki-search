DWKL (92.7 FM), broadcasting as 92.7 Brigada News FM, is a news and music FM radio station owned and operated by Brigada Mass Media Corporation in the Philippines. The studio and transmitter are located at The Crossroads Building, Maharlika Highway, Brgy. Isabang, Lucena City.

Despite its proximity to its flagship sister, the station primarily served the Lucena-Gumaca radio market with its own local personnel under its former news format.
