Island FM is an Independent Local Radio station broadcasting across the Bailiwick of Guernsey on 104.7FM and 93.7FM in Alderney. Launched in 1992, Island FM remains the sole commercial station in the island and continues to be extremely successful with high listenership figures.

Island FM, along with Channel 103 in Jersey were merged with the Midlands 103 in Ireland in 2013 as part of an internal split within the Tindle group.

The music aired on Island FM consists mainly of popular music, from the 1980s to present day.

Listening figures under a 2018 survey showed that out of a population of 53,000 adults, 31,000 listened each week, at an average of 11.6 hours per person giving a 37% market share.
