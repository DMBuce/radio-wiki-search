WTVY-FM (95.5 FM) is an American radio station licensed to serve the community of Dothan, Alabama, United States. The station is owned by Gulf South Communications. The station received the WTVY-FM call letters from the Federal Communications Commission on December 22, 1978; the station was originally owned by Woods Communications Corporation as a sister to WTVY television.
