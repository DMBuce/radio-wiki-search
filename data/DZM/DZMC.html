DZMC 98.3 (98.3Â MHz in Metro Manila) is a low-power FM campus radio station owned by the Polytechnic University of the Philippines, Manila College of Communication - Broadcast Circle from 2006-2008 and the PUP Young Communicators' Guild since July 2018. It is housed at the College of Communication Building, Polytechnic University of the Philippines, Anonas Street, Sta. Mesa, Manila. It is used as a laboratory for the College of Communication (COC) students of the university. Its programming at the onset of both histories of operation include news, sports, educational/children's programs, talk shows, commentaries, to music programs and request shows, mostly geared towards the interests of students, faculty and administration.

The station operates on the weekdays from 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM, except during semestral breaks and regular holidays. The station was on hiatus from November 2008 until recommencing test broadcasts in July 2018 with resumption of full operations since September 2018, but its ongoing conflict with a pirate station on the same frequency is still unresolved.
