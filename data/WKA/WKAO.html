WKAO (91.1 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a Christian Contemporary Music format, licensed to Ashland, Kentucky, United States. One of the 7 "Walk FM" Network of stations covering parts of the Tri-State are and Mid-Ohio Valley. The station serves the areas of Huntington, West Virginia, Ashland, Kentucky, and Ironton, Ohio is currently owned by Positive Alternative Radio, Incorporated.
