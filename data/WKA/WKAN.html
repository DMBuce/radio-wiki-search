WKAN (AM 1320/FM 101.3) is a radio station broadcasting a Talk/Personality format. Licensed to Kankakee, Illinois, United States. The station is currently owned by STARadio Corporation and features programming from Fox News Radio, Premiere Radio Networks and Westwood One.
