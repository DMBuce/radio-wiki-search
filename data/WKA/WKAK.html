WKAK (branded as Nash FM 104.5) is a radio station serving Albany, Georgia and surrounding cities with a modern country format. This station broadcasts on FM frequency 104.5 MHz and is under ownership of Cumulus Media.

WKAK programming includes Morning Koffee in the AM, Bill Dollar in Middays, Jaxon Riley in the afternoons and Nash Nights Live with Elaina.

On April 30, 2020, Cumulus Media sold its entire Albany cluster for First Media Services for $450,000.
