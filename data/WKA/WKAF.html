WKAF is a radio station in the Boston, Massachusetts market licensed to Brockton, Massachusetts, airing a rhythmic classic hits format branded as "97.7 The Beat." Owned and operated by iHeartMedia, it broadcasts on 97.7 MHz, and serves the Metro Boston and South Shore areas of Massachusetts. The station's studios are located in Medford and the transmitter site is atop Great Blue Hill.
