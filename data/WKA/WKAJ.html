WKAJ (1120 AM) is a radio station licensed to and transmitting from Saint Johnsville, New York, United States. The station airs Cumulus Media Networkʼs Real Country format, and is owned by Cranesville Block Company, Inc. When it first signed on, it featured a classic hits format with the slogan "The Bigfoot".
