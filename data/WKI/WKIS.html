WKIS (99.9 MHz) is a commercial FM radio station licensed to Boca Raton, Florida, and serving South Florida, including the Miami-Fort Lauderdale radio market. It is owned by Entercom and airs a country music radio format. The studios and offices are on NW 2nd Avenue (U.S. Route 441) in Miami. The transmitter tower is off NW 210 Street in Miami Gardens, shared with WTVJ Channel 6.

WKIS broadcasts in the HD Radio format. Its HD2 subchannel carries the sports radio programming of co-owned AM 560 WQAM.
