WKIM (98.9 MHz, "98-9 The Bridge") is a commercial FM radio station licensed to Munford, Tennessee, and serving the Memphis metropolitan area. It is owned by Cumulus Media and airs an adult contemporary radio format. From early November until the Christmas holidays, WKIM switches to all-Christmas music. Weekday mornings, WKIM carries the syndicated Bob & Sheri Show from WLNK Charlotte.

The studios are located on Murray Road at the Memphis Radio Group building in East Memphis. The transmitter site is in Cordova, Tennessee, off Raleigh-Lagrange Road. WKIM has an effective radiated power of 100,000 watts horizontal, the maximum for non-grandfathered FM stations.
