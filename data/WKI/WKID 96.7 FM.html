WKID 96.7 FM is an unlicensed low powered FM radio station in Clearwater, Florida, operating under the authority of Title 47 CFR Part 15. Known as "the world's only kid owned and operated FM radio station", they have received praise for their work in providing kids with a proactive place to build life skills all while transmitting across the world via online streaming.
