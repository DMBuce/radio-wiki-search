WMAY (970 kHz) is a commercial AM radio station in Springfield, Illinois. The station is owned by Mid-West Family Broadcasting and the license is held by Long Nine, Inc. The station airs a News/Talk radio format. On weekdays, WMAY features local hosts in the daytime and nationally syndicated shows at night. Most hours begin with ABC Radio News. WMAY's principal competitor in the talk radio format is 1240 WTAX.

Programming is simulcast on FM translator W234CC at 94.7 MHz in Sherman, Illinois. WMAY's transmitter, studios and offices are all located on North Third Street in Riverton, Illinois.
