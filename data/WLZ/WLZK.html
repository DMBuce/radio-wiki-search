WLZK (94.1 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a Hot Adult Contemporary format. Licensed to Paris, Tennessee, United States, the station serves the Jackson TN area. The station is currently owned by Benton-Weatherford Broadcasting Inc. of TN and features programming from Jones Radio Network.
