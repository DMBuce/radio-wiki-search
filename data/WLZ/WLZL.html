WLZL (107.9 FM, "El Zol 107.9 FM") is a commercial FM radio station licensed to serve College Park, Maryland. The station is owned by Entercom through licensee Entercom License, LLC and broadcasts a Spanish tropical format. Studios are located in Washington, D.C. while the station's broadcast tower is located east of Crofton, Maryland at (38°59′46.0″N 76°39′25.0″W).

WLZL broadcasts using HD Radio.
