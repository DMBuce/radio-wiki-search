W282CA is a Classic Hip Hop formatted broadcast radio station licensed to and serving Richmond, Virginia. W282CA is owned by Edgewater Broadcasting, LLC and operated by SummitMedia.

The programming heard on W282CA is fed by and a direct simulcast of WKHK-HD2.
