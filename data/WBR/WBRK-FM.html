WBRK-FM (101.7 FM, "Star 101.7") is a radio station broadcasting a hot adult contemporary format. Established in 1970, WBRK-FM is licensed to serve Pittsfield, Massachusetts, United States. The station is owned by WBRK, Inc. and features programming from Westwood One.
