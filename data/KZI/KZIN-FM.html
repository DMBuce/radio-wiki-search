KZIN-FM (96.7 FM, "Wide Open Country"), is a radio station licensed to serve Shelby, Montana. The station is owned by Townsquare Media, and licensed to Townsquare Media Shelby License, LLC. It airs a country music format.

The stations studios are at 830 Oilfield Avenue in Shelby, along with KSEN. Transmitter site is located on Cathedral Road, southwest of Shelby.

The station was assigned the KZIN-FM call letters by the Federal Communications Commission on June 19, 1979.
