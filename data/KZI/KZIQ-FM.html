KZIQ-FM (92.7 FM, "92.7 Qlite") is a commercial radio station that is licensed to Ridgecrest, California, United States. Owned by Adelman Broadcasting, it broadcasts a hot adult contemporary format, providing music and local news and information to the Indian Wells Valley.
