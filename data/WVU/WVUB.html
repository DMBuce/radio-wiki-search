WVUB (91.1 FM, "Blazer 91.1") is a radio station in Vincennes, Indiana. It is licensed to Vincennes University and operates out of Davis Hall on the Vincennes University Main Campus. Its calls stand for Vincennes University Broadcasting. Its transmitter is located approximately two miles southeast of Vincennes near Vincennes Lincoln High School. It uses a shortened version of the school's mascot, the Trailblazers, in its moniker. WVUB was previously used by a radio station operated by the US armed forces in the Panama Canal Zone.

WVUB is licensed by the FCC to broadcast in the HD Radio (hybrid) format.
