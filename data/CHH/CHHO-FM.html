CHHO-FM is a French-language community radio format which operates at 103.1 MHz (FM) in Louiseville, Quebec, Canada, with rebroadcaster CHHO-FM-1 104.9 MHz in Saint-Alexis-des-Monts. Owned by La Coop de Solidarité Radio Communautaire de la MRC de Maskinongé, the station earned approval by the Canadian Radio-television and Telecommunications Commission (CRTC) on July 28, 2005.

The station is a member of the Association des radiodiffuseurs communautaires du Québec.
