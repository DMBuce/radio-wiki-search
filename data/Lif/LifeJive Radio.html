LifeJive Radio is a network of Internet Radio Stations broadcasting a variety of channels and genres. It has been run by Ben Hendrix since May 2005. LifeJive Radio's Eclectic Heaven is listed as one of the top 10 Internet Radio Stations by Listen2RadioOnline.

LifeJive.com is listed as the 4th most popular online radio station in the Pop category from TopOnlineRadioStations.co.uk. It also has a 4/5 star rating on InternetRadioTopSites.com. Based on unique visits, LifeJive is listed as one of the top 100 Internet Stations on RadioTopList.com. Under the Shoutcast directory's 'Chill' category, LifeJive Radio's Cafe Monet is listed as one of the top 10 most popular stations.

Eclectic Heaven was chosen as the only internet radio station to broadcast The Americana Rock Mix Podcast. It can be heard Wednesdays at 10am and Sundays at 5pm
