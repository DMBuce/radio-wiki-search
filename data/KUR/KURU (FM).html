KURU (89.1 FM) is a radio station licensed to serve the community of Silver City, New Mexico. The station is owned by Gila / Mimbres Community Radio. It airs a variety radio format.

The station was assigned the KURU call letters by the Federal Communications Commission on April 10, 2011.
