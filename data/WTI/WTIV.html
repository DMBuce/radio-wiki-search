WTIV is an American radio station, licensed to the community of Titusville, Pennsylvania. WTIV operates at the federally assigned frequency of 1230 (kHz) at a full-time power of 1,000 Watts. Though licensed to Titusville, programming, sales and administrative functions originate out of Meadville, Pennsylvania.
