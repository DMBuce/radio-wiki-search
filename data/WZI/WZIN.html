WZIN is an Alternative Rock formatted broadcast radio station licensed to Charlotte Amalie in the U.S. Virgin Islands. WZIN serves the U.S. Virgin Islands, the British Virgin Islands, and Puerto Rico. The station is owned by the Friedman Family and operated by Pan Caribbean Broadcasting de P.R., Inc.
