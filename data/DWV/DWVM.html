DWVM (103.9 FM), broadcasting as Spirit FM 103.9, is a radio station owned and operated by the Roman Catholic Diocese of Lucena. The station's studio and transmitter are located at the Centro Pastoral Bldg., Diocesan Compound, Brgy. Isabang, Lucena City.
