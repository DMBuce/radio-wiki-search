DXUM (819 AM) is the flagship radio station of UM Broadcasting Network, under its affiliate Mt. Apo Broadcasting System (MBS–DXUM). The station's studio is located at UMBN Media Center, C. Bangoy corner Palma Gil Streets, Davao City, while the transmitter is located near the University of Mindanao Matina Campus, Davao City.

The main format of the station is music and news/talk. Radyo Ukay - Radyo means radio and Ukay means to dig. To dig issues and reports and that is what Radyo Ukay is known to many as "Investigative" radio.
