WLDY (1340 AM, "NBC Sports Radio") is a radio station broadcasting a Sports talk format. Licensed to Ladysmith, Wisconsin, United States, the station is currently owned by Randy Hudzinski, through licensee Flambeau Broadcasting Co., Inc.

It features programming from NBC Sports Radio and Salem Radio Network. It also features The Playbook, a locally produced national sports talk show, each Saturday from 10-noon CT, produced by Station Manager Chris Brooks and co-hosted by Alan C. and guests.
