WLDE is an FM radio station from Fort Wayne, Indiana, which broadcasts a classic hits format. The station operates on the FM radio frequency of 101.7 MHz. Popular personalities during the week are Jim and Carrie, Angie Nash, Captain Chris and Mark Evans. Classic Hits 101.7 is Fort Wayne’s Classic Hits station.
