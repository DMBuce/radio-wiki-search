KQBG (99.5 FM, "99-5 The Bridge") is a radio station broadcasting a hot adult contemporary format. Licensed to Rock Island, Washington, United States, the station serves the Wenatchee area. It is currently owned by Cherry Creek Media and licensed to CCM-Wenatchee IV, LLC and features programming from Premiere Radio Networks.
