KATJ-FM (100.7 FM, "Kat Country 100.7") is a commercial radio station located in Victorville, California that serves the Victor Valley area. The official city of license is George, referring to George Air Force Base, which at its 1989 sign-on was an active military base and census-designated place. The base was decommissioned in 1992 and later annexed by the city of Victorville. The station is owned by El Dorado Broadcasters and broadcasts a country music format.

KATJ-FM broadcasts in HD Radio.
