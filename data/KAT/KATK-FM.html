KATK-FM (92.1 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a country music format. Licensed to Carlsbad, New Mexico, United States, the station serves the Santa Fe area. The station is currently owned by Carlsbad Radio, Inc. and features programming from ABC Radio and Jones Radio Network.
