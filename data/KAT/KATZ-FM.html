KATZ-FM (100.3 FM, "The Beat") is a radio station serving the area of St. Louis, Missouri, United States, with an urban contemporary format. The iHeartMedia (previously Clear Channel Communications) outlet broadcasts at 100.3 FM MHz with an ERP of 17 kW and is licensed to Bridgeton, Missouri. Its transmitter is located in Overland, and operates from studios in St. Louis south of Forest Park.
