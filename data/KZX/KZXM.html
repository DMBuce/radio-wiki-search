KZXM (94.3 FM; "94.3 The Well") is a terrestrial FM radio station licensed to Bullard, Texas, United States, and in full simulcast with its sister station KFRO-FM Gilmer. The station is owned by the Educational Radio Foundation of East Texas, Inc., and features a Christian preaching and education format, branded as 94.3 & 95.3 The Well.
