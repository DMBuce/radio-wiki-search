WRGY (90.5 FM) is a radio station licensed to Rangeley, Maine, United States. The station is currently owned by Tranet, a non-profit organization operated by Bill and Margaret Ellis. [1] The station has studios on Main Street in Rangeley with transmitter atop Saddleback Mountain.
