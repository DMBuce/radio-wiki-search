WRGS (1370 AM, "Hometown Radio") is a radio station broadcasting a Southern gospel and classic country music format. Licensed to Rogersville, Tennessee, United States, the station is currently owned by WRGS, Inc. and features programming from USA Radio Network. By 2020, southern gospel was discontinued by the station.
