WRGG-LP is a Full-Service and Oldies broadcast radio station licensed to Greencastle, Pennsylvania, serving Greencastle, Antrim Township, and southern Franklin County in Pennsylvania. WRGG-LP is owned and operated by the Greencastle-Antrim Education Foundation.
