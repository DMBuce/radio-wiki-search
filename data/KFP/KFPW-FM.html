KFPW-FM is an American commercial radio station located in Barling, Arkansas, broadcasting to the Fort Smith, Arkansas, area on 94.5 FM. KFPW-FM airs an active rock music format from Citadel Media's The Nerve and is branded as "The Fort 94.5".
