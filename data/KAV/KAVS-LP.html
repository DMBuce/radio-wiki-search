KAVS-LP (93.9 FM) is a radio station licensed to serve Fallon, Nevada. The station, established in 2005, is owned by Oasis Christian Radio, Inc.

KAVS-LP airs a Christian radio format. KAVS-LP derives a portion of its programming from the Three Angels Broadcasting Network.
