KBRF (1250 AM, "Good Neighbor Radio") is a radio station broadcasting a news talk information format. Licensed to Fergus Falls, Minnesota, United States, the station serves the Fergus Falls-Detroit Lakes area.

The station is currently owned by Leighton Broadcasting, through licensee Leighton Radio Holdings, Inc., and features programming from CNN Radio, Premiere Radio Networks and Westwood One.

The studios and offices are west of downtown Fergus Falls, at 728 Western Avenue North, near I94. The transmitter site is east of town, on Highway 210.
