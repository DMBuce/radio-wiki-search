KBRQ (102.5 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a mainstream rock format. Licensed to Hillsboro, Texas, United States, the station serves the Waco area. The station is currently owned by iHeartMedia, Inc. and features programming from Premiere Radio Networks. Its studios are located on Highway 6 in Waco, and its transmitter is located west of West, Texas.
