WXUT (88.3 FM) is a radio station broadcasting an alternative rock format. Licensed to Toledo, Ohio, United States, the station serves the University of Toledo campus. WXUT shares the 88.3 frequency with WXTS-FM, which broadcasts when WXUT is off the air. The station is currently owned by the University of Toledo. In 1967, the "TU" radio station went by the call letters of WERC on an AM frequency of 680KHz prior to moving to FM and sharing the TPS frequency of 88.3MHz.
