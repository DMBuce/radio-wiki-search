WXUS, known as "U.S. 102.3", is a commercial FM radio station licensed to Dunnellon, Florida, and broadcasting to the Gainesville-Ocala media market on 102.3 MHz. It is owned by JVC Broadcasting and airs a radio format combining country music and Southern-influenced classic rock. WXUS's transmitter is off West Highway 328 in Dunnellon.
