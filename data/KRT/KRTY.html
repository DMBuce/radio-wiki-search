KRTY (95.3 MHz) is a commercial FM radio station located in San Jose, California and licensed to nearby Los Gatos, airing a country music format. KRTY has two booster stations on 95.3 MHz; KRTY-FM1 licensed to Scotts Valley and KRTY-FM2 licensed to New Almaden. In addition to locally programmed country music, KRTY carries the syndicated Country Countdown USA show on Sunday mornings and Golden State Warriors games.
