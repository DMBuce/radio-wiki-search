KLBG-LP (92.5 FM, "Life At Its Best") was a low-power radio station broadcasting an educational format. Formerly licensed to Glide, Oregon, United States, the station was owned by Life At Its Best, Inc.

The station's license was cancelled and the call sign deleted from the Federal Communications Commission's database on February 13, 2012.
