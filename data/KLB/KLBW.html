KLBW (1530 AM) was a radio station broadcasting a religious radio format. Licensed to New Boston, Texas, United States, it served the Texarkana area. The station was owned by Chapel of Light.

As of 2018, the station had been off the air for some time. The studios and transmitter facilities appear to be long since abandoned and neglected.
