WETI is an FM radio station licensed to Lake Village, Arkansas and broadcasting on a frequency of 103.5 MHz.

The format is known as POWER 103.5 FM and plays a mix of Classic Hip-Hop Old-School Funk and Blues.

WETI is owned by Eternity Media Group.
