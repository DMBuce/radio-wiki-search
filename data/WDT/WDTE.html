WDTE (88.3 FM) is a religious radio station transmitting from Grosse Pointe Shores, Michigan, United States. The station is owned by Smile FM. It began operation in June 2012. Programming comes from flagship station WLGH in Lansing and WHYT in Imlay City, Michigan. Coverage includes downtown Detroit, Warren, and communities along Lake St. Clair.

WDTE was granted a construction permit on January 25, 2018, to move to 88.5 with a different directional pattern. When constructed reception will improve to the west.
