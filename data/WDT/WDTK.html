WDTK, known on the air as The Patriot, is a conservative-oriented news/talk radio station broadcasting at 1400 kHz on the AM dial and broadcasts on a translator at 101.5 MHz on the FM dial in Detroit, Michigan, United States. The station is owned by Salem Communications, one of the nation's largest owners of Christian-oriented radio stations.

WDTK is the Detroit outlet for such syndicated conservative talkers as Sean Hannity, Mike Gallagher, Dennis Prager, Hugh Hewitt, Larry Elder and Michael Medved as well as local talkers such as John McCulloch.
