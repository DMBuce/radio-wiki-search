KEAA-LP (97.9 FM) is a high school radio station licensed to serve Eagle, Alaska. The station is owned by Eagle Community School. It airs a high school radio format.

The station was assigned the KEAA-LP call letters by the Federal Communications Commission on November 7, 2003.
