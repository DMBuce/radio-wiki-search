KEAU (104.7 FM) is a radio station licensed to serve the community of Elko, Nevada. The station is owned by Elko Broadcasting Company. It airs a sports radio format, with programming from Fox Sports Radio.

The station was assigned the KEAU call letters by the Federal Communications Commission on September 12, 2014.
