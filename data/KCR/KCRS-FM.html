KCRS-FM (branded as 103.3 Kiss FM) is a radio station that serves the Midland–Odessa metropolitan area with Top 40 (CHR) music. The station is owned by ICA Broadcasting, a local company. They acquired the contract for $3 million in 2010 from Gap Broadcasting (whom acquired many stations including KCRS-FM from Clear Channel Communications), and its hosts are Danny G, Nate Rodrigez, and Jackson Blue.

The station broadcasts Party Playhouse every weekday from 6:00 pm until 11:00 pm and Open House Party every weekend from 6:00 pm until 11:00 pm.
