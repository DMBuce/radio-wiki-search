WVOR (102.3 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a soft adult contemporary format. Licensed to Canandaigua, New York, United States, the station serves the Rochester area. The station is owned and operated by iHeartMedia. Its studios are located at the Five Star Bank Plaza building in downtown Rochester, and its transmitter site is in Canandaigua. The station also plays Christmas music during the holidays.
