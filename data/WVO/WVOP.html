WVOP (970 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a talk format and licensed to Vidalia, Georgia, United States. The station is currently owned by Vidalia Communications Corporation and features programming from ABC Radio and Jones Radio Network.

On February 1, 2010 WVOP changed format from oldies to news and talk.
