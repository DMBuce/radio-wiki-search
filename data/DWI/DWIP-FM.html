DWIP (94.5 FM), branded as 94.5 Love Radio Santiago, is a commercial FM radio station owned and operated by Manila Broadcasting Company in the Philippines. The station's studio and transmitter is located at 4/F Dermaplaza Bldg, Villasis Highway, Santiago City, Isabela.

It operates 24 hours a day (except on Saturdays and Sundays where it signs off at midnight to 4:00 AM and except Holy Week of each year where it signs off at midnight of Maundy Thursday and lasts until 4:00 AM of Easter Sunday).
