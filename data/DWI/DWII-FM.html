DWII (90.7 FM), broadcasting as 90.7 Radyo Oragon, is a radio station owned and operated by Mid-tone Broadcasting Network, a subsidiary of iTransmission. Its studios are located at Hi-Tone Bldg., Alternate Rd., Legazpi City.

The frequency was formerly owned by Ago Medical and Educational Center's Institute of Mass Communication from 1988 to 1995.
