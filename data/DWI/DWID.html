DWID (98.3 FM), on-air as 98.3 Love Radio, is a radio station owned and operated by Manila Broadcasting Company . The station's studio and transmitter are located at the 3rd floor, Carried Lumber Realty Bldg., M.H. del Pilar St., Dagupan.
