DWIC (93.3 FM) was a radio station formerly owned and operated by Bombo Radyo Philippines through its licensee Newsounds Broadcasting Network, Inc. It was formerly known as Star FM from 1992 to 2004, when it went off the air. The frequency is currently owned by ACWS-United Broadcasting Network.
