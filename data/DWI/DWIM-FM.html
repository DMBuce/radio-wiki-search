DWIM (89.5 FM), broadcasting as 89.5 Star FM, is a radio station owned and operated by Bombo Radyo Philippines under its licensee People's Broadcasting Service, Inc. The station's studio, offices and transmitter are located at Bombo Radyo Broadcast Center, 87 Lourdes Subdivision Rd., Baguio City. Star FM operates daily from 4:00 AM to 12:00 MN.
