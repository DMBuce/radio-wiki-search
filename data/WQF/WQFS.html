WQFS (90.9 FM) is Guilford College's student-run radio station, with both students and members of the community serving as disk jockeys. Broadcasting in a variety format, it serves Greensboro, North Carolina and the greater Piedmont Triad area. It is also broadcast on the internet via streaming audio at TuneIn. WQFS is currently ranked number six in the Princeton Review's "The Best 378 Colleges." The station has maintained a spot in the contest's top ten for seven consecutive years. In October 2008, Greensboro News & Record's Go Triad also named WQFS as the Triad's Best College Radio Station.
