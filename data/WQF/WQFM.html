WQFM is a radio station in Wilkes-Barre, Pennsylvania broadcasting at 1240 kHz on the AM band. It simulcasts the sports radio format of WEJL in Scranton, filling in the gaps in WEJL's signal outside Lackawanna County. It is owned by Times-Shamrock Communications of Scranton.
