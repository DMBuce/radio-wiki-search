WNCI (97.9 MHz) – branded 97.9 WNCI – is a commercial FM radio station in Columbus, Ohio. It carries a Top 40/CHR radio format and is owned by iHeartMedia. The studios and offices are on the Columbus west side. WNCI carries several nationally syndicated shows from co-owned Premiere Networks, including On Air with Ryan Seacrest in middays.

WNCI has an unusually high effective radiated power (ERP): 175,000 watts horizontal polarization and 105,000 vertical. By contrast, most FM stations in Ohio are limited to 50,000 watts. The transmitter is atop One Nationwide Plaza in Downtown Columbus. WNCI broadcasts in the HD Radio hybrid format. Its HD2 digital subchannel carries iHeart's "Pride Radio" for the LGBTQ community. The station also streams online via iHeartRadio.
