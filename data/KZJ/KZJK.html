KZJK (104.1 MHz) is a commercial FM radio station licensed to St. Louis Park, Minnesota, and serving the Minneapolis-St. Paul radio market. The station is owned by Entercom and airs an adult hits radio format known as "Jack FM." Studios and offices are on Second Avenue South in Minneapolis.

KZJK has an effective radiated power of 100,000 watts. The station's transmitter is on the KMSP Tower in Shoreview, with an auxiliary transmission facility located atop the IDS Center in Downtown Minneapolis.
