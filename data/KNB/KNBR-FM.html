KNBR-FM (104.5 FM) – branded KNBR 104.5 – is a commercial sports radio station licensed to San Francisco, California, serving the greater San Francisco Bay Area. Owned by Cumulus Media, KNBR is a full-time FM simulcast of sister station KNBR (680 AM). Both stations are the San Francisco affiliate for CBS Sports Radio; the flagship station for the San Francisco Giants Radio Network and a co-flagship station for the San Francisco 49ers Radio Network (along with sister stations KSAN and KGO); and the radio home of Greg Papa and Tom Tolbert. The KNBR studios are located on Battery Street in San Francisco, while the station transmitter resides on Mount Sutro, off La Avanzada Street. In addition to a standard analog transmission, KNBR simulcasts over low-power analog Pleasanton translator KNBR-FM3 (104.5 FM), broadcasts over a single HD Radio channel, and is available online.
