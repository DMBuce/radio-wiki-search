WNER is a sports/talk radio station in Watertown, New York. The station broadcasts at 1410Â kHz and is owned by Stephens Media Group.

WNER broadcasts a national sports format from Fox Sports, having formerly broadcast the format of ESPN Radio. It previously used call letters WGME. Other calls used on 1410 include: WOTT (as a big-band station); WNCQ (a country station, the format now on WFRY-FM); WCIZ (simulcasting the classic rock format of WCIZ-FM, then on 97.5); and WUZZ (as a R&B station).
