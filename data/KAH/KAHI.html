KAHI (950 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a news/talk format. Licensed to Auburn, California, United States, the station serves the Sacramento area. The station is currently owned by Immaculate Heart Media, Inc..

It broadcasts locally produced programming, Oakland Athletics baseball, Sacramento Kings basketball, San Jose Sharks hockey, Sierra College and local high school football. When not carrying live broadcasts, KAHI carries nationally syndicated talk radio shows.

As of October 2015, the station produced several local programs that aired weekdays, including "The KAHI Morning News with Sarah McCormick" (6 - 9 a.m.), "The KAHI Noon News with Mary Jane Popp" (noon - 1 p.m.), "The KAHI Afternoon News with Casey Freelove" (4 - 6 p.m.) and "Poppoff with Mary Jane Popp" (6 - 7 p.m.). The station's moniker then was "The Voice of the Foothills."
