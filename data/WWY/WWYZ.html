WWYZ (92.5 MHz) is a commercial FM radio station licensed to Waterbury, Connecticut, and serving the Central Connecticut, Greater Hartford and Southern Connecticut areas. It is owned by iHeartMedia, and airs a country music radio format. Local hosts are heard most of the day, with the syndicated CMT After Midnight with Cody Alan heard overnight. On weekends, WWYZ features the syndicated shows Bob Kingsley's Country Top 40 and The Bobby Bones Show.

The studios and offices are on Columbus Boulevard in Hartford with other radio stations. The transmitter is in Meriden, on West Peak in the Hanging Hills.
