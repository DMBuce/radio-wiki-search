KXTA-FM (99.1 FM) is a commercial radio station located in Gooding, Idaho, broadcasting to the Twin Falls/Sun Valley, Idaho area. KXTA-FM airs a Regional Mexican format. KXTA-FM is known as "99.1 La Perrona."
