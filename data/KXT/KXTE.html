KXTE (107.5 FM, Alt 107.5) is an Alternative Rock radio station licensed to Pahrump, Nevada. Owned by Entercom, the station's studios are located in the unincorporated Clark County area of Spring Valley, while its transmitter is atop Potosi Mountain.
