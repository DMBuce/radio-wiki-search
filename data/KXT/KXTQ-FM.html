KXTQ-FM (106.5 FM), known as "Magic 106.5", is a radio station owned by Ramar Communications of Lubbock. The station's community of license is Lubbock, Texas, and it serves the greater Lubbock area at 106.5 MHz with an ERP of 34 kW. Its studios and transmitter are based from co-owned KJTV-TV's (along with other sister TV & radio properties) in south Lubbock.
