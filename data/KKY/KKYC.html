KKYC (102.3 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a Classic rock/Rock format. Licensed to Clovis, New Mexico, US, the station serves the Clovis area. The station is currently owned by James Anderson, through licensee Tejas Broadcasting Ltd., LLP.
