KRFN (100.9 MHz) is a commercial FM radio station licensed to Sparks, Nevada and broadcasting to the Reno–Sparks metropolitan area. The station is owned by the Evans Broadcast Company, Inc. and it airs an adult contemporary radio format branded as "Fun 101."

KRFN has studios and offices on East Plumb Lane in Reno. Its transmitter is in the Steamboat Hills section of Reno, near Interstate 580. KRFN is a Class A FM station, which means it is limited in power to 6,000 watts. Programming is also heard in Carson City, Nevada on 150-watt translator station K286AG at 105.1 MHz.
