KRFY (88.5 FM) is a radio station licensed to serve the community of Ponderay, Idaho. The station is owned by Panhandle Community Radio, Inc., and airs a variety format.

The station was assigned the KRFY call letters by the Federal Communications Commission on September 29, 2009.
