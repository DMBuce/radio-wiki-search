WTJT (90.1 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a Christian radio format. Licensed to Baker, Florida, United States, the station serves the Ft. Walton Beach area. The station is currently owned by Okaloosa Public Radio, Inc.

The morning Gospel Cruise with Scott Dennis began on April 11, 2011. Weekdays from 6 a.m.- 10 a.m.
