WTJS (93.1 FM, "The Talk of Jackson 93.1") is a radio station broadcasting a talk format. Licensed to Alamo, Tennessee, United States, the station is currently owned by Grace Broadcasting Services, Inc. and features programming from Salem Radio Network.
