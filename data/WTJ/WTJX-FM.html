WTJX-FM (93.1 MHz) is a non-commercial, educational radio station, serving as an NPR affiliate for the United States Virgin Islands. The station is licensed to Charlotte Amalie. The station is owned and operated by the Virgin Islands Public Broadcasting System, which also owns sister station WTJX-TV.
