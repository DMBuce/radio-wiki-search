WONN-FM (92.7 FM, "ESPN Radio 92.7") is a commercial FM radio station licensed to serve Starview, Pennsylvania. The station is owned by Hall Communications and broadcasts a sports radio format as an affiliate of ESPN Radio.

WONN's studios and offices are located at 1996 Auction Road, Manheim, Pennsylvania. Its broadcast tower is located near Copenhaffer Road in the Starview York County at (40°4′32.0″N 76°48′2.0″W). The station's service contour covers the cities of York and Harrisburg.

WONN-FM programming is simulcast on sister station AM 1490 WLPA in Lancaster, Pennsylvania. WLPA programming is simulcast on FM translator W223CH. This brings WONN-FM's ESPN Radio programming to the FM dial in Lancaster on 92.5 FM.
