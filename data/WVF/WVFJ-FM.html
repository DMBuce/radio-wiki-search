WVFJ-FM (93.3 FM, "The JOY FM") is a Christian radio station that serves southwestern metro Atlanta and western central Georgia, and has listeners in the nearby metro areas of Columbus, Georgia and Auburn, Alabama, and to some extent west of Macon, Georgia. Its city of license is Greenville, Georgia. The WVFJ studio is current located in Tyrone, Georgia. It broadcasts a Contemporary Christian format.
