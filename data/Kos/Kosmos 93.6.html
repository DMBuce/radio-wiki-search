Kosmos 93.6 (Greek: Κόσμος 93.6) is the fourth public radio channel of Greece's state broadcaster, ERT. The programme consists of reggae, jazz, folk-ethnic and world music. The radio controller for Kosmos is Giannis Dimitriadis.
