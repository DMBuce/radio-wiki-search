Air3 Radio is the student radio station of the University of Stirling. Air3 Radio broadcasts a full schedule during normal semester dates (September - December and February - May) and a limited number of shows within holiday and exam periods. It also produces podcasts and music using a recording studio built in 2017. It won Best Media Society in the National Society Awards in 2018.

The station currently has shows running from 07:00 - 03:00, 7 days a week.
