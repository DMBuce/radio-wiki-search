KZGD (1390 AM) is an American radio station licensed to serve Salem, Oregon, United States. The station, founded in 1934 as KSLM, is currently owned by Edward C. Distell. KZGD broadcasts a Regional Mexican format.

The station was previously assigned the KWOD call sign by the Federal Communications Commission (FCC) on May 29, 2009. The station was later assigned the KZZD call sign on March 31, 2014.
