WJBT (or 93.3 The Beat) is a commercial Urban Contemporary radio station in Jacksonville, Florida broadcasting on 93.3 MHz. The station is owned and operated by iHeartMedia, Inc. and is licensed to Callahan, Florida. The station's studios are located on Central Parkway in Jacksonville's Southside section, and the transmitter is in the Arlington section.
