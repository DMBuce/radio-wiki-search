WJBM (1480 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a News Talk Information format. It is licensed to Jerseyville, Illinois, United States. The station is currently owned by DJ Two Rivers Radio which purchased WJBM in 2003, along with its sister station WBBA-FM, from Brown Radio Group Inc. for $320,000. WJBM broadcast an oldies format at the time of its sale.
