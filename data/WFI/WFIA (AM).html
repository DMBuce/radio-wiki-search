WFIA is an AM radio station on 900 kHz in Louisville, Kentucky. WFIA is owned by Salem Media Group through its subsidiary Salem Media of Massachusetts, LLC, though it is operated by Word Media Group. WFIA broadcasts with a daytime power of 930 watts and a nighttime power of 162 watts and carries Christian programming.
