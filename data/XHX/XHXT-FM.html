XHXT-FM is a radio station on 105.7 FM in Tepic, Nayarit. The station is operated by Multimedios Radio and carries its La Caliente grupera format. XHXT is owned by Amplitudes y Frecuencias de Occidente, whose parent also holds 25 percent
