KJR-FM (95.7 FM) is a Seattle radio station that broadcasts a classic hits format. It operates at 95.7 MHz with 100,000 watts of effective radiated power with a transmitter near Issaquah, Washington on Cougar Mountain, and operates from its studios in Seattle's Belltown neighborhood northwest of downtown. KJR-FM is owned by iHeartMedia.

KJR-FM broadcasts in HD.
