WZAM (970 AM / 93.3 FM) is a radio station affiliated with ESPN Radio. Licensed to Ishpeming, Michigan, it has also used the call signs WMVN and WUPY. The station airs a local sports talk show "The Sports Pen" weekdays from 4-5pm ET and is currently hosted by Tanner Hoops. Former hosts include Blake Froling, Ryan Myrehn, Ryan Mayer, Casey Ford, and Bill Blohm. ESPN UP has been the radio home for Westwood High School sports since 2014.
