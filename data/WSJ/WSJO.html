WSJO (104.9 FM, "SOJO 104.9") is a commercial FM radio station licensed to serve Egg Harbor City, New Jersey. The station is owned by Townsquare Media, through licensee Townsquare Media Atlantic City III License, LLC, and broadcasts a Top-40 format.

WSJO broadcasts to the Atlantic City area and greater southern New Jersey. When the station first hit the air in 2004, its programming originated from studios shared with New Jersey 101.5 in Ewing Township, New Jersey, outside its primary coverage area. Today, the station is programmed from the Townsquare cluster in Northfield, NJ, well within the Atlantic City market. That cluster includes WFPG, Atlantic City (Lite Rock 96.9), WENJ, Millvile (97.3 ESPN), WPUR, Atlantic City (Cat Country 107.3) and WPGG, Atlantic City (WPG Talk Radio 95.5).
