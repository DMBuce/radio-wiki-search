WGER (106.3 FM, "Mix 106.3") is a radio station licensed to Saginaw, Michigan broadcasting a Hot Adult Contemporary format. The station broadcasts from a transmitter southeast of I-675 Exit 6 (Tittabawassee Road) in Carrollton Township in Saginaw County.
