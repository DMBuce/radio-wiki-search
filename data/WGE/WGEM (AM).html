WGEM (1440 AM; "WGEM Sports Radio") is a radio station in Quincy, Illinois broadcasting a sports radio format. The station is owned by Quincy Media and is an affiliate of ESPN Radio. The station is also broadcasting via FM translator W255CY, 98.9 FM, licensed to Quincy.
