WFRB-FM is a Country formatted broadcast radio station licensed to Frostburg, Maryland, serving Central Maryland, Western Maryland, Potomac Highlands of West Virginia and South-Central Pennsylvania. WFRB-FM is owned and operated by Forever Media.
