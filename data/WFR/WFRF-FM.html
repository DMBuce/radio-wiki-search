WFRF-FM (105.7 FM, "Faith Radio") is an American non-commercial educational radio station city of Monticello, Florida, United States. The station, established in 1989 as WJPH, is currently owned and operated by Faith Radio Network, Inc. Its studios are located in the north side of Tallahassee close to Lake Jackson and its transmitter is based in Jefferson County, Florida.
