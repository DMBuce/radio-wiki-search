WFRX (1300 AM) is a radio station licensed to West Frankfort, Illinois, United States. The station airs a Sports radio format, and is owned by Withers Broadcasting, through Withers Broadcasting of Southern Illinois, LLC. It is the first radio station in the United States to play a Beatles record on air.
