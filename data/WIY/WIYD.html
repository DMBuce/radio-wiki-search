WIYD (1260 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a Classic Country radio format. It is licensed to Palatka, Florida, and is currently owned by Hall Broadcasting Company. It features programming from Westwood One.

The FCC first licensed this station to begin operations on June 24, 1947 using callsign WWPF.
