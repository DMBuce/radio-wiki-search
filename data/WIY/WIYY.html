WIYY (97.9 FM, "98 Rock") is a commercial FM radio station licensed to serve Baltimore, Maryland. The station is owned by Hearst Communications and broadcasts a mainstream rock format. WIYY shares a studio/office facility with sister stations WBAL (1090 AM) and WBAL-TV (channel 11) on Television Hill in the Woodberry section of Baltimore, near the transmission tower it shares with WBAL-TV. WIYY and WBAL are the only two radio stations owned by the Hearst Corporation, and are also co-owned with the TV station. WBAL Radio is simulcast through WIYY's HD2 HD Radio subchannel.

WIYY and WBAL are the flagship stations of the Baltimore Ravens radio network, sharing the 'official broadcaster' title with WBAL-TV (the city's NBC affiliate), which airs the team's television-focused programming.
