WZMJ is a variety hits radio station licensed to Batesburg-Leesville, South Carolina and is located within the Columbia, South Carolina DMA. The Midlands Media Group, LLC station is licensed by the Federal Communications Commission (FCC) to broadcast at 93.1 MHz with an effective radiated power (ERP) of 2.1 kW.
