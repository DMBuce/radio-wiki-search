WXMT is a commercially licensed FM radio station licensed to Smethport, Pennsylvania, the seat of McKean County. The station broadcasts at 106.3 MHz with an effective radiated power of 1,030 watts. WXMT is owned by Twilight Broadcasting and runs a classic hits format known as "106.3 XMT".

Prior to 2008, the station was known as WQRM.
