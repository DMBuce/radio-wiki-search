WXMK (105.9 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a Christmas music format (the station's regular format is Hot Adult Contemporary). Licensed to Dock Junction, Georgia, United States, the station serves the Brunswick area. Better known as Magic 105.9 "The Golden Isles' Number 1 Hit Music Station." The station is currently owned by Golden Isles Broadcasting, LLC.
