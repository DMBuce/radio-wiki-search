WXMA, also known as "102.3 Jack FM", is a variety hits station located in Louisville, Kentucky. The station is licensed by the Federal Communications Commission (FCC) to broadcast on 102.3 FM with an effective radiated power (ERP) of 6 kW. The station's studios are located in downtown Louisville and the transmitter site is blocks away atop The 800 Apartments building.
