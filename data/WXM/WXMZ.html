WXMZ (99.9 FM) is an American radio station that is licensed to serve and located in Hartford, Kentucky. The station is owned by Radio Active Media, Inc., and it currently broadcasts an Oldies format. The station's studios are located at 314 South Main Street in Hartford and its transmitter is located along Bald Knob Road off US 231 near Cromwell.
