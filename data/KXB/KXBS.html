KXBS (95.5 FM) is a radio station licensed to Bethalto, Illinois, and serving the Greater St. Louis area. The station is owned by Radio One, and is in the process of being sold to Gateway Creative Broadcasting, with Gateway to take over operations via a local marketing agreement on January 4, 2021. Its transmitter is located in St. Louis, Missouri, just north of Forest Park, and operates from studios in Olivette (with a St. Louis address).
