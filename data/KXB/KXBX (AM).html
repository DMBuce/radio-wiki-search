KXBX (1270 AM) is a radio station broadcasting an Adult Standards/MOR format. Licensed to Lakeport, California, United States, the station is currently owned by Bicoastal Media Licenses, LLC and features programming from CNN Radio and Westwood One, formerly Dial Global.
