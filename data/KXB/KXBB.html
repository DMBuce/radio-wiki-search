KXBB (101.7 FM) is a radio station licensed to serve the community of Cienega Springs, Arizona. The station is owned by Sanford and Terry Cohen, through licensee River Rat Radio, LLC, and airs a blues format.

The station was assigned the KXBB call letters by the Federal Communications Commission on January 7, 2019.
