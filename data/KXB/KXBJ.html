KXBJ (96.9 FM) is a radio station licensed to El Campo, Texas, United States, it serves the Texas Gulf coast area. The station is currently owned by KSBJ Educational Foundation.

The station simulcasts Contemporary Christian music from KSBJ whose studios are in Humble, Texas. It is part of a pentacast with 4 other full power FM stations, all of which are owned by the Foundation. The studio is located in Humble, and the transmitter is located west of Blessing.
