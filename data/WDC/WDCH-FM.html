WDCH-FM (99.1 FM) is a radio station currently broadcasting a business news format to the Baltimore, Maryland/Washington, D.C. metropolitan area. Licensed to Bowie, Maryland the station is currently owned by Entercom and operated by Bloomberg L.P. Its transmitter is located near Crofton, Maryland, and the broadcasting studios are located near the Washington Navy Yard along the north/west bank of the Anacostia River (Eastern Branch of the Potomac River) in Southeast Washington.

WDCH is one of the few stations in the Baltimore/Washington corridor that directly targets both markets. This is because its transmitter is located halfway between the two cities, providing a strong city-grade signal to both.

WDCH-FM often airs D.C. United soccer and Washington Wizards pro basketball games in the NBA whenever sister station WJFK-FM (106.7 "The Fan") is already covering a game elsewhere.
