WDCG, (105.1 MHz G105) is a commercial Top 40 (CHR) station licensed to Durham, North Carolina and serving the Raleigh-Durham radio market. Its studios are located on Smoketree Court in Raleigh's Highwoods Office Park and owned by iHeartMedia, along with B93.9, Alt 95.3, Classic Rock 100.7 WRDU, and 106.1 FM More Stimulating Talk. The transmitter site for the station is in Apex. WDCG is the Raleigh-Durham primary entry point for the Emergency Alert System.

WDCG broadcasts in the HD radio format.
