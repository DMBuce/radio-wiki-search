WAOL (99.5 FM) is a radio station in Ripley, Ohio owned by Donald Bowles and Venita Frame Bowles, through licensee Dreamcatcher Communications, Inc. WAOL airs a Hot AC music format as "99-5 The Edge." It formerly aired an Adult Hits music format as "Max FM," then relaunched as "The Edge" in October 2013 after a period of identifying as simply "The New 99.5 WAOL."
