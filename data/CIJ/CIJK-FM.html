CIJK-FM is a Canadian radio station broadcasting at 89.3 FM in New Minas, Nova Scotia owned by Stingray Group. The station currently broadcasts an active rock format branded as 89.3 K-Rock. The station is one of several new radio stations approved in 2007 for the Atlantic Provinces.

According to the June 16 edition of the Northeast Radio Watch, CIJK signed on the air on June 12, 2008, at 8:09 a.m. and changed to an updated format in July 2012.
