WNTP (990 kHz) is an AM radio station located in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, United States. WNTP is owned by Salem Media Group and broadcasts a conservative talk radio format. The station's studios and transmitter facilities are shared with co-owned WFIL (560 AM) in Lafayette Hill, Pennsylvania.
