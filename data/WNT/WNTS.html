WNTS is an AM radio station broadcasting on a frequency of 1590 kHz and owned by Marvin Kosofosky's Continental Broadcast Group, L.L.C., in Indianapolis, Indiana, U.S., though technically licensed in Beech Grove, Indiana. The studios and transmitters are located at 4800 E. Raymond St. on the southeast side of Indianapolis.

1590 AM is a Regional broadcast frequency.
