WNTI (990 AM is an American radio station, licensed to serve Somerset, Pennsylvania; the seat of news/talk for Somerset County. The station broadcasts on a frequency of 990 kHz and a maximum output power of 10,000 watts, using a two-tower directional antenna system. The station is a simulcast of WNTJ in Johnstown, Pennsylvania.
