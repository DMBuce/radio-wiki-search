Northsound 2 is a commercial radio station broadcasting to Aberdeen and Aberdeenshire on 1035 medium wave, DAB digital and online. Owned and operated by Bauer Radio, Northsound 2 forms part of Bauer's Greatest Hits Radio network of classic hit music stations in Scotland and Northern England, carrying networked programmes originating mainly from Glasgow and Manchester 24 hours a day, featuring local news and traffic bulletins.
