DYFE (97.5 FM) is a radio station owned and operated by Far East Broadcasting Company. Its studio and transmitter are located at P. Burgos St., Tacloban City. It broadcasts every Monday to Saturday, 5:00 AM to 2:00 PM.

The frequency was formerly owned by Radio GMA under the brand Campus Radio. On February 13, 2007, it closed down due to lack of advertisers' support and financial problems. A few years later, it was used by FEBC for emergency broadcasts. On May 22, 2018, it was inaugurated as DYFE.
