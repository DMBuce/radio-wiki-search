DYFX (1305 AM) Radyo Agila is a radio station owned and operated by the Eagle Broadcasting Corporation. Despite being a simulcast of DZEC in Manila, the station carries its own originating programs. The station's studio and transmitter facilities are located along Cebu South Coastal Rd., Brgy. San Roque, Talisay, Cebu.
