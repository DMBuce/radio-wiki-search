DYFR (98.7 FM), broadcasting as UP 987, is a non-commercial radio station owned and operated by the Far East Broadcasting Company. The station's studio and transmitter are located at the Immanuel Bible College Compound, Banawa Hills, Cebu City. Since August 1, 2019, DYFR operates 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.
