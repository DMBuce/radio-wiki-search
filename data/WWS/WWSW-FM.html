WWSW-FM (94.5 FM) – branded 94.5 3WS – is a commercial classic hits radio station licensed to serve Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania. Owned by iHeartMedia, the station covers the Pittsburgh metropolitan area. The WWSW studios are located in the Pittsburgh suburb of Green Tree, while the station transmitter resides in Pittsburgh's Perry South neighborhood. Besides a standard analog transmission, WWSW broadcasts over two HD Radio channels, and is available online via iHeartRadio.

WWSW is notable for being an FM "superpower station", with an effective radiated power exceeding Federal Communications Commission (FCC) restrictions for Class B FM signals.
