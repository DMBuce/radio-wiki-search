WWSM (1510 AM, "Your Country Friend") is a daytime-only radio station broadcasting a classic country music format. Licensed to Annville-Cleona, Pennsylvania, United States, the station is owned by Patrick H. Sickafus and features programming from USA Radio Network and Westwood One.
