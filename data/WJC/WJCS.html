WJCS (89.3 FM) is a religious formatted, non-commercial radio station in Allentown, Pennsylvania, with translator W249CA Bangor (97.7 FM). Its slogan is "Your station for inspiration". The station is owned by Beacon Broadcasting Corporation, a not-for-profit corporation. It is affiliated with Moody Broadcast Network, and approximately 85% of its programming originates with Moody.

In 2018, WJCS added two additional translators, W292EQ serving Lehigh Township on 106.3 FM and W293CP serving Kutztown on 106.5 FM.
