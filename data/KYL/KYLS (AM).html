KYLS (1450 AM) is a radio station licensed to Fredericktown, Missouri, which broadcasts at 1,000 watts. It airs a hot AC format and is branded K-Hits 94.3. The station is simulcast on an FM translator at 94.3 (K232FS). KYLS is owned by Dockins Broadcast Group, LLC.
