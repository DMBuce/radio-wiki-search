KHDR and KHRQ are of a pair of adult hits radio stations branded as The Highway Drive.

KHWZ 100.1 FM in Ludlow, CA was a part of the Highway Country trimulcast until 2004 when it switched to The Drive and then went silent on February 25, 2011.

The stations are an affiliate of the Vegas Golden Knights radio network.
