KHDN (1230 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a News Talk Information format. Licensed to Hardin, Montana, United States, the station serves the Billings area. The station is currently owned by Sun Mountain, Inc. and features programming from Cumulus Media Networks and Westwood One.
