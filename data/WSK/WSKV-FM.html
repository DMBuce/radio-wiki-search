WSKV-FM (104.9 FM) Pickup Country is a radio station broadcasting a Full Service format including Talk, Appalachian, Local, Real Country, Bluegrass and Music format. Licensed to Stanton, Kentucky, United States. Pickup Country operates Studios in both Stanton, Kentucky and Irvine, Kentucky. WSKV-FM Pickup Country offers radio and digital advertising. The station is owned by Moore Country 104, LLC.
