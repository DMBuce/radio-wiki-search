KOBM (1420 AM) is a radio station broadcasting an oldies format, simulcasting KIBM (1490 AM). Licensed to Omaha, Nebraska, United States, the station serves the Omaha area. KOBM's license is owned by Hickory Radio, LLC, located in Omaha, Nebraska.

KOBM's studios are located on Burt Street (near North 120th Street and Dodge Road) in West Omaha, while its two-tower transmitter array is located in south Council Bluffs, Iowa, across the Missouri River from South Omaha.
