KLHT-FM (91.5 FM) is a radio station licensed to serve the community of Honolulu, Hawaii. The station is owned by Calvary Chapel of Honolulu, Inc. and airs a religious radio format, with a mixture of inspirational worship music and Bible teaching.

The station was assigned the KLHT-FM call letters by the Federal Communications Commission on July 29, 2014.
