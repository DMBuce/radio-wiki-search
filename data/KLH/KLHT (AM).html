KLHT (1040 AM) is a radio station in Honolulu, Hawaii with a Christian radio format. KLHT broadcasts with 10,000 watts. The station is owned and operated by Calvary Chapel Honolulu, its on-air branding is "K-Light 1040 AM" and its slogan is "The Pure Light of Hawaii."

KLHT first signed on the air on March 18, 1957. For a time in the 1970s, it ran an all-news radio format using the service of CBS Radio News.
