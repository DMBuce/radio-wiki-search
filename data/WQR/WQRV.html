WQRV (100.3 FM, "100.3 The River") is an adult hits-formatted radio station with a main focus on Classic Hits serving the Huntsville, Alabama, market, which includes counties in northern Alabama and southern central Tennessee. Owned and operated by iHeartMedia, the station's studios are located in Madison, Alabama and its transmitter is located north of Huntsville.
