WQRL (106.3 FM) is a radio station licensed to Benton, Illinois, covering Southern Illinois, including Marion, Benton, and Harrisburg. WQRL has an oldies format and is owned by Dana Communications Corporation.

The station is an affiliate of the syndicated Pink Floyd program "Floydian Slip."
