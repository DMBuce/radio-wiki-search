WTKK (106.1 MHz), known as "106.1 WTKK, More Stimulating Talk Radio", is an FM radio station that is licensed to Knightdale, North Carolina and serves the Raleigh-Durham media market (also known as the Research Triangle). WTKK airs a Talk radio format and is owned and operated by iHeartMedia. Sister stations include WDCG, WNCB, and WRDU. The station's studios are located in Raleigh, and the transmitter site is in Garner.

WTKK also is relayed over translator W270AW.

WTKK broadcasts in the HD radio format.
