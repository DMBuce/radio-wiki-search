WTKS (1290 kHz, "NewsRadio 97.7 & AM 1290") is a radio station licensed to Savannah, Georgia. The station is owned by iHeartMedia, Inc., with the Capstar TX Limited Partnership holding the license. WTKS airs a talk radio format. Its transmitter is located behind WTKS's studios in Garden City, Georgia (with a Savannah address).

Programming is simulcast on an FM translator station, 97.7 W249BS. The station uses this FM dial position in calling itself "NewsRadio 97.7 and AM 1290."
