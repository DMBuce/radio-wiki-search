WYQE (92.9 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a Spanish Variety format. Licensed to Naguabo, Puerto Rico, United States, it serves the Puerto Rico area. The station is currently owned by JN Music Group, a subsidiary of HMS Distributors, and the licensee is Fajardo Broadcasting Company, Inc..
