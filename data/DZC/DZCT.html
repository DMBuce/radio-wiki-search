105.3 Super Tunog Pinoy (DZCT 105.3 MHz) is an FM station owned and operated by DCG Radio-TV Network. Its studios and transmitter are located at 1022 DCG Tower 1, Maharlika Hi-Way, Brgy. Isabang, Tayabas
