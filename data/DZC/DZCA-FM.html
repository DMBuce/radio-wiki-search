DZCA, branded as CAT College Radio 105.9 (Formerly Callsign Letters: DWYR-FM) is a college radio station of the Computer Arts and Technological College. Its studios are located at the CATC Bldg., Balintawak St., Albay District, Legazpi City. The station is currently off the air due to technical problems.
