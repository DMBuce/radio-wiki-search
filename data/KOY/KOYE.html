KOYE (96.7 FM; La Invasora 96.7) is a radio station broadcasting a Regional Mexican format in full simulcast with sister station KTLH Hallsville. Licensed to Frankston, Texas, United States, the station serves the western portion of the Tyler-Longview area. The station is currently owned by Alpha Media LLC, through licensee Alpha Media Licensee LLC. Its studios are located on Broadway Avenue in downtown Tyler, and its transmitter is located northwest of Jacksonville, Texas.
