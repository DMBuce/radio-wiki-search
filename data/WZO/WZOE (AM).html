WZOE (1490 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a News Talk Information format. Licensed to Princeton, Illinois, United States, the station serves the LaSalle-Peru area. The station is currently owned by WZOE, Inc. and features programming from CBS Radio, Premiere Radio Networks and Westwood One.
