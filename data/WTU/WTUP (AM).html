WTUP (1490 AM, "Alt 107.7") is a radio station broadcasting an alternative rock format. Licensed to Tupelo, Mississippi, United States, the station serves the Tupelo area. The station is currently owned by iHeartMedia, Inc., through licensee Capstar TX LLC.
