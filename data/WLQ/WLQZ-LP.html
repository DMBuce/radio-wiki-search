WLQZ-LP is a broadcast radio station licensed to and serving Warsaw, Indiana, United States. As of June 2015, the station is on the air with a format of Top 40 music with a mix of hit country music as Z93.9, Warsaw's Hit Music Station. WLQZ-LP is owned and operated by Blessed Beginnings.
