KDXX (107.9 FM), is a Spanish CHR radio station serving the Dallas/Fort Worth Metroplex in Texas. The station is licensed to Lewisville, Texas, and owned and operated by Uforia Audio Network, a division of Univision Communications. The station's studios are located along the John W. Carpenter Freeway in the Stemmons Corridor of Northwest Dallas, and the transmitter is located north of Denton.
