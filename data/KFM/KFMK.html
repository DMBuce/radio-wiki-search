KFMK (105.9 FM) is an Austin, Texas radio station operating a Contemporary Christian format as an affiliate of the K-Love radio network. It is licensed to Round Rock, Texas with an ERP of 4,500 watts from a transmitter site near West Lake Hills, TX, and is currently owned by CRISTA Media with a pending sale to Educational Media Foundation.
