KFML (94.1 FM, "FM94") is a radio station licensed to Little Falls, Minnesota, United States. Previously the call letters belonged to a Denver, Co free-form station. The station is currently owned by Little Falls Radio Corporation. The station carries a contemporary hits format, mixing with Hot AC and active rock. KFML carries "The Dark" hosted by JZ, playing active rock music from the 2000s to the present with a strong empha sis on cutting edge new active rock. KFML is also the radio home for Pierz Pioneer sports.

KFML is located in a studio at 16405 Haven Road, with its two sister stations.
