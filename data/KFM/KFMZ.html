KFMZ is a radio station at 1470 AM in Brookfield, Missouri. The station is owned by the Best Broadcast Group and carries an adult contemporary format, primarily simulcasting KZBK 96.9 FM.

KFMZ is also heard on FM translator K241BT at 96.1 FM.
