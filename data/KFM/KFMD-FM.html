KFMD-FM (101.5 FM) is a radio station in Greenland, Arkansas, serving the Fayetteville area. KFMD-FM airs a classic hits format that plays hits from the 1970s, 1980s and 1990s. They also play a select number of 1960s hits as well, most notably Martha and the Vandellas original recording of Dancing In The Street. Starting in the 2020s, some hits from the 2000s were thrown in. These include songs such as It's My Life by Bon Jovi, Underneath It All by No Doubt, and Counting Crows' version of Joni Mitchell's Big Yellow Taxi, which features Vanessa Carlton. Star 101.5 airs local morning show Brock & Marci in the morning, Roe mid-days, and Tim in the afternoons with Throwback Nation Radio late evening. The Tom Kent show is aired late evening on weekends.
