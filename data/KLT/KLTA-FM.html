KLTA-FM (98.7 FM (MHz), "Big 98.7") is a radio station based in Fargo, North Dakota, though licensed by the Federal Communications Commission (FCC) to Moorhead, Minnesota, owned by Jim Ingstad's Radio FM Media. The station carries an Adult-oriented Top 40 (CHR) format. KLTA and Rhythmic CHR formatted HD2 translator W245CM "96.9 Hits FM" compete against heritage Top 40 (CHR) KOYY "Y94".

Its studios are located on 7th Avenue South in Fargo, while its transmitter is located near Sabin.
