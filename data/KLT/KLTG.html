KLTG (96.5 FM, The Beach 96.5) is a radio station broadcasting an adult top 40 music format. Licensed to Corpus Christi, Texas, United States, the station serves the Corpus Christi area. The station is currently owned by Starlite Broadcasting. Its' studios are located in the Six Points district south of downtown Corpus Christi and the transmitter is in Sinton, Texas.
