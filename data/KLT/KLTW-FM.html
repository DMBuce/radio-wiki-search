KLTW-FM is a commercial radio station in Prineville, Oregon, broadcasting to the Bend, Oregon, area on 95.7 FM.

KLTW-FM moved from 95.1 FM to 95.7 FM on August 28, 2012.

On June 23, 2014 at 12 Midnight, KLTW rebranded as 95.7 My FM.

On September 8, 2016 at 12:00 pm, KLTW changed their format to adult hits, branded as "Bend 95.7".
