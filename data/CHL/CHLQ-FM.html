CHLQ-FM is a Canadian radio station broadcasting at 93.1 FM in Charlottetown, Prince Edward Island. The station is owned by the Maritime Broadcasting System and currently broadcasts a mainstream rock format branded on-air as Q93. The station has been on the air since March 1982.

On July 16, 2012, CHLQ switched from adult contemporary/classic hits to mainstream rock.

As of early 2017, the Morning Show portion of the program has been renamed from "The Q Morning Crew with Paul and Anne" to "The Unbalanced Breakfast with Joee Adams."
