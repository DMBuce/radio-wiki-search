CHLG-FM (104.3 MHz, 104.3 The Breeze) is a radio station in Vancouver, British Columbia. Owned by Stingray Group, the station broadcasts a soft adult contemporary format. Its studios are located in Richmond, and its transmitter is on Mount Seymour.
