WOLZ (95.3 FM, "95.3 OLZ") is a radio station broadcasting a classic hits format. Licensed to Fort Myers, Florida, United States, the station is currently owned by iHeartMedia, Inc..

WOLZ broadcasts in the HD Radio format.
