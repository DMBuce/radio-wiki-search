WOLA (1380 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a Spanish Contemporary format. Licensed to Barranquitas, Puerto Rico, United States, the station serves the Puerto Rico area. The station is currently owned by Centro Media Group, through licensee Radio Procer, Inc.
