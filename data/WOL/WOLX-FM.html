WOLX-FM (94.9 FM) is a commercial radio station that is licensed to Baraboo, Wisconsin and serves the Madison metropolitan area. The station is owned by Entercom and airs a classic hits format. The studios and offices are on Ganser Way in Madison. WOLX broadcasts from a transmitter in Devil's Lake State Park near Baraboo in Sauk County, Wisconsin.

WOLX-FM broadcasts in HD Radio.
