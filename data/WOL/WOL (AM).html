WOL is an urban talk radio station in Washington, D.C. Broadcasting on 1450 AM, this is the flagship radio station of Radio One. It is co-owned with WKYS, WMMJ, WPRS, WTEM, and WYCB and has studios located in Silver Spring, Maryland. The transmitter site is in Fort Totten in Washington.
