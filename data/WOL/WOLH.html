WOLH (1230 AM) is a radio station airing a sports format known as "ESPN 98.9". Licensed to Florence, South Carolina, United States, the station serves the Florence area. The station is owned by Community Broadcasters, LLC. The station's programming also aired on WHYM in Lake City and W255BD in Darlington.
