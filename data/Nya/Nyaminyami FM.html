Nyaminyami FM is a local commercial radio station broadcasting in Kariba, Zimbabwe broadcasting in 3 main languages namely Tonga, Korekore and Shona. Nyami Nyami FM covers Mashonaland-west province with 2 frequencies 94.5FM in Kariba, Makuti, Chirundu, Magunje e.t.c and frequency 103.4 from Karoi to Battlefields. The Station hosts one of the countries biggest events "NyamiNyami Lakeside Festival" which attracts more than 50 000people every October.
