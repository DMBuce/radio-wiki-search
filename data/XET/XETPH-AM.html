XETPH-AM is a radio station located in Santa María de Ocotán, Durango. Broadcasting on 960 AM, XETPH is operated by the Cultural Indigenist Broadcasting System (SRCI) of the National Commission for the Development of Indigenous Peoples (CDI) and is known as Las Tres Voces de Durango (The Three Voices of Durango). It is the 21st station in the SRCI system of indigenous radio stations, coming to air on November 8, 2012 with programming in O´dam-audam, Náhuatl and Wixarika. It was the first new SRCI AM radio station since XENKA-AM signed on in 1995.
