DZAT (1512 AM) Life Radio is a radio station owned and operated by End-Time Mission Broadcasting Service, a religious broadcast arm of the Pentecostal Missionary Church of Christ (4th Watch). The station's studio, office and transmitter are located at Purok Rosal, Brgy. Silangang Mayao, Lucena City.
