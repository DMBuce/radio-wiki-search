DZAU (1233 AM) is a radio station owned and operated by R.A. Broadcasting Corporation, a subsidiary of Polytechnic Foundation of Cotabato and Asia. Its studios and transmitter are located at Heritage Homes, Brgy. Binanuan, Talisay, Camarines Norte.
