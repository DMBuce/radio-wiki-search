DZAS (702 AM) is a non-commercial radio station owned and operated by Far East Broadcasting Company, an international Christian broadcast company which operates several radio stations in the Philippines. The station's studio facility is located at 46th floor of One Corporate Centre, Meralco Avenue corner Doña Julia Vargas Avenue, Ortigas Center, Pasig City and the transmitter is located at McArthur Highway, Barangay Wakas, Bocaue, Bulacan. DZAS broadcasts on the AM band from 4:35 AM to 12:00 MN daily. Few of the station's programs are also aired on television via GCTV and TBN Philippines on cable.
