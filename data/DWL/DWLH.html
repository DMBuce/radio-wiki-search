DWLH (101.5 FM), broadcasting as 101.5 Brigada News FM, is a FM station owned by Brigada Mass Media Corporation. Its studios & transmitter are located at Maharlika Highway, Brgy. Pangpang, Sorsogon City. It is currently headed by Meljessa Balino.

It used to be known as The Wave under Hypersonic Broadcasting Center from 1995 to 2016, when Brigada acquired the station.
