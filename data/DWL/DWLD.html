DWLD (88.7 FM), broadcasting as Majic 88.7, is a radio station owned by DCG Radio-TV Network and operated by BPS Broadcasting Media Services. The station's studio and transmitter are located at 3rd Floor, Zen's Building, Ayala Highway, Brgy. Balintawak, Lipa, Batangas.
