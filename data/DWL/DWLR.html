DWLR, broadcasting as 104.7 DCG FM, is an FM station of DCG Radio-TV Network in the Philippines. The station's studio and transmitter is located at the 4th Floor, J.A. Abucar Bldg., Maharlika Highway, Brgy. Mabolo, Naga City, Camarines Sur.

It used to be on air as Power 104 under Caceres Broadcasting Corporation until early 2010s, when it went off the air. DCG bought the station in 2015 and went on air a year later as DCG FM. The station is currently shut down.
