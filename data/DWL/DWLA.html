DWLA (105.9 FM), on-air as Neo Retro, is an FM station owned by Bright Star Broadcasting Network. Its studios are located at the 3rd Floor, Silver City Mall, Frontera Verde, Bgy. Ugong, Pasig City, while its transmitter is situated at the Seventh-day Adventist compound, Nuestra Señora de la Paz Subd., Brgy. Santa Cruz, Antipolo, Rizal (sharing tower space with Hope Channel Philippines). The station operates daily from 5:00 AM to 12:00 MN and airs 24/7 via online streaming through Radio Garden App.
