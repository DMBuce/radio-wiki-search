XEPE-AM (1700 AM) is a radio station in Tecate, Baja California, Mexico, serving the San Diego-Tijuana area. The station's signal is owned and operated by Media Sports de México (itself owned by Mexican businessman Jaime Bonilla Valdez).

XEPE is on the air 24 hours a day at 10,000 watts in the AM expanded band, commonly known as the "X band". The 1700 kHz frequency has de facto clear channel status. There are no expanded AM band stations at 1700 AM in the United States west of Texas or in the rest of Mexico. Unlike X-band stations in the U.S., which reduce their power to 1,000 watts at night, XEPE keeps its 10,000 watts of power around the clock.
