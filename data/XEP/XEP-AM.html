XEP-AM (1300 kHz) is a commercial AM radio station in Ciudad Juárez, Chihuahua, also heard in the El Paso metropolitan area along the U.S.-Mexico border. XEP is known as Fiesta Mexicana and is owned by Radiorama. It airs a Spanish-language talk radio format.
