CKMW-FM is a Canadian radio station licensed to Winkler, Manitoba, serving the Pembina Valley region broadcasting at 88.9 FM with a country format branded as Country 88.9. The station is currently owned & operated by Golden West Broadcasting.

CKMW shares its location with sister stations CJEL-FM and CFAM.
