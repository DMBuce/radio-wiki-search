CKMM-FM is a Top 40/CHR-formatted radio station owned by Bell Media, branded as 103.1 Virgin Radio. The station broadcasts from 1445 Pembina Highway, Winnipeg, Manitoba with sister stations CFWM-FM and CFRW, while its transmitter is located at Duff Roblin Provincial Park.

Based on the current format, CKMM-FM competes with CHWE-FM.
