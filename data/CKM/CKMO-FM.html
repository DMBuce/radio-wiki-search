CKMO-FM (myFM) is a Canadian radio station, which broadcasts an English language adult contemporary format on the frequency of 101.5 MHz in Orangeville, Ontario. The station operates from a studio and offices on Mill Street, in downtown Orangeville. The My Broadcasting Corporation station received approval to broadcast from the CRTC on July 18, 2014. The station broadcasts with an average effective radiated power (ERP) of 338 watts (maximum ERP of 625 watts) with an effective height of antenna above average terrain of 55.1 metres, and is located north of the city on Blind Line Road. CKMO-FM is the only Orangeville-based station to specifically target Orangeville; the town's only other licensed radio station, CIDC-FM, focuses on the entire Greater Toronto Area.
