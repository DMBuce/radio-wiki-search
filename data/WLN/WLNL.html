WLNL (1000 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a Christian radio format. It is licensed to Horseheads, New York, United States, and serves the Elmira-Corning area. The station is owned by Trinity Media, Ltd and features programming from Salem Communications.

1000 AM is a United States and Mexican clear-channel frequency; WLNL must leave the air from sunset to sunrise to prevent interference with the nighttime skywave signals of the Class A stations.
