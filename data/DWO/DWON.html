DWON (104.7 FM), broadcasting as 104.7 iFM, is a radio station owned and operated by the Radio Mindanao Network. The station's studio and transmitter are located at the 3rd floor, Marigold Bldg., M.H. del Pilar St., Brgy. Herrero, Dagupan. It operates 24/7.
