KTHN (92.1 FM, Big County 92.1) is a radio station broadcasting a country music format. Licensed to La Junta, Colorado, United States, the station is currently owned by 25-7 Media, Inc. The programming is primarily produced by 25-7 Media employees. The station does air Intelligence for Your Life featuring John Tesh in the evenings.
