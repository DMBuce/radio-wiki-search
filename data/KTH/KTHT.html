KTHT (97.1 MHz "Country Legends 97.1") is a commercial FM radio station licensed to Cleveland, Texas, and serves the northern section of Greater Houston. The station airs a classic country radio format and is owned by Cox Radio, along with 107.5 KGLK, 106.9 KHPT and 92.9 KKBQ. The studios and offices are in 3 Post Oak Central in the Uptown Houston district.

KTHT has an effective radiated power (ERP) of 100,000 watts, the maximum permitted for non-grandfathered FM station. The transmitter site is off Route 222 at Bob McGowan Road in Shepherd, Texas, near Sam Houston National Forest. That puts it about halfway between Houston and Lufkin, Texas. KTHT programming is simulcast in HD radio on sister station 92.9 KKBQ's HD-2 subchannel.
