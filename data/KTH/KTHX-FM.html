KTHX-FM (100.1 FM, "The X") is an adult album alternative commercial radio station located in Dayton, Nevada broadcasting to the Reno and Lake Tahoe area. It is owned by Lotus Communications. Its studios are located on Plumb Lane in South Reno, and its transmitter is located on McClellan Peak northeast of Carson City, Nevada.
