DZEL (1260 AM) Radyo Agila is a radio station owned and operated by the Eagle Broadcasting Corporation in the Philippines. Despite being a simulcast of DZEC in Manila, the station has its own originating programs. The station's studio and transmitter are located at Brgy. Mayao Silangan, Lucena City.
