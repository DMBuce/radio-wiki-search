KAGL (93.3 FM, "The Eagle") is a radio station broadcasting a classic rock format. Licensed to El Dorado, Arkansas, United States, the station serves the El Dorado area. The station is currently owned by Noalmark Broadcasting Corporation and features programming from ABC Radio .
