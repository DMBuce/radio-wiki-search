KAGC (1510 AM, 97.3 FM) is an American terrestrial radio station, paired with an FM relay translator, broadcasting a Southern Gospel format. Licensed to Bryan, Texas, United States, the station serves the Bryan-College Station area. The station is owned by Bryan Broadcasting Corporation through its licensee Bryan Broadcasting License Corporation and features programming from Salem Communications. The station's studios are located in College Station and its transmitter is in Bryan.

1510 AM is a United States clear-channel frequency, on which WLAC in Nashville, Tennessee is the dominant Class A station.
