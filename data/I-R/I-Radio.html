I-Radio is a radio station in Jakarta, Indonesia. I-Radio only plays Indonesian pop songs, unlike rivals like RDI and Bens Radio that mix with dangdut music or Gen FM who plays a few Western songs.

Its headquarters are at Sarinah Tower Lt 8, Jl. MH. Thamrin 11 Central Jakarta.
