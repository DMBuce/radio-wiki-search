CFTK is a Canadian radio station that broadcasts an Adult contemporary format at 590 AM in Terrace, British Columbia. The station is branded as EZ Rock. The station is owned by Bell Media.

The station began broadcasting in 1960 at 1140 AM, and later moved to its current frequency in 1963.
