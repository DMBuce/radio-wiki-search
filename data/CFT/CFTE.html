CFTE (1410 AM) is a radio station located in Vancouver, British Columbia, Canada. On 1410 AM, it is owned by Bell Media and currently carries a business news format as BNN Bloomberg Radio 1410, which primarily simulcasts programming from the television channel BNN Bloomberg, as well as programs from the U.S. Bloomberg Radio network.

CFTE's studios are located on Robson and Burrard Street in Downtown Vancouver; however the station's local programming is currently limited to weekend specialty programming, and traffic and weather updates. Its transmitters are located near Delta.
