KBHT (104.9 FM, "Bob FM") is a radio station broadcasting a variety hits format. Licensed to Bellmead, Texas, United States, the station serves the Waco area. The station is currently owned by M&M Broadcasters. Its studios are in Waco, and its transmitter is located west of Mexia, Texas.
