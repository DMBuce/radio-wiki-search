KBHU-FM (89.1 FM) and KJKT-FM (90.7 FM) are radio stations broadcasting a College radio format. Licensed to Spearfish, South Dakota, United States, the station is currently owned by Black Hills State University.

KBHU-FM and KJKT-FM is affiliated with the Intercollegiate Broadcast Corporation and the National Association of Broadcasters.
