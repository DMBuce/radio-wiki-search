WTWB (1570 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a Spanish music format. Licensed to Auburndale, Florida, United States, the station serves the Lakeland area. The station is currently owned by Carpenter's Home Church, Inc. and features programming from ABC Radio, Fox News Radio and Westwood One.

1570 AM is a Mexican clear-channel frequency; XERF is the dominant Class A station.
