WCHO-FM (105.5 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a country music format. The station is licensed to Washington Court House, Ohio, in the United States. The station is currently owned by iHeartMedia, Inc. and licensed as Citicasters Licenses, L.P. and features programming from ABC Radio and Premiere Radio Networks, as well as agriculture programming from the Agri Broadcast Network.
