WCHD (99.9 MHz, "Channel 999") is a commercial radio station licensed to Kettering, Ohio, and serving the Dayton metropolitan area. The station is owned by iHeartMedia, Inc. and airs a Mainstream Top 40 (CHR) radio format. Its studios are located just outside downtown Dayton and its transmitter is off Sandridge Drive near Interstate 75 in Moraine, Ohio

WCHD broadcasts in the HD format. Its HD-2 channel is the primary station for the iHeart Smooth Jazz format.
