WCHB is an Urban Gospel station at 1340 AM licensed to Royal Oak, Michigan and broadcasting in the Detroit area. It is known as AM 1340 WCHB and owned by Crawford Broadcasting with WMUZ-FM 103.5 FM and WRDT 560 AM. WCHB broadcasts in the HD format. The station is a reporter to Billboard's Nielsen/BDS Gospel airplay panel.
