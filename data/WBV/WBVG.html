WBVG (1050 kHz) is an AM radio station licensed to Baldwinsville, New York serving the Syracuse metropolitan area. WBVG is owned by Leatherstocking Media Group and it airs a 1950s - 1960s oldies radio format. The transmitter is on Hencle Road (Route 631) in Baldwinsville.

WBVG is powered at 2,500 watts by day. But because AM 1050 is a clear channel frequency, WBVG must reduce power at night to only 19 watts to avoid interference. Outside of Baldwinsville, the 1050 frequency in Central New York at night is either occupied by WEPN New York City or CHUM Toronto, both 50,000 watt stations. They sometimes are heard over WBVG at night, even a mile or two from its transmitter.
