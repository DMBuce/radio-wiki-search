WBVX (92.1 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a classic rock format. Licensed to Carlisle, Kentucky, United States, the station serves the Lexington metropolitan area and Bluegrass region of central Kentucky. The station is currently owned by L.M. Communications of Kentucky, LLC. The station's studios are located at Triangle Center in downtown Lexington, and its transmitter is located in far northeast Fayette County, Kentucky.
