WBVR-FM (96.7 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a country music format. The station, which is licensed to Auburn, Kentucky, United States, and serving the Bowling Green area, is owned by Forever Communications, Inc.

The station's transmitter is located in northwestern Allen County just east of Alvaton.
