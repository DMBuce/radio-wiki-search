KPPV (106.7 FM, "The Mix 106.7 FM") is an American radio station licensed to serve Prescott Valley, Arizona, United States. The station is owned by Arizona's Hometown Radio Group and licensed to Prescott Valley Broadcasting Co. Inc. It airs an adult contemporary music format.

The station was assigned the "KPPV" call sign by the Federal Communications Commission on October 7, 1994.
