KRVL (94.3 FM) is a radio station licensed to Kerrville, Texas, United States. It broadcasts a classic rock format as "Rev FM". The station is currently owned by Justin McClure, Angela Krause, and Michael Krause, through licensee JAM Broadcasting, LLC.
