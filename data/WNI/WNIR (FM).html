WNIR (100.1 FM) – branded WNIR 100 FM – is a commercial talk radio station licensed to Kent, Ohio, serving the Akron metro area. Owned by Media-Com, Inc., the station is the local affiliate for ABC News Radio, syndicated personalities Jim Bohannon and Mike Gallagher, and is the radio home of Bob Golic. The WNIR studios are in Franklin Township, while the station transmitter resides in Brimfield. In addition to a standard analog transmission, WNIR is available online.
