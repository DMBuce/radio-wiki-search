WNIO (1390 AM - branded 1390 The Gambler) — is an American radio station in Youngstown, Ohio, broadcasting at 1390 kHz with a sports talk radio format, serving as the Youngstown affiliate for Fox Sports Radio and the BetR Network. WNIO also carries Ohio State University football and basketball, Pittsburgh Pirates baseball, Cleveland Cavaliers basketball, Pittsburgh Steelers football, and the Indianapolis 500 from the Indianapolis Motor Speedway Radio Network.
