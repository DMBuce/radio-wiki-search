KTBA (AM) is a radio station licensed to serve Tuba City, Arizona, United States. The station is owned by Western Indian Ministries. It airs a Religious radio format including teaching programs in English, Navajo, and Hopi languages plus syndicated programming from Moody Broadcasting Network.
