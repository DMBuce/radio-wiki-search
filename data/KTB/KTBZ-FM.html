KTBZ-FM (94.5 FM) – branded 94.5 The Buzz – is a commercial active/alternative rock radio station licensed to Houston, Texas. Owned by iHeartMedia, the station serves Greater Houston. The KTBZ-FM studios are located in Uptown Houston, while the station transmitter resides in Southeast Houston; KTBZ-FM has an effective radiated power of 100,000 watts, the highest permitted for non-grandfathered FM stations.

KTBZ-FM broadcasts in the HD Radio format, with its HD2 subchannel carrying a simulcast of co-owned KBME "Sportstalk 790", and its HD3 subchannel carrying a simulcast of co-owned KPRC.
