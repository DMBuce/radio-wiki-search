KTBT (92.1 The Beat) is a Top 40 (CHR) radio station, serving the Tulsa area. The iHeartMedia outlet broadcasts at 92.1 MHz with an ERP of 27 kW and is licensed to Broken Arrow, Oklahoma. The station can be heard as far north as southeast Kansas. Its studios are located at the Tulsa Event Center in Southeast Tulsa and its transmitter site is near Lookout Mountain in southwest Tulsa.

KTBT broadcasts in the HD digital format.
