KTBZ (1430 AM, "1430 the Buzz") is a radio station licensed to serve Tulsa, Oklahoma. The station is owned by iHeartMedia, Inc. and licensed to Clear Channel Broadcasting Licenses, Inc. It airs a Sports radio format. Its studios are located at the Tulsa Event Center in Southeast Tulsa and its transmitter site is in North Tulsa.

The station has been assigned these call letters by the Federal Communications Commission since June 5, 2001.
