Radio Caritas Mariae 98.3 is a non-commercial Catholic FM station owned by the Archdiocese of Cáceres under Global Broadcasting System. The station's studios and transmitter is located beside the Mary Coredemptrix Church at KM 4.2 Pacol Road, Brgy. San Felipe, Naga, Camarines Sur. It operates daily from 5:00 AM to 12:00 MN.
