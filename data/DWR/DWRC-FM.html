DWRC, branded as Sigaw 95.5 Music & News FM, is a radio station, owned by Filipinas Broadcasting Network. Its studios are located at Capt. F. Aquende Dr., Legazpi City. It broadcasts Monday to Saturday from 5:00 AM to 5:00 PM and Sunday from 6:00 AM to 12:00 NN.
