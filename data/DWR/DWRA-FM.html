DWRA (92.7 FM), on-air as Barangay FM 92.7, is a radio station owned and operated by GMA Network Inc.. The station's studio is located at the 2nd Floor, Baguio Midland Courier, #16 Kisad Rd., Baguio and its transmitter is located at La Trinidad, Benguet.
