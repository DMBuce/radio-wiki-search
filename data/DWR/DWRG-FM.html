DWRG (105.5 MHz), broadcasting as 105.5 Idol News FM, is a news/talk and music FM radio station owned and operated by Apollo Broadcast Investors. Its studios are located at the 5th floor, S.T. Bldg., Naga College Foundation Compound, Brgy. Peñafrancia, Naga City, Philippines (sharing the same site with One Media TV-48 Naga).
