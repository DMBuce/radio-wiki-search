DWRT (99.5 FM), on-air as 99.5 Play FM (stylized as PL>Y), is a commercial radio station owned and operated by Real Radio Network Inc., and is one of the partner stations of Tiger 22 Media. Its studio is located at Unit 906-B, Paragon Plaza Building, EDSA corner Reliance St, Mandaluyong City and its transmitter located at Palos Verdes, Antipolo City.
