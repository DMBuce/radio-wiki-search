Cambridge 105 Radio is the Ofcom regulated community radio station for the city of Cambridge. A volunteer-run, community-owned, not-for-profit organisation. The station broadcasts from its studios on the Gwydir Street Enterprise Centre across the City and South Cambridgeshire on FM, online and DAB Digital Radio seven days a week.
