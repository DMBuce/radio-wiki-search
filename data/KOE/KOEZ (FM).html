KOEZ (104.1 MHz, ”104.1 EZ FM") is a commercial FM radio station broadcasting a soft adult contemporary music format. Licensed to Ames, Iowa, United States, the station serves the Des Moines metropolitan area. The station is currently owned by Saga Communications. The station's studios are located on Locust Street in Des Moines, while its transmitter is located near Woodward.
