KAKT (105.1 FM, "The Wolf") is a radio station broadcasting a country music format. Licensed to Phoenix, Oregon, United States, the station serves the Medford-Ashland area. The station is currently owned by Mapleton License of Medford, LLC.

Syndicated programming includes After Midnite with Blair Garner hosted by Blair Garner from Premiere Radio Networks.
