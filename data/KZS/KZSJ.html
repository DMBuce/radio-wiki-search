KZSJ (1120 AM) is a broadcast radio station licensed to San Martin, California, United States. Broadcasting with a Vietnamese format, the station serves San Jose and the Santa Clara Valley. The station is currently owned by Bustos Media. Programming is produced by Quê Hương Media, which used to air on a digital subchannel of KAXT-CD.
