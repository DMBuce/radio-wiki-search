KEZQ (92.9 FM, "The Big EZ") is a radio station licensed to serve West Yellowstone, Montana. The station is owned by Chaparral Broadcasting, Inc. It airs a Soft Adult Contemporary music format.

The station was assigned the KEZQ call letters by the U.S. Federal Communications Commission (FCC) on November 30, 1998.
