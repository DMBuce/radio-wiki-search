KRRW (105.9 FM; "North Star Country 105.9") is a radio station broadcasting a classic country format. Licensed to serve Winthrop, Minnesota, the station serves the New Ulm area, as well as the Mankato metropolitan area with a fringe signal. KRRW is owned by Linder Radio Group.
