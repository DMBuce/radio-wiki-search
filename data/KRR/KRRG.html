KRRG ("Big Buck 98.1 Today's Best Country") FM is a country music-formatted radio station that serves the Laredo, Texas, United States and Nuevo Laredo, Tamaulipas, Mexico border area.

KRRG broadcasts four HD radio subchannels HD1 is the analog signal, HD2 aires a Bilingual CHR format branded as Mega Mix 98.1 HD2, HD3 aires a 1980s hits format branded as Energy 98 HD3, HD4 aires a Tejano format as Super Tejano 98.1 HD4.
