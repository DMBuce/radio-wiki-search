KRRM (94.7 FM) was a radio station broadcasting a classic country music format. Licensed to Rogue River, Oregon, United States, the station serves the Medford-Ashland area. The station was owned by Shirley M. Bell.KRRM aired talk programming during the evenings airing the syndicated show, Coast to Coast AM.
