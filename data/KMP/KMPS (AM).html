KMPS (910 AM) is a commercial radio station that is licensed to Hesperia, California and broadcasts to the Victor Valley area. It is owned by El Dorado Broadcasters and airs a sports radio format with programming from CBS Sports Radio. KMPS's offices and studios are in Victorville; previously, they were on Hesperia Road in Hesperia. The station's transmitter is located near Mesa Linda Street in Oak Hills. KMPS carried the Los Angeles Angels baseball games before going silent.
