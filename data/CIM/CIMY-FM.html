CIMY-FM is the call sign of a radio station located in Pembroke, Ontario on the FM dial at 104.9 FM. Owned by My Broadcasting Corporation, the station airs an adult contemporary format branded as myFM.

The station was licensed by the Canadian Radio-television and Telecommunications Commission in 2005.

On November 2, 2010, My Broadcasting applied to change the authorized contours by increasing the average effective radiated power (ERP). This application was approved on January 13, 2011.
