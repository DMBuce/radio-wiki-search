CIMA-FM (92.1 myFM) is an FM radio station, operating on the frequency of 92.1 MHz in Alliston, Ontario, Canada. The My Broadcasting Corporation station broadcasts a gold-based adult contemporary format. My Broadcasting received approval for the new station from the CRTC on September 12, 2012.

On August 30, 2013, CIMA-FM began on-air tests. The station began regular broadcasting on September 19, 2013.
