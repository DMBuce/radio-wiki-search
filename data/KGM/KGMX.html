KGMX (106.3 FM, "K-Mix 106.3") is a commercial radio station that is licensed to Lancaster, California, United States and serves the Antelope Valley area. The station is owned by High Desert Broadcasting and broadcasts a Top 40 (CHR) format. The station is also simulcasts on 106.9 FM KMVE California City, California.
