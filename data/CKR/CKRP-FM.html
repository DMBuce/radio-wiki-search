CKRP-FM, branded as Nord-Ouest FM since 2020, is a Canadian French language community radio station that operates at 95.7 FM in Falher, Alberta. The station was established in the early 1990s-via several temporary on-air samples-and signed on October 1996. From 1996 to 2017, it was operated by the regional ACFA.

On November 21, 2017, it was announced that CKRP-FM would cease to operate. The station was relaunched under a new committee independent of the ACFA in 2019.
