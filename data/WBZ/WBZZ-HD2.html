WBZZ HD-2 is a 2000s hit radio station in New Kensington, Pennsylvania which carries the B94 branding from its former frequency at 93.7, currently occupied by KDKA-FM. The last time the B94 moniker was used prior to this station was in 2010 when the sports talk format took over the 93.7 frequency.
