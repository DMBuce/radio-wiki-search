WBZZ (100.7 FM, "100.7 Star") is an adult-oriented Top 40 radio station licensed to New Kensington, Pennsylvania, targeting Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, and owned by Entercom. Its transmitter is located in Pittsburgh's Spring Hill district and its studios are located west of downtown.

Due to the presence of co-channel WMMS in Cleveland, WBZZ only partially covers the northwestern Pittsburgh radio market.
