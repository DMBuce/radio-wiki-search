WBZX (103.9 FM) is a radio station in Big Rapids, Michigan. Owned by Up North Radio, LLC, the station broadcasts a classic hits format branded as B103.9.

The station first broadcast on August 24, 2016, stunting with a beautiful music format until its official launch on August 27, 2016. The station is owned by Up North Radio, who also owns and operates WCKC, Cadillac, and WCDY, McBain-Cadillac.
