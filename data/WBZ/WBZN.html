WBZN (107.3 MHz) is a commercial FM radio station licensed to Old Town, Maine, and serving Bangor and Central Maine. It is owned by Townsquare Media and it airs a Top 40 - CHR radio format. The studios and offices are on Acme Road in Brewer.

WBZN has an effective radiated power (ERP) of 50,000 watts. The transmitter is off Miller Way in Alton.
