KMGE (94.5 FM, "94-5 Mix FM") is a radio station licensed to serve Eugene, Oregon, United States. The station, which began broadcasting in 1962, is currently owned by McKenzie River Broadcasting. KMGE broadcasts an adult contemporary music format to the Eugene-Springfield, Oregon, area.
