WYBP (90.3 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a Traditional Christian music format, including national teaching/preaching programming.

Licensed to Fort Lauderdale, Florida, United States, the station is currently owned by the Bible Broadcasting Network, Inc. Translator stations are 88.3 WYBX Key West, Florida and 106.9 Marathon, Florida. The station (WAFG) was purchased effective July 27, 2012 from the Westminster Academy with transmission facilities at Coral Ridge Presbyterian Church. A construction permit has been granted to relocate and upgrade the station's transmission facilities.
