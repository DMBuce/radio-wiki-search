WYBB (98.1 FM, "98 Rock") is a mainstream rock music formatted radio station in Charleston, South Carolina. The station is licensed by the Federal Communications Commission (FCC) to Folly Beach and broadcasts with an effective radiated power (ERP) of 23 kW on 98.1 FM. The station's studios are located in Charleston due west of downtown (west of the Ashley River) and the transmitter site is in Mount Pleasant.
