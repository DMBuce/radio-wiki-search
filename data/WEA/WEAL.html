WEAL ("Big WEAL") is a gospel radio station in Greensboro, North Carolina targeting African Americans. It is at 1510 and broadcasts only during daylight hours allowing "clear channel" station WLAC in Nashville, Tennessee to cover the southern portion of the Atlantic coast. Owned by Entercom, the station's studios are near Piedmont Triad International Airport in Greensboro, and a transmitter site is downtown.
