WSLK (880 AM, "Lake Radio 880") is a radio station licensed to serve Moneta, Virginia, United States. The station is owned and operated by Smile Broadcasting, LLC.

WSLK broadcasts an Adult Standards and Oldies music format serving Smith Mountain Lake area in parts of Bedford and Franklin Counties in Virginia.
