WSLV is a country music formatted broadcast radio station licensed to Ardmore, Tennessee, serving Southern Giles and Southern Lincoln counties in Tennessee and Northern Limestone and Northern Madison counties in Alabama. WSLV is owned and operated by Gra Communications, LLC.
