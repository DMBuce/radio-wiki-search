DXFX (96.3 FM) - branded as 96.3 Star FM Davao - is the FM commercial radio station owned and operated by People's Broadcasting Service, Inc. and managed by Bombo Radyo Philippines. The station's studio, offices and transmitter are located at Bombo Radyo Broadcast Center, San Pedro St., Davao City, Philippines. It airs everyday from 5:00 AM to 9:30 PM (after Holy Rosary) and except Holy Week of each year where it signs-off at 3:00 PM of Good Friday and lasts until 4:00 AM of Easter Sunday.
