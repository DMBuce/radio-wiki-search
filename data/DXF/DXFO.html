DXFO (87.9 FM), broadcasting as Republika 87.9 FM1, is a radio station owned and operated by government-run Philippine Broadcasting Service. Its studios is located at Braveheart Bldg., Gen. Douglas MacArthur Highway, Matina, Davao City, and its transmitter is located at Shrine Hills, Matina, Davao City. It operates daily from 5:00 AM to 12:00 MN.
