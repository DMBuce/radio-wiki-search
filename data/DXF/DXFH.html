DXFH (98.7 FM), broadcasting as MOR 98.7 For Life!, was a radio station owned and operated by the ABS-CBN Corporation. The station's studio and transmitter were located at the ABS-CBN Broadcast Center, San Jose Rd., Zamboanga City.
