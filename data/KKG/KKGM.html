KKGM is an AM Ministries radio station that serves the Dallas-Fort Worth Metroplex, and is owned by Mortenson Broadcasting.

In addition to Southern Gospel Music and ministries, KKGM is also the Dallas radio broadcast affiliate of Oklahoma Sooners football games, Texas A&M Aggies men's basketball games, and UT Arlington Mavericks men's basketball games.
