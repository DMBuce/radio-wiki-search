KKGR (93.1 FM, 680 AM), also known as KGR Radio, is a radio station licensed to serve Helena, Montana. The station is owned by KGR, LLC. It airs a Classic Hits music format.

The station has been assigned the KKGR call letters by the Federal Communications Commission since November 26, 1996 and has been owned by KGR, LLC since 1999.

It can be streamed live online through its website, KGRradio.com.
