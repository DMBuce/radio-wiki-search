WSRB (106.3 MHz) is an urban Adult Contemporary radio station serving the Chicago metropolitan area and Northwest Indiana. It is licensed to the Southland suburb of Lansing, Illinois, and transmits within that city from a tower along the north side of the Kingery Expressway. The station is owned by Crawford Broadcasting and its studios are in Hammond, Indiana.

WSRB broadcasts in the HD Radio format.
