WSRA (1250 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a Sports radio format. Licensed to Albany, Georgia, United States, the station serves the Albany, GA area. The station is currently owned by Livingston W. Fulton and features programming from ESPN Radio. The stations website currently shows the NBC Sports logo.
