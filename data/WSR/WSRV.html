WSRV (97.1 MHz "97.1 The River") is a commercial FM radio station in Atlanta, Georgia. It airs a classic rock radio format and is owned by the Cox Media Group. WSRV's studios and offices are in the Cox Television and Radio Facility on West Peachtree Street near the Brookwood neighborhood of Atlanta.

WSRV has an effective radiated power (ERP) of 100,000 watts, and transmits from a tower off Eagle Ranch Road in Braselton, at the northeastern edge of Metro Atlanta. WSRV broadcasts in the HD Radio hybrid format, with its HD2 subchannel carrying an alternative rock format known as "The Other Side of The River."
