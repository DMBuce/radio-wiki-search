WSRW-FM (105.7 MHz "Star 105.7") is a commercial FM radio station in Grand Rapids, Michigan. It airs an adult contemporary radio format and is owned by iHeartMedia. The studios and offices are on Monroe Center Street in Downtown Grand Rapids. The transmitter is off Payne Lake Road in Middleville.

WSRW-FM broadcasts in the HD Radio format. Smooth Jazz airs on its "HD2" subchannel, and is also available online at: https://www.iheart.com/live/smooth-jazz-4242/
