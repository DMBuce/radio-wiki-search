CISS-FM is a Canadian radio station in broadcasting at 105.3 FM in Ottawa, Ontario. The station currently broadcasts a hot adult contemporary format branded on-air as KiSS 105.3 and is owned by Rogers Sports & Media. CISS-FM's studios are co-located with its sister stations at Thurston Drive and Conroy Road, while its transmitter is located in Camp Fortune, Quebec.
