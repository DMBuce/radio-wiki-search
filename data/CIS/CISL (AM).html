CISL (650 kHz) is a commercial AM radio station in Richmond, British Columbia, owned by Rogers Sports & Media. The station, which serves the Metro Vancouver media market, airs a sports radio format branded as Sportsnet 650 Vancouver.

CISL's studios are located at 2440 Ash Street in the Fairview neighbourhood of Vancouver, while its transmitter is located on Lulu Island.
