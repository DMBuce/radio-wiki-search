WLUZ (88.5 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a Spanish Christian Contemporary format. Licensed to Levittown, Puerto Rico, it serves the northern Puerto Rico area. The station is currently owned by La Gigante Siembra Inc.. The station is also simulcast on 91.7 WZCA in Quebradillas, Puerto Rico & 89.5 W208AE in Mayaguez, Puerto Rico. WLUZ's programming is served via booster station WLUZ-FM1 88.5 FM in Guayama, Puerto Rico.
