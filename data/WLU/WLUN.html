ESPN 100.9-FM (WLUN) is a radio station broadcasting a sports radio format in mid-Michigan owned by the Michigan Baseball Foundation (owner of the Great Lakes Loons), under the licensee name of "Michigan Radio Communications." The station's antenna is located in Pinconning, Michigan, and reaches Midland, Bay City, Saginaw, among other surrounding cities. Its studios are housed at Dow Diamond.
