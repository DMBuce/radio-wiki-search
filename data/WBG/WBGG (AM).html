WBGG is a sports radio station based in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, owned by iHeartMedia. The station broadcasts at 970 kHz with 5,000 watts day and night. WBGG's studios are located in Green Tree, and its transmitter is located in Ross Township, Pennsylvania.
