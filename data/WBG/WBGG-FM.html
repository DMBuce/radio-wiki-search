WBGG-FM, branded as Big 105.9, is a commercial radio station licensed to Ft. Lauderdale, Florida and serving the Miami-Ft. Lauderdale media market. Owned by iHeartMedia, the station airs a classic rock radio format. WBGG's studios are located on Rivera Boulevard in Miramar and the transmitter site is off Fifth Street in Pembroke Park.

WBGG is licensed for HD Radio and carries the talk radio format of sister station WIOD AM 610 on its HD2 channel. The HD3 channel carries an iHeartRadio soft adult contemporary format, known as "The Breeze."
