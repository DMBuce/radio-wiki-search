WBGI (1340 AM) was a radio station licensed to Connellsville, Pennsylvania, United States. The station was owned by Keymarket Licenses, LLC. The station began broadcasting April 23, 1947, and held the call sign WCVI. Its license was cancelled on July 19, 2012.
