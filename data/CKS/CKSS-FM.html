CKSS-FM is an FM radio station in Parkland County, Alberta, Canada. Licensed to Stony Plain, Alberta and owned by Blackgold Broadcasting, it broadcasts a country music format branded as 88.1 The One

Programming team consists of Katie Stanners on One Mornings, Justin Hartman on the Drive Home and Shane Michaels on One Evenings.
