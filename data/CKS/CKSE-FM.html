CKSE-FM is a Mainstream Rock formatted broadcast radio station licensed to Estevan, Saskatchewan, Canada, serving Estevan, Saskatchewan in Canada and Divide and Burke counties in North Dakota, United States. CKSE-FM is owned and operated by Golden West Broadcasting. As of the 2016/2017 hockey season, the station is also now the official carrier of Estevan Bruins hockey broadcasts.
