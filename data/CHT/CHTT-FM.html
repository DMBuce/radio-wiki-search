CHTT-FM is a Canadian radio station in Victoria, British Columbia, which airs at 103.1 FM. The station, owned by Rogers Sports & Media, airs an Adult hits format, branded as JACK 103.1.

The station placed 7th (last) in the fall 2018 Numeris Diary Survey for Victoria.
