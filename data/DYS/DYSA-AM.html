DYSA (1053 kHz) is an AM station owned and operated by Ang Dios Gugma Ministries under Global Broadcasting System. Its studios are located at Iloilo Sports Complex Bldg., Magsaysay Village, La Paz District, Iloilo City, and its transmitter is located at Brgy. Navais, Mandurriao District, Iloilo City.
