DYSS (pronounced DY-double-S; 999Â kHz AM) Super Radyo Cebu is a commercial radio station owned and operated by RGMA Network Inc. (RGMA, a subsidiary of GMA Network, Inc.). The station's studio is located at the GMA Skyview Complex, Nivel Hills, Apas, Cebu City, while its transmitter is located at Alumnos, Brgy. Mambaling, Cebu City. DYSS-AM operates Mondays to Saturdays from 3:30 AM to 11:00 PM and on Sundays from 4:30 AM to 9:00 PM.

At present, Super Radyo DYSS 999 Cebu is considered as one of the top stations in the AM band in Metro Cebu and Central Visayas.
