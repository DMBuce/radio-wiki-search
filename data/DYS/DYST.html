DYST-FM, under the brand 92.3 Easy Rock Iloilo, is a music FM station owned by Cebu Broadcasting Company and managed by RVV Broadcast Ventures in Iloilo, under the Easy Rock Network of the Manila Broadcasting Company. Its studio is located at Kahirup Bldg., Guanco Street, Iloilo City, along with their sister station Love Radio Iloilo DYMB 97.5.
