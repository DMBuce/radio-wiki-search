DYSM (972 AM) Aksyon Radyo is a radio station owned and operated by Cebu Broadcasting Company, a subsidiary of Manila Broadcasting Company. The station's studio is located at #102 National Highway, Brgy. Cawayan, Catarman, Northern Samar.

On December 17, 2015, DYSM's transmitter was knocked down by Typhoon Melor (Nona).
