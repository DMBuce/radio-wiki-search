DYSP (909 kHz AM) Super Radyo Palawan is a news/talk radio station in Puerto Princesa. It is owned by Radio GMA Network, Inc. (RGMA, a subsidiary of GMA Network Inc.) in the Philippines. The station's studio and transmitter are located at Solid Road, San Manuel, Puerto Princesa City, Palawan.

At present, Super Radyo DYSP 909 is considered as one of the top stations in the AM band in the province. The station operates daily from 5:30 AM to 9:00 PM.
