DYSR (95.1 FM), broadcasting as SR95, is a radio station owned and operated by the National Council of Churches in the Philippines, and is part of the Magic Nationwide network. Its studio and transmitter are located at Camp SEA Site, Brgy. Banilad, Dumaguete. The station broadcasts daily from 5:30 AM to 12:00 MN.
