WJAD (branded as Rock 103) is a radio station serving Albany, Georgia and surrounding cities with a rock format. This station broadcasts on FM frequency 103.5 MHz and is under ownership of Cumulus Media. Programming includes, John Boy and Billy in the mornings, Nikki Miller in middays, Bill Dollar in afternoons and Flashback on Sundays.

On April 30, 2020, Cumulus Media sold its entire Albany cluster for First Media Services for $450,000.
