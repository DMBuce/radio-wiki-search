KWHN (1320 AM, "Newstalk 1320 AM") is a radio station broadcasting a News Talk Information format to the Fort Smith, Arkansas, United States, area. The stations is licensed to Capstar TX Limited Partnership and owned by iHeartMedia, Inc..
