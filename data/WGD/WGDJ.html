WGDJ (1300 kHz) is a commercial AM radio station licensed to Rensselaer, New York and serving the Capital District. The station is owned and operated by Capital Broadcasting, Inc., which airs a talk radio format. The transmitter is off River Road (Route 9J) in Rensselaer. Programming is also heard in Albany and Rensselaer on 80-watt FM translator W254DA at 98.7 MHz.

WGDJ, TALK 1300/98.7 features local talk hosts weekdays, from morning till evening, including the “The Paul Vandenburgh Show” hosted by the baronial Paul Vandenburgh. His show is considered to be the longest running radio program in The Capital District. “Road Rage”, hosted by the pugnacious Kevin McCashion airs at 3pm, Monday to Friday. Weekends feature shows on money, law, home, car repair as well as a local swap and trade show. Syndicated hosts include Dennis Prager, Mark Levin and Brian Kilmeade, among others. The station also features local news at the top and bottom of most hours with national headlines and reports delivered by ABC News.
