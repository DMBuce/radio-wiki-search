WGDL (1200 AM and translator W227DR 93.3 FM, "Radio Grito") is a radio station broadcasting a Spanish Variety format. Licensed to Lares, Puerto Rico, United States, it serves the Puerto Rico area. The station is owned by Lares Broadcasting Corporation.
