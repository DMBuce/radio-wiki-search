WMKT (1270 AM) is a radio station licensed to Charlevoix, Michigan, broadcasting a talk radio format. The station broadcasts on 1270 AM as well as an FM translator on W272CR 102.3 (formerly 92.1) and streams online http://www.wmktthetalkstation.com/. The station features local programming from 7 to 10 am Eastern Time with Greg Marshall, whose first hour is also played on television station WLLZ, known on the air as MY TV 12, to tie in with their My Network TV affiliation. The other WMKT local program is hosted 10 am to noon by longtime TV and radio personality Vic McCarty. The rest of the station's lineup are from syndicated sources. The station is a Fox News Radio affiliate.
