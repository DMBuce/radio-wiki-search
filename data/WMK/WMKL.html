WMKL (91.9 FM) is a non-commercial radio station broadcasting a contemporary Christian radio format. The station is owned by Call Communications Group, Inc.

Call Communications Group, Inc. was formed during the summer of 1994 by a group of college-age young adults in the Miami area. WMKL was purchased in late 1999 and began broadcasting at 8:05 PM on February 9, 2000. Call Communications Group owns or operates additional radio stations that serve Southwest Florida, the Glades region, and the Florida Keys.

WMKL's HD-2 and HD-3 subchannels also air Catholic programming from Radio Maria USA. (originated by KJMJ in Alexandria, Louisiana).

In May 2019, Call Communications announced it would be "phasing out traditional FM radio" in favor of its online operations.
