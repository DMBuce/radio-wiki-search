WMKF is a Contemporary Christian-formatted broadcast radio station licensed to Masontown, Pennsylvania, serving Uniontown, Pennsylvania, Morgantown, West Virginia, and Kingwood, West Virginia.

WMKF is owned and operated by Educational Media Foundation, broadcasting EMF's K-LOVE format.
