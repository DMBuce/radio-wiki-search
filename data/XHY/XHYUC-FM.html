XHYUC-FM is a radio station in Mérida. Broadcasting on 92.9 FM, XHYUC-FM is owned by the Instituto Mexicano de la Radio and broadcasts an oldies music format under the name "Yucatán FM".

XHYUC-FM broadcasts in HD.
