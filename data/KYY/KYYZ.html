KYYZ (96.1 FM) is a radio station licensed to Williston, North Dakota. It broadcasts a country music format. The station was first licensed as KLAN on April 1, 1980.

It also has two sister stations, KEYZ and KTHC. All three stations are owned by Cherry Creek Radio of Denver, Colorado, and are located at 410 6th Street East, on Williston's east side.
