KQID-FM (93.1 FM, "Q93") is a Top 40 (CHR) radio station licensed to Alexandria, Louisiana. The station serves the Central Louisiana, northern Acadiana, Natchez, Mississippi, and Monroe, Louisiana. The station is owned by Cenla Broadcasting. Its studios are located on Texas Avenue in Alexandria, and its transmitter is located southwest of Jena, Louisiana.
