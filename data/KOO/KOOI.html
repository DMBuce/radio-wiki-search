KOOI (106.5 FM; 106-5 Jack FM) is a radio station broadcasting a variety hits format. Licensed to Jacksonville, Texas, United States, the station serves the Tyler-Longview area. The station is currently owned by Alpha Media LLC, through licensee Alpha Media Licensee LLC, and features programming from Fox News Radio. Its studios are located on Broadway Avenue in downtown Tyler, and its transmitter is located north of Jacksonville, in the town of Mt. Selman.
