KTNK (1410 kHz) is a commercial AM radio station that is licensed to Lompoc, California, United States and serves the Santa Maria-Lompoc area. The station, established in 1963, is owned by Michael Alan Day's Cross and Crown Broadcasting Corporation and broadcasts a classic country music format. KTNK features programming from ABC Radio, Jones Radio Network, and Westwood One.
