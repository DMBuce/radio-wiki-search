WMQX (102.3 FM, "Max 102") is a commercial FM radio station licensed to serve Pittston, Pennsylvania. The station is owned by Entercom, through licensee Entercom License, LLC, and airs a Classic Hits format branded as "Max 102". Its broadcast tower is located near Dupont, Pennsylvania at (41°18′20.0″N 75°45′37.0″W).

WMQX uses HD Radio and broadcasts Entercom's LGBTQ talk and dance music service branded as "Channel Q" on its HD2 subchannel.
