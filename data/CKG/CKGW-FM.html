CKGW-FM is a Christian music radio station broadcasting at 89.3 FM in Chatham, Ontario, Canada. The station is owned by United Christian Broadcasters Canada (UCB). It was originally a rebroadcaster of CKJJ from Belleville, but became an independent station in April 2007.
