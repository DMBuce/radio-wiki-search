WZWW (95.3 FM) is a music radio station licensed to Bellefonte, Pennsylvania, serving the State College area. It currently airs a Hot Adult Contemporary format. It is the sister station to WIFT in Dubois, Pennsylvania and WKFT in Strattanville, Pennsylvania.
