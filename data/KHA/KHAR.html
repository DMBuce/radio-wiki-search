KHAR is a commercial sports radio station in Anchorage, Alaska, United States, broadcasting on 590 AM. It features programming from the CBS Sports Radio network. Owned by Alpha Media LLC, its studios are located in Anchorage (two blocks west of Dimond Center), and its transmitter is in the Bayshore neighborhood in South Anchorage.
