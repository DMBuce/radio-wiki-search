NME Radio is a radio station operated under NME magazine branding, that broadcast an alternative music format. It first began broadcasting on 24 June 2008 and ceased on 25 March 2013. The station was then revived in 2018 at http://www.nme.com/audio. There are now two radio stations, NME 1 and NME 2, that broadcast 24/7.
