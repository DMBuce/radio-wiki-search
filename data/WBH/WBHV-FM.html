WBHV-FM (94.5 FM), known as "B 94.5 All The Hits", is a contemporary hit radio station licensed to serve State College, Pennsylvania. The station is owned by Kristin Cantrell, through licensee Southern Belle, LLC. The Program Director is Angela Steco.
