WBHR is a sports talk radio station broadcasting on 660 kHz serving the St. Cloud area of Minnesota. The station is owned by Tri-County Broadcasting, and is an affiliate of ESPN Radio and the Chicago Cubs Radio Network. It is the only station in Minnesota to broadcast Chicago Cubs games. The station shares towers with its three other sister stations. There is a total of seven towers at the site.
