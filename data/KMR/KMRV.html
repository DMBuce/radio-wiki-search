KMRV (1160 AM, "99.1 The River") is a commercial radio station serving the Waukon, Iowa, area. The station broadcasts an adult contemporary format. KMRV is licensed to Wennes Communications Stations, Inc. KMRV was originally a daytime-only station broadcasting on 1140 kHz, with the call letters KNEI.

Greg Wennes is 70% owner of Wennes Communications.
