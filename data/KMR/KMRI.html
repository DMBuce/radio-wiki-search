KMRI (1550 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a Regional Mexican format. Licensed to West Valley City, Utah, United States, the station serves the Salt Lake City area. The station is owned by KMRI LLC. KMRI LLC is owned by communications attorney Barry Wood.
