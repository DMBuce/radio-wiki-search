Sports Radio 88.9 Brila FM also known as Brila FM is a sports radio station founded on October 1, 2002 by Larry Izamoje as Nigeria's first and only sports radio station. Licensed by the Nigerian Broadcasting Commission, Brila FM has three sister stations located in Abuja, Kaduna and Onitsha. During the 2014 FIFA World Cup, the station was appointed as one of the three lead broadcast partners by Optima Sports Management International.
