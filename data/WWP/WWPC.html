WWPC (91.7 FM) is a non-commercial educational radio station licensed to serve New Durham, New Hampshire, United States. The station is owned by Word Radio Educational Foundation. WWPC broadcasts a Christian radio format as a simulcast of WRKJ (88.5 FM) in Westbrook, Maine and WMTP (91.1 FM) in Conway, New Hampshire.
