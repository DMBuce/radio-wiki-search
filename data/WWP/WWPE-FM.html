WWPE-FM (92.1 MHz; "Sasquatch 92.1") is an FM radio station licensed to Hermantown, Minnesota and serves the Twin Ports. Established in 1996, the station is owned by Townsquare Media, through licensee Townsquare Media Duluth License, LLC. It airs a classic rock format branded as "Sasquatch 92.1" (Sasquatch or Bigfoot is a mythical ape-like creature said to inhabit the northern woods of the U.S. and Canada).

The studios and offices are located in the Central Hillside, 806 East 4th street, Duluth and the transmitter is located off South Blackman Avenue, in the Duluth antenna farm.
