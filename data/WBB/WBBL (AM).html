WBBL is a radio station licensed to Whitehall, Michigan. The station broadcasts at 1490 kHz, and is owned by Cumulus Media.

As WUBR ("The Bear"), the station has played adult standards. Later, the format changed to sports, carrying ESPN Radio. In December 2005, the station switched to a talk radio format, but returned to sports in simulcast with WJRW following the January 2019 discontinuation of WBBL-FM's sports format.
