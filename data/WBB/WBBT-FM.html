WBBT-FM (107.3 MHz) is a non-commercial FM radio station licensed to Powhatan, Virginia and serving the Greater Richmond Region. WBBT-FM is owned and operated by VPM Media Corporation. It airs an eclectic music format, focusing mostly on classical music.

Along with simulcast partner WWLB in Ettrick, Virginia, WBBT is branded as VPM Music, and is a sister station to the area's flagship NPR station, WCVE-FM. WBBT serves the northern of the Richmond market, while WWLB serves the southern portion.

WBBT-FM studios and offices are on Sesame Street in Richmond, and its transmitter is off Dry Bridge Road in Midlothian, Virginia.
