WBBA-FM is a News/Talk/Sports formatted broadcast radio station licensed to Pittsfield, Illinois, serving the Pittsfield and Pike County, Illinois. WBBA-FM is owned and operated by DJ Two Rivers Radio. DJ Two Rivers Radio purchased WBBA-FM in 2003, along with sister station WJBM, from Brown Radio Group Inc. for $320,000 in 2003. WBBA-FM aired a country music format at the time of its sale, and was branded "Double barreled country".
