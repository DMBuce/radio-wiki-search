WJXA (92.9 FM) is an adult contemporary radio station. Licensed to Nashville, Tennessee, United States, the station serves the Nashville, Bowling Green, Clarksville-Hopkinsville area. The station is currently owned by Duey Wright, through licensee Midwest Communications, Inc. Its studios are located in south central Nashville near the Tennessee State Fairgrounds and the transmitter site is in far west Nashville.
