WJXQ (106.1 MHz, "Q106") is a commercial FM radio station licensed to Charlotte, Michigan, and serving the Lansing radio market. WJXQ is owned by Midwest Communications and airs an active rock radio format. Studios and offices are located on Cedar St. in Holt. The transmitter is on Prime Road in Springport.

The station begins each weekday with The Bob & Tom Show, syndicated from Indianapolis. Local DJs are heard the rest of each weekday. Weekends feature The House of Hair with Dee Snider, Racing Rocks with Riki Rachtman and Hard Drive with Lou Brutus
