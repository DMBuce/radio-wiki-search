WJXN (100.9 FM, "G100.9") is a radio station serving the Jackson, Mississippi area, broadcasting a classic hip-hop format. The station is owned by Flinn Broadcasting Corporation. Its studios are located in Ridgeland and the transmitter site is in Crystal Springs, Mississippi.
