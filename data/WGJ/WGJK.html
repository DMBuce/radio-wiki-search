WGJK (1360 AM, "99.5 The Jock") is a radio station broadcasting a sports format, with programming from ESPN Radio. Licensed to Rome, Georgia, United States, the station serves the Rome GA area. The station is currently owned by Woman's World and is in an LMA through Rome Radio Partners, LLC.
