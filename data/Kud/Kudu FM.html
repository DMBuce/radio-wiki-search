Kudu FM is a radio station based in Windhoek, Namibia. It has been on the air since 30 November 1998. Though based in Windhoek, Kudu FM can be heard by 95% of Namibia's people. The radio station recently relaunched as JACC, with a format designed to appeal to mature adults in Namibia.
