KFFK (1390 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a Spanish News/Talk format. It is licensed to Rogers, Arkansas, United States, and serves the Fayetteville (Northwest Arkansas) area. The station is owned by the Butler Broadcasting Company, LLC.
