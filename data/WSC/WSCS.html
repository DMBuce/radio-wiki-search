WSCS (90.9 FM) is a radio station licensed to serve New London, New Hampshire. The station is owned by Robert and John Landry, through licensee Sugar River Foundation, Inc. It airs a classical music format.

The station has been assigned the WSCS call letters by the Federal Communications Commission since August 25, 1995.

Formerly owned by Colby-Sawyer College, WSCS was sold to the Vinikoor Family Foundation, Inc. on September 1, 2014, for $4,000. The Vinikoor Family Foundation sold WSCS to Sugar River Foundation, Inc. effective March 7, 2017 for $10,000.
