WSCI (89.3 FM) is a noncommercial Classical/News/Talk in Charleston, South Carolina featuring both Classical music as well as news and other programs from NPR. The station is part of the statewide "Classical NPR network" from ETV Radio. There is only one locally produced program on this remotely fed station.
