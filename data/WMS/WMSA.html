WMSA (1340 AM) is a radio station broadcasting Local and National News, Community Information and Oldies format. Licensed to Massena, New York, United States, the station is owned by the Stephens Media Group.

The station was previously owned by Martz Communications Group, and was acquired by Stephens as of February 1, 2008.

As of 2015, WMSA has been serving Massena and surrounding community for 70 years. WMSA went on the air at 2pm on October 12, 1945.
