WMSX is an FM radio station licensed to and broadcasting from Buffalo, New York on 96.1 FM, which has a soft adult contemporary format. The station is owned by Townsquare Media and has studios based in the Rand Building downtown; the station's transmitter is located on the roof of the same building.
