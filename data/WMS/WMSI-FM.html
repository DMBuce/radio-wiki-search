WMSI-FM (102.9 FM, "Miss 103") is a radio station in Jackson, Mississippi, and owned by iHeartMedia, Inc. (formerly Clear Channel Communications until September 2014). WMSI's signal covers a roughly 90 mile radius around the city with an ERP of 100,000 watts. It is Mississippi's primary entry point for the Emergency Alert System.
