WDSW-LP is a radio station broadcasting an Adult Album Alternative, Roots, Blues, and Classic Rock format. WDSW-LP licensed to Cleveland, Mississippi and Cleveland, Renova, and Boyle in Mississippi. WDSW-LP is owned and operated by Delta State University. It broadcasts from the Nowell Student Union Building on the third floor.
