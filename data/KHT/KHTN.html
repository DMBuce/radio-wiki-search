KHTN (104.7 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a Rhythmic Top 40 format. Licensed to Planada, California, United States, the station serves the Merced area. The station is currently operated by Stephens Media via a LMA deal from Mapleton Communications, with a sale to Stephens pending regulatory approval. Its studios are in Merced and its transmitter is near Mount Bullion. KHTN has been a monitored Rhythmic reporter in both Mediabase and Nielsen BDS since their 1992 launch.
