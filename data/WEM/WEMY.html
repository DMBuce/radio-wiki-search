WEMY is a Christian radio station broadcasting on 91.5 FM, serving the Green Bay, Wisconsin area. The station's format consists of Christian contemporary music with some Christian talk and teaching. WEMY is also heard in the Manitowoc - Two Rivers area through a translator on 95.5. It is part of "The Family" radio network with WEMI.
