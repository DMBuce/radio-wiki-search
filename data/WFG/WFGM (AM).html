WFGM (1300 AM) is a contemporary Christian-formatted broadcast radio station licensed to Morgantown, West Virginia, serving Morgantown and Monongalia County, West Virginia. WFGM is owned by West Virginia Radio Corporation and operated under their AJG Corporation licensee.
