WQOS (1080 AM) is a radio station licensed to Coral Gables, Florida, serving the South Florida Metropolitan Area, including Miami. The station is owned by Immaculate Heart Media, Inc. and is an affiliate of Catholic talk network Relevant Radio. The transmitter is located in Everglades, Florida.

Programming is also heard on low-powered FM translator W249DM (97.7 FM) in Miami. Much of WQOS's programming is also simulcast on WZAB in Sweetwater.
