WQOF (1260 kHz) is a commercial AM radio station licensed to Washington, D.C., and serving the Washington metro area. The station is owned and operated by Relevant Radio and broadcasts its nationally syndicated Catholic talk and teaching programming.

WQOF operates with 35,000 watts during the day and 5,000 watts at night. Its transmitter is off Brookville Road in Silver Spring, Maryland.
