WRKM (1350 AM, "AM 1350 SB Nation Radio") is a radio station broadcasting a Sports radio format. Licensed to Carthage, Tennessee, United States, the station is currently owned by Wood Broadcasting, Inc. and features programming from SB Nation Radio.
