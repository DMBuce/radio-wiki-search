WRKI (95.1 FM, "I-95") is a Classic rock radio station licensed to Brookfield, Connecticut, serving Fairfield, Litchfield and New Haven Counties in Connecticut. The station is owned by Townsquare Media and its studios and transmitter are located on Federal Road in Brookfield, Connecticut.
