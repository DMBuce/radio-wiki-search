WRKJ is a Christian radio station licensed to Westbrook, Maine, broadcasting on 88.5 MHz FM. WRKJ is owned by Word Radio Educational Foundation. The station is also simulcast on 91.1 WMTP in Conway, New Hampshire and 91.7 WWPC in New Durham, New Hampshire.
