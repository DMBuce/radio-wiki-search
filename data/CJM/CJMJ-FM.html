CJMJ-FM (100.3 MHz) is a commercial radio station in Ottawa, Ontario, Canada. The station uses its on-air brand name Majic 100 with an adult contemporary radio format. From late November until December 25, CJMJ-FM plays all-Christmas music. CJMJ-FM is owned by Bell Media, along with three other Ottawa radio stations and two TV stations.

CJMJ's studios and offices are located in the Bell Media Building on George Street in Downtown Ottawa's ByWard Market, while its transmitter is located on the Ryan Tower in Camp Fortune, Quebec, within Gatineau Park.

CJMJ broadcasts in the HD Radio format. Its HD2 subchannel carries the talk programming of sister station CFRA, while co-owned sports station CFGO is heard on an HD3 subchannel.
