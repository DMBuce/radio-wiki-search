CJMV-FM is a Canadian radio station, broadcasting at 102.7 FM in Val-d'Or, Quebec. The station, branded as Énergie Val-d'Or 102.7, airs a francophone CHR format.

The majority of the station's schedule is simulcasted with its sister station in Rouyn-Noranda, CJMM-FM.
