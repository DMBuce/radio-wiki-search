WMMS-HD2 (100.7-2 FM) – branded Cleveland's BIN 99.1 – is a digital subchannel of Cleveland radio station WMMS (100.7 FM). Owned by iHeartMedia, Inc., WMMS-HD2 functions as a commercial black-oriented all-news radio station, serving Greater Cleveland and much of surrounding Northeast Ohio. Using the proprietary technology HD Radio for its main digital transmission, WMMS-HD2 also rebroadcasts over low-power analog Cleveland translator W256BT (99.1 FM), and streams online via iHeartRadio. The WMMS-HD2 studios are located at the former Centerior Energy building in the Cleveland suburb of Independence, while the WMMS-HD2 and W256BT transmitters reside in Seven Hills and Parma, respectively. WMMS-HD2 serves as the Cleveland affiliate for the Black Information Network.
