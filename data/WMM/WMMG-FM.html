WMMG-FM (93.5 FM is a radio station Licensed to Brandenburg, Kentucky, United States. The station is currently owned by Meade County Communications, Inc. and broadcasts a variety of music and programming.

The station was destroyed by an F5 tornado during the 1974 Super Outbreak.

The current on-air staff includes "Super" Dave Clark, Randy Johnson, Richard Brown, Alex Allen and "Fearless Em".
