WJLK (94.3 FM, "The Point") is an Adult Top 40 radio station. The station broadcasts on the 94.3 FM frequency. The station is owned by Townsquare Media Group in New Jersey. Its transmitter is located near exit 100 along the Garden State Parkway in Tinton Falls, New Jersey. It is the sister station of 105.7 The Hawk WCHR and 1310 WADB.
