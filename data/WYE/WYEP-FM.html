91.3 WYEP is a non-commercial radio station with a AAA format. It airs music programming 24 hours a day. WYEP is run by the Pittsburgh Community Broadcasting Corporation and is licensed to Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania. The station operates on 91.3 MHz with a signal strength of 18,000 watts of ERP.
