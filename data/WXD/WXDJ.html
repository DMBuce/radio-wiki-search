WXDJ (106.7 MHz "El Nuevo Zol 106.7 FM") is a commercial FM radio station licensed to Fort Lauderdale, Florida, and serving the Miami-Ft. Lauderdale-Hollywood radio market with secondary coverage of the West Palm Beach-Boca Raton market. The station airs a Spanish Tropical radio format and is owned by WRMA Licensing, Inc., a division of the Spanish Broadcasting System.

WXDJ's studios and offices are located at the Raul Alarcon Broadcast Center on Northwest 77th Avenue in Medley, while the transmitter is located on Fifth Street in Pembroke Park in southern Broward County. The station is also broadcast in HD Radio.
