KOAK (1080 AM) is a commercial radio station serving the Red Oak, Iowa area. The station primarily broadcasts a country music format. KOAK is licensed to Hawkeye Communications, Inc as a daytime-only station. During broadcast hours, KOAK simulcasts its FM sister station (KCSI) content.
