WJJF (94.9 FM, "94.9 News Now") is a radio station licensed to Montauk, New York and serving the eastern Long Island and southeastern Connecticut areas. The station is owned by Full Power Radio (controlled by John Fuller), and offers a news/talk format. WJJF signed on February 27, 2012.
