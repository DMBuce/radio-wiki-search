WJJQ and WJJQ-FM are a pair of full-service radio stations in Tomahawk, Wisconsin. They are owned by Albert Broadcasting, Inc. The company's FM service broadcasts a mixed adult contemporary and country music format, and the AM station is an ESPN Radio affiliate. The company is a family-owned community radio station. Their slogan is: "Your information and entertainment station of the northwoods." It is a 25,000 watt station that broadcasts at 92.5Â MHz. It uses the nickname "The Big Q.".
