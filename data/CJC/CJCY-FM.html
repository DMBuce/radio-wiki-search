CJCY-FM is a Canadian radio station, broadcasting at 102.1 FM in Medicine Hat, Alberta and broadcasts throughout South-eastern Alberta and South-western Saskatchewan. Owned by Rogers Sports & Media, the station broadcasts a classic hits format branded as Classic Hits 102.1 CJCY.
