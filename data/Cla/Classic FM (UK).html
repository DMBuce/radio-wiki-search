Classic FM (stylised as Classic fM) is one of the United Kingdom's three Independent National Radio stations. The station broadcasts classical music.

Classic FM was the first national classical music station to have been launched since the opening of BBC Radio 3, 25 years earlier, in September 1967, and 46 years since the opening of Radio 3's predecessor, The Third Programme, in September 1946. As of 2019, the station has a weekly audience of 5.6 million listeners.
