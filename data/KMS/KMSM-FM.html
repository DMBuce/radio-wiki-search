For the FM radio station in Rolla, Missouri formerly known as KMSM, see KMNR.

KMSM-FM (103.9 FM) is a radio station licensed to serve Butte, Montana. The station is owned and operated by The Associated Students of Montana Tech. It airs a Variety format.
