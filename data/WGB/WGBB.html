WGBB (1240 AM, 95.9 FM via translator) is a radio station licensed to Freeport, New York and serving Nassau County, New York. It is Long Island's oldest radio station, founded in 1923. It broadcasts the Chinese–language "Chinese Radio Network" and various English and spanish language religious and ethnic brokered programming. The studio is located in Merrick, New York and its transmitter is located in Freeport, former location of the studio.
