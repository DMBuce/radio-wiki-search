Radyo Bandera 97.7 Sweet FM (DYFH 97.7 MHz) is an FM station owned and operated by Xanthone Plus Broadcasting Services. Its studios and transmitter are located at Dumaguete Diversion Rd. cor. W. Rovira Rd., Brgy. Camanjac, Dumaguete.
