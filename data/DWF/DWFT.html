DWFT (104.3 FM), broadcasting as 104.3 Capital FM2, is a radio station owned and operated by the Philippine Broadcasting Service, the broadcast arm of the Presidential Communications Group. Its studios and transmitter are located at 4th Floor, Philippine Information Agency Building, Visayas Avenue, Quezon City. The station operates Mondays to Saturdays from 5:00 AM to 12:00 MN and on Sundays from 5:00 AM to 9:00 PM. Its online counterpart (FM2.ph), since 2020, offers an uninterrupted 24-hour programming service, all-year-round.
