DWNX (91.1 MHz & 1611 kHz) RMN Naga are AM and FM stations owned and operated by Radio Mindanao Network in the Philippines. The station's studio and transmitter are located at the RMN Broadcast Center, Brgy. Del Rosario, Milaor, Naga City, Camarines Sur. Both stations operate on a simulcast daily from 4:00 AM to 12:00 MN.

It was inaugurated on February 14, 1992 as Smile Radio, with a masa format. On June 1, 1996, it launched its first ever AM on FM format. In 2017, DWNX opened a relay station at 1611 kHz. There were plans for DWNX to transfer its broadcast to AM, with its FM to become iFM. However, due to consistent success of the station, the plan was shelved.
