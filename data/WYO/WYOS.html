WYOS is the CBS Sports Radio affiliate of Binghamton, New York, branded as "CBS Sports Radio 1360". It is currently under ownership of Townsquare Media.

Prior to 2007, it was an all talk format, in its most recent incarnation as a liberal talk station.

In January 2017 WYOS switched affiliations from ESPN Radio to CBS Sports Radio.
