WLXO (96.1 FM, "Hank 96.1") is a radio station broadcasting a Classic Country format. Licensed to Stamping Ground, Kentucky, United States, the station serves the Lexington metropolitan area. The station is currently owned by Clarity Communications, Inc. and is operated and managed by LM Communications via a local marketing agreement; it features programming from Westwood One.
