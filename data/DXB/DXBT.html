DXBT (99.5 FM), broadcasting as Monster Radio BT 99.5, is a music radio station owned by Audiovisual Communicators, Inc. The studio and transmitter are located at The Peak, Gaisano Mall of Davao, J.P. Laurel Avenue, Bajada, Davao City. It broadcasts daily from 5:00 AM to 10:00 PM.
