DXBE (89.1 FM) – known as Magic 89.1 – is a Top40 FM radio station owned by Quest Broadcasting Inc. Its studio and transmitter is located at the 6th floor unit 13/14 Metrolifestyle Complex Bldg.,E. Jacinto ext & F. Torrres intersection, Davao City. It broadcasts daily from 6:00 AM, to 8:00 PM,

Launched as Killerbee 89.1 in 1991, this station, along with the other KillerBee stations, were relaunched under the Magic moniker (adopted from its parent station) by April 29, 2013.
