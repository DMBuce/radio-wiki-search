DXBL (95.7 FM), broadcasting as Mellow Touch 95.7, is a radio station owned and operated by FBS Radio Network. The station's studio and transmitter is located at 3rd Floor, TS Midpoint Hub, Corrales Ave., Cagayan De Oro.
