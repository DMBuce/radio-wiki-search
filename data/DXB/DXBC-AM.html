DXBC (693 kHz), commonly known as Radyo Mo Nationwide Butuan, is an AM radio station of Radio Mindanao Network in the Philippines. The station's studio and transmitter is located at I. Elloso St., Brgy. Imadejas, Butuan City. The main format of the station is drama, public service and news.

RMN-DXBC Butuan is the first AM radio station in Butuan City when it went on air for the first time on August 17, 1959.
