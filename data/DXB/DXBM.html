DXBM (90.7 FM) - branded as 90.7 Love Radio Davao - is an FM radio station owned and operated by Manila Broadcasting Company in the Philippines. The station's studio is located at MBC Compound, R. Castillo Ave. Gov. Vicente Duterte Agdao, Davao City and the transmitter is located at Broadcast Ave., Shrine Hills, Matina, Davao City. This station is a member of the Kapisanan ng mga Brodkaster ng Pilipinas (KBP).
