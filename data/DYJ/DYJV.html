DYJV (106.1 FM), better known as Radio Boracay, is an FM radio station in Boracay, Philippines. It is owned by OneMedia Boracay Inc. (formerly Capricom Production and Management), and currently serves as an affiliate station of state-owned PBS station FM2. Its studios and main office are located at Zone 5, Boracay Bulabog Road, Malay, Aklan.
