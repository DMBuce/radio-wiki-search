WTRC-FM (95.3 FM; "News Talk 95.3 MNC") is a commercial radio station, licensed to Niles, Michigan and featuring a news/talk format. The station's licensee is Federated Media. Its programming and on-air presentation are closely patterned after Federated's news/talk station in Fort Wayne, WOWO. The station broadcasts syndicated programming, including Glenn Beck, Rush Limbaugh and Sean Hannity. WTRC-FM is a Fox News Radio affiliate, and simulcasts Penn High School football from the Regional Radio Sports Network.

WTRC-FM is licensed by the FCC to broadcast in the HD Radio digital hybrid format.
