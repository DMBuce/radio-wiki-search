WTRM (91.1 MHz) is a Religious Talk formatted broadcast radio station licensed to Winchester, Virginia, serving Western Frederick County and the Independent City of Winchester in Virginia and Southeastern Hampshire County in West Virginia. WTRM is owned and operated by the American Family Association.
