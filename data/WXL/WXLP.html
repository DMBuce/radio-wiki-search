WXLP (96.9 MHz, "97X")is a Quad Cities area commercial classic rock radio station, licensed to Moline, Illinois. The station is owned by Townsquare Media, with studios located in Davenport, Iowa (along with the co-owned KJOC, KBEA-FM, KIIK-FM and KBOB) and its 50,000 watt transmitter located in Orion, Illinois.
