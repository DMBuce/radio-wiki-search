WXLY (102.5 FM, "Y102.5") is a radio station in Mount Pleasant, South Carolina. Owned by iHeartMedia, Inc. and broadcasting at 102.5 FM, the station airs an adult contemporary format using the brand name Y102.5 and the slogan "Better Music For A Better Workday." The station's studios and transmitter are located (separately) in Mount Pleasant.
