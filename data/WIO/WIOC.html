WIOC (105.1 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a bilingual Top 40/CHR format. Licensed to Ponce, Puerto Rico, United States, the station serves the Puerto Rico area. WIOC was founded in 1965. The station is currently owned by International Broadcasting Corporation.
