KAZU (90.3 FM) is an NPR-member radio station, licensed in Pacific Grove, California, United States. The station is currently owned and operated by the California State University, Monterey Bay.

On April 5, 2017 CSUMB filed applications with the Federal Communications Commission to purchase the licenses of the K207CN (Santa Cruz), K217EK (Palo Colorado Canyon) and K237EV (Big Sur Valley) translators owned by KUSP. The purchase price was $5,000. The applications were consummated on June 30, 2017 and July 7, 2017.
