KAZR (103.3 MHz) is a commercial FM radio station licensed to Pella, Iowa, and serving the Des Moines metropolitan area. It is owned by Saga Communications, with studios located on Locust Street in Des Moines. KAZR airs a hard-edged mainstream rock radio format, known as "Lazer 103.3."

The transmitter is located off Pinion Avenue near Runnells, broadcasting at 100,000 watts, the maximum for non-grandfathered FM stations. KAZR broadcasts in the HD Radio format.
