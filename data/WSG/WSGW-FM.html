WSGW-FM (100.5), branded FM Talk & Sports 100.5, is a radio station broadcasting a news, talk, and sports format to the greater Tri-Cities area. Licensed to Carrollton, Michigan, it first began broadcasting in 1989 under the WCWK call sign. It is sister station of, and shares some local programming with, WSGW-AM 790. The station is owned by Alpha Media, through licensee Alpha Media Licensee LLC.
