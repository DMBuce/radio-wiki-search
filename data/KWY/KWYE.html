KWYE (101.1 FM, "Y101") is a radio station located in the Fresno, California, area that airs a Hot Adult Contemporary music format. The station is owned by Cumulus Media. Its studios are located at the Radio City building on Shaw Avenue in North Fresno and its transmitter is northeast of Clovis.
