DWJC (90.3 FM), broadcasting as 90.3 Radyo Bandera News FM, is an FM radio station of Bandera News Philippines. The station's main studio is located at Purok 4, Brgy. Mabolo, Malolos, Bulacan.

The station currently operates during daytime hours from 6:00 AM to 6:00 PM.
