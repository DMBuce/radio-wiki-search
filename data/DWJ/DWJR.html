DWJR (96.1 MHz), branded as 96.1 Farm Radio, is radio station owned and operated by Farm Expression Broadcasting. The station's studio is located at Bgy. Tarosanan, Gainza, Camarines Sur.. The station air news and talk programs every morning and plays music for the rest of the day. The station also plays Vocaloid music every Saturday.
