DZJA (103.5 FM), broadcasting as 103.5 Aguila News FM, is a radio station owned and operated by Rapido Media Production. The station's studio and transmitter are located at Purok 2, Brgy. Sumalig, Camalig.

It was formerly known as Star FM under Bombo Radyo Philippines from March 1, 1998 to January 30, 2006, when it went off the air. Back then, it was located at Bombo Radyo Broadcast Center, Central City Subdivision, Tahao Rd., Legazpi, Albay.
