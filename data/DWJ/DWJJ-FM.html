102.7 Radyo Polangui is a radio station, owned by Allied Broadcasting Center. Its studios are located at the 2nd Floor, Polangui Commercial Arcade, Brgy. Basud, Polangui, Albay.

The station was formerly an affiliate of Hot FM from 2002 to 2015.
