KTIA-FM (99.3 MHz) is a commercial FM radio station licensed to Huxley, Iowa and serving Central Iowa and the northern suburbs of the Des Moines metropolitan area. The station broadcasts a Christian talk and teaching radio format. KTIA-FM is owned by Truth Broadcasting, Inc. The main studio is located in the Merle Hay Tower on the 6th floor in Urbandale, Iowa.

According to the Antenna Structure Registration database, the tower is 90 m (295 ft) tall with the transmitter antenna mounted at the 87 m (285 ft) level. The calculated Height Above Average Terrain (HAAT) is 107 m (351 ft). The FM antenna array is an FMC-4A manufactured by Harris, located off NW 158th Street in Polk City, Iowa. KTIA-FM broadcasts with an effective radiated power (ERP) of 5,300 watts.
