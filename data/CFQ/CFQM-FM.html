CFQM-FM (103.9 MHz) is a Canadian FM radio station broadcasting from Moncton, New Brunswick, and owned by the Maritime Broadcasting System. The station currently airs a classic hits radio format and is branded on-air as 103.9 Max FM. Since 1977, the station has had numerous music formats such as easy listening, middle of the road and adult contemporary. From 1979 to 1998, it had a successful country music format.

Every weekend, CFQM plays vintage American Top 40 countdown shows hosted by Casey Kasem, one from the 1980s every Saturday morning, and one from the 1970s every Sunday morning. Sister stations CJYC-FM in Saint John, CHNS-FM in Halifax and CKPE-FM in Sydney also do this.
