KYZS (1490 AM, 95.7 FM; ESPN Tyler) is a terrestrial radio station, relayed by an FM translator, broadcasting a Sports radio format. Licensed to Tyler, Texas, United States, the station is currently owned by Paul Gleiser through licensee ATW Media, LLC. and currently features programming from ESPN Radio.
