KCYT (96.7 FM) is a radio station licensed to serve Fayetteville, Arkansas, United States. The station is owned by Jay and Teresa Bunyard, through licensee Hog Radio, Inc.

Key personnel at KCYT are Candace Dixon-Horne, general manager, Mark "HayStack" Wells, Program Director, and the CMA Award-winning Wilhite and Wall, the morning show team.
