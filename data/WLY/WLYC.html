WLYC (1050 AM) was a sports radio station in Williamsport, Pennsylvania, United States, branded as FOX Sports Williamsport. WLYC's first air date was June 26, 1951. The station went silent on May 16, 2020.

The station primarily carried Fox Sports Radio but also shared Pennsylvania College of Technology athletics with sister station WEJS and served as the local affiliate for the NFL on Westwood One and select syndicated college football broadcasts.
