WRYP (90.1 FM, "Renew FM") is a radio station licensed to Wellfleet, Massachusetts, United States, that serves the Cape Cod market. Before going silent, it broadcast a Christian radio format. The station is an affiliate of RenewFM and is licensed to Horizon Christian Fellowship.

The station was assigned the WRYP call letters by the Federal Communications Commission on May 9, 2006.
