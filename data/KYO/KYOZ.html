KYOZ (1330 kHz) is a commercial AM radio station in Spokane, Washington, owned by Legend Broadcasting, LLC. It airs a Regional Mexican radio format, known as "Ke Buena 95.7."

KYOZ has a daytime power of 5,000 watts. But to protect other stations on AM 1330, it drastically drops its nighttime power to 23 watts. It uses a non-directional antenna at all times. The transmitter is on East 42nd Avenue near Regal Avenue in Spokane.
