WZEZ (104.9 FM, "EZ 104.9") is a radio station broadcasting a soft adult contemporary format. Licensed to Balsam Lake, Wisconsin, United States, it serves the Turtle Lake, Wisconsin area. Established in 1997 as WWLC, the station is owned by Zoe Communications, Inc. and features local programming.
