WZEV (90.5 FM) is a radio station licensed to serve the community of Lineville, Alabama. The station is owned by B. Jordan Communications Corporation. It airs an oldies music format.

The station was assigned the WZEV call letters by the Federal Communications Commission on September 3, 2012.
