WSDC (88.5 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a Southern gospel format. Licensed to Sneedville, Tennessee, United States, the station is currently owned by Duck Creek Baptist Church. The station has obtained a construction permit from the FCC for a power increase to 5,000 watts.
