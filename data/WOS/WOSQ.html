WOSQ (92.3 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a Sports radio format. Licensed to Spencer, Wisconsin, United States, the station is currently owned by Seehafer Broadcasting Corp and features programming from ABC Radio, ESPN Radio and Motor Racing Network.
