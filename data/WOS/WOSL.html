WOSL (100.3 FM; "100.3 R&B") is a radio station licensed to Norwood, Ohio. The station is owned by Radio One, and airs an urban AC format. Its studios are located at Centennial Plaza in Downtown Cincinnati and the transmitter site is west of the downtown area.
