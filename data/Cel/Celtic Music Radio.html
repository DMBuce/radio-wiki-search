Celtic Music Radio is a Community Radio station in Scotland, broadcasting to the Glasgow area on 95.0 FM, and also worldwide via the internet. Celtic Music Radio is a Scottish Charity, registration number SC041172.

The station broadcasts continuously, with live programming during most daytime and evening periods.
