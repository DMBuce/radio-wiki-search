WXHR-LP (103.5 FM) is a radio station licensed to serve the community of Hillman, Michigan. The station is owned by Hillman Community Radio. It airs a community radio format.

The station was assigned the WXHR-LP call letters by the Federal Communications Commission on June 9, 2015.
