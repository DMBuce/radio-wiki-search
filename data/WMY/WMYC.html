WMYC (1580 AM) – branded as CD 92.9 – is a commercial alternative rock radio station licensed to serve Columbus, Ohio, servicing the Columbus metropolitan area. Owned by TSJ Radio, LLC and operated by WWCD Limited, the WMYC studios are located in Delaware, while the station transmitter resides in Columbus' Brandywine neighborhood. In addition to a standard analog transmission, WMYC is relayed over low-power analog Columbus translator W225CS (92.9 FM), and is also available online.
