WFXM is a radio station serving the Macon, Georgia area with a mainstream urban format. This station broadcasts on FM frequency 107.1 MHz and is licensed to WFXM-FM Radio, LLC. The majority stockholder (75% as of 2006) in WFXM-FM Radio LLC is Murray Communications, Inc of Macon, Georgia.[1]
