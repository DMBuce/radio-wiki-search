DZBS (1368 AM) Radyo Ronda is a radio station owned and operated by the Radio Philippines Network. The station's studios are located at A205 Lopez Bldg., Session Rd., Baguio, while its transmitter is located at Sitio Lamut, Brgy. Beckel, La Trinidad, Benguet.
