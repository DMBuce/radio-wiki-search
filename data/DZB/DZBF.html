DZBF (1674 AM) Radyo Marikina is a low-power community radio station owned and operated by the Marikina City Government under its Public Information Office. The studio is located at the 2nd floor, Marikina City Hall, Shoe Ave., while its transmitter is at the Engineering Center, Gil Fernando Ave cor. Aquilina St.

DZBF used to carry the branding Del Radio during Del de Guzman's stint as Mayor of the city from 2010 to 2016.
