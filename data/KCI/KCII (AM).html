KCII is a radio station based in Washington, Iowa, 30 minutes south of Iowa City. The station is broadcast on 1380 AM and its translator 102.5Â MHz FM. It is a full-service Classic Hits music station that broadcasts news, sports, community events, severe weather bulletins, and agriculture information. KCII simulcasts with KCII-FM. KCII is a part of the Cedar Rapids ADI market as recorded by Arbitron. It was first licensed on February 1, 1962.

KCII is on air 24 hours per day. A full programming list is at kciiradio.com.

KCII 1380 AM was first started in 1961. The studio is located at 110 E Main Street near the Town Square in Washington.
