KCIG-LP (99.7 FM) is a low-power FM radio station licensed to Craig, Colorado, United States. The station is currently owned by State of Colorado Telecommunication Services. The station provides Craig, Colorado with local NOAA Weather alerts, since coverage in the area is limited. The nearest NOAA Weather Radio station is in Steamboat Springs, Colorado.
