WCST is a News/Talk/Sports formatted broadcast radio station licensed to Berkeley Springs, West Virginia, serving Berkeley Springs and Morgan County, West Virginia. WCST is owned Metro Radio, Inc. and operated by West Virginia Radio Corporation. WCST simulcasts Martinsburg, West Virginia-based sister-station WEPM.
