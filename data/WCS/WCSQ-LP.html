WCSQ-LP (105.9 FM) was a radio station licensed to Cobleskill, New York, United States. Branded on air as "Radio Cobleskill," the station first began broadcasting on October 16, 2016 and featured a music-intensive adult hits/hot adult contemporary format, as well as specialty music and public affairs programming. The station is currently owned by Dialogos of Cobleskill, Incorporated.

The station went off the air on February 29, 2020 and turned in its license on September 29, 2020.
