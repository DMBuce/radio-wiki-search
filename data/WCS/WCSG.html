WCSG 91.3 MHz is a radio station in Grand Rapids, Michigan, United States, that broadcasts a Christian adult contemporary format. The station is a community outreach ministry of Cornerstone University. WCSG claims to be "the most listened-to Christian radio station in West Michigan", providing contemporary inspirational Christian music and programming to the local market and streaming “live” on the Internet.

WCSG is rebroadcast on three other stations throughout West Michigan:

  • 90.9 WCFG - Springfield/Battle Creek
  • 88.3 WCXK - Kalamazoo (Formerly WAYK)
  • 89.9 WCXB - Benton Harbor (Formerly WAYO)
