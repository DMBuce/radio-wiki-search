DXTS (94.3 FM), broadcasting as 94.3 DZRH News FM, is a commercial radio station owned by Cebu Broadcasting Company, an affiliate of Manila Broadcasting Company. The station's studio and transmitter are located at the 3rd floor, RD Plaza Bldg., Pendatun Ave., Brgy. Dadiangas West, General Santos. It is operating 24 hours a day.

Despite carrying the DZRH brand, it usually airs its own programming. It is the second FM station of MBC to carry a hybrid of music & news after Ben FM in Northern Cebu.
