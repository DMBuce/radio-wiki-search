DWHY (100.7 FM), broadcasting as 100.7 Star FM, is a radio station owned and operated by Bombo Radyo Philippines through its licensee People's Broadcasting Service, Inc. The station's studio and transmitter are located at the Bombo Radyo Broadcast Center, Maramba Bankers Village, Brgy. Bonuan Catacdang, Dagupan. It operates daily from 5am to 10pm.
