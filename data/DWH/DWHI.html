DWHI, branded as Z100.3 Zagitsit News FM, is a radio station owned by Hypersonic Broadcasting Center. Its studios are located at Unit 3, Camelo Bldg., Imelda C. Roces Ave., Bgy. Gogon, Legazpi City. and the transmitter are located at the bariw hill Brgy. Estanza.
