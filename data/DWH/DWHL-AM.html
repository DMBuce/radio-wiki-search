DWHL (756 AM) Apo Radyo is a radio station owned and operated by Beta Broadcasting System. The station's studio is located at #8 Kessing St., Brgy. Asinan, Olongapo, and its transmitter is located at Brgy. Kalaklan, Olongapo. It operates daily from 05:00 am to 09:00 pm, except on Holy Week annually, where it goes off the air from 09:00 pm of Holy Wednesday until 05:00 am of Easter Sunday.
