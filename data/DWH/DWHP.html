DWHP (99.5 FM), broadcasting as 99.5 iFM, is a radio station owned and operated by the Radio Mindanao Network. The station's studio is located at the Ground Floor, Hernandez Bldg., F. R. Castro St. cor. Dr. T. Samonte St., Laoag, and its transmitter is located at the rooftop of the said building.
