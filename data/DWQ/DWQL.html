DWQL (91.1 FM), on-air as Barangay LS 91.1, is a music FM radio station owned and operated by Radio GMA Network, Inc. (RGMA), a subsidiary of GMA Network Inc. in the Philippines under the brand. The station's studio and transmitter are located at Merchant Street, Lucena, Philippines.

Two of the station's alumni are Erwin David, known as Papa Kiko, and Papa Piolo, now known as Papa Dudut of the flagship station Barangay LS 97.1 in Manila.
