DWQS (107.3 FM), broadcasting as 107.3 Wow Smile Radio, is a radio station owned by Radio Sorsogon Network and operated by Wow Smile Media Services. Its studios & transmitter are located along Matanilena St., Roseville Subd., Brgy. Bibincahan, Sorsogon City.
