Pulse Rated was a radio station based in Salhouse in Norfolk, England. The station was previously known as Pulse Unsigned, and changed to Pulse Rated in Autumn 2005. The station is owned by Pulse Global.

Pulse Rated played a wide range of music from new, unsigned, and emerging artists.
