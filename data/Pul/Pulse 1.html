Pulse 1 (formerly Pennine Radio and The Pulse) is a local radio station owned and operated by Bauer as part of the Hits Radio network. It broadcasts to Bradford, Calderdale and Kirklees and from 1 September 2020 the station started broadcasting on DAB in Leeds, using the space previously occupied by Radio Aire, thereby providing a local breakfast show for all of West Yorkshire on weekdays.
