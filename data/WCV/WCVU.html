WCVU (104.9 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a Soft Oldies format. It is licensed to Solana, Florida, United States. The station is currently owned by iHeartMedia, Inc. and licensed as Citicasters Licenses, L.P. and features programming from Westwood One and Fox News Radio.
