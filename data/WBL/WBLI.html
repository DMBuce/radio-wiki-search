WBLI (106.1 FM, 106.1 'BLI) is a commercial radio station owned by Cox Radio and licensed to Patchogue, New York. It airs a Top 40 (CHR) radio format. The station mainly serves Suffolk County, New York on Long Island. Its studios and offices are located on Sunrise Highway (NY 27) in West Babylon, New York.

WBLI is a Class B FM station with an effective radiated power (ERP) of 49,000 watts, the highest powered station on Long Island. In order to protect two adjacent channel stations from interference, 105.9 WQXR-FM, Newark, New Jersey, and 105.9 WHCN Hartford, Connecticut, WBLI uses a directional antenna. The station's transmitter is located in Farmingville, New York.
