WRUS (610 AM) is a radio station licensed to serve the community of Russellville, Kentucky. WRUS began broadcasting on August 28, 1953. The station originally broadcast at 800 kilohertz, but in 1957 its frequency was changed to 610 kHz where it still operates today. WRUS operates with a daytime power of 1800 watts and 59 watts at night per FCC authorization. WRUS is owned by Logan Radio Inc.

610 AM is a Regional broadcast frequency.
