WRUM (100.3 MHz) is a commercial FM radio station in Orlando, Florida, known as "Rumba 100.3." It airs a Spanish-language radio format featuring Latin pop, Reggaeton and Tropical music, and is owned by iHeartMedia The studios and offices are on Maitland Center Parkway in Maitland.

WRUM has an effective radiated power (ERP) of 100,000 watts, the maximum for non-grandfathered FM stations. The transmitter is in Bithlo, off Fort Christmas Road (Route 420).

WRUM broadcasts using the HD radio hybrid format. The HD2 subchannel carries a bilingual CHR format, known as "Mega 97.1." The subchannel feeds two FM translators at 97.1 MHz.
