WRUL (97.3 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a country music format. Licensed to Carmi, Illinois, United States, the station serves the Evansville, Indiana area. The station is currently owned by Mark and Saundra Lange, through licensee The Original Company, Inc., and features programming from Westwood One and AP Radio.
