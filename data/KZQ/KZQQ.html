KZQQ (1560 AM) is a radio station with a sports radio format licensed to and serving the area around Abilene, Texas. On March 29, 2010, KZQQ dropped the Sports format for Classic rock. The format flip will bring in Dave Andrews for mornings, and Ben “Candy Man” Gonzalez hosting afternoons.

On December 28, 2010 KZQQ changed their format back to sports.
