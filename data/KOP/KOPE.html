KOPE (88.9 FM) is an American radio station licensed by the FCC to serve the community of Eldorado, Texas. The station license is assigned to The Center for Education Nonprofit Corp. KOPE airs a jazz format.

The station has held the KOPE callsign since September 21, 2009.
