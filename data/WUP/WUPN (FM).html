WUPN (95.1 FM) is an American radio station licensed to serve the community of Paradise, an unincorporated community in Whitefish Township, Chippewa County, Michigan. The station's broadcast license is held by Darby Advertising, Inc. WUPN broadcasts a classic hits format to the Sault Ste. Marie, Michigan, area.
