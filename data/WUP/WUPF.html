WUPF (107.3 FM) is a radio station licensed to Powers, Michigan and currently broadcasts a classic rock format as "Eagle 107.3 FM". The station is currently operated by AMC Partners Marquette, LLC, and was granted its license on March 2, 2008. After going silent for the first six months the station signed on in September, 2008 with its current calls and a broad-based variety hits format. The station had been assigned WXPT since 2006.
