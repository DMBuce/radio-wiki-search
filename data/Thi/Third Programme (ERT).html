The Third Programme (Greek: Τρίτο Πρόγραμμα, Trito Programma) is the third public radio station of Greece's state broadcaster, ERT. The station's main program broadcasts focused on culture and classical music, and enjoyed great prosperity during the period when it was headed by Manos Hatzidakis. Since February 2016, the station chief is Dionysis Mallouchos.
