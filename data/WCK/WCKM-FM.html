WCKM-FM (98.5 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a classic hits format. It is licensed to Lake George Village, New York, United States, and serves Essex, Hamilton, Saratoga, Warren and Washington Counties. The station is owned by Regional Radio Group, LLC and features news and sports from ABC Radio. The station's programming is simulcast in Poultney, Vermont on WDKM (92.5 FM).
