KRXO-HD2 (104.5/107.7-HD2;FM) – branded 104.5 KRXO – is the HD2 digital subchannel of radio station KRXO-FM/Oklahoma City. Broadcasting a classic rock format, the feed also simulcasts over translator K283BW (104.5 FM).

KRXO-HD2 is relayed by a single FM translator to extend its broadcast area.
