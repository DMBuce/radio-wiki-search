KRXV, KHWY, and KHYZ are a group of FM radio stations, licensed to Yermo, California, Essex, California and Mountain Pass, California respectively. They collectively broadcast a hot adult contemporary format branded as Highway Vibe. The stations are owned by Richard Heftel's Heftel Broadcasting Company, with studios in Barstow, California.

The trimulcast targets listeners travelling on Interstate 15 and Interstate 40 from Southern California towards Las Vegas and Laughlin, Nevada; alongside their music programming, the stations carry traffic and weather information, information and advertising for events, casinos, nightclubs, and other businesses around Southern Nevada, and businesses in the Barstow area. Heftel owns two other sets of stations with similar formats, including the country music KIXW-FM/KIXF, and adult hits KHDR/KHRQ.
