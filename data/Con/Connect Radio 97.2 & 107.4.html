Connect FM was a commercial radio station in the United Kingdom broadcasting to Northamptonshire and Peterborough and the home counties. It was owned by Adventure Radio and based in Kettering.

On 7 February 2019, the station was purchased by Communicorp UK and is set to merge with the group’s existing East Midlands license, and be rebranded as Smooth East Midlands. The station played 'hit music' which is songs from the 1980s to the present day, with a selection of presenters. Connect FM closed and merged with Smooth East Midlands on 1 October 2019.
