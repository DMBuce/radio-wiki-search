KRDY (1160 kHz) is an AM radio station in San Antonio, Texas. The station is owned by Immaculate Heart Media and is an affiliate of its Catholic talk network, Relevant Radio. The transmitter is off Braun Road, near Loop 1604, in San Antonio.
