WZZL (106.7 FM, "106-7 ZZL Rocks!") is a radio station licensed to the community of Reidland, Kentucky, and serves the greater Paducah, Kentucky, area. The station is owned by Withers Broadcasting and licensed to Withers Broadcasting Company of Paducah, LLC.

The station was assigned the WZZL call letters by the Federal Communications Commission on November 1, 1992.
