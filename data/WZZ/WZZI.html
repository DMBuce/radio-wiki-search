The radio station that was named WZZI until October 2009, is now WVMP.

WZZI (106.9 FM) is an oldies and classic hits formatted broadcast radio station licensed to Bedford, Virginia, serving Bedford and Bedford County, Virginia. WZZI is owned and operated by Todd Robinson's WVJT, LLC.
