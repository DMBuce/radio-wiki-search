Choose The Hits was an all request Australian national Digital Radio station. It was run by Austereo and was renamed on 1 February 2010. Previously the station was trialled with the name Hot30 Jelli, launching on 27 November 2009. The radio station ceased broadcast on 21 May 2010.

The station was powered by the Jelli platform.
