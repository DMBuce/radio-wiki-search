CJLD-FM (93.1 FM, 93.1 The One) is a country formatted broadcast radio station licensed to Leduc, Alberta, Canada, serving the City of Leduc, Leduc County and the south side of Edmonton. CJLD-FM is owned by Mark Tamagi and operated by Blackgold Broadcasting Inc.
