WSTJ (1340 AM) is a radio station broadcasting an Adult Standards/MOR format. Licensed to St. Johnsbury, Vermont, United States, the station is currently owned by Vermont Broadcast Associates, Inc. WSTJ has been broadcasting the Boston Red Sox Radio Network since the station signed on as WTWN in 1949. WSTJ also broadcasts the New England Patriots Radio Network.
