KEES (1430 AM, 96.5 FM) is a terrestrial American radio station, relayed by an FM translator, broadcasting an Urban Gospel format. KEES is licensed to Gladewater, Texas, and serves the Longview-Marshall area. The station is currently owned by Salt of the Earth Broadcasting and features Gospel programming and Christian teachings.
