WBFK (91.1 FM) is a radio station licensed to serve the community of Hiseville, Kentucky. The station is owned by Bethel Fellowship, Inc., and airs a Christian radio format.

The station was assigned the WBFK call letters by the Federal Communications Commission on August 14, 2012.
