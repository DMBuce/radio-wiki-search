WBFG (96.5 FM) is a radio station licensed to Parker's Crossroads, Tennessee, United States, the station serves rural West Tennessee as the areas main sports station in West Tennessee. The station is currently owned by Lexington Broadcasting, LLC. The station brands itself as "ESPN for West Tennessee" and also produces local coverage of high school football in west Tennessee.
