WBFY-LP is a low-power, community radio station in Belfast, Maine, United States. It broadcasts at 100.9 FM from a studio in the basement of Waterfall Arts (a former elementary school) on High Street. The range of the signal is 10–20 miles depending on geographic and weather conditions.
