KQHE (92.7 FM) is a radio station licensed to serve the community of Fairbanks, Alaska. The station is owned by Little Flower Ministries and airs a Catholic religious format, using a mixture of locally produced programming and content from EWTN Radio.

The station was assigned the call sign KCUF by the Federal Communications Commission on August 24, 2012. The station changed its call sign to KQHE on August 29, 2012.
