CKKC-FM is a Canadian radio station that broadcasts an adult contemporary format at 106.9 FM in Nelson, British Columbia. The station is owned by Bell Media. The station's programming is produced partly from its own studios, and from its sister station CJAT in Trail. The station was launched in 1939 by the Nelson Daily News on AM as CKLN, it adopted its current call sign in 1967 and moved to the FM band in 2006.

As CKLN, the station was a CBC Radio affiliate until the 1960s.
