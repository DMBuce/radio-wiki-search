CKKL-FM (93.9 FM) is a radio station in Ottawa. Owned by Bell Media, it broadcasts a country music format branded as Pure Country 94. CKKL's studios are located in the Bell Media Building on George Street in Downtown Ottawa's ByWard Market, while its transmitter is located in Camp Fortune, Quebec.
