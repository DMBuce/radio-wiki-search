CKWB-FM is a Canadian radio station, broadcasting at 97.9 FM in Westlock, Alberta. The station also serves the Barrhead area. The station currently airs a country music format branded on-air as Real Country 97.9 and was owned by Newcap Radio until they were bought out by Stingray Digital.
