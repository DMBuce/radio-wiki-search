DYTY, known as Brigada News FM Tacloban, is a news and music FM radio station owned and operated by Brigada Mass Media Corporation in the Philippines. The studio and transmitter is located in P. Burgos street, Tacloban City. It operates from 24 hours a day Sign-off on Monday at 12:00 Midnight to Sign-on at 4:00 AM except holy week of each year where its Sign-off at 3:00 PM on Good Friday and resumes broadcasting at 4:00 AM on Easter Sunday.

It recently acquired by Brigada Mass Media Corporation from the owner, Baycomms Broadcasting Corporation in February 2013, On August 1, 2020 after ceased operation of Leyte 93.5 Brigada News FM Tacloban was officially launched on September 28, 2020 broadcasting from Tacloban City and nearby provinces of Eastern Visayas it broadcasts programs in Waray Language.
