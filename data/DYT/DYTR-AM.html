DYTR (1116 AM) Tagbilaran Radio is a radio station owned and operated by Tagbilaran Broadcasting System. The station's studio is located at CAP Bldg., J. Borja St. cor. Carlos P. Garcia Ave., Tagbilaran, and its transmitter facilities are located at Brgy. Dao, Tagbilaran.
