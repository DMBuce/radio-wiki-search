WPBV-LP (98.3 FM) is a radio station licensed to serve the community of Palm Beach, Florida. The station is owned by WPBV-FM.Inc, and airs a Catholic radio format, including programming from EWTN Radio.

The station was assigned the WPBV-LP call letters by the Federal Communications Commission on January 30, 2015.
