WPBZ-FM (103.9 MHz "The Breeze") is a commercial FM radio station licensed to Rensselaer, New York, and serving the Capital District of New York, including the Albany-Schenectady-Troy radio market. The station is owned by Townsquare Media and airs a soft adult contemporary radio format. The studios and offices are on Kings Road in Schenectady.

WPBZ-FM has an effective radiated power (ERP) of 6,000 watts, with its transmitter off Smultz Road in Glenmont, New York.
