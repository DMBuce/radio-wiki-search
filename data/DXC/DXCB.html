DXCB (93.9 FM), broadcasting as 93.9 Star FM, is a radio station owned and operated by Bombo Radyo Philippines under its licensee, People's Broadcasting Service, Inc. Its studios and transmitter are located at the 4th floor AJS Bldg., Concuera cor. Valderosa St., Zamboanga City. Io operates Weekdays from 5:00 AM to 12:00 MN, and Weekends from 4:00 AM to 12:00 MN.

It is one of the Star FM stations formerly licensed to Consolidated Broadcasting System from its inception in 1989 to 2018, when their licensee was transferred to People's Broadcasting Service.
