DXCC (828 AM) RMN Cagayan de Oro is a radio station owned and operated by the Radio Mindanao Network. The station's studio is located at RMN Broadcast Center (Canoy Bldg.), Don Apolinario Velez St., Cagayan de Oro City, and the transmitter is located at Opol, Misamis Oriental. Established on August 28, 1952, DXCC is the pioneer radio station in the city and serves as the flagship station of Radyo Mo Nationwide.
