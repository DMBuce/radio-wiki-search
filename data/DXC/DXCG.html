102.3 Prime Gospel Radio (DXCG 102.3 MHz) is an FM station in the Philippines owned by Prime Broadcasting Network and operated by Yeshua Victorious Discipler's Team. Its studios and transmitter are located at Nats & Bikes Comp., J. Abad Santos St., Tagum.
