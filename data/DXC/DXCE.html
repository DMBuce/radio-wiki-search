95.7 Brigada News FM is an FM radio station owned by Brigada Mass Media Corporation. Its studios are located at the 2nd Floor, Janro Glass Bldg., Gensan Dr., Koronadal City, and its transmitter is located at Brgy. Paraiso, Koronadal. Operates 24 hours a day Sign off on Monday at 12:00 MN to Sign on 4:00 AM except holy week of each year. Sign off Good Friday at 3:00 PM to Black Saturday and resumes broadcasting at 4:00 AM on Easter Sunday.

Formerly owned by Hypersonic Broadcasting Center, Brigada News FM Koronadal was inaugurated on February 18, 2013. This is the second Brigada station after its flagship station in General Santos. It is the first FM radio station in the city airing an AM and FM format. In less than a year, it easily gained wide listenership. As per 2015 KBP-Kantar Media Survey, it is ranked as the over-all #1 Radio Station in Koronadal.
