DXCF (97.9 FM), broadcasting as 97.9 Spring Radio, is a community radio station owned by the Government of Tampakan. Its studios and transmitter are located at the 2nd Floor, Fitz Building, Poblacion, Tampakan, South Cotabato.

It first signed on in 2010 at 100.9 MHz. At that time, it was an affiliate of Radyo Natin. On January 2013, it moved to 97.9 MHz, to make way for HI FM based from Matalam, Cotabato.
