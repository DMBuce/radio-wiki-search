DXCK (91.9 FM), broadcasting as 91.9 iFM, is a news/talk and music FM station owned and operated by Radio Mindanao Network in the Philippines. The station's studio and transmitter is located at the RMN Broadcast Center, Bulaong National Highway, Dadiangas North, General Santos City, Philippines.

According to the latest KBP-RRC Kantar Media radio survey, 91.9 iFM is Gensan's #1 FM radio station in the news and music category and the #1 FM station overall, besting the longtime market leader 89.5 Brigada News FM, as well as a number of FM stations in both music/entertainment and news radio formats.
