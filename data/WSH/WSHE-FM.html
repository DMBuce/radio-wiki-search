WSHE-FM (100.3 FM, "100.3 WSHE") is a radio station licensed to Chicago, Illinois. The station is currently owned by Hubbard Broadcasting, WSHE-FM is also broadcast on HD radio. It is currently broadcasting an adult contemporary format, but has had a number of owners and utilized a variety of call letters and broadcast a variety of formats since its original incarnation in 1947 as WFMF. Its studios are located at One Prudential Plaza and transmitter facilities atop the John Hancock Center in Downtown Chicago.
