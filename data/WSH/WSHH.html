WSHH (99.7 MHz) is a commercial FM radio station in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania. The station is the flagship of Renda Broadcasting and airs an adult contemporary radio format. From mid-November until December 25, WSHH switches to all Christmas music.

WSHH has an effective radiated power (ERP) of 17,000 watts. Its power is lower than some of its rivals because it broadcasts from a taller antenna, at 260 meters (853 ft) in height above average terrain (HAAT). The transmitter is co-located with NBC network affiliate WPXI-TV on Lanark Street in Pittsburgh near Interstate 279. The tower is in the Fineview neighborhood on Pittsburgh's North Side, with studios and offices located on Parish Street, also in Pittsburgh.
