KFXU (90.5 FM, "Keeping Him Close By 90.5") is a radio station broadcasting a Gospel Music music format. Licensed to Chickasha, Oklahoma, United States, the station is currently owned by Bruce Munsterman, through licensee Houston Christian Broadcasters, Inc., and features programming from Dial Global.
