KFXR-FM (107.3 FM) is a radio station licensed to serve Chinle, Arizona. The station is owned by iHeartMedia, Inc. and licensed to iHM Licenses, LLC. It airs a country music format.

The station was assigned the KFXR-FM call letters by the Federal Communications Commission on November 12, 2001.
