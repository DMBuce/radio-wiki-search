WTMP is a radio station serving the Tampa Bay, Florida region broadcasting on 1150 AM. The station is licensed to Neal Ardman of NIA Broadcasting, Inc. It also broadcasts on FM translator W248CA on 97.5 MHz. WTMP's studios are located on Howard Avenue in West Tampa, and its transmitter is located northwest of downtown.
