KZFX (93.7 FM, "Z-93.7") is a commercial radio station that is licensed to Ridgecrest, California, United States and serves the area along US-395 from Ridgecrest to Lone Pine. The station is owned and programmed by Chuck Contreras and broadcasts a classic rock format.
