KWSN (1230 AM) is a radio station carrying a sports format with NBC Sports Radio programming. The station serves the Sioux Falls, South Dakota, area. The station is currently owned by Duey E. Wright, through licensee Midwest Communications, Inc. This station is also aired on 98.1 FM.
