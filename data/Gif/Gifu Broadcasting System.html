Gifu Broadcasting System (岐阜放送株式会社, Gifu Hōsō Kabushiki-gaisha, GBS) is a regional radio and television service serving Gifu prefecture, Japan. Its radio service is an independent AM station and its television service is a member of the JAITS. The station is branded as Gifu Chan (ぎふチャン).
