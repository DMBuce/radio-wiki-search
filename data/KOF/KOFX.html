KOFX (92.3 MHz "92.3 the Fox") is a commercial FM radio station in El Paso, Texas. The station airs a Classic Hits radio format that leans toward Classic Rock. The station plays artists such as Van Halen, The Rolling Stones, and Pat Benatar, but not Madonna. KOFX is owned by Entravision Communications, with the license held by Entravision Holdings, LLC. KOFX shares studio facilities with four other Entravision radio stations and two television stations, KINT-TV and KTFN, all of which are located on North Mesa Street (Texas State Highway 20) in Northwest El Paso.

The transmitter is off Grand Teton Drive, located atop the Franklin Mountains in the El Paso city limits. KOFX has an effective radiated power (ERP) of 98,000 watts (100,000 with beam tilt). Programming is simulcast on co-owned AM 1150 KHRO.
