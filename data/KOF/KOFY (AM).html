KOFY (1060 kHz) of Gilmer, Texas (FCC facility ID #34558), is a defunct daytime-only AM medium wave radio station. It first aired on March 16, 1979, and formerly used the call signs KBNB, KHYM, and KTLG until becoming KOFY in 1999. It was deleted in 2012.
