WRDU (100.7 FM, "Classic Rock 100.7 WRDU") is a commercial FM radio station licensed to serve Wake Forest, North Carolina. The station is owned by iHeartMedia though licensee Capstar TX, LLC and broadcasts a classic rock format. Its broadcast tower is east of Zebulon at (35°49′52.8″N 78°08′42.8″W). The station's service contour covers a large portion of Eastern North Carolina, including the cities of Raleigh, Durham, Rocky Mount, Greenville, and Roanoke Rapids.

WRDU uses HD Radio.
