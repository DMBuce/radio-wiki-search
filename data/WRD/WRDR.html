WRDR (89.7 FM, "Bridge FM") is a radio station broadcasting a Christian radio format. Licensed to Freehold Township, New Jersey, it serves the New York metropolitan area. The station is currently owned by Bridgelight, LLC. Bridgelight, LLC was created as a counterpart to Christian denomination Calvary Chapel Old Bridge.

The station simulcasts on WPLJ 95.5-HD4 and on several FM translators, as well as on WJUX, formerly known as "Jukebox Radio."
