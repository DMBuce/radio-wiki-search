WRDW (1630 AM) was a radio station licensed to Augusta, Georgia, United States. The station was owned by Beasley Broadcast Group.

On April 11, 2019, WRDW went silent (off the air), due to its transmitter site having been sold. Its license was surrendered September 27, 2019, and cancelled by the Federal Communications Commission on February 13, 2020.
