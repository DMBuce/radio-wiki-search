WNRS (1420 AM) is a radio station broadcasting an Spanish language tropical music format. Licensed to Herkimer, New York, United States, the station serves the Utica area. Owned by Arjuna Broadcasting Corp., the station also simulcasts on translator station W252DO at 98.3 FM.

The format is known as El Zorro 98.3 FM 1420 AM.
