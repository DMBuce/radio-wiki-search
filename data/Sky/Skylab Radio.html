Skylab Radio is a British digital radio station, specialising in various forms of chillout music, including downtempo, lounge, triphop, balearic beat and ambient music. The radio station broadcasts worldwide, via RadioPlayer, TuneIn, Radio Garden and iTunes, as well as on DAB+ Digital Radio in the Portsmouth and Norwich areas of England. Skylab Radio is owned and operated by Skylab Radio Limited - a private company limited by guarantee.
