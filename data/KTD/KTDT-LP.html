KTDT-LP is a low-power FM radio station on 99.1 FM in Tucson, Arizona. The station was founded by Jason LeValley and is better known as Downtown Radio. It is owned and operated by LPFM Downtown Tucson, a non-profit LLC that LeValley formed in order to start the station. The station format is rock Monday through Saturday and other genres, including talk, on Sunday. The purpose of the station is to provide an alternative to corporate radio and DJs have been directed to play songs that aren't typically played on corporate stations. LeValley served as the executive director from its launch date of September 13, 2015, to December 30, 2019.
