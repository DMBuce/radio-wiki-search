WAJJ (89.3 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a religious radio format. Licensed to McKenzie, Tennessee, United States, the station is currently owned by the Madisonville Christian School.

WAJJ's programming can also be heard on one repeater, W242AS 96.3 FM in Paris, Tennessee.
