CHYR-FM (96.7 MHz, Mix 96.7) is a commercial Canadian radio station in Leamington, Ontario, serving the Southwestern Ontario radio market. It is owned by Blackburn Radio and airs a hot adult contemporary format branded as Mix 96.7.
