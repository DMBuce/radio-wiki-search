DYNU (107.5 FM), broadcasting as 107.5 Win Radio, is a radio station owned by Progressive Broadcasting Corporation and operated by ZimZam Management, Inc. The station's studio and transmitter is located at the 4/F Ludo and Luym Bldg., Plaridel St., Cebu City. The station operates 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.
