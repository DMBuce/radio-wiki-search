WCLV (104.9 FM) – branded WCLV Classical 104.9 – is a non-commercial educational classical radio station licensed to Lorain, Ohio. Owned by Ideastream, the station serves Greater Cleveland and parts of surrounding Northeast Ohio. The WCLV studios are located at Playhouse Square in Downtown Cleveland, while the station transmitter resides in the Cleveland suburb of Avon. Besides a standard analog transmission, WCLV broadcasts over a single HD Radio channel, and streams online.
