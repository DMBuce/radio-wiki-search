WCLO (1230 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a News Talk Information format. Licensed to Janesville, Wisconsin, United States, the station serves the Janesville area. The station is currently owned by Southern Wisconsin Broadcasting, LLC. and features programming from CBS Radio, Premiere Radio Networks and Westwood One.
