WCLC is a broadcast radio station licensed to Jamestown, Tennessee, serving Jamestown and the vicinity. WCLC is owned and operated by New Life Studios, Inc. and it simulcast their programming from sister station WCLC-FM "New Life 105" . This station operates only during the daytime hours.
