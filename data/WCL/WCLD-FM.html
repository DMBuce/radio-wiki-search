WCLD-FM is a Urban Contemporary formatted broadcast radio station. The station is licensed to Cleveland, Mississippi and serves Cleveland, Clarksdale, Greenville, Greenwood, and Indianola in Mississippi. WCLD-FM is owned by Radio Cleveland, Inc.
