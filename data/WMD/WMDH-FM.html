WMDH-FM (branded as "Nash FM 102.5") is a radio station licensed to New Castle, Indiana, and serves the Muncie-Marion, Indiana, radio market with a country music format. This station operates at an effective radiated power of 50,000 watts on FM frequency 102.5 under ownership of Cumulus Media. Studios are located in New Castle with tower facilities located between New Castle and Muncie near the Delaware/Henry County line. On September 6, 2013, WMDH dropped the "Hit Country 102.5 WMDH" branding and became "Nash FM 102.5."
