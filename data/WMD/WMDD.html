WMDD (1480 AM, "El 1480") is a radio station licensed to serve Fajardo, Puerto Rico. The station is owned by Pan Caribbean Broadcasting de P.R., Inc. It airs a Spanish Tropical & news-talk format.

The station was assigned the WMDD call letters by the Federal Communications Commission on November 26, 1947.
