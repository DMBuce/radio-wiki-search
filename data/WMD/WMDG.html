WMDG (1260 AM) was a radio station broadcasting a News/Talk radio format. Licensed to East Point, Georgia, the station served the Atlanta metropolitan area. It is currently silent.

The station is owned by Michael and Dara Glinter, through licensee Northwest Georgia Broadcasting. The station featured nationally syndicated programming from the Premiere Radio Networks and Fox News Radio.
