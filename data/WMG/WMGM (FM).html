WMGM (103.7 WMGM Rocks) is a radio station licensed to Atlantic City, New Jersey playing active rock. WMGM serves most of southern New Jersey from Toms River to Cape May. Its studios are in Linwood, New Jersey and its transmitter is in Pleasantville, New Jersey.
