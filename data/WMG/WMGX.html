WMGX (93.1 MHz "Coast 93.1") is a commercial FM radio station in Portland, Maine. The stations coverage area reaches across all of Southern Maine. WMGX airs an Adult Top 40 radio format and is owned by Saga Communications. Studios and offices are on Western Avenue in South Portland.

WMGX has an effective radiated power (ERP) of 50,000 watts. The transmitter is off Presumpscot Street in Portland, near Interstate 295. It broadcasts in the HD Radio hybrid format. The HD2 subchannel carries the talk radio programming of sister station AM 560 WGAN.
