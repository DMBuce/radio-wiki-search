WMGL (branded as "Magic @ 107.3") is a radio station that plays an urban adult contemporary in the Charleston, South Carolina area. It is under ownership of Cumulus Media Inc., through its licensee Radio License Holding CBC, LLC. Its studios are located in North Charleston and the transmitter tower is on St. Thomas Island (within Charleston city limits).
