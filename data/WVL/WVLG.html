WVLG (640 AM) is a radio station licensed to Wildwood, Florida. The station is also broadcast on 102.7 FM in the northern part of the listening area and on 104.5 in the southern part.

WVLG broadcasts mainly to The Villages, Florida retirement community with an oldies format and is an affiliate of Fox News Radio.
