WVLY-FM (100.9 FM, "The Valley") is a commercial FM radio station licensed to serve Milton, Pennsylvania. The station is owned by Sunbury Broadcasting Corporation and broadcasts an adult contemporary format. Its broadcast tower is located southeast of Milton.
