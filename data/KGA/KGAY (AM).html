KGAY (1270 kHz, "106.5 KGAY") is an AM radio station broadcasting to LGBT community with a talk and EDM radio format. Licensed to Thousand Palms, California, United States, it serves the Coachella Valley area. The station is owned by Sunnylands Broadcasting, L.L.C. and is operated by Qchella Media Corporation. Programming is simulcast on FM translator station 106.5 K293CL, also in Thousand Palms.
