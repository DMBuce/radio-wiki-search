KGA (1510 AM, "103.5 The Game") is a radio station serving Spokane, Washington. Owned by Stephens Media Group, it broadcasts an urban contemporary format. KGA's studios and offices are located on East 57th Avenue and its transmitter site is in Spangle, Washington. Going on the air in 1927, it was one of the earliest radio stations in Washington. It is also broadcast by a translator in Spokane on 103.5 FM.

The station carries broadcasts of the Spokane Indians minor league baseball team, the Spokane Chiefs junior ice hockey team, and teams fielded by Gonzaga University.
