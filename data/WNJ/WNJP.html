WNJP (88.5 FM) is a radio station licensed to Sussex, New Jersey. The station is owned by New York Public Radio, and is an affiliate of their New Jersey Public Radio network.

WNYC assumed control of the stations that make up NJPR under a management agreement on July 1, 2011.
