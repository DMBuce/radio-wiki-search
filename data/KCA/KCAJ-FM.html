KCAJ-FM is an Adult Contemporary formatted broadcast radio station licensed to Roseau, Minnesota, serving Northwest Minnesota, Southeast Manitoba, and Northwest Ontario. KCAJ-FM is owned and operated by North Country Media. Studios and offices are at 107 Center St West, in downtown Roseau. Their transmitter site is along Highway 89 in Wannaska, MN.
