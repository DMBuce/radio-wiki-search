KCMR (Inspiration 97.9 FM) is a Christian radio station licensed to serve the community of Mason City, Iowa, United States. KCMR is owned by TLC Broadcasting Corporation, a non-profit corporation overseen by a local 16-member board of directors and supported by listener contributions. KCMR also broadcasts area church services every Sunday for those unable to attend, usually a Lutheran service as well as a Methodist service. Studios are located at 316 N Federal Ave, Mason City. The transmitter site is just south of Mason City along U.S. Route 65.
