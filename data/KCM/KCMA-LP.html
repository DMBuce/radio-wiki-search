KCMA-LP (92.1 FM) is a nonprofit, listener-supported radio station licensed to serve Payson, Arizona. The station is owned by Payson Classical Music Appreciation Inc. It airs a Classic Hits format that the station describes as "musical favorites from the '50s, '60s and '70s". In addition to its usual music programming, KCMA-LP airs news programming from the USA Radio Network.

The station was assigned the KCMA-LP call letters by the Federal Communications Commission on June 24, 2002.
