KSQM (91.5 FM) is a non-commercial, educational radio station broadcasting music largely from the 1940s through the 1960s and local news and weather reports. Licensed to Sequim, Washington, United States, the station serves the Northwest Washington area. The station is currently owned by Sequim Community Broadcasting. KSQM's signal can be heard as far east as Anacortes, Washington and west to Port Angeles.
