CHGK-FM is a Canadian radio station in Stratford, Ontario with a hot AC format at 107.7 FM, branded as 107.7 2day FM. CHGK's studios are located at 376 Romeo Street South in Stratford while its transmitter is located on Line 29 just south of Stratford.
