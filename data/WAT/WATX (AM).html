WATX (1220 AM Stereo) is a radio station licensed to Hamden, Connecticut. The station, owned by Quinnipiac University, is currently Silent. The station, under its previous call sign WQUN, aired the adult standards format America's Best Music from Westwood One, before going silent on May 31, 2019. The station resumed broadcasting on May 28, 2020, to satisfy FCC requirements and retain the license for another six months.
