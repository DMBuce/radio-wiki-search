KSAF-LP (104.1 FM) is a low power radio station licensed to True Light Broadcasting, Inc., a ministry of the Seventh-day Adventist Church in Minot, North Dakota. It airs a Christian radio format.

The station was assigned the KSAF-LP call letters by the Federal Communications Commission on March 26, 2004.
