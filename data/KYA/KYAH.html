KYAH (540 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a talk format. Licensed to Delta, Utah, United States, the station is currently owned by DCO Holding LC.

KYAH's program schedule consists of an assortment of religious, alternative far-right and alt-right, paranormal and brokered talk radio programs. Its schedule includes Alex Jones, Jeff Rense, Ammon Bundy, John B. Wells and Heather Wade (the latter two of whom are tied to shows previously hosted by the deceased Art Bell). The Overcomer Ministry with Brother Stair was on the station until it was withdrawn from syndication. Convicted provocateur Hal Turner is also featured on KYAH as one of his two radio affiliates (the other being shortwave WBCQ). KYAH lists the Hal Turner Radio Show on its online program schedule most week nights from 7 pm to 8 pm, Wednesday being a special 2-hour program, 7 pm - 9 pm.
