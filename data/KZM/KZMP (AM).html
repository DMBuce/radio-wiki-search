KZMP (1540 kHz) is a commercial AM radio station licensed to University Park, Texas, and serving the Dallas-Fort Worth Metroplex. It is owned by Estrella Media and airs a Classic Regional Mexican radio format. Programming is simulcast from co-owned 106.7 KZZA.
