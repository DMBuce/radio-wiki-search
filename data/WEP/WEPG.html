WEPG (104.9 FM and 910 AM, "104.9 The River") is a radio station broadcasting a country music format. Licensed to South Pittsburg, Tennessee, United States. The station is owned by Spencer Travis Hickman and features local programming and syndicated programming from ABC News Radio.The station simulcasts on a co-located FM translator at 104.9 mHz, which is also licensed to South Pittsburg.
