Left of Center was a College rock/Indie rock radio station on SIRIUS Satellite Radio channel 26 and DISH Network channel 6026. It was replaced by Sirius XMU as part of the Sirius/XM merger of channels on November 12, 2008.

Artists featured on the station included The Shins, Death Cab for Cutie, Arcade Fire, Bloc Party, The White Stripes and Sonic Youth.

Its morning timeslot consisted of a music show hosted by Jake Fogelnest of SQUiRT TV and VH1's I Love The... series fame. Other prominent DJs with shows on the channel included Matt Pinfield and DJ Liquid Todd. A program entitled Left of Sessions featured indie rock bands performing live in a concert setting.
