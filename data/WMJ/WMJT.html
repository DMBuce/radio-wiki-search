WMJT (96.7 FM, "96.7 Flash FM") is an American radio station licensed to serve the community of McMillan, Michigan. The station serves the Newberry, Michigan area. Similar to sister station WMJZ in Gaylord, WMJT broadcasts a hot adult contemporary format.
