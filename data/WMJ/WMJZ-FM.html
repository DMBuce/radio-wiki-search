WMJZ-FM (Eagle 101.5 FM) is a commercial radio station licensed to the city of Gaylord, Michigan. It broadcasts at the assigned frequency of 101.5 Megahertz with a power output of 50,000 watts. The station airs an Adult Hits format as Eagle 101.5, and is owned by 45 North Media Inc, a company owned by Bryan & Joyce Hollenbaugh.
