WMJM, "Magic 101.3", is an urban adult contemporary FM station licensed to Jeffersontown, Kentucky serving the Louisville metropolitan area owned and operated by Alpha Media. It currently carries the Steve Harvey Morning Show. The station's studios are located in downtown Louisville and the transmitter site is atop Kaden Tower in east Louisville.
