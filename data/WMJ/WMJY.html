WMJY (93.7 FM, "Magic 93.7") is a mainstream adult contemporary music formatted radio station based in Gulfport–Biloxi-Pascagoula, Mississippi. The station is owned by iHeartMedia, Inc., and they broadcast with an ERP of 100 kW.
