WMJC (91.9 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a Christian Talk and Teaching and Modern Praise and Worship Music format as an affiliate of the Calvary Radio Network. WMJC is licensed to Richland, Michigan, United States. The station was purchased by Calvary Radio Network, Inc. in July 2011 from Horizon Broadcasting Network. WMJC was formerly using WTNP as its call sign until May 2011.

WMJC is simulcast on a translator in Kalamazoo, 97.3 W247AM.
