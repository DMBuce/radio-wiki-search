WYDA "Air 1" is an FM station operating at 96.9 MHz licensed to Troy, Ohio. The station airs the Christian Worship formatted Air 1 network programmed by the Educational Media FOundation. WYDA is broadcast to Dayton, its northern suburbs and the Upper Miami Valley region of Miami, Clark, Shelby, Champaign, Darke and surrounding counties in West Central Ohio with local offices are located on south Main in Dayton.
