WYDE (1260 kHz) is a commercial AM radio station. The station is owned by the Crawford Broadcasting Company and airs a Southern Gospel radio format with some Christian talk and teaching programs. It is simulcast with WYDE-FM 92.5 MHz in Cordova.

The transmitter is located near the eastern edge of downtown Birmingham, and its studios are in Homewood. By day, it is powered at 5,000 watts. But at night, to avoid interfering with other stations on AM 1260, it greatly reduces power to 41 watts. Programming is also heard on FM translator 95.3 W237EK. And the station is also heard on co-owned 93.7 WDJC-HD-4. WYDE broadcasts in the HD Radio format.
