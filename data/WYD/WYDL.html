WYDL (100.3 FM, "Hot 100") is a radio station licensed to Middleton, Tennessee, U.S., serving Corinth, Mississippi, with a top 40 (CHR) format. The station is currently owned by Flinn Broadcasting Corporation. WYDL is operated by Southern Broadcasting Corporation.
