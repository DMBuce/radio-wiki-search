WANB (1210 AM) is an American radio station broadcasting a Country format. Licensed to Waynesburg, Pennsylvania, United States, it serves southwestern Pennsylvania and portions of West Virginia and Ohio.. The station is currently owned by Broadcast Communications, Inc.
