KUEZ (104.1 FM, Easy 104.1) is a radio station in Fallon, Nevada serving the Reno, Nevada area with a Soft Oldies format.

The previous KRZQ callsign had been held by another Reno-area station which also used the Alternative rock format. The station changed its callsign to KMXW shortly after changing to the Hot Adult Contemporary format in September 2011, and is now KRFN.

On August 2, 2017, KRZQ changed their format from Alternative rock to soft oldies, branded as Easy 104.1 under new KUEZ calls.
