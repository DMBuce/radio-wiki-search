KJXX 1170 AM is a radio station licensed to Jackson, Missouri. The station broadcasts a format consisting of Christian Talk and Teaching during the day and Adult Standards at night. The station is owned by Dana Withers' Withers Broadcasting, with the broadcast license being held by Withers Broadcasting Company of Missouri, LLC. KJXX is also heard on 92.3 FM through a translator in Cape Girardeau, Missouri.
