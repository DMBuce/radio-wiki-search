3PB was Melbourne's second B-Class radio station, opening in September 1925, after 3UZ and before 3DB. Most Australian B-Class stations were renamed as Commercial stations in 1930, but 3PB had closed down prior to that date. (This early station has no links whatsoever with the currently-existing 3PB which commenced in 1994 - 3PB now being the official call sign for the Melbourne outlet for ABC NewsRadio.)

The original 3PB broadcast between the hours of 8.00 and 10.00 pm, with a power of 1,500 watts.
