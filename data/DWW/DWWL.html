DWWL (92.7 FM), broadcasting as 92.7 Beecool FM, is a radio station owned and operated by Peñafrancia Broadcasting Corporation (affiliated with Eagle Broadcasting Corporation) in the Philippines. Its studios and transmitter are located along Maharlika Highway, Purok 3, Brgy. San Rafael, Guinobatan.

It was formerly known as Wow FM from 2010 to 2015, when the station manager, Johnny Dematera, died. During its existence, it was located at the 3rd floor, Rivero bldg. Capt. F. Aquende Drive, Legazpi City. In 2019, it was relaunched as Kadunong News FM and transferred its operations to Guinobatan.
