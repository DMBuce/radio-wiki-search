DWWQ (89.3 FM), broadcasting as Barangay FM 89.3, is a radio station owned and operated by GMA Network Inc. subsidiary Radio GMA. Its station's studio and transmitter is located at the 4th Floor of VillaBlanca Hotel, Pattaui St., Brgy. Ugac Norte, Tuguegarao.
