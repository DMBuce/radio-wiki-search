DWWG (101.5 FM), broadcasting as 101.5 Big Sound FM, is a radio station owned and operated by Vanguard Radio Network, serving as the flagship station of the Big Sound FM network. The station's studio and transmitter are located at Pan-Philippine Highway, Brgy. Sangitan East, Cabanatuan City. This is the pioneer FM sttaion in the city.
