WSWT, branded as "Mix 106-9", is a radio station broadcasting an Adult Contemporary format. It is licensed to Peoria, Illinois in the Peoria radio market. The station is currently owned by Midwest Communications. Mornings are hosted by Randy Rundle and Steph Blue.
