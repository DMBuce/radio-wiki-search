WSWV-FM is a Country and Bluegrass-formatted broadcast radio station licensed to Pennington Gap, Virginia, serving the Pennington Gap/Big Stone Gap/Jonesville area. WSWV-FM is owned and operated by B C Broadcasting Company, Inc.
