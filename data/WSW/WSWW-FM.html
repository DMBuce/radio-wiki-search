WSWW-FM is a Talk Radio and Country music formatted broadcast radio station licensed to Craigsville, West Virginia, serving the Summersville/Webster Springs/Richwood area. WSWW-FM is owned by West Virginia Radio Corporation and licensed to AJG Corporation.

WSWW-FM simulcasts the format of WJLS.
