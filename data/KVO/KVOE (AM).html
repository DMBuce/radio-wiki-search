KVOE (1400 AM) is an area news, talk, and sports radio station that airs local programming such as Talk of Emporia, The Emporium, and Area Coach's Corner in Emporia, Kansas. KVOE also carries national programs such as ABC News Radio. KVOE is licensed to Emporia, Kansas and owned by Emporia's Radio Stations, Inc.
