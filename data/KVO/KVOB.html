KVOB (95.5 FM, "95.5 The Rock") is a commercial radio station licensed to serve Lindsborg, Kansas, United States. The station is owned by Rocking M Media, LLC.

KVOB broadcasts a mainstream rock format branded as "95.5 The Rock" to the greater Salina, Kansas, area.
