KVOH (Kay-Vo) or the "Voice of Hope", is a non-commercial, 50 kW shortwave (SW) Christian/Gospel radio station located in Rancho Simi, California, though their studios are in Los Angeles, which they claim as their home city. Programming can be heard on 9.975 Mhz and 17.775 Mhz, often using two languages: English and Spanish.
