KWJJ-FM (99.5 MHz) is a commercial radio station in Portland, Oregon. It is owned by Entercom Communications and airs a country music radio format. The studio is on SW Bancroft Street, near downtown Portland. The station transmitter is atop Portland's West Hills, off SW Fairmount Court.
