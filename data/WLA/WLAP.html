WLAP (630 kHz) is a commercial AM radio station in Lexington, Kentucky, serving the Central Kentucky region. It airs a talk radio format and is owned by iHeartMedia, Inc. The studios and offices are on Nicolasville Road in Lexington.

WLAP is powered at 5,000 watts by day. But to protect other stations on AM 630 at night when radio waves travel further, WLAP reduces power to 1,000 watts. A directional antenna is used at all times. The transmitter is on Russell Cave Road in Lexington. A four-tower array is used.
