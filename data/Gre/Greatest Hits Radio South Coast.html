Greatest Hits Radio South Coast is a classic hits radio station broadcasting on 106 MHz & 106.6 MHz FM, on DAB on the South Hampshire multiplex across the South Coast of England and on DAB+ across a limited area on the Portsmouth small scale trial multiplex. During the week most programming is shared with the Greatest Hits radio network from studios across the UK, apart from the drivetime show which is recorded at home by former Spire FM and Breeze presenter Chris Wright between 4-7pm. The station is part of the Greatest Hits Radio network, and is owned by Nation Broadcasting which is under licensing agreement with operator Bauer Radio.
