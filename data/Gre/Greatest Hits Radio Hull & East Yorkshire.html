GHR Hull & East Yorkshire, Formerly KCFM, is an Independent Local Radio station serving Kingston upon Hull and the East Riding of Yorkshire. This new licence was advertised by Ofcom in 2006. It was owned by the Lincs FM Group, but in early 2019 it was sold on to Nation Broadcasting.
