Greatest Hits Radio Bristol & The South West is a radio station that broadcasts on 107.2 FM in Bristol England, it is owned by Bauer Radio and is part of Greatest Hits Radio network of stations. The station is aimed at 40- to 59-year-olds, playing classic and contemporary easy listening music. The station predominantly airs networked shows apart from a regional Drive show 4-7pm. Prior to the 1 September 2020 the station was known as The Breeze Bristol at which point it was re-branded as Greatest Hits Radio Bristol & The South West.
