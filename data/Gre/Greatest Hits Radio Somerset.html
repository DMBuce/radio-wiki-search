Greatest Hits Radio Somerset is a radio station that broadcasts across Somerset, England, it is owned by Bauer Radio and is part of Greatest Hits Radio network of stations. The station is aimed at 40- to 59-year-olds, playing classic and contemporary easy listening music. The station predominantly airs networked shows apart from a regional Drive show 4-7pm. Prior to 1 September 2020 the station was known as The Breeze Bridgwater & West Somerset, The Breeze North Somerset and The Breeze Yeovil & South Somerset but these merged to form Greatest Hits Radio Somerset.
