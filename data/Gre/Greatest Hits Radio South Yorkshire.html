Greatest Hits Radio South Yorkshire is a local radio station owned and operated by Bauer as part of the Greatest Hits Radio network. It broadcasts to South Yorkshire and the North Midlands on 990, 1305 & 1548 AM and DAB radio. It forms part of Greatest Hits Radio Yorkshire.
