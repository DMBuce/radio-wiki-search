Greatest Hits Radio North Derbyshire is part of GHR Yorkshire region of the Greatest Hits Radio radio station. Prior to being bought by Bauer it was a fully fledged station known as 'Peak FM' outputting local content during the daytime produced at its own studios in Chesterfield.
