Greatest Hits Radio Gloucestershire is a radio station that broadcasts on 107.5 FM in Cheltenham, Gloucestershire, England, it is owned by Bauer Radio and is part of Greatest Hits Radio network of stations. The station is aimed at 40- to 59-year-olds, playing classic and contemporary easy listening music. The station predominantly airs networked shows apart from a regional Drive show 4-7pm. Prior to the 1 September 2020 the station was known as The Breeze Cheltenham and North Gloucestershire when it re-branded as Greatest Hits Radio Gloucestershire.
