Greatest Hits Radio Bath & The South West is a radio station that broadcasts on 107.5 FM in Warminster, Wilstshire, England and on 107.9 FM in Bath, Somerset, England, it is owned by Bauer Radio and is part of Greatest Hits Radio network of stations. The station is aimed at 40- to 59-year-olds, playing classic and contemporary easy listening music. The station predominantly airs networked shows apart from a regional Drive show 4-7pm. Prior to 1 September 2020 the station was known as The Breeze Bath and The Breeze Frome & West Wiltshire but these merged to form Greatest Hits Radio Bath & The South West.
