WYFE is a non-commercial radio station in Tarpon Springs, broadcasting to the Tampa-St. Petersburg-Clearwater area on 88.9 FM. It is part of the Bible Broadcasting Network, along with Belleview-based WYFZ, Gainesville-based WYFB, Lakeland-based WYFO,and Port St. Joe-based WDBW-LP. WYFE's format is religious programming, and the station is broadcast on translators in Crystal River (100.9 FM) and Leesburg (99.3 FM).
