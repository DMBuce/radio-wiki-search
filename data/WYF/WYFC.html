WYFC (95.3 FM) is an affiliate of the Bible Broadcasting Network in Clinton, Tennessee, broadcasting to the Knoxville, Tennessee, area. The station was a commercial Contemporary Hit Radio station (WTNZ-FM) from 1986 to 1989.
