WYFN (980 AM) is a radio station serving the Nashville, Tennessee area with a religious radio format. It is a Bible Broadcasting Network (BBN) owned-and-operated station.

Prior to being acquired by BBN in 1991, this station was WSIX, one of Nashville's heritage radio stations.
