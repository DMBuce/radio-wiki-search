WYFO is a non-commercial radio station in Lakeland, Florida, broadcasting to the Lakeland-Winter Haven area on 91.9 FM. It is part of the Bible Broadcasting Network, along with Belleview-based WYFZ, Gainesville-based WYFB, Port St. Joe-based WDBW-LP and Tarpon Springs-based WYFE. WYFO's format is religious programming, and the station is broadcast on translators in Bradenton, Sarasota, and Sebring.
