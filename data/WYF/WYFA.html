WYFA is a non-commercial Christian radio station in Augusta, Georgia owned by the Bible Broadcasting Network. The station is licensed by the Federal Communications Commission (FCC) to Waynesboro, Georgia, and broadcasts on 107.1 FM with an effective radiated power (ERP) of 25 kW.
