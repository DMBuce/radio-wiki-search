WHTQ (96.7 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a Top 40 (CHR) format. Licensed to Whiting, Wisconsin, United States, the station serves the Wausau-Stevens Point area. The station is currently owned by NRG Media through licensee NRG License Sub, LLC.
