KELY (1230 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a News-Talk format. Licensed to Ely, Nevada, United States, the station is currently owned by Ely Radio, LLC and features programming from USA Radio Network. It is best known as the flagship station for the nationally syndicated Classic Radio Theater with Wyatt Cox.
