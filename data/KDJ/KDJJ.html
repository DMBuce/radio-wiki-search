KDJJ (94.1 FM) is a radio station licensed to serve the community of Fernley, Nevada. The station is owned by Northway Broadcasting, LLC, and airs a country music format.

The station was assigned the KDJJ call letters by the Federal Communications Commission on September 9, 2016.
