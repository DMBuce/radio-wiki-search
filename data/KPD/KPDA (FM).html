KPDA (100.7 FM, "La Poderosa") is a commercial radio station that is licensed to Mountain Home, Idaho, United States and serves the Boise, Idaho area. The station is owned by Kevin Terry, through licensee Radio Rancho, LLC and broadcasts a regional Mexican format.
