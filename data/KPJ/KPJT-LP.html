KPJT-LP (99.1 FM) is a low power FM radio station with a Christian format. It is licensed to the city of Maple Grove, Minnesota. The transmitter for KPJT is located adjacent to Interstate 94 near the split of Interstate 494 in Maple Grove. The station carries a mix of religious programming including music and talk. Its signal can be heard throughout portions of the northwest metropolitan area of the Twin Cities.
