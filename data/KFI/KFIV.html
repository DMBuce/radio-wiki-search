KFIV (1360 kHz) is a commercial AM radio station in Modesto, California. It airs a talk radio format and is owned by iHeartMedia, Inc. Its studios and offices are on Lancey Drive in Modesto.

KFIV is powered at 4,000 watts by day. But to avoid interfering with other stations on 1360 AM, it reduces power at night to 950 watts. It uses a directional antenna at all times. The transmitter is off Sylvan Avenue on the east side of Modesto.
