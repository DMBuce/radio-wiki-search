WTAW (1620 AM) – branded News Talk 1620 – is a commercial talk radio station licensed to serve College Station, Texas. Owned by the Bryan Broadcasting Company, WTAW covers College Station, Bryan and much of the Brazos Valley. The WTAW studios are located in College Station, as is the station transmitter. In addition to a standard analog transmission, WTAW broadcasts in HD Radio utilizing the in-band on-channel hybrid standard, is simulcast over low-power FM translator K233DU (94.5 FM) College Station, and is available online.
