Triple M Perth (call sign: 6PPM) is a commercial FM radio station broadcasting in Perth, Western Australia, on a frequency of 92.9 MHz, and was until 1 December 2020 part of Southern Cross Austereo's Hit Network and since 1 December 2020 is a be part of Southern Cross Austereo's Triple M. Triple M Perth's studios used to be located at 283 Rokeby Road, Subiaco. In March 2007, they moved to a purpose-built broadcast centre with sister station Mix 94.5, at 450 Roberts Road, Subiaco.
