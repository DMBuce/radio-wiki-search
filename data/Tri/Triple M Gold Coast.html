Triple M Gold Coast (ACMA callsign: 4GLD) is an Australian radio station in Queensland. Owned and operated as part of Southern Cross Austereo's Triple M network, it broadcasts an Active Rock format to Gold Coast, Queensland. First broadcast as 4GG in 1967, the station moved to the FM band in 1989 as Triple G - later KROQ, then Gold FM. In November 2019 it was rebranded Triple M.
