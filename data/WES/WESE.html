WESE (92.5 FM), known as "92.5 The Beat", is an Urban Contemporary radio station based in Guntown, Mississippi, United States, and serves Tupelo and Northeast Mississippi with an ERP of 12,000 watts. WESE is owned by iHeartMedia, Inc., through licensee Capstar TX LLC.
