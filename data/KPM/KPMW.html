KPMW, also known as Mix 105.5, is a Contemporary hit radio outlet serving the Maui, Hawaii, United States, area. The station, which is owned by Rey-Cel Broadcasting, Inc. and whose COL is Haliimaile, HI, broadcasts at 105.5 MHz with an ERP of 9 KW.

On January 1, 2013, Wild 105.5 changed the brand to the new "Mix 105.5" and continued to play Top 40 until late 2013 when it flipped to Contemporary Filipino music.
