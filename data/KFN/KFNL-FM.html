KFNL-FM (104.3 FM, "Fun 104.3") is a radio station licensed to Spring Valley, Minnesota, serving the Rochester, Minnesota area. KFNL-FM airs a classic hits format and is owned by Townsquare Media Rochester License, LLC.

On September 26, 2019, after three days of stunting, KVGO shifted their format from oldies to classic hits, branded as "Fun 104.3". On October 3, 2019, KVGO changed call letters to KFNL-FM. They play the same music as AM 1390 KFRO in Owatonna, Minnesota.
