KFNV-FM (107.1 FM, "The Bridge") is a radio station broadcasting a classic hits music format. Licensed to Ferriday, Louisiana, United States.

On April 15, 2017 KFNV-FM changed their format from adult contemporary (branded as "The River") to classic hits, branded as "107.1 The Bridge". (info taken from stationintel.com)
