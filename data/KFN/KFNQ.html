KFNQ (1090 kHz) is a commercial AM radio station in Seattle, Washington. It airs a sports radio format and is owned by iHeartMedia, Inc. KFNQ carries programming from Fox Sports Radio and CBS Sports Radio as a brand extension of sister station AM 950 KJR. The studios and offices are in the Belltown neighborhood northwest of Downtown Seattle.

KFNQ is powered at 50,000 watts, the maximum for AM stations in the U.S., and is a Class B station. Because AM 1090 is a clear-channel frequency reserved for Class A KAAY Little Rock, WBAL Baltimore and XEPRS Rosarito-Tijuana, KFNQ must use a directional antenna at all times to avoid interference. The transmitter is off Dockton Road SW on Vashon Island. KFNQ also airs on the HD3 sub-channel of co-owned KJAQ 96.5 FM.
