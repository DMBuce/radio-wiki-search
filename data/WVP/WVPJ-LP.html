WVPJ-LP (107.9 FM, Radio Sion) is a radio station broadcasting a Religious format. Licensed to Mayaguez, Puerto Rico, the station serves the western Puerto Rico area. The station is currently owned by Milton Cuevas Vazquez, through licensee, Iglesia Evangelica Sion, Inc.
