WIZR (930 AM) is a radio station that broadcasts a Hot AC format. Licensed to Johnstown, New York, United States, the station serves the Mohawk Valley area. The station is owned by the Cranesville Radio Network and features hit songs from the '90s, 2000s, and 2010s.
