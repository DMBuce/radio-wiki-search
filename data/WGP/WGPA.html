WGPA (1100 kHz) is a Class D daytimer radio station, licensed to Bethlehem, Pennsylvania, serving the Lehigh Valley. Owned by CC Broadcasting, LLC, airing a Full-Service radio format as "Ameripolitan," including classic country music, rockabilly, oldies and polka music.
