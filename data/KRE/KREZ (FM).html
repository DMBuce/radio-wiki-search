KREZ is a radio station airing an Adult Contemporary format licensed to Chaffee, Missouri, broadcasting on 104.7 MHz FM. The station serves the areas of Cape Girardeau, Missouri and Sikeston, Missouri, and is owned by Withers Broadcasting.
