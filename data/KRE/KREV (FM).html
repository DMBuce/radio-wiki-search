KREV (92.7 FM), branded as 92.7 REV The Revolution, is a Top 40 music formatted radio station that serves the greater San Francisco Bay Area. Its city of license is Alameda, California, and it is under receivership by Patrick Communications. The station's studios are located in the Visitacion Valley district of San Francisco. The transmitter, formerly located atop of Bellaire Tower in the city's Russian Hill neighborhood, now transmits from a shared tower on Candlestick Hill in Bayview Park, San Francisco.
