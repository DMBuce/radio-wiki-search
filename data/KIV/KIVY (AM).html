KIVY and K275AS (1290 AM, 102.9 FM) is an AM radio station combined with an FM translator broadcasting an Adult Standards format to Crockett, Latexo, and the surrounding rural communities of Houston County, Texas. Licensed to Crockett, Texas, United States, the station serves the Lufkin-Nacogdoches area. The station is currently owned by Leon Hunt.

Current ESPN announcer Carter Blackburn began his broadcast career at KIVY, calling high school football games.
