Absolute Radio 70s is a Semi-National Digital radio station owned and operated by Bauer as part of the Absolute Radio Network. It broadcasts locally on Bauer’s 11B Tayside DAB Multiplex. It broadcasts nationally via Smart Speaker streaming and online web streaming. It’s output is non-stop 1970s hits.
