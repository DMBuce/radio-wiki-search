WBSA (1300 AM, "Total Christian Radio") is a radio station licensed to serve Boaz, Alabama. The station is owned by Watkins Broadcasting, Inc. It airs a Christian radio format featuring Southern Gospel music. WBSA is the only radio station licensed to Boaz, Alabama, by the Federal Communications Commission. WBSA plays southern gospel and bluegrass gospel music. They also have on-air devotions.

On air personalities include Dale Johnson, Chris Watkins, James Cornelius, and Beecher Hyde. WBSA carries the weekly football games of the Boaz High School Pirates.
