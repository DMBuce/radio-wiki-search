KWKZ (106.1 FM, "C106") is a country music-formatted radio station based in Charleston, Missouri, with studios located in Cape Girardeau, Missouri. KWKZ is a Class C2 station with an ERP of 50,000 watts. C106 was founded in the spring of 1993.
