KWKR is a Sports formatted broadcast radio station licensed to Leoti, Kansas, serving West-Central Kansas. KWKR is owned by Mark Yearout, through licensee Southwind Broadcasting, LLC.

On November 27, 2019, KWKR changed their format from sports to mainstream rock, branded as "The Rock 99.9".
