CINB-FM is a Canadian radio station in Saint John, New Brunswick. Branded as Oldies96, Memories and More, it broadcasts Classic Hits of the 50's, 60's, and 70's, local talk, and specialty shows, and is found at 96.1 MHz on the FM dial.
