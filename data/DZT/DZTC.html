DZTC (828 AM) Radyo Pilipino is a radio station owned and operated by the Radio Corporation of the Philippines, serving as the flagship station of the Radyo Pilipino network. The station's studios and transmitter are located at RCP Broadcasting Center, Mcarthur Highway, Brgy. San Nicolas, Tarlac City. This station operates everyday from 4:45 AM to 6:45 PM.
