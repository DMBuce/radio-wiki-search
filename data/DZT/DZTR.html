DZTR (89.5Â MHz in Naga City, Philippines), branded as 89.5 Radyo Natin, is an FM radio station owned & managed by the Radyo Natin Network under Cebu Broadcasting Company. The station's studio is located on the 3rd Floor, Naga City Market, Naga City, Camarines Sur.

The station first started broadcast as Yes FM in 1998. In 2005, Ateneo de Naga University took over management of the station, as it was rebranded as The Beat FM. It aired a rock-leaning CHR/Top 40 until going off the air on the last quarter of 2009. On January 4, 2010, it was relaunched as a mainstream CHR/Top 40 Station. During that time, its studios were located at the 3rd Floor, Phelan Building, Ateneo de Naga University. In 2014, it went off the air due to technical difficulties in antenna and transmitter. In 2016, Radyo Natin Network took over management of the station.
