CFMB (1280 kHz) is a multilingual Canadian radio station located in Montreal, Quebec, owned by Evanov Radio Group. It broadcasts with a power of 50,000 watts full-time as a class B station, using a directional antenna with different patterns day and night (the nighttime pattern being significantly tighter). Its transmitter is located near Saint-Mathieu, while its studios are located on Papineau Avenue, Montreal.
