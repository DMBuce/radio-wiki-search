CFMM-FM is a radio station in Prince Albert, Saskatchewan. Owned by the Jim Pattison Group, it broadcasts a contemporary hit radio format branded as Power 99 FM. The station was previously owned by Rawlco Communications until its sale in 2014.

CFMM began broadcasting in 1982 and also has a rebroadcaster at 92.1 FM in Waskesiu Lake with the call sign CFMM-FM-1.
