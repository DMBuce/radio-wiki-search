WXCE (1260 AM) is a radio station broadcasting an active rock format. Established in 1978, the station ls licensed to serve Amery, Wisconsin, United States. The station is owned by Zoe Communications, Inc.

WXCE has a construction permit from the FCC for an FM translator on the frequency of 107.3 MHz. It will simulcast programming of WXCE.

On April 28, 2020 WXCE changed their format to active rock, simulcasting WXNK 940 AM Shell Lake.
