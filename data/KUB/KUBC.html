KUBC ("AM580/FM104.5 KUBC GOLD") is a radio station broadcasting a Classic Hits format. It previously broadcast a news/talk format for 14 years & previously had been Country, Classic Country, Standards, A/C & Pop. KUBC GOLD is a Classic Hits format programmed from & for Montrose. Core Artists of KUBC GOLD include the Eagles, Michael Jackson, Bruce Springsteen, The Bee Gees, Billy Joel, Chicago & many more. The station is focused musically on the 1970s with 55% of the songs coming from that decade, 35% from the 80's & 10% from the 1960s. The KUBC GOLD Local personalities: Dan Lynch on 'The Dan Show' from 6am to 9am, Stacy Lynn from 9am to 12 noon, B.G. Carter from 12 noon to 3pm, 'PM Dan' from 3pm to 6pm & D.J. Roberts from 6pm to 10pm. Otto Mation rounds out the Monday thru Friday line-up, performing from 10pm to 6am. Licensed to serve Montrose, Colorado, United States, the station serves the Uncompahgre Valley & can be heard in Grand Junction as well. The station is owned by Cherry Creek Media through licensee CCR-Montrose IV, LLC. The Vice-President/General Manager of Cherry Creek Media Montrose is Tony Driskill. Lisa Carlson is the Sales Team & Digital Leader. Belinda Haddock is the Business manager & Dan Lynch is the Operations Manager. KUBC GOLD's sister stations are 94 Kix Country & 103-7 The River, The Valley's Hit Music!

On February 19, 2020 KUBC changed their format from news/talk to classic hits, branded as "KUBC Gold". KUBC is simulcast on FM translator K283CZ 104.5 FM Montrose.
