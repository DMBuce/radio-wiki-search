KUBE (93.3 MHz, "KUBE 93.3") is a commercial FM radio station licensed to Seattle, Washington. The station airs a rhythmic contemporary radio format and is owned and operated by iHeartMedia. The studios and offices are on Elliott Avenue West in Seattle's Belltown neighborhood northwest of downtown. The transmitter is located on Cougar Mountain.
