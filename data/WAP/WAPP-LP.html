WAPP-LP (100.3 FM) is a low-power FM radio station licensed to Westhampton, New York. The station was silent and its license was set to expire on July 2, 2013 if they did not resume operations. It resumed operations on June 27, 2013. The station has been silent since August 25, 2016.
