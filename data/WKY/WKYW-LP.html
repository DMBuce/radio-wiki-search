WKYW-LP (102.9 FM) is a radio station licensed to serve the community of Keyser, West Virginia. The station is owned by Mineral County Historical Society, Inc. It airs a variety radio format. The station's studios are located on the campus of Potomac State College of West Virginia University.

The station was assigned the WKYW-LP call letters by the Federal Communications Commission on May 22, 2014.
