WKYI (1600 AM, "My 94.1") is a radio station broadcasting a southern rock/classic country format. Licensed to Eminence, Kentucky, United States, the station serves the Louisville, Kentucky, area. The station is currently owned by New Albany Broadcasting Co., Inc.
