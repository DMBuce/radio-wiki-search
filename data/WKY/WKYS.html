WKYS (93.9 FM, "93.9 WKYS") is a commercial FM radio station licensed to serve Washington, D.C.. The station is owned by Urban One through licensee Urban One Licenses, LLC, and broadcasts an Urban Contemporary format. It is co-owned with WMMJ, WOL, WPRS-FM, WTEM, and WYCB and has its studios located in Silver Spring, Maryland. The station's broadcast tower is located south of Bethesda, Maryland at (38°56′24.0″N 77°04′53.0″W).

WKYS is licensed by the FCC to broadcast using HD Radio. The station simulcasts the Urban Talk programming of sister station WOL on its HD2 subchannel. Sputnik Radio English programming is broadcast on its HD3 subchannel.
