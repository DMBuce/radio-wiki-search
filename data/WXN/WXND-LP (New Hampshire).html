WXND-LP (107.3 FM) was a radio station licensed to serve Etna, New Hampshire, United States. The station was owned by nDimension and served the Lebanon-Rutland-White River Junction area.

The station aired an Album Adult Alternative music format. Notable programming included a dance/trace mix show which aired each Friday night on both WXND-LP and WXGR-LP.

The station was assigned the WXND-LP call letters by the Federal Communications Commission on July 31, 2003.

WXND stopped broadcasting in late January 2008 due to what the station's website described as "unfavorable business circumstances". The station's license was cancelled and the callsign deleted from the FCC database on June 3, 2008.
