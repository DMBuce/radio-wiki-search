WXTG (1490 kHz "The Groove") is a commercial AM radio station licensed to Hampton, Virginia and serving Hampton Roads, including Norfolk, Portsmouth, Newport News and Virginia Beach. WXTG is owned and operated by Terry Suggs, through licensee TL Broadcasting LLC.
