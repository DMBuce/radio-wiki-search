KKDQ (99.3 FM) is a radio station in Thief River Falls, Minnesota. It is owned by Thomas E. Ingstad and airs a country music format with the additional title Northern Country 99.3. The station also has coverage in Grand Forks, North Dakota and can be listened to online.
