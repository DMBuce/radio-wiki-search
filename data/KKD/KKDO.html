KKDO (94.7 MHz) is a commercial FM radio station licensed to Fair Oaks, California, and serving the Sacramento radio market. The station calls itself "Alt 94.7" and it programs an Alternative Rock radio format. The Entercom outlet has its transmitter off Rosebud Lane in Citrus Heights. Its studios are located in North Highlands (with a Sacramento address).

KKDO broadcasts in the HD Radio format. Its HD2 subchannel carries "Channel Q," a Radio.com format of LGBTQ talk and EDM dance music.
