KKDG (99.7 FM, "99X") is a radio station broadcasting a top 40 format. Licensed to Durango, Colorado, United States, the station serves the Four Corners area. The station is currently owned by Winton Road Broadcasting Co, LLC and features programming from ABC Radio.
