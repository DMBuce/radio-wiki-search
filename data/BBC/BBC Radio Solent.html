BBC Radio Solent is the BBC Local Radio service for the English counties of the Isle of Wight, Dorset and the southern two-thirds of Hampshire. Its studios are located in Southampton, in the same purpose-built office block in Havelock Road as the BBC South Today news studios, and there are district offices in Portsmouth, Newport, Bournemouth, Poole and Dorchester. It was based until 1991 in South Western House, the former railway hotel at the old Southampton Terminus station.
