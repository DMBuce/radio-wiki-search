BBC Radio Northampton is the BBC Local Radio service for the English county of Northamptonshire. It broadcasts from its studios in Broadcasting House, Abington Street, Northampton (a listed building) on 104.2FM (Northampton) and 103.6FM (Geddington, between Kettering and Corby), and also on DAB.

The station's Managing Editor is Helen Grimes, with Lucy King as Assistant Managing Editor, and Laura Cook as News Editor.

According to RAJAR, the station has a weekly audience of 90,000 listeners and a 8.9% share as of December 2018.
