WZUS 100.9 MHz is a commercial FM radio station broadcasting a talk radio format. Licensed to Macon, Illinois, United States, the station serves the Decatur Area, and is currently owned by The Cromwell Group, Inc. of Illinois. WZUS was originally licensed to Pana, Illinois.

WZUS carries a local morning talk and farm news show, as well as nationally syndicated talk programming such as Glenn Beck, Dave Ramsey, Michael Savage, Mark Levin, and Red Eye Radio.
