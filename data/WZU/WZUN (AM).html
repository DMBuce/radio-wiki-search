WZUN (1070 AM) is a radio station airing a classic hits format, twin-licensed to both Sandy Creek and Pulaski, New York, and effectively serving the Syracuse market to the south. The station is owned by Galaxy Communications, and is part of a simulcast with 102.1 WZUN-FM. It is a daytime-only station, but uses an FM translator station, W291BU (106.1, licensed to Fulton), though this translator mainly covers Oswego.
