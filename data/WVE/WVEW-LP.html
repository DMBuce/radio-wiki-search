WVEW-LP (107.7 FM) is a radio station licensed to Vermont Earth Works. The license to operate the station is currently held by Vermont Earth Works. The broadcast radius is from 3 to 5 miles (4.8 to 8.0 km). There are 56 volunteers on the staff.
