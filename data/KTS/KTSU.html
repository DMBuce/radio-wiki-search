KTSU (90.9 FM), known as "The Choice", is a variety radio station based on the campus of Texas Southern University Home of "The Ocean Of Soul" marching band in Houston, Texas. It was founded on June 23, 1972.

Its studios have been located on campus at the Center for Professional Media Studies since 2005, and the station's transmitter is located nearby, also on campus.
