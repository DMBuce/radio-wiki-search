DXGM (1125 kHz Davao City) Super Radyo is an AM station of GMA Network Inc.'s subsidiary RGMA (Radio GMA Network, Inc.) in the Philippines. The stations studio is located at the GMA Network Complex, along Shrine Hills, Matina in Davao City, while the transmitter is located at Ma-a, Davao City. The main format of the station is news, public service and religious broadcasting.
