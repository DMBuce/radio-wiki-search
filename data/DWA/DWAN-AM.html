DWAN (1206 AM) was a radio station owned and operated by the Metropolitan Manila Development Authority. Its studios and transmitter were located in the MMDA's Communications and Command Center, Epifanio de los Santos Avenue cor. Orense St., Brgy. Guadalupe Nuevo, Makati City. It operated daily from 4 a.m. to Midnight.
