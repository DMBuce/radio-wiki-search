DWAY (104.3 FM), broadcasting as Care 104.3, is a radio station of the Far East Broadcasting Company in the Philippines. Its studios are located along Maharlika Hi-way, Brgy. Arimbay, Legazpi City, and its transmitter is located at Bariw Hill, Brgy. Estanza, Legazpi City.
