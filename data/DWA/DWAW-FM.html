DWAW (99.9 FM), broadcasting as 99.9 Padaba FM is a radio station owned by Allied Broadcasting Center and operated by Lann's Radio Media Network, a company owned by Sorsogon 2nd District Board Member Renato Guban. Its studios & transmitter are located at the 2nd Floor, Dineros Bldg., Alegre St., Brgy. Balogo, Sorsogon City.
