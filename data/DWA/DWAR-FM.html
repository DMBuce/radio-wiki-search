DWAR (103.9 MHz) RMN Palawan is an FM radio station owned by Romeo Servando's Rolin Broadcasting Enterprises, Inc. It is an affiliate of Radio Mindanao Network. Its main studio is located at BM Road, Puerto Princesa City, with repeaters located in various towns in Palawan.

Gerry Ortega, the pro-environment journalist slain last 2011, had a program in this station.
