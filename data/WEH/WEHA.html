WEHA (88.7 FM) is a Stellar Award winning radio station broadcasting a Gospel music format. Licensed to the suburb of Port Republic, New Jersey, it serves the Atlantic City metropolitan area. It first began broadcasting in 2003 under the call sign WIBF. The station is currently owned by Spread The Gospel Inc.
