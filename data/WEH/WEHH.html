WEHH (1600 kHz) is a commercial AM radio station which is licensed to two adjacent cities in the Southern Tier of New York, Elmira Heights and Horseheads, New York. It is owned by Tower Broadcasting and airs an adult standards radio format, which also includes some soft oldies.

The music is programmed in-house and ranges from Frank Sinatra and Dean Martin to The Beatles and Dionne Warwick. Weekends include the syndicated "Sundays with Sinatra," hosted by Sid Mark, and polka music hosted by Jimmy Sturr. Most hours begin with world and national news from CBS Radio News.
