KKTX-FM is a Classic Rock formatted broadcast radio station licensed to Kilgore, Texas, serving the Longview/Marshall/Tyler area. KKTX-FM is owned and operated by Townsquare Media. Its studios are in Tyler, and its transmitter is in Kilgore.
