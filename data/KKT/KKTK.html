KKTK (1400 AM, "Fox Sports 1400") is an American radio station broadcasting a sports format. Licensed to Texarkana, Texas, United States, it serves the Texarkana metropolitan area. The station, established in 1946, is currently owned by American Media Investments, Inc.

The station was reassigned the KKTK call sign by the Federal Communications Commission on February 7, 2007.
