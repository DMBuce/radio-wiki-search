KWCX-FM (104.9 FM) is a radio station licensed to Willcox, Arizona, United States. The station is owned by KZLZ, LLC.

KWCX-FM has been granted a U.S. Federal Communications Commission construction permit to move to a new transmitter site, change the city of license to Tanque Verde, Arizona, downgrade to Class A, increase ERP to 3,000 watts and decrease HAAT to −70.0 meters (−229.7 ft).
