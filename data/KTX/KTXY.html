KTXY, branded as "Y107," is a Top 40 Mainstream FM radio station that serves Columbia and Jefferson City, Missouri, United States. The Zimmer Radio Group outlet broadcasts at 106.9 MHz with an ERP of 100 kW. Its transmitter is located approximately 20 miles (32 km) west of Jefferson City in McGirk.
