WHYL (960 AM) is a commercial AM radio station licensed to serve Carlisle, Pennsylvania. The station is owned by WHYL, Inc., and broadcasts an oldies music format.

WHYL programming is simulcast on FM translator W275CJ licensed to serve Carlisle. The translator is located on Blue Mountain northeast of Carlisle.
