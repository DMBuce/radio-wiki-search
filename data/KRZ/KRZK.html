KRZK (106.3 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a classic country format. Licensed to Branson, Missouri, United States, it serves the Springfield, Missouri, area. The station is currently owned by KOMC-KRZK.

KOMC-KRZK, LLC is owned by Earls Family Broadcasting, in Branson, MO.

KRZK was originally owned by Turtle Broadcasting Company of Branson, a subsidiary of Orr & Earls Broadcasting, Inc. It was purchased in 1986 along with KRZK-FM which were off the air at the time. Co-principal, Roderick Orr, sold his interest in the company to Charles Earles in 2004.
