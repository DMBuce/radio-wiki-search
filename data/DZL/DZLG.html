DZLG (927 AM) Bombo Radyo is a radio station owned and operated by Bombo Radyo Philippines. The station's studio and transmitter are located at Bombo Radyo Broadcast Center, Central City Subdivision, Tahao Rd., Legazpi City. Bombo Radyo Legazpi airs everyday from 4:00 AM to 9:30 PM.
