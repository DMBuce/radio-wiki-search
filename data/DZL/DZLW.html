DZLW (711 AM) Radyo Bicolandia is a radio station owned and operated by Peñafrancia Broadcasting Corporation. The station's studio and transmitter are located at Zone 1, Brgy. San Agustin, Canaman, Camarines Sur.

DZLW is currently an affiliate station of Eagle Broadcasting Corporation and adopted the Radyo Agila branding from 2015 to 2019.
