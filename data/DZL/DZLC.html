DZLC (98.5 FM), broadcasting as 98.5 Big Radio is a music radio station owned by Vanguard Radio Network. The station's studio and transmitter are located at Lucky 8 Bldg., Brgy. San Sebastian, Lipa, Batangas.

The frequency was formerly owned by GV Broadcasting System (now Apollo Broadcast Investors) from 2003 to September 2010 under the brand Da Best 98.5.
