WPHD (104.9 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a classic hits format. Licensed to Montour Falls, New York, United States, the station serves the Elmira-Corning area. The station is currently owned by Seven Mountains Media. In 2020, WNGZ moved to the 93 frequency and tweaked to a full blown Active Rock format using the moniker The New Rock Edge. On July 3, 2020, WNGZ changed its callsign to WPHD and flipped to classic hits as part of a five-station frequency swap.
