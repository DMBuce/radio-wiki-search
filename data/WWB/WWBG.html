WWBG (1470 AM, "Activa") is an American radio station licensed to serve Greensboro, North Carolina, United States. The station, launched in 1999, is currently owned by Mahan Janbakhsh's TBLC Holdings, LLC and the broadcast license is held by TBLC Greensboro Stations, LLC.

WWBG broadcasts a Spanish-language contemporary music format to the Greensboro/High Point/Winston-Salem, North Carolina area. WWBG is now part of "Activa TRIAD, 1470 AM (WWBG), 1380 AM WWNT and 1040 AM WSGH". As of August 1, 2018, the station is silent.
