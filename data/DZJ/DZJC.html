DZJC (747 AM) Aksyon Radyo is an radio station in the Philippines owned and operated by Manila Broadcasting Company through its licensee Cebu Broadcasting Company. The station's studio and transmitter are located along J. P. Rizal St., Brgy. Sto. Tomas, Laoag.
