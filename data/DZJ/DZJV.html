DZJV (1458 AM) is a radio station owned and operated by ZOE Broadcasting Network. Its studio, transmitter and tower site are located at #140 Brgy. Parian, Calamba City, Laguna (near Riverview Resort) and serves the Calabarzon, Metro Manila and Mimaropa region, including parts of the Bicol Region, Central Luzon and Central Visayas via weak signal.

Initially, it used to operate 12 hours a day, from 6:00 AM to 6:00 PM. From April 2014 to March 2015, it expanded its broadcast hours to 10:00 PM, airing programs by 8TriMedia Broadcasting. From March 27, 2017 to February 2019, it reduced its airtime from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM. Since March 2019, with the Light TV Radyo block was launched, it expanded its broadcast hours once more from 6:00 AM to 6:00 PM.
