95.6 BRFM is a community radio station serving the Isle of Sheppey in Kent which launched on 30 October 2006.

The station broadcasts 24 hours a day on 95.6 FM. As there is no existing local commercial station for the Isle of Sheppey, BRFM is allowed to broadcast local commercials and on-air sponsorships.
