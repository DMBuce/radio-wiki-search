KCGM (95.7 FM), "FM 95", is a radio station licensed to serve Scobey, Montana. The station is owned by Prairie Communications, Inc. Its studios are located at 20 Main Street in Scobey. The transmitter and 400-foot tower are east of town.

It airs a community-oriented full-service Country music format.

The station was assigned the KCGM call letters by the Federal Communications Commission.
