KCGP-LP (106.7 FM) is a radio station licensed to Grants Pass, Oregon, United States. The station is currently owned by Calvary Chapel of Grants Pass.

KCGP-LP is located at 269 W. Harbeck Road in Grants Pass, Oregon, USA, and can be heard at 106.7 FM in Grants Pass and the surrounding area, and online at kcgp.us.
