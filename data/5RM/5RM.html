5RM (801 AM) is a South Australian radio station broadcasting from its transmitter site at Renmark into the Riverland and Mallee (Victoria)

5RM was opened in 1935 and was originally part of the 5KA network along with sister station 5AU Port Augusta and the church run 5KA Adelaide

The station broadcasts from studios located in Berri, South Australia.

As of Feb 2020, 5RMs Programming consists of Anthony Milich on the 5RM Breakfast Show 6-9 Weekdays, The Leon Byner Morning Show (originating from 5AA Adelaide 9-12 Weekdays and Lachie Winnall 12-6 Weekdays.
