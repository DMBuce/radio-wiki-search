WQMX (94.9 FM) – branded FM 94.9 WQMX – is a commercial country radio station licensed to Medina, Ohio. Owned by Rubber City Radio Group, Inc., the station primarily serves the Akron metro area, but also can be heard in Greater Cleveland and Canton. The WQMX studios are located in Akron, while the station transmitter resides in the neighboring suburb of Copley Township. Besides a standard analog transmission, WQMX's programming is also available online.
