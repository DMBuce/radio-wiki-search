WQME (98.7 FM) is a non-commercial radio station licensed to Anderson, Indiana, serving the Indianapolis, Indiana and Muncie, Indiana markets. Owned by the Educational Media Foundation, the station broadcasts EMF's Air1 network.

The station previously aired a locally-programmed Christian format branded as 98.7 The Song. In December 2017, the university announced its intent to sell WQME. On May 31, 2018, the station ceased local programming, switching to the Educational Media Foundation's Air1 network. The university formally sold WQME to EMF outright on October 31, 2018; the sale was completed on November 30.
