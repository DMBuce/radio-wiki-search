WMXW (103.3 MHz) is a commercial FM radio station licensed to Vestal, New York and serving the Binghamton metropolitan area. The station airs an adult contemporary radio format and is owned by iHeartMedia. Local DJs are heard on weekdays, with the syndicated Delilah show on weeknights and Ellen K on Saturday mornings.

The studios and offices are on North Jensen Road in Vestal. The transmitter is off Ingraham Hill Road in Binghamton, amid other towers for local TV and FM stations.
