WMXH-FM (105.7 FM) is a Contemporary Christian formatted broadcast radio station licensed to Luray, Virginia, serving Page County, Southern Shenandoah County and Northern Rockingham County, all in Virginia. WMXH-FM is owned and operated by Hayden Hamilton Media Strategies, Inc.
