WMXN-FM (101.7 FM, "101.7 The Torch") is a radio station licensed to serve Stevenson, Alabama, United States. The station is owned by KEA Radio, Inc. Launched in 1977 as WVSV (the "Voice of Sequatchie Valley"), this station broadcasts to Jackson County, Alabama, and the surrounding counties.
