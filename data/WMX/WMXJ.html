WMXJ (102.7 MHz, "102.7 The Beach") is a commercial FM radio station licensed to Pompano Beach, Florida, United States, and serving the Miami media market. The station is owned by Entercom and airs a classic hits radio format. Its studios and offices are located on Northwest Second Avenue in Miami Gardens. The transmitter is off Northwest 210th Street, also in Miami Gardens.

WMXJ broadcasts in the HD Radio format.
