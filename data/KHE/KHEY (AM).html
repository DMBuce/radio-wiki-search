KHEY (1380 AM) is a Fox Sports Radio affiliate in the El Paso, Texas, United States, area. The station is owned by iHeartMedia, Inc.. The station is licensed to broadcast in HD radio, but does not currently broadcast in HD.

Historically, KHEY was one of several country music stations in the El Paso, Texas market, and was the last AM station in the market to keep its country format. The station was assigned a frequency of 690 AM, owner Clear Channel communications swapped frequencies with station KTSM in the year 2000.
