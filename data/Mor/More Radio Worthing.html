More Radio Worthing (known as Splash FM from 2003-2016), is an Independent Local Radio station serving Worthing, Shoreham, Littlehampton and surrounding areas. It is owned and operated by Media Sound Holdings and broadcasts from studios at The Guildbourne Centre in Worthing, as part of a network of stations across Sussex.
