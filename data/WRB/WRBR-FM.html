WRBR-FM (103.9 FM) is a radio station serving the South Bend, Indiana market and broadcasting an active rock format. Licensed to South Bend, Indiana, United States, WRBR-FM hosts several concerts and activities in the South Bend area. WRBR-FM is formerly an oldies station before switching to an active rock format.
