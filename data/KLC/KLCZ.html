KLCZ (88.9 FM) is a student operated college station broadcasting a freeform format. KLCZ has sampled from a variety of often very different genres recently. Licensed to Lewiston, Idaho, United States, the radio station serves the Lewiston area. The station is currently owned by Lewis–Clark State College.
