XTSR is a College radio station owned by Towson University and managed by the Department of Electronic Media & Film. Their Internet radio programming operates 24/7 with live student DJ shifts and a Broadcast automation system.

At the station, radio students learn first hand about the basics of running a radio station. The station's music library includes alternative rock, local and underground acts, and music from different cultures. They also have contacts with most major and independent music labels.

XTSR can be streamed through the website, www.xtsr.org, through iTunes under the College Radio tuner, or through the College Radio tuner App for the iPhone and iPod touch.

Students living on Towson University's campus can listen on channel 26 on the campus cable television network.
