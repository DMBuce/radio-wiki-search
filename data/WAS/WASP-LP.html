WASP-LP is a campus variety-formatted broadcast radio station licensed to Huntington, West Virginia and serving Huntington and Kenova in West Virginia and Burlington in Ohio. WASP-LP is owned and operated by Wayne County Board of Education.

Programming includes The Weekend Throwdown with Jagger, Remix Top30 with Hollywood Hamilton, and M.G. Kelly's Amazing 80s
