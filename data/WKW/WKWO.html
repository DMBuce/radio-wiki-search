WKWO (90.9 FM) is a radio station licensed to serve Wooster, Ohio. The station operated from 1968 to 2019 as the campus radio station of The College of Wooster, before the college transitioned it to online-only operation in April 2019. The station is now owned by Educational Media Foundation, and carries EMF's K-LOVE contemporary Christian format.
