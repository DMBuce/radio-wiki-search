CHCM is an AM radio station located in Marystown, Newfoundland and Labrador, Canada broadcasting at 740 kHz. Owned by Stingray Group, CHCM first went on the air on May 23, 1962. It is an affiliate of VOCM. CHCM was originally broadcast on 560 before moving to 740 in 1990.

In September 2016, CHCM and CKVO in Clarenville cancelled their remaining local programming and now simulcast VOCM in St. John's full-time.
