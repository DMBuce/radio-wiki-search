WVQR (90.1 FM) is a community radio station broadcasting a News Talk/Music format. Licensed to Vieques, Puerto Rico, it serves the eastern Puerto Rico area. The station is currently owned by Comite para el Desarrollo y Rescate de Vieques (CPRDV) through Radio Vieques-La Voz del Este, Inc.
