VIBE FM is a Romanian radio station with the headquarters in Bucharest. Lagardere is the owner of Radio 21 and Europa FM. Vibe FM is dedicated to all between 15 and 22 years. At 2 March 2015 at midnight, VIBE FM was changed from radio with electronic club music to radio with gold music (AC genre).
