WRCD (101.5 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a classic rock format. Licensed to Canton, New York, United States, the station is owned by Stephens Media Group.

The station was previously owned by Martz Communications Group, and was acquired by Stephens as of February 1, 2008.

WRCD is an affiliate of the Bob and Tom Show.

WRCD Covers St. Lawrence County, New York; Eastern Ontario including Brockville, parts of Ottawa, Cornwall; and Western Quebec.
