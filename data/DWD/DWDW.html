DWDW (88.7 FM), broadcasting as 88.7 Radyo Bandera News FM, is the flagship news and music radio station owned by Radyo Bandera Network Philippines. The station's main studio is located in Macasaet Business Complex, Roxas St., Puerto Princesa City, with repeaters located in various towns in Palawan.

It is currently the overall #1 FM radio station in the province and ranked #1 in the news FM segment, according to the latest KBP-RRC Kantar Media radio survey.
