DWDP (106.9 MHz) Tropang Pinoy FM is an FM station owned and operated by Civil Affairs Group of the Armed Forces of the Philippines. Its studios and transmitter are located at 2nd Floor, Municipal Gymnasium, Brgy. Bagumbayan, Roxas, Oriental Mindoro.
