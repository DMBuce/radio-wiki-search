DYAF (1143 FM) Radyo Totoo is a religious AM radio station owned and operated by the Diocese of Bacolod, a member of the Catholic Media Network. Its studio and transmitter are located at San Juan St., cor. Antonio Y. Fortich Ave., Bacolod.
