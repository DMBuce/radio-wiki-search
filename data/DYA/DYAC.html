DYAC (90.7 FM) - on-air as Q Radio 90.7 - is a radio station owned by Mareco Broadcasting Network and operated by Horizon of the Sun Communications. The station's studio and transmitter are located at Room 210, 3rd Floor Doña Luisa Bldg. Fuente Osmena, Cebu City. This station operates daily from 5:00 AM to 10:00 PM.
