DYAP (765 kHz Palawan) Radyo Patrol Palawan was a commercial broadcast radio station owned by Palawan Broadcasting Corporation affiliated to ABS-CBN Corporation, located at the ABS-CBN Broadcast Center, Corner of Mabini and Valencia Streets, Barangay Masipag, Puerto Princesa, Palawan.

DYAP is the first locally owned radio station to broadcast in the entire province, which has the largest land area of any island in the Philippines. The station was established in 1965 as DYPR, before the Ferdinand Marcos presidency. It transmits news, information, talk shows, and dramas to a local population for home access to radio signals from Luzon and Visayas is limited. In October 2011, ABS-CBN bought the station's entire airtime and rebranded it as Radyo Patrol. A few months later, the former staff of DYPR established DZIP.
