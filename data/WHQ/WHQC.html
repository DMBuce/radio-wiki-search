WHQC (96.1 FM, "Hits 96.1") is an American radio station in Charlotte, North Carolina. Licensed to serve the city of Shelby, North Carolina, it is owned by iHeartMedia. WHQC broadcasts a Top 40 (CHR) format branded as "Hits 96.1." Its transmitter is located in Dallas, North Carolina, and its studios are located along with the rest of iHeartMedia's Charlotte stations in the Wood Ridge Center office complex off Billy Graham Parkway in south Charlotte. WHQC is in a very tight ratings race with Beasley Media Group's WNKS, As of the February 2020 Nielsen ratings, the two stations are separated by just one tenth of a share, with WHQC having a 4.1 share and WNKS having a 4.0 share

WHQC is licensed to broadcast in the HD Radio format. HD 1 is a simulcast of the analog "All the Hits" broadcast signal, while HD-2 signal is known as "Air1" which programs Contemporary Worship music on W262BM 100.3 FM.
