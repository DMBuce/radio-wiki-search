KFRO (1370 AM, "The Voice of Longview") is a terrestrial American radio station licensed to Longview, Texas, United States. The station serves the Longview-Tyler-Kilgore-Marshall area. KFRO is owned by RCA Broadcasting, LLC, and is the sister station to KPRO in Marshall, Texas.
