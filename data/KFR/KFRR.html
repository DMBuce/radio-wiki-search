KFRR (104.1 FM) is a commercial modern rock music radio station in Woodlake, California, broadcasting to the Fresno, California area. Its studios are located at One Putt Broadcasting in Downtown Fresno, across from the historic Warnors Theater, while the transmitter is at Eshom Point.
