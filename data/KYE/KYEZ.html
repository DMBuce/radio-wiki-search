KYEZ (93.7 FM, "Y93.7") is a radio station licensed to serve Salina, Kansas, United States. The station is owned by Rocking M Media, LLC. The station was assigned the KYEZ call sign by the Federal Communications Commission.

KYEZ broadcasts a country music format. Prior to being branded Y93.7, KYEZ was branded as KY Country 94 and KY94.
