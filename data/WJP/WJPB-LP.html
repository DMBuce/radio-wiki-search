WJPB-LP (99.1 FM) is a low-power radio station at 99.1 MHz with 100 watts of power in Muncie, Indiana.

It was formed as an FM simulcast with the former WLHN 990 kHz (currently WJCF), also located in Muncie. WJPB first signed on in 2005.

WJPB (now a stand-alone FM) airs a southern gospel format and is owned and operated by Jackson Park Baptist Church.
