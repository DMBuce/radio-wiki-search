DWSE (103.7 FM), on-air as 103.7 Boom FM, is a radio station owned by Kaissar Broadcasting Network and operated by SOL Broadcasting, a media firm owned by multi-awarded broadcaster Sonia O. Leano. Its studios and transmitter are located at Dolor Hotel, #16 Vinzons Ave., Brgy. Magang, Daet, Camarines Norte.
