DWSU-FM (95.9 MHz Dasmariñas) or commonly known as Green FM is a low-power FM campus radio station aired for the first time on August 2005 and owned by De La Salle University - Dasmariñas Its studio and transmitter are located at De La Salle University - Dasmariñas, Dasmariñas, Cavite. Broadcast airing from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM during weekdays. The station is being headed by Ms. Maria Aurora B. Quadra- Balibay as its station manager and Mr. Christian De Ramos, station's technician.
