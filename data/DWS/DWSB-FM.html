DWSB (89.5 FM), broadcasting as 89.5 Subic Bay Radio, is a public radio station owned and operated by the Subic Bay Metropolitan Authority. The station's studio and transmitter are located at Bldg. N, Quezon Street, Subic Bay Freeport Zone, Olongapo.
