DWSG (91.9 FM), broadcasting as 91.9 Radyo Natin, is a radio station owned and operated by Manila Broadcasting Company. Its studios & transmitter are located along Maharlika Hi-way, Brgy. Pangpang, Sorsogon City. It operates daily from 4:00 AM to 10:00 PM.
