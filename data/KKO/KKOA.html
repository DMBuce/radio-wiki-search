KKOA (107.7 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a New Country format. Licensed to Volcano, Hawaii, United States, the station serves the Hilo area. The station is currently owned by Resonate Hawaii LLC through licensee Resoante Hawaii LLC, and features programming from ABC Radio's "Today's Best Country" satellite feed.
