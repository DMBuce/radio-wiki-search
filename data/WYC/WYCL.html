WYCL (1540 AM) is a radio station licensed to Niles, Ohio that serves the Youngstown-Warren area from its studio/transmitter facility in Mineral Ridge. The station is currently dark due to technical issues, and will resume broadcasting once repairs are made. The station is owned and operated by Sagittarius Communications, LLC. Prior to August 2019, the station carried programming Salem Radio Network, Fox Sports Radio, Westwood One, Fox News Radio, and SB Nation Radio.
