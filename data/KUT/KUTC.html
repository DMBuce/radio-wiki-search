KUTC (95.7 FM) is an American radio station broadcasting a Classic rock format. It is licensed to Gunnison, Utah, and serves central Utah. The station is owned by Sanpete County Broadcasting Company, and is an affiliate of the syndicated Pink Floyd program "Floydian Slip."
