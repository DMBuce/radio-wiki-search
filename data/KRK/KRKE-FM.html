KRKE-FM (101.3 MHz) is a radio station in Albuquerque, New Mexico, broadcasting to the Albuquerque metropolitan area at 3,700 watts from a tower atop Nine Mile Hill in west Albuquerque. Its studios are located in Northeast Albuquerque (a mile north of Central Avenue).

The station is owned by Vanguard Media. KRKE-FM airs a classic hits music format branded as "Sunny 101.3".
