KQZB is an FM radio station licensed to Troy, Idaho and broadcasting on a frequency of 100.5 Megahertz. From 2016–2019, the station was known as Hits 100 and owned by Pullman Radio Inc. In September 2019, the station was renamed as 100.5 KQZB and was sold to the Pacific Empire Radio Corporation

KQZB has a Classic hits format and is owned by Pacific Empire Radio Corporation.
