KBFO-FM (106.7 FM, "106.7 Point FM") is a radio station broadcasting an Adult based Top 40 (CHR) hybrid music format. Licensed to Aberdeen, South Dakota, United States, the station serves the Aberdeen area. The station is currently owned by Prairie Winds Broadcasting, Inc.

Notable weekday programming includes syndicated Bob & Sheri (5 am – 9 am), Rusty Rokit (9 am – 2 pm), The Dude (2 pm – 7 pm) and syndicated Party Playhouse (6 pm – 11 pm). Syndicated American Top 40, and Open House Party are featured programs on the weekends.
