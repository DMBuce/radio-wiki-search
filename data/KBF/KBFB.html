KBFB (97.9 FM, "97.9 The Beat") is a Dallas/Fort Worth-based radio station with an Urban Contemporary format. It is owned by Radio One with sister station KZMJ. Its studios (alongside KZMJ) are located in the Galleria Area in North Dallas, and its transmitter is located in Cedar Hill with syndicated Morning Show, The Morning Hustle.
