Khulumani FM also known as KFM95.0 is a community radio station for the metropolitan province of Bulawayo.

The station provides music, culture talk shows, news and current affairs to the audiences in the Bulawayo metropolitan province and surrounding areas within a 60Â km radius. It is owned by the state controlled ZBC. The station is based in ZBC's Montrose Studios in Bulawayo.

The station was welcomed well by Bulawayo residents, artistes and businesses as an additional flavour to the existing headbanger, Skyz Metro FM.

The station broadcasts in English, Ndebele, Xhosa, Kalanga, Venda, Sotho and Tonga.

The station's slogan is Izwi Lethu, Igugu Lethu (Our Voice, Our Pride).
