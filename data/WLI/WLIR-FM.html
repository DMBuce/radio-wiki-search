WLIR-FM (107.1 FM, Talkradio 107.1) is a radio station licensed to Hampton Bays, New York, and serving eastern Long Island. The station's transmitter is located in Northampton, Suffolk County, New York. WLIR-FM simulcasts the talk radio format of WABC in New York City, and is operated under a local marketing agreement by the station's owner Red Apple Media.
