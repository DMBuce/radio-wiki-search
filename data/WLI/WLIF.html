WLIF (101.9 FM, "Today's 101.9") is a commercial FM radio station licensed to serve Baltimore, Maryland. The station is owned by Entercom through licensee Entercom License, LLC and broadcasts an adult contemporary format. Its studios are located on Clarkview Road in the Mount Washington neighborhood of Baltimore, while its broadcast tower is located near Loch Raven Reservoir near Towson. at (39°25′7.0″N 76°33′16.2″W).
