KQDJ-FM (101.1 FM, "Q101") is a radio station licensed to serve Valley City, North Dakota, serving the Jamestown and Valley City areas. The station is owned by Ingstad Family Media. It airs a Top 40 (CHR) music format.

The station was assigned the KQDJ-FM call letters by the Federal Communications Commission on November 1, 1996.
