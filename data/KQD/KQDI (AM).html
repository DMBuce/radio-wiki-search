KQDI (1450 AM, "NewsTalk 1450") is a radio station broadcasting a News Talk Information format. Licensed to Great Falls, Montana, United States, the station serves the Great Falls area. The station is currently owned by STARadio Corporation and features programming from Westwood One, CNN Radio and Premiere Radio Networks.
