WLFX (106.7 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a Soft AC format. Licensed to Berea, Kentucky, United States, the station serves the Richmond area and southern parts of the Lexington area. The station is currently owned by Wallingford Communications, LLC and features programming from Cumulus Media Networks.
