KXAZ (93.3 FM, "93.3 Jack FM") is a radio station licensed to serve Page, Arizona, United States. The station is owned by Lake Powell Communications. It airs an adult hits music format, specifically the Jack FM brand.

The station was assigned the KXAZ call letters by the Federal Communications Commission on November 2, 1981.
