Onda Cero is Spain's number three radio station, part of Atresmedia media group.

Some of its programs are these: Más de uno, La Brújula, Julia en la Onda, Noticias Mediodía, Te doy mi palabra, Radioestadio, Gente Viajera, Noticias Fin de Semana.
