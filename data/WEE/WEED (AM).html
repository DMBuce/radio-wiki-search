WEED "1390 Jammin' Gospel" is an AM radio station broadcasting a gospel music format. Licensed to Rocky Mount, North Carolina, United States, it serves the Rocky Mount/Wilson area. The station is currently owned by Northstar Broadcasting Corporation.
