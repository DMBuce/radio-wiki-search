WAIR (104.9 FM) is a radio station owned by Superior Communications, which began broadcasting in 2004 after funds to build the station were raised by local churches. Programming for the station is provided by WLGH-FM, the lead station for Smile FM, a network of contemporary Christian stations. The station increased power to 2,800 watts from 1,600 watts in April, 2017.
