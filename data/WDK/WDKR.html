WDKR (107.3 FM) is a radio station licensed to Maroa, Illinois, serving the Decatur, Illinois area. WDKR airs a format consisting of oldies from the mid to late 1960s, the 1970s, and the early to mid 1980s. The station is owned by WDKR, INC.
