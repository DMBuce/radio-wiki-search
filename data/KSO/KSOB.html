KSOB (96.7 FM, "Kansas Country 96.7") is a commercial radio station licensed to serve Larned, Kansas, United States. The station is currently owned by Rocking M Media, LLC.

KSOB broadcasts a classic country music format branded as "Kansas Country 96.7". It previously aired the ""Jack FM and "Bob FM" adult hits formats.

The station was assigned the KSOB call sign by the Federal Communications Commission on November 27, 2007.
