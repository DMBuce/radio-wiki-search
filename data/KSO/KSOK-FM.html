KSOK-FM (95.9 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a country music format. Licensed to Winfield, Kansas, United States, the station serves southeast of the Wichita area. The station is currently owned by Cowley County Broadcasting, Inc. and features programming from ABC Radio .
