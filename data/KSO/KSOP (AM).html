KSOP (1370 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a Classic Country format. Licensed to South Salt Lake, Utah, United States, the station serves the Salt Lake City area. The station is currently owned by Ksop, Inc. The station was founded in 1955 by Henry Hilton, a Utah native who had worked in other local radio stations before founding this independent business.
