KSOO-FM (99.1 FM, "ESPN 99.1") is a radio station with a Sports format with programming from ESPN Radio. Licensed to Lennox, South Dakota, United States, the station serves the Sioux Falls area. The station is currently owned by Townsquare Media.

Its studios are located on Tennis Lane in Sioux Falls, while its transmitter is located near Lennox.
