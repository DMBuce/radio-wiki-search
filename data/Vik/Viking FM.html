Viking FM is an Independent Local Radio station owned and operated by Bauer as part of the Hits Radio network. It broadcasts to East Riding of Yorkshire and Northern Lincolnshire.

As of October 2019, the station broadcasts from shared studios with Hallam FM, outside its broadcast area in Sheffield.
