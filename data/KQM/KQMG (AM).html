KQMG (1220 AM) is a commercial radio station serving Independence, Iowa and Buchanan County, Iowa. Former call letters were KOUR. KQMG AM and FM are owned by KM Communications, Inc and licensed to KM Radio of Independence, LLC. KQMG-FM which began broadcasting in 1972 is located at 95.3 MHz.
