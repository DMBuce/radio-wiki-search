KQMQ-FM (93.1 FM) is a radio station based in Honolulu, Hawaii. The Pacifc Media Group, LLC outlet broadcasts at 93.1 MHz with an ERP of 100 kW. The station also transmits on Spectrum digital channel 868 for the entire state of Hawaii. It offers a proprietary blend of Hawaiian/Island Reggae and traditional Reggae music format branded as "HI93". The programming is led by local radio vet Rick Thomas. The station's studios are located in Downtown Honolulu and its transmitter is located near Akupu.
