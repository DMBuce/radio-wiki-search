WKRM (1340 AM, "Today's Hits 1340") is a radio station broadcasting an Country & Americana music format. Licensed to Columbia, Tennessee, United States, the station is currently owned by Middle Tennessee Broadcasting Co., Inc. and features programming from Citadel Media, Motor Racing Network, and Premiere Radio Networks.
