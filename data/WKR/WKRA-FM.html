WKRA-FM (92.7 FM) is a radio station licensed to serve the community of Holly Springs, Mississippi. The station is owned by Billy R. Autry, and airs a gospel music format.

The station was assigned the WKRA-FM call letters by the Federal Communications Commission on December 2, 1974.
