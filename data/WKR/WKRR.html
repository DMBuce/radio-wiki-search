WKRR (92.3 MHz) is a commercial FM radio station licensed to Asheboro, North Carolina, and serving the Piedmont Triad including the Greensboro/Winston-Salem/High Point radio market. It broadcasts a classic rock radio format, calling itself "Rock 92," and is the Triad's affiliate for the Carolina Panthers radio network. WKRR is owned by Dick Broadcasting, along with WKZL "107.5 KZL" in Winston-Salem. Both stations broadcast from studios and offices on East Lewis Street in Greensboro.

WKRR has an effective radiated power (ERP) of 100,000 watts, the maximum for non-grandfathered FM stations. The transmitter is off Island Ford Road in Randleman, North Carolina (35°50′0.0″N 79°50′1.0″W).
