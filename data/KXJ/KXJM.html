KXJM (107.5 MHz, "Jam'n 107.5") is a commercial FM radio station licensed to Banks, Oregon and serving the Portland metropolitan area. KXJM's studios and offices are in Tigard and the transmitter is located in Portland's West Hills. The station is owned by iHeartMedia, Inc., and airs a Rhythmic CHR format.

KXJM is the second station in Portland to use these call letters. They previously were found on 95.5, which is now KBFF.
