KNOD (105.3 FM, "Kool Gold 105") is a radio station broadcasting a classic hits music format. Licensed to Harlan, Iowa, United States, the station is currently owned by Wireless Broadcasting, L.L.C. and features programming from Citadel Media and Dial Global.
