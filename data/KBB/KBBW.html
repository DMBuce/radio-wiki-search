KBBW (1010 AM, 100.3 FM, 101.3 FM, 105.9 FM) is a Waco, Texas, Christian broadcast radio station, relayed by 3 FM translators, K290CV (105.9 FM) in Waco, K267CA (101.3 FM) in Temple/Killeen, and 100.3 K262DG in Georgetown/Round Rock. KBBW broadcasts national and local ministries such as David Jeremiah, Billy Graham, Chuck Swindoll, The Lutheran Hour, Charles Stanley, J. Vernon McGee, Greg Laurie, Adrian Rogers, Steve Arterburn, Jay Sekulow, and James (Jim) Dobson.
