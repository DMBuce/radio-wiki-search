KBBX-FM (97.7 FM, "Lobo 97.7") is a radio station broadcasting a Regional Mexican format. Licensed to Nebraska City, Nebraska, United States, the station serves the Omaha and Lincoln metropolitan areas. The station is currently owned by Flood Communications of Omaha, LLC. KBBX's studios are located on John Galt Boulevard in Omaha, while its transmitter is located just north of Weeping Water, Nebraska.
