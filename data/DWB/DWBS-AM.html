Veritas 1008 (DWBS 1008 kHz) is an AM radio station owned by the Diocesan Multi-Media Services, Inc., the media arm of the Diocese of Legazpi. The station's studio is located at the 2nd Floor, SEDCen Bldg., Landco Business Park, Brgy. Capantawan, Legaspi City. Its transmitter is located at the former St. Jude Catholic School Brgy. Bitano.
