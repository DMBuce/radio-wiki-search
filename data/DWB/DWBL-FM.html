DWBL, operating as 91.9 Bright FM, is a music FM radio station owned and operated by the Archdiocese of San Fernando. It was formerly owned by the Bright Light Broadcasting Service before the Archdiocese of San Fernando acquired it on September 7, 2015. The radio station is affiliated with the Catholic Media Network. Its studio and transmitter are located 4th Floor, DSF Building, Gen. Hizon Avenue, San Fernando, Pampanga.

It was formerly owned by Bright Light Broadcasting Service until September 7, 2015, when it was acquired by the Archdiocese of San Fernando. It is currently a member of the Catholic Media Network.
