DWBG (95.9 FM), broadcasting as 95.9 Big Sound FM, is a radio station owned and operated by Vanguard Radio Network. The station's studio is located along Dominican Rd., Baguio.

The station was formerly located at the roofdeck of Abanao Square along Abanao St. cor. Zandueta St. from its inception in 1994 until January 2007, when it went off the air. It returned to air in 2013 on its current location.
