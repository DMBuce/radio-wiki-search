DWBL (1242 AM) is a radio station owned and operated by FBS Radio Network in the Philippines. The station's studio is located at Unit 908, Paragon Plaza, EDSA cor. Reliance Street, Mandaluyong City, while its transmitter is located at Malanday, Valenzuela City (co-shared with sister station DWSS, and Manila Broadcasting Company's DZRH). It broadcasts daily from 4:30 AM to 12:00 MN.
