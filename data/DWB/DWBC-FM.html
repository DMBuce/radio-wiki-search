DWBC (87.9 FM), broadcasting as 87.9 Radyo Biñan, is a low-power community radio station owned and operated by the Biñan City Government. Its studio and transmitter is located at the City Hall, Brgy. Zapote, Biñan, Laguna. The main format of the station is music, news and public service.
