KXKS-FM (93.7 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a Country format. Licensed to Shreveport, Louisiana, United States, it serves the areas of Shreveport – Bossier City, Minden, Louisiana, and Marshall, Texas. The station is currently owned by Townsquare Media. From its inception, the 93.7 has been country. It has been known as T93, Thunder 93.7, and Thunder country, among others. Its studios are shared with its other five sister stations in West Shreveport (one mile west of Shreveport Regional Airport, and the transmitter is in Mooringsport, Louisiana.
