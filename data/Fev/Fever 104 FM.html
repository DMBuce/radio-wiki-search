Fever FM (by HT Media Ltd.) is an FM radio station in India. It is currently available in 13 cities in India:

  • Delhi (as Fever 104 FM)
  • Mumbai (as Fever 104 FM)
  • Bangalore (as Fever 104 FM)
  • Kolkata (as Fever 104 FM)
  • Chennai (as Fever 91.9 FM)
  • Hyderabad (as Fever 94.3 FM)
  • Lucknow (as Fever 104 FM)
  • Kanpur (as Fever 95 FM)
  • Agra (as Fever 93.7 FM)
  • Aligarh (as Fever 94.9 FM)
  • Allahabad (as Fever 94.3 FM)
  • Gorakhpur (as Fever 94.3 FM)
  • Bareilly (as Fever 94.9 FM)
