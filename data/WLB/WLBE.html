WLBE (790 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a full service format with an emphasis on classic country. Licensed to Leesburg, Florida, United States, it serves the Lake County area. The station is currently owned by WLBE 790, Inc.. As of 6/6/2019, the station has gone silent due to financial difficulties.

The station originally signed on in 1947 using the call letters WEUS, but changed to WLBE effective 10/19/1948.
