WLBJ-LP (104.1 Low Power FM) was a locally-owned and operated LPFM station in Fostoria, Ohio. A construction permit was granted by the Federal Communications Commission (FCC) on November 12, 2014. The station was owned by the Christoper Center Corporation. It aired Catholic programming from the EWTN Global Catholic Radio Network through an affiliation with Toledo-based Annunciation Radio, which fed its programming. WLBJ-LP was branded as Christ The Shepherd Radio.

The station's license was cancelled by the FCC on October 2, 2020, for failing to file a license renewal application.
