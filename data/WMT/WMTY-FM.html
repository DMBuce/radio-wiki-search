WMTY-FM (98.3 FM) is a radio station broadcasting an oldies music format. Licensed to Sweetwater, Tennessee, United States, the station is currently owned by Horne Radio, LLC and features The True Oldies Channel from Citadel Media.

On August 15, 2007 WLOD-FM changed their format from oldies to classic rock, branded as "D 98.3".

On September 1, 2010 WLOD-FM changed their format from classic rock to oldies, branded as "True Oldies". On September 15, 2010 WLOD-FM changed their call letters to WMTY-FM.
