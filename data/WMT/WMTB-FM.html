WMTB-FM (89.9 FM) is the student radio station of Mount Saint Mary's. Licensed to Emmitsburg, Maryland, United States, the station offers mainly student-produced programming daytime/evenings, while overnights and mornings are used by "Maryland Catholic Radio" to broadcast EWTN Radio.
