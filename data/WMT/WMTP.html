WMTP is a Christian radio station licensed to Conway, New Hampshire and serving the North Conway, New Hampshire and Fryeburg, Maine areas, broadcasting on 91.1 MHz FM. WMTP is owned by Word Radio Educational Foundation. The station is also simulcast on 88.5 WRKJ in Westbrook, Maine and 91.7 WWPC in New Durham, New Hampshire.
