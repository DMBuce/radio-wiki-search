WEOW, is a commercial radio station broadcasting at 92.7 FM with a Top 40 (CHR) branded as "WEOW 92.7" (Pronounced as "wow"). The station is currently owned by Robert Holladay, through licensee Florida Keys Media, LLC, and is the southernmost Top 40 (CHR) station in Continental United States. Its 100,000 watt signal covers an area from Key Largo, Fl, to Havana, Cuba.
