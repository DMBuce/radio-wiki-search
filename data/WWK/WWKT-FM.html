WWKT-FM (99.3 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a country music format. Licensed to Kingstree, South Carolina, United States, the station serves the Florence/Sumter/Kingstree area. The station is currently owned by Community Broadcasters, LLC. The station is commonly known as "99-3, the Cat", and their line-up includes "The Mudflap & Palmer Show" featuring Mudflap & Ed Palmer from 6-10am weekdays, Mike Montgomery from 10a-3p, and Bo Reynolds from 3p-7p. .
