WIEZ (1490 AM, "94.7 WIEZ) is an American radio station licensed to Decatur, Alabama, that serves the western Huntsville, Alabama, market. The station broadcasts a soft oldies variety format.

WIEZ carries Austin High School football on Friday evenings during the fall.
