bTV Radio is a Bulgarian radio station, part of bTV Radio Group, owned by the Central European Media Enterprises (owned by WarnerMedia/AT&T). It started broadcasting from May 1, 2009 in Sofia, and later launched in Pernik and Gotse Delchev. Its program is also transmitted via satellite across Europe. The station originally launched as a radio program of the television station PRO.BG - PRO.FM. From October 2011, it was re-branded to the eponymous radio station of the television channel bTV, transmitting much of the television output. The radio will go on broadcasting only in Sofia.
