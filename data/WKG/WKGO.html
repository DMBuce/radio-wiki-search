WKGO is a beautiful music formatted broadcast radio station licensed to Murrysville, Pennsylvania, serving Murrysville, Plum, and Vandergrift, all in Pennsylvania. Before that, as WRWJ, it was a Southern Gospel and Contemporary Christian station owned by and broadcasting the "He's Alive" Radio Network, which originated from Grantsville, Maryland and flagship station WAIJ.

WKGO is owned and operated by Robert and Ashley Stevens, through licensee Broadcast Educational Communications, Inc. After changing ownership in 2016, the station was silent for about 6 months, returning to the air in September of that year with the beautiful music format.

As of December 19, 2019, WKGO is simulcast on KQV.
