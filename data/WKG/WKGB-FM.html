WKGB-FM (92.5 FM) – branded 92.5 KGB – is a commercial mainstream rock radio station licensed to Conklin, New York, serving Greater Binghamton. Owned by iHeartMedia, WKGB-FM is the local affiliate for Rover's Morning Glory and Sixx Sense with Nikki Sixx. The WKGB-FM studios are located in the Binghamton suburb of Vestal, while the station transmitter resides in Binghamton. In addition to a standard analog transmission, WKGB-FM is available online via iHeartRadio.
