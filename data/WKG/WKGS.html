WKGS (106.7 FM) – branded "KISS 106-7" – is a Top 40 (CHR) station licensed to Irondequoit, New York, and serves the Rochester area. The iHeartMedia outlet broadcasts at 106.7 MHz with 4,600 watts effective radiated power. Its studios are located at the Five Star Bank Plaza building in downtown Rochester, and its transmitter site is in Brighton.
