WKGE is an AM radio station licensed to Johnstown, Pennsylvania and broadcasting on 850 kHz with 10 kW day and night. The station has a classic hits format, branded as "101.3 WKGE", that is simulcast on FM translator W267CM (101.3 FM Johnstown).

WKGE currently uses a complex directional antenna array to protect other stations on 850 kHz. It has a Construction Permit (CP) to move to 870 kHz and become a daytime-only Class D station. This includes a power cut to 7 kW, and a reduction from a 9-tower to a simpler 3-tower array.
