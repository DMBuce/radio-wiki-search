WRFJ is a non-commercial radio station located in Fort Mill, South Carolina, United States, that broadcasts on a frequency of 91.5 MHz

Programming originates from RTN's HIs Radio Network. Since August 2014, RTN has featured HIs Radio Praise, their Christian Contemporary Praise & Worship format.
