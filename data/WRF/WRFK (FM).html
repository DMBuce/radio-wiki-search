WRFK (107.1 FM, "Frank FM") is a radio station licensed to Barre and serving Central Vermont. Established in 1974, the station is owned by Jeffrey Shapiro's Great Eastern Radio. WRFK airs a classic rock format, also found on other FM stations in Northern New England, including WFNK in the Portland, Maine area and on WFNQ in the Manchester, New Hampshire area. The transmitter is located off Websterville Road and the studios and offices are on Jacques Street.

WRFK began simulcasting on 107.1 FM WPLA (now WWFK) in Dannemora, New York which brings the stations format and branding into the Burlington, Vermont/Plattsburgh, New York/Champlain Valley area.
