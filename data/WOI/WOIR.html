WOIR (1430 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a Spanish Contemporary Christian/Spanish News/Talk format. Licensed to Homestead, Florida, United States, the station serves the areas of Miami, Homestead, Florida City, and the Florida Keys. The station is currently owned by ERJ Media, LLC.
