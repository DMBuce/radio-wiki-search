KFYV (105.5 FM, "Live 105-5") is a commercial radio station that is licensed to Ojai, California and serves the Oxnard-Ventura radio market. The station is owned by Gold Coast Broadcasting and airs a Top 40 (CHR) format. The KFYV studio is located in Ventura and the transmitter is situated among several other radio and television towers off Red Mountain Fire Road in Ventura. KFYV uses two booster stations to strengthen its signal: KFYV-FM1 in Oak View and KFYV-FM2 in Ventura, both at the 105.5 FM frequency.
