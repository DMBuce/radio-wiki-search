KFYI (550 kHz) is a commercial AM radio station in Phoenix, Arizona. KFYI has a talk radio format and is owned by iHeartMedia, Inc. The studios and offices are located on East Van Buren Street, off Hohokam Freeway, near Sky Harbor Airport. The signal covers the Phoenix metropolitan area and it uses the slogan "The Valley's Talk Station."

KFYI is powered at 5,000 watts by day. At night, to protect other stations on AM 550, KFYI reduces power to 1,000 watts. It uses a non-directional antenna. The transmitter is on South 36th Street near East Vinyard Road in Phoenix. KFYI transmits in analog AM. Programming is also heard on an HD Radio subchannel of co-owned 95.5 KYOT-HD2.
