WZRK (1550 AM) was a radio station licensed to Lake Geneva, Wisconsin, United States. Its studio and transmitter were located east of Lake Geneva. The station ran 1,000 watts during daytime hours, with a two tower array and a directional pattern to the east and west. Prior to being taken off the air, nighttime operations were added, running 1 watt.
