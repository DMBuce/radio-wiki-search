WZRX-FM (107.5 FM) is a commercial radio station broadcasting a mainstream rock format. Licensed to Fort Shawnee, Ohio, United States, the station serves the Lima area. The station is currently owned by iHeartMedia, Inc.. Its studios and offices are located on West Market Street in Lima, Ohio, with its transmitter located in Fort Shawnee.
