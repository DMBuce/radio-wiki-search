WDOV (1410 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a News/Talk format. Licensed to Dover, Delaware, United States, the station serves the Dover area. The station is currently owned by iHeartMedia, Inc. and features programming from Fox News Radio and Premiere Radio Networks.

WDOV's programming is typical of that of most iHeartMedia, Inc. news talk stations, with Glenn Beck, Rush Limbaugh, Sean Hannity, and Coast to Coast AM. Its morning show is simulcast on WILM in Wilmington.
