WDOS (730 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a Sports and News/talk format. Licensed to Oneonta, New York, United States, the station is currently owned by Townsquare Media. It features local programming from 6 am to 10 am weekdays followed by Glenn Beck and Fox Sports Radio .
