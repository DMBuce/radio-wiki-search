KFOW (1170 AM) is a news/talk radio station licensed to serve Waseca, Minnesota. The station serves the Owatonna-Waseca area. The station is owned by Linder Radio Group.

On October 7, 2020 KFOW dropped its classic hits format and began stunting with "Tie Me Kangaroo Down, Sport" by Rolf Harris, a common stunt on stations owned by Linder switching formats.

On October 12, 2020 KFOW stopped stunting and switched formats to news/talk, and is now affiliated with Fox News Radio.
