DXOC (1494 AM) Radyo Pilipino is a radio station owned and operated by the Radio Corporation of the Philippines. The station's studio is located at Purok 3, Brgy. Manabay, Ozamiz. DXOC is one of the few stations of RadioCorp affiliated with Radio Mindanao Network.
