DXOO (97.5 FM) is a news and talk FM radio station owned by Nation Broadcasting Corporation and operated by TV5 Network Inc., serving as a relay of its flagship station in Manila. Its studios and transmitter are located in PLDT Building, Beatiles St., General Santos City.
