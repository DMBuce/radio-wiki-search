WSSM (104.9 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a talk/classic hits format licensed to Prentiss, Mississippi, United States. The station is currently owned by Sunbelt Broadcasting Corporation.

On July 1, 2017, the then-WCJU-FM changed their format from oldies to talk/classic hits.
