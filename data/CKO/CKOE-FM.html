CKOE-FM is a Canadian FM radio station, broadcasting a Contemporary Christian radio format at 107.3 MHz in Moncton, New Brunswick. The station is branded as CKO-1, and should not be confused with Canada's defunct CKO radio news network. The station started as CKOE (Christ, King Of Everything) and later dropped the "E" to appeal to a wider, non-Christian audience.
