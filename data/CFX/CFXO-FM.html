CFXO-FM is a Canadian radio station that broadcasts a country music format at 99.7 FM in High River, Alberta. The station is branded as SUN Country 99.7 and is owned by Golden West Broadcasting.

The station began broadcasting on October 30, 2007 at 7:00 AM Mountain Time.
