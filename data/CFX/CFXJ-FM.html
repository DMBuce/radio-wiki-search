CFXJ-FM (93.5 FM, Flow 93.5) is a radio station in Toronto, Ontario. Owned by Stingray Group, it broadcasts an urban contemporary format, and was Canada's first station to adopt the format upon its launch in 2001.

CFXJ's studios are located at Yonge and St. Clair in Toronto's Deer Park neighbourhood, while its transmitter is located at the top of First Canadian Place in Toronto's Financial District.
