WQVE (101.7 FM, "V 101.7") is a radio station serving Albany, Georgia, United States, and surrounding cities with an urban adult contemporary music format. This station is under ownership of Cumulus Media and the broadcast license is held by Cumulus Licensing, LLC.

On April 30, 2020, Cumulus Media sold its entire Albany cluster for First Media Services for $450,000.
