WQVN 1360 is an American AM radio station, licensed by the FCC to operate on the frequency of 1360 kHz, to serve the South Florida area of Miami and Fort Lauderdale. As of 2019, the station broadcasts its programming in the Haitian creole language.

The station is authorized to operate at 9,300 watts during the day and 400 watts at night. The station's FM translator (W272DS) is authorized for 250 watts on the 102.3 FM frequency.
