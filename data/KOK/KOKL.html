KOKL (1240 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a Classic hits format, licensed to Okmulgee, Oklahoma. The station is owned by Third Day Broadcasting Inc. KOKL's first license, as KHBG, was granted on November 9, 1937. In 1959 the call letters were changed to KOKL.
