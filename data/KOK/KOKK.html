KOKK (1210 AM, "Dakota Country") is a radio station licensed to serve Huron, South Dakota. The station is owned by Dakota Communications, Ltd. It airs a full-service Country music format. It has changed its frequency since signing on the air. It was previously at 1190 AM.

The station was assigned these call letters by the Federal Communications Commission.
