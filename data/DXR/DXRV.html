DXRV-FM (103.5 FM) - operating as Barangay FM 103.5 - is an FM station of Radio GMA Network Inc. (RGMA, a subsidiary of GMA Network Inc.) in the Philippines. The station's studio and transmitter are located at GMA Network Complex, Shrine Hills, Matina, Davao City.
