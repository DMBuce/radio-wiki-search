DXRR (94.3 FM), broadcasting as Radyo Serbisyo 94.3, is an commercial FM station operating in Nasipit, Agusan del Norte in the Philippines. The station's is located at the 2nd Floor VHAO Building, along Jose Rizal Street, Nasipit, Agusan del Norte, and its transmitter is located at Brgy 3, Nasipit, Agusan del Norte, Philippines. The station broadcasts at 4:00am 12:00mn.

Radyo Serbisyo 94.3 Nasipit is clearly heard within throughout Nasipit and some barangays of Buenavista and Carmen
