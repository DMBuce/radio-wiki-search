DXRA (783Â kHz Davao City) Radyo ni Juan is an AM station of Rizal Memorial Colleges Broadcasting Corporation in the Philippines. The station's studio is located at Door 1C, Anda Corporate Center F. Inigo St. Davao City. The main format of the station is public service and news.

The station has always been critical of the left and their violent atrocities against the Filipino people. On August 27, 1987, DXRA was attacked by the New People's Army, in what became known as the DXRA Massacre, resulting in nine deaths.
