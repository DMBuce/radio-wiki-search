DXRP (675 AM) Radyo Pilipinas is a radio station owned and operated by Philippine Broadcasting Service in the Philippines. Its studios is located at Braveheart Building, Gen. Douglas MacArthur Highway, Matina, Davao City and transmitter is located at Shrine Hills, Matina, Davao City.

From February to August 2018, following the preparation of the Mindanao Broadcast Hub, DXRP was temporarily broadcasting on FM at 87.5 MHz while currently looking for a new transmitter location for its main frequency on its AM counterpart. However, the station moved to 88.7 MHz on October 22 after 87.5 FM1 Davao was launched on August 1 at 1pm.
