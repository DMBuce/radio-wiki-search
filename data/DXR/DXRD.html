DXRD (711 AM) Sonshine Radio is the flagship radio station of Sonshine Media Network International. The station's studio is located at the Kingdom of Jesus Christ compound, Philippine-Japan Friendship Highway, Catitipan, Davao City (which is the former home of sister station, DXAQ).
