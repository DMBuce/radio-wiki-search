CJED-FM is a radio station in Niagara Falls, Ontario, Canada and targeting the Greater Niagara Falls area, including the Niagara Regional Municipality in Ontario and Niagara County in New York. Broadcasting at 105.1 FM, the station operates a hot adult contemporary format branded as "The River". CJED's studios are located on Ontario Avenue in Niagara Falls. Initially the transmitter was located on the Skylon Tower next to Niagara Falls, but it was later moved to a new site in Thorold.
