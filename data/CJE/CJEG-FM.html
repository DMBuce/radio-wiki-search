CJEG-FM is a Canadian radio station that broadcasts a CHR/Top 40 format at 101.3 FM in Bonnyville, Alberta, branded as Hot 101.3. The station was owned by Newcap Broadcasting until they were bought out by Stingray Digital.

The station was licensed by the CRTC on March 10, 2006. Testing began on May 5 and the station officially began broadcasting two weeks later (May 19) as Kool 101.3.

On October 7, 2019, CJEG-FM was rebranded as Hot 101.3 but kept the same format.
