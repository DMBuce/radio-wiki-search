CKTP-FM is a Canadian radio station in Fredericton, New Brunswick which goes by the name of "Rock 'n' Blues 95.7 The Wolf". The station broadcasts a mix of roots rock and blues from its studios on the St. Mary's First Nation, at an FM frequency of 95.7 MHz.
