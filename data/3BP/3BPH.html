3BPH is a radio station based in Bendigo, Victoria. It is part of the Vision Australia Radio network, a reading and information service for those persons unable to read or easily access information in print. The station is run and operated by volunteers.

When not broadcasting local programs, the station is a relay of 3RPH in Melbourne.
