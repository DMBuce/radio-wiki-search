WBXB (100.1 FM The-B 100.1) is a radio station broadcasting an Urban AC format. Licensed to Edenton, North Carolina, United States, it serving in Elizabeth City, Ahoskie, Windsor, Murfreesboro area. The station is currently owned by Edenton Christian Radio.
