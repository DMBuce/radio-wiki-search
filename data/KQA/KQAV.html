KQAV (93.5 FM, "Old School 93.5") is a radio station that is licensed to Rosamond, California and serves the Antelope Valley area. The station is owned by High Desert Broadcasting Ltd., a subsidiary of Ventura, California-based Point Broadcasting Ltd., and broadcasts a rhythmic oldies format.
