KQAZ (101.7 FM, "Majik 101.7") is a radio station licensed to serve Springerville, Arizona, United States. The station is owned by William and Mary Ann Konopnicki through licensee WSK Family Credit Shelter Trust UTA It airs a Soft Adult Contemporary music format.

The station was assigned the KQAZ call letters by the Federal Communications Commission on February 6, 1984.
