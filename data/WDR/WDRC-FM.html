WDRC-FM, known as 102.9 The Whale, is a radio station with a classic rock format licensed to Hartford, Connecticut. The station began broadcasting in 1959 and was the first commercial FM station in the Hartford radio market. The station is owned by John Fuller's Red Wolf Broadcasting Corporation, with studios are located on Blue Hills Avenue in Bloomfield, Connecticut with other radio stations and transmitter site in Meriden, Connecticut.
