WMFR (1230 AM; "Triad Sports Network") is a radio station broadcasting a sports radio format. Licensed to High Point, North Carolina, United States, the station serves the Piedmont Triad area. The station is currently owned by Curtis Media Group and features programing from ESPN Radio.
