VOKFM (Voice of Kinabalu FM) is a radio station from Sabah, Malaysia. The station features local topics by promoting local cultures to younger listeners. Their offers a More Music, Less Talk concept with a local touch content. It was launched on 16 September 2017 during the celebration of Malaysia Day.
