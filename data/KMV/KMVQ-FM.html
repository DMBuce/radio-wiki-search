KMVQ-FM (99.7 FM) is a radio station licensed to San Francisco, California. The station currently broadcasts a Top 40/CHR format branded as 99.7 Now. KMVQ's studios are located at 3rd and Howard in downtown San Francisco, while its transmitter is located on San Bruno Mountain.

KMVQ-FM is owned by Bonneville International. KMVQ directly competes with crosstown Top 40s KYLD and KREV. Other competing stations include heritage Urban KMEL and Rhythmic AC KRBQ.

KMVQ broadcasts in the HD Radio format.
