KMVA (Hot 97.5/103.9 FM) is an Adult Top 40 radio station in the Phoenix, Arizona, area. The station is licensed to Dewey-Humboldt and is owned by Riviera Broadcasting, LLC. KMVA forms a full-time simulcast with KZON in Gilbert in order to cover the entire Phoenix metropolitan area.
