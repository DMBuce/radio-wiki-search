KZLR (98.3 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a Spanish Christian radio format. Licensed to Odem, Texas, United States, the station serves the Corpus Christi area. The station is owned by Educational Media Foundation and broadcasts the Radio Nueva Vida network. The transmitter is located on the city's west side.
