DXQR (93.5 FM), broadcasting as 935 Home Radio, is a radio station owned and operated by Aliw Broadcasting Corporation. Its studios and transmitters are located along Don Apolinar Velez St. cor. Hayes St., Cagayan de Oro. It operates daily from 5:00 AM to 10:00 PM.
