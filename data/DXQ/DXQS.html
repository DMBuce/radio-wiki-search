DXQS (98.3 FM), broadcasting as 983 Home Radio, is a radio station owned and operated by the Insular Broadcasting Systems Inc. Its studios and transmitters are located at Atasha Hotel & Dormitory, Sampaguita St., General Santos City.

In the last quarter of 2015 the station was in low power after the transfer of its studios and transmitters and some equipment upgrades. On July 2016, the station started to operate in full power with better signal and audio quality.

Home Radio GenSan airs Ang Dating Daan every night from 9:00 PM to 10:00 PM.

Temporarily, it operates from 6 am to 9 pm due to the Community Quarantine brought by the COVID-19 Pandemic.
