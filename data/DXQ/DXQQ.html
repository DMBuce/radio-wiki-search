DXQQ (87.5 FM), broadcasting as 87.5 Davao City Disaster Radio, is a 24-hour community radio station owned and operated by the City Government of Davao. Its studios are located at RJ Homes Bldg., Pelayo St., Davao City, and its transmitter is located at Shrine Hills, Matina, Davao City.

The frequency was occupied by FM1 Davao until January 2020.
