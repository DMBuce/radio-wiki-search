WHMJ (99.3 FM) and WXMJ (104.5 FM) are a Top 40 music formatted radio station combo broadcasting in northwest Pennsylvania, United States. The station is owned by Forever Broadcasting, and broadcasts from the Downtown Mall in Meadville.
