The Breeze was a radio station broadcasting to the towns of Warminster, Westbury and Trowbridge in West Wiltshire and Frome in East Somerset, England, on 107.5MHz FM and is owned by Bauer Radio and is part of The Breeze network of stations. The station merged with another local station The Breeze Bath to fom Greatest Hits Radio Bath & The South West on 1 September 2020.
