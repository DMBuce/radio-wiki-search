The Breeze (formerly Quay West and Total Star Somerset) is a radio station which is broadcasting on 100.8, 102.4, and 107.4 in the Sedgemoor District, Bridgwater and West Somerset, England, and is owned by Bauer Radio and is part of The Breeze network of stations. The station will re-brand as Greatest Hits Radio on 1 September 2020 with the end of all local shows apart from a regional Drive show 4-7pm.
