theJazz was a British jazz digital radio station run by GCap Media that started on Christmas Day of 2006 on Digital One, DAB ensemble, Sky Digital, Virgin Media, and the internet.

The station played blues and traditional and modern jazz and stopped broadcasting on 31 March 2008 on DAB. The station was a generic automated two-hour loop on Sky, Virgin, and onlne without disc jockeys. This was closed down and removed from Sky and Virgin on 3 November. Chill started on Digital TV using theJazz's old channel numbers on both Sky and Virgin.
