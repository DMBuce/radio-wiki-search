The Breeze (formerly Kick FM and Newbury Sound) is an Independent Local Radio station serving Newbury and West Berkshire. The station is owned & operated by Bauer Radio and is part of The Breeze network of stations. It broadcasts from studios at Eastgate House in Andover.
