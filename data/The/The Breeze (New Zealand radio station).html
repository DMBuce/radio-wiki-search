The Breeze is a New Zealand radio station playing an adult contemporary music format owned by MediaWorks New Zealand, playing a variety of music from the 70s, 80s, 90s and also current hits. The Breeze network broadcasts to 21 markets throughout New Zealand, with each station presenting a mixture of local and network programming. From 2017 through to now, The Breeze had phenomenal ratings success becoming New Zealand's number one music station a rise from 5th in 2016 and in 2020 reaching its highest ever audience in excess of 622,000 listeners. Breeze TV is a music TV channel. It was launched in April 2020 as a replacement of ThreeLife + 1. The channel is very similar to sister channel The Edge TV as they are both run as 24-hour jukebox channels with advertisements. The only show currently played on Breeze TV is Take it Easy, named after the stations slogan. Ownership was transferred to Discovery, Inc. on 1 December 2020, as part of MediaWorks' sale of the TV side of its business.
