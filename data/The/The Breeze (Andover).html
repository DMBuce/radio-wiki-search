The Breeze (formerly Andover Sound) is an independent local radio station serving Andover & North West Hampshire and parts of East Wiltshire. The station is owned & operated by Bauer Radio and is part of The Breeze network of stations. It broadcasts from studios at Eastgate House in Andover.
