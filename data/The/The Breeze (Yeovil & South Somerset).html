The Breeze (formerly Ivel FM and Midwest Radio) was a United Kingdom radio station that broadcast on 105.6 & 106.6 FM to Yeovil, South Somerset and West Dorset and was part of The Breeze network of stations. The station re-branded as Greatest Hits Radio Somerset on 1 September 2020 with the end of all local shows apart from a regional Drive show 4-7pm.
