Wave 91.7 FM, is a mixed news, talk, and music radio station based in Port Harcourt, Nigeria. The station began operating on a regular basis on 1 September 2011. It is currently owned by South Atlantic Media and broadcasts from studios in Forces Avenue, Old GRA at the frequency of 91.7 on the FM band.
