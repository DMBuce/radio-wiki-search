91.9 Sarthak FM has two FM radio stations broadcast from Bhubaneswar & Rourkela. This channel provides 24x7 Odia language and music. It is part of Sitaram Agarwal & Namita Agarwal's Sarthak Music, Sarthak Films and Sidharth TV Network group.

Enriched with a vast library of Odia Songs, the station was launched on 29 April 2017.

91.9 Sarthak FM has also aired live cricket commentary for the first time during IPL2019 & ICC CWC 2019 (India match day, semifinals & Final) as well as live commentary of Ratha Jatra event.

The first independent song of 91.9 Sarthak FM is Niswasa To Bina Mora Chalena.
