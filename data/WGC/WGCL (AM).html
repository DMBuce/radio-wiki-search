WGCL (1370 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a News Talk Information format to the Bloomington, Indiana, United States, area. The station is owned by Sarkes Tarzian, Inc. along with sister station WTTS (92.3 FM), and features programming from ABC Radio, Premiere Radio Networks and Westwood One.

WGCL is not related to WGCL-TV, a CBS-affiliated television station in Atlanta, Georgia, and owned by Meredith Corporation.
