XHUTX-FM is a radio station in Tlaxcala, Tlaxcala. Broadcasting on 99.5 MHz from a transmitter on Cerro Ostol, XHUTX is owned by the Universidad Autónoma de Tlaxcala and broadcasts a university radio format known as Radio Universidad.
