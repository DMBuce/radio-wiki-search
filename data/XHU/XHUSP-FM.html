XHUSP-FM is a radio station in San Luis Potosí, San Luis Potosí. It broadcasts on 88.5 MHz and is one of two radio stations owned by the Universidad Autónoma de San Luis Potosí, known as Radio Universidad 88.5 FM.

XHUSP broadcasts in HD.
