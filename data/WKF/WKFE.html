WKFE (1550 AM, "Radio Café 1550") is a radio station licensed to serve Yauco, Puerto Rico. The station is owned by Media Power Group, Inc. WKFE is part of the Radio Isla Network. It airs a Spanish language News Talk Information format.

The station was assigned the WKFE call letters by the Federal Communications Commission.
