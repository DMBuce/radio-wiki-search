WKFT (101.3 FM, "Bigfoot Country 102.1 & 101.3") is a radio station licensed to Strattanville, Pennsylvania. The station broadcasts a country music format with sister station WIFT and is owned by Kristin Cantrell, through licensee Southern Belle, LLC.
