KDHT is a commercial radio station in Rohnert Park, California, broadcasting to the Santa Rosa, California, area on 104.9 FM.

KDHT airs an 80s-based classic hits music format branded as K-Hits 104.9. This station as well as the other Maverick Media of Santa Rosa stations has been purchased by Lawrence Amaturo, through licensee Amaturo Sonoma Media Group, LLC.
