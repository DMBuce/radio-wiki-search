KDHK (100.5 FM) is a mainstream rock radio station in Decorah, Iowa. The station provides local news that covers the Tri-State area of northeastern Iowa, southeastern Minnesota and southwestern Wisconsin. Hawk Rawk's on-air staff consists of Pete and Rachel in the morning, And Demitre Ellis in the Afternoon. KDHK 100.5 is also the home for the Iowa Hawkeyes, broadcasting Iowa Hawkeyes football and basketball.
