ExmouthAiR Radio (Exmouth UK) is a community radio station, based in Exmouth, Devon, United Kingdom.

The station was launched on 11 September 2006 as Bay FM Radio with a month-long trial FM broadcast, followed by a continuing series of annual trial FM broadcasts in the Exmouth area, coinciding with the Exmouth festival.

On 12 June 2010, Bay FM began a regular weekend online service. This was increased to cover all weekday evenings. The station’s online reach is worldwide. For the week 10–18 September 2011, the station's online coverage was extended with full daytime and evening shows. The extended coverage coincided with the Tour of Britain, the fifth stage of which finished in Exmouth on Thursday 15 September.

In April 2012, Bay FM were given the news by OFCOM that their application for a full-time community FM licence had been successful. The station was launched full-time at mid-day on 29 March 2013 on 106.4 MHz FM. The coverage of the station increased dramatically, covering a wider area that includes Budleigh Salterton, Otterton, Woodbury, Exminster, Kenton, Dawlish and Lympstone. The station also reaches areas of Pennsylvania, Stoke Hill, Pinhoe, Sowton, Middlemoor, Digby and Countess Wear in Exeter on 94.6 FM as well as Sidmouth and the Sid Valley on 95.0 FM.
