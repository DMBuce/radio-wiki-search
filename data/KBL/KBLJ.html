KBLJ (1400 AM) is a radio station licensed to La Junta, Colorado, United States. The station is owned by Aaron Leiker, through licensee 25-7 Media, Inc.

It first aired on 1370 kHz as KOKO in 1937. It moved to 1400 kHz in 1941 as a result of the NARBA agreement.
