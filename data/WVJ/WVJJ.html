WVJJ (1510 AM) is a commercial AM radio station licensed to Salem, New Jersey, and serving the southern part of Greater Philadelphia, including Wilmington, Delaware. It broadcasts an urban adult contemporary radio format. WVJJ is owned and operated by Forever Media.

The station is heard around the clock on its FM translator in Wilmington, W245CJ, broadcasting at 96.9 FM.
