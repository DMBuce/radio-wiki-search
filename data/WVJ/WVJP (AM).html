WVJP (1110 AM, VJP 1110) is a radio station licensed to serve Caguas, Puerto Rico. The station is owned by Borinquen Broadcasting Company. It airs a Talk/Personality format, and also simulcasts the Dimensión 103 network.

The station was assigned the WVJP call letters by the Federal Communications Commission in 1947.
