KMLK (98.7 FM, "The Heart and Soul") is an American radio station airing an urban adult contemporary format licensed to El Dorado, Arkansas, broadcasting on 98.7 MHz FM. The station is owned by Noalmark Broadcasting Corporation.

On July 13, 2017, the then-KLBQ and its country format moved to 101.5 FM Junction City, swapping call signs and frequencies with urban adult contemporary-formatted KMLK.
