KEGA, branded as 101.5 The Eagle is a country music radio station broadcasting to the Salt Lake City metropolitan area. Licensed to Oakley, Utah, United States, the station is currently owned by Dell Loy Hansen, through licensee Broadway Media LS, LLC of Salt Lake City. The station's studios are located in Downtown Salt Lake City and its transmitter site is located on Humpy Peak, 50 miles east.
