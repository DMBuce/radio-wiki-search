KEGL (97.1 FM, "97-1 The Eagle") is a radio station licensed to Fort Worth, Texas, United States. The station broadcasts a Mainstream Rock music format to the Dallas/Fort Worth Metroplex. KEGL is owned and operated by iHeartMedia. The station's studios are located along Dallas Parkway in Farmers Branch (although it has a Dallas address), and the transmitter site is in Cedar Hill.
