KLGE (94.1 FM) is a radio station in Hydesville, California, serving the Eureka area. KLGE broadcasts an Adult Standards format and is owned by Lost Coast Communications, Inc. KLGE brands itself as "94.1 - The Lounge". Larry Trask (formally of KHUM) is the Program Director/Music Director of KLGE.
