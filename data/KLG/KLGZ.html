KLGZ (1600 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a country music format. Licensed to serve Algona, Iowa, United States, the station is owned by Riverfront Broadcasting of Iowa, LLC - a subsidiary of Riverfront Broadcasting LLC of Yankton, South Dakota, and features programming from ABC Radio. Prior to Country music, KLGZ had an Adult Contemporary format, simulcasting its sister station, KLGA-FM. KLGZ began broadcasting in 1956 as KLGA, a daytime-only station, and added nighttime power at a later date.

KLGA changed its call sign to the current KLGZ on July 15, 2014.
