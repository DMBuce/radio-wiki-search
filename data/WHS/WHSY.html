WHSY (950 AM, "Sports Newstalk 950") is a radio station broadcasting a Sports and news/talk/information format. Licensed to Hattiesburg, Mississippi, United States, the station serves the Hattiesburg-Laurel area. The station is currently owned by Southern Air Communications, Inc.
