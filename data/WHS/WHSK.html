WHSK (91.1 FM, "The Revolution") is a college radio station licensed to serve the community of Bloomsburg, Pennsylvania. The station is owned by Bloomsburg University of Pennsylvania, and airs a variety format.

The station was assigned the call sign WBUQ by the Federal Communications Commission on October 11, 1984. It changed the call sign to WHSK on July 3, 2019.
