WHSR (980 AM) is a radio station that is currently silent. Licensed to Pompano Beach, Florida, United States, the station is owned by the Beasley Broadcast Group, Inc., through licensee Beasley Media Group, LLC. Its studios are in Boca Raton and the transmitter was in Parkland.
