WWTP (89.5 FM) is a Catholic talk radio station licensed to serve Augusta, Maine, United States. The station is an affiliate of Relevant Radio and is owned by Immaculate Heart Media, Inc. The station signed on in April 2012 as a simulcast of WXTP in Portland. The station is also heard in the Bangor area on WXBP.
