WWTG is a Christian radio station licensed to Carpentersville, Illinois, broadcasting on 88.1 MHz FM. The station serves Northwest suburban Chicago, Northern Kane County, and Southern McHenry County. WWTG is an affiliate of LifeTalk Radio and is owned by Cary Grove Adventist Fellowship. It began broadcasting in early 2012.

WWTG's programming includes Christian talk and teaching shows such as Back to Genesis by the Institute for Creation Research, Family Life Today with Dennis Rainey and Bob Lepine, Joni & Friends, and Unshackled!. WWTG also airs a variety of Christian music, as well as children's programs, such as Adventures in Odyssey.
