WWTK is a commercial radio station in Lake Placid, Florida, broadcasting to the Sebring area on 730 AM. WWTK's format is News/Talk, and they run a slate of conservative commentators including The Rush Limbaugh Show, The Sean Hannity Show, Doctor Joy, Jerry Doyle and Jim Bohannon. The late night program is Coast to Coast AM. Sundays are dominated by religious programming. The station fares moderately well in the Sebring radio market, pulling a relatively stable 5.2 share, placing them sixth in the market.
