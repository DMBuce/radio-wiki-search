WNHG 89.7 FM radio is a radio station in Grand Rapids, Michigan, broadcasting a Christian radio programming format as a simulcast of WGCP 91.9 FM in Cadillac, MI. Both WGCP and WNHG are owned and operated by West Central Michigan Media Ministries.
