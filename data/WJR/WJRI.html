WJRI (1340 AM 100.5 FM) is a radio station broadcasting an oldies format. Licensed to Lenoir, North Carolina, United States, the station is currently owned by Foothills Radio Group.

On May 29, 2017, WJRI changed their format from news/talk to an oldies format branded as "Just Right Radio."
