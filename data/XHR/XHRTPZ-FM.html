XHRTPZ-FM is a community radio station in Tepoztlán, Morelos, broadcasting on 92.3 FM. It is owned by Teponaztle Cultura y Comunicación, A.C. and brands as Radio Tepoztlán. XHRTPZ received its permit in February 2010.
