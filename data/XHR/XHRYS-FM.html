XHRYS-FM (branded as Hits FM) is a Spanish-language FM radio station that serves the Reynosa, Tamaulipas (Mexico) / McAllen, Texas (USA) / Rio Grande Valley border area. It broadcasts from the Multimedios tower at El Control, Tamaulipas.
