XHRAF-FM is a noncommercial radio station on 98.3 FM in Rafael Delgado, Veracruz. It is owned by Cultura es lo Nuestro, A.C. and known as Radio Banana.

XHRAF is simulcast on XHCAY-FM 102.9 in Acayucan.
