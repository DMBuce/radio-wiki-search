XHRCG-FM is a radio station in Ciudad Acuña, broadcasting on 105.1 FM and carrying a Regional Mexican format known as La Banda de la 105.1 FM. It is owned by Saltillo-based Grupo RCG; the group's two Ciudad Acuña radio stations, XHRCG and XHRG-FM 95.5 are operated by RCommunications. The US company is owned by Roberto G. González, the son of Roberto Casimiro González, founder of Grupo RCG.
