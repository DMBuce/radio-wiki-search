DXXM (97.7 MHz) is a Philippine FM radio station owned by Subic Broadcasting Corporation and operated by People's Radio And Broadcasting Services. Its studios are located at Ground Floor, Door 18, Bougainvilla St., Brgy. Ising, Carmen, Davao del Norte, and its transmitter is located at Mawab, Compostela Valley.
