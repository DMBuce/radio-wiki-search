KEMR (1090 AM) is a radio station licensed to serve Milan, New Mexico, United States. The station is owned by the Isleta Radio Company.

KEMR broadcasts a Christian radio format.

The station was assigned the KQNM call sign by the Federal Communications Commission on May 18, 2009. On September 22, 2015, the KQNM call letters were moved to 1550 in Albuquerque with 1090 picking up the KRKE callsign previously held by that station. On November 1, 2017, the KRKE call letters were transferred to 101.3 in Albuquerque with 1090 picking up the KSFE call sign.

According to the FCC database, the station has filed an application to move to 1080 kHz and move the station to Moriarty, New Mexico. A construction permit was granted by the FCC on September 20, 2017.

The station changed its call sign to KMYN on March 7, 2019, and then again to KEMR on April 9, 2019.
