KSGR (branded as "Solid Ground Radio 91.1") is an FM religious radio station that serves the Corpus Christi, Texas area. It is under ownership of Calvary Chapel of the Coastlands, Inc. "Solid Ground" is a reference to the Biblical Parable of the two builders.
