DZWI, branded as Power 108 FM, was an FM radio station in the Philippines which operated from 2001 to 2004. It was owned by Katigbak Enterprises (San Pablo City) under ConAmor Broadcasting System. The station was operated by 713 Productions, owned by Marcelle John Marcelino (aka DJ Htown) under lease agreement. Its studios are located in Mandaluyong City, Metro Manila, while its transmitter is located at Laurel, Batangas. The radio station took its name from the famous "Power 106" (KPWR) in Los Angeles, California. The flamboyant logo of Power 108 FM represented its love of the urban culture.
