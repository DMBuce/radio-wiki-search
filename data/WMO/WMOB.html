WMOB (1360 AM) is a radio station licensed to serve Mobile, Alabama, United States. The station, founded in 1961 as WLIQ, is owned by Buddy Tucker Association, Inc.

WMOB broadcasts a religious format to the Mobile metropolitan area. WMOB's broadcast schedule consists of a mix of local, brokered, and syndicated Christian ministry and inspirational programming.
