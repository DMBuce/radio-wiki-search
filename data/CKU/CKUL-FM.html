CKUL-FM (96.5 MHz, 96.5 The Breeze) is a radio station serving Halifax, Nova Scotia. Owned by Stingray Group, it broadcasts a soft adult contemporary format. CKUL's studios and offices are located on Kempt Road in Halifax, while its transmitter is located on Washmill Lake Drive in Clayton Park.

Based on the current format, CKUL-FM competes with CKHY-FM.
