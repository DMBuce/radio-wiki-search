WPCO (1230 AM, "94.9 The Palm") is a radio station in Columbia, South Carolina. Owned by Alpha Media, it broadcasts an adult album alternative (AAA) format. Its studios are on Pineview Road in Columbia, while the transmitter tower is located near Bicentennial Park along the Congaree River in downtown Columbia. The station operates an FM translator, W235CH on 94.9 in St. Matthews, South Carolina.
