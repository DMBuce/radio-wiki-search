WPCK (104.9 FM) is a radio station licensed to Denmark, Wisconsin and serving Green Bay. The station is owned and operated by Educational Media Foundation. WPCK's studios are located on Victoria Street in Green Bay, while its transmitter is located off Finger Road in Montpelier.
