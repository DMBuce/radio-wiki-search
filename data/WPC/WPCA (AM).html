WPCA (800 AM) is a radio station broadcasting an Active Rock format as "100.7 the Foundry". Licensed to Waupaca, Wisconsin, United States, the station serves the Wausau-Stevens Point area. The station is owned by Donald Grassman and Keith Bratel, through licensee Tower Road Media.
