WRTU (89.7 FM) is a National Public Radio member station broadcasting a variety format, together with programming from NPR, PRI, the BBC and other distributors. Licensed to San Juan, Puerto Rico, the station is currently owned by the University of Puerto Rico.

WRTU's programming can also be heard in Mayagüez on WRUO 88.3 FM.
