WRTW is a Christian radio station licensed to Crown Point, Indiana, broadcasting on 90.5 MHz FM. It is owned by Hyles Anderson College.

WRTW airs traditional Christian music and locally produced Christian talk and teaching programs, as well as national programs such as Thru the Bible with J. Vernon McGee, Family Altar with Lester Roloff, and Unshackled!

The Key is also heard in East-Central Illinois on 90.5 WRTK in Paxton, Illinois.
