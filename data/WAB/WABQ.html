WABQ (1460 kHz) branded 1460 AM WABQ, is a commercial AM radio station licensed to Painesville, Ohio, serving Lake County and eastern parts of Greater Cleveland. It airs an urban gospel radio format and is owned by Radio Advantage One, Inc.

WABQ is a local affiliate for the Cleveland Cavaliers Radio Network and the Ohio State Sports Network. The studios and transmitter tower for WABQ are located off of One Radio Place on the northeastern end of Painesville.
