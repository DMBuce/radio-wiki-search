KXEO (1340 AM, "1340 Mexico's Radio") is a radio station broadcasting an adult contemporary music format. Licensed to Mexico, Missouri, United States, the station serves the Columbia, Missouri area. The station was first licensed February 8, 1949. The station is currently owned by KXEO Radio, Inc. and features programming from CBS Radio.
