KXEA (104.9 FM, "Rock 104.9") is an American radio station licensed to serve Lowry City, Missouri, United States. The station is owned by Radford Media Group and the broadcast license is held by Radford Media Group LLC.

KXEA broadcasts a classic rock format to the west-central Missouri area.

The station was assigned the call sign "KXEA" by the Federal Communications Commission (FCC) on February 25, 2010.

On December 30, 2018, KXEA rebranded as "Rock 104.9".
