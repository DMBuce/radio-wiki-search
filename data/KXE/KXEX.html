KXEX (1550 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a talk format branded as "TalkRadio 1550". Licensed to Fresno, California, United States, the station serves the Fresno area. The station is currently owned by Compass Broadcasting, Inc.

On May 1, 2020 KXEX changed their format from Spanish religious to talk, branded as "TalkRadio 1550".
