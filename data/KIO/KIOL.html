KIOL (1370 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a News/Talk format. The station's previous call letters were KALN, prior to August 12, 2008. Licensed to Iola, Kansas, United States, the station serves Southeast Kansas. The station is currently owned by Iola Broadcasting, Inc. and features programming from Fox News Radio, Premiere Radio Networks and Talk Radio Network.
