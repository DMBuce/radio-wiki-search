KIOA (93.3 MHz) is a commercial FM radio station in Des Moines, Iowa. It is owned by Saga Communications and airs a Classic Hits radio format. The station's studios are located at 1416 Locust Street along with Saga's other Des Moines stations (KSTZ, KAZR, KOEZ, KRNT and KPSZ). The transmitter is located off 24th Street SE, near 6th Avenue SE, in Altoona.

KIOA broadcasts in the HD Radio hybrid format. Its HD-2 subchannel carries a Rhythmic Contemporary format branded as "Hits 99.9." It is also heard on 250–watt FM translator station 99.9 K260AM in Des Moines.
