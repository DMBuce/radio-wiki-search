KANJ (91.1 FM) is a non-commercial educational radio station licensed to serve Giddings, Texas, United States. The station, established in 1998, is currently owned by Houston Christian Broadcasters, Inc.

KANJ broadcasts a Christian radio format. The station's programming is a simulcast of KHCB-FM in Houston, Texas, as part of the KHCB Radio Network.
