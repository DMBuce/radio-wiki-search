WZCT (1330 AM, "Southern Gospel Z-13") is a radio station licensed to serve Scottsboro, Alabama. The station is owned by Bonner and Carlile Enterprises. It airs a Southern Gospel music format.

The station was assigned the WZCT call letters by the Federal Communications Commission on March 5, 1990.
