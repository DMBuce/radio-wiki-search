WZCH (102.5 FM) is a Christian radio station that is an affiliate of K-LOVE. The station is owned by Educational Media Foundation. WZCH was a simulcast of sister station WPCH (96.5 FM). This station was assigned the WZCH call letters by the Federal Communications Commission on September 17, 2008.
