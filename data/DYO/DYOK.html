DYOK (720 AM) Aksyon Radyo is a radio station owned and operated by Pacific Broadcasting Systems, an affiliate of Manila Broadcasting Company in Iloilo City, Philippines. Its studio is located at 3rd Floor, Carlos Uy Bldg., Benigno Aquino Ave. cor. Diversion Rd., Brgy. San Rafael, Mandurriao, Iloilo City, while its transmitter is located at Brgy. Nabitasan, La Paz, Iloilo City. This Station operates daily from 3:30AM to 12:00MN.
