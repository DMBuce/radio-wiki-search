KZHC (1230 AM) is a radio station licensed to serve Burns, Oregon, United States. The station, established in 1957, is owned by Randolph and Debra McKone, through licensee KJDY, LLC. KZHC and sister station KZHC-FM are the two of only four radio stations with Burns as their community of license.
