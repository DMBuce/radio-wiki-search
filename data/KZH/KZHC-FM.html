KZHC-FM (92.7 FM) is a radio station licensed to serve Burns, Oregon, United States. The station, which began broadcasting in 1997, is currently owned by Alexandra Communications, Inc. KZHC-FM and sister station KZHC are the only commercial radio stations with Burns as their community of license.
