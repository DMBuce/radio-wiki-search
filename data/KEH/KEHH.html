KEHH (92.3 FM; 89.3 KSBJ) is a terrestrial American radio station broadcasting a Christian Contemporary format in full pentacast with sister stations KSBJ Humble, KWUP Navasota, KHIH Liberty, and KXBJ El Campo. Licensed to Livingston, Texas, United States, the station serves the areas of Livingston, Huntsville, and Lufkin, Texas. The station is currently owned by the KSBJ Educational Foundation.
