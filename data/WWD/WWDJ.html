WWDJ (1150 AM) – branded Relevant Radio – is a non-commercial Catholic radio station licensed to Boston, Massachusetts. Owned by Immaculate Heart Media, Inc., the station serves Greater Boston. WWDJ doesn't broadcast any local programming, functioning as a repeater for the Relevant Radio network. WWDJ's studios and offices are located in the Boston suburb of Quincy and the station transmitter are located in nearby Lexington.
