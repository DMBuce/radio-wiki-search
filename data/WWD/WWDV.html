WWDV (96.9 FM) is a radio station in Zion, Illinois, known as "The Drive". The station is currently owned by Hubbard Broadcasting, and is a full-time simulcast of Chicago-licensed WDRV (97.1 FM), serving Chicago's northern suburbs and southeastern Wisconsin. "The Drive" programs a broad-based classic rock format.
