WHGL-FM (100.3 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a country music format. Licensed to Canton, Pennsylvania, United States, the station serves the Elmira-Corning, NY and Troy-Canton, PA areas. The station is currently owned by Cantroair Communications Inc. and features programming from CBS Radio. The station broadcasts from Canton, PA on 100.3-FM and from Elmira, NY on 102.9-FM.

WHGL has three sister stations: WTTC-FM, 95.3 "The Bridge," WTZN-AM and WTTC-AM, 1310 and 1550 "The Zone."
