WUSJ (96.3 FM, "US96.3") is a radio station licensed to the community of Madison, Mississippi, and serving the greater Jackson, Mississippi, area. The station is owned by Meridian, Mississippi-based The Radio People and licensed to New South Radio, Inc. It airs a country music format. Its studios are located in Ridgeland and the transmitter site is in Raymond.

The station was assigned the WUSJ call letters by the Federal Communications Commission on July 13, 1999.
