WUSL (98.9 MHz), branded Power 99 FM, is a commercial FM radio station licensed to Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. It carries an urban contemporary radio format and is owned by iHeartMedia, Inc. The studios and offices are on Presidential Boulevard in Bala Cynwyd.

WUSL is a Class B station with an effective radiated power (ERP) of 27,000 watts. Its transmitter is off Wygard near the Schuylkill River in the Roxborough section of Philadelphia. WUSL broadcasts in the HD Radio hybrid format. Its HD2 digital subchannel airs a classic hip-hop format and its HD3 subchannel plays Spanish-language contemporary hits.
