WUSH-HD2 is a sports formatted broadcast radio station licensed to Poquoson, Virginia, serving Hampton Roads. WUSH-HD2 is owned and operated by Sinclair Telecable, Inc.

The station is also heard over a translator which serves the Southside of Hampton Roads.

On June 26, 2017 WUSH-HD2 changed their format from mainstream rock to soft oldies, branded as "Vintage 96.5".

On September 15, 2017 WUSH-HD2 changed their format from soft oldies to a simulcast of sports-formatted WTAR 850 AM Norfolk.
