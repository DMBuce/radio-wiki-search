WKSF ("Kiss Country") is a country music station licensed to serve Old Fort, North Carolina. The iHeartMedia, Inc. station broadcasts from a tower on Mount Pisgah, southwest of Asheville at 99.9 FM with an ERP of 53,000 watts. The tower is shared with former sister station WLOS-TV (ABC) and WUNF-TV (PBS).
