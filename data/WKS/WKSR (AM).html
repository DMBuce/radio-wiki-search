WKSR (1420 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a classic hits format, Licensed to Pulaski, Tennessee, United States, the station is currently owned by Roger Wright through licensee Radio 7 Media, LLC, and features programming from Westwood One.

The Rocky 1420 branding was replaced by Oldies 1420 KSR as of spring 2010. On March 1, 2018, the station switched from a country music format to classic hits and adopted the branding "Classic KSR".
