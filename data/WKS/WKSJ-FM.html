WKSJ-FM (94.9 FM, "95 KSJ") is a radio station licensed to serve Mobile, Alabama, United States. The station is owned by iHeartMedia and the broadcast license is held by iHM Licenses, LLC. Its studios are located inside the building of unrelated television station WKRG-TV on Broadcast Drive in Mobile, and the transmitter is near Robertsdale, Alabama.

WKSJ-FM broadcasts a country music format to the Mobile metropolitan area.

Notable programs include The Bobby Bones Show on mornings, Johnna on middays, Bill & Shelby on afternoons, Wayne D on nights, and the syndicated CMT After Midnite with Cody Alan from Premiere Networks overnight.
