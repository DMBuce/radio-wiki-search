KRUZ (103.3 FM, "KRUZ 103.3") is a commercial radio station that is licensed to Santa Barbara, California and broadcasts a classic hits radio format throughout Santa Barbara, Ventura, and San Luis Obispo counties. The station is owned by Cumulus Media and has its studios in Ventura. KRUZ broadcasts with an effective radiated power (ERP) of 105,000 watts from a transmitter located atop Broadcast Peak north of Santa Barbara in the Santa Ynez Mountains at a height above average terrain (HAAT) of 906 meters (2969 feet). These factors give KRUZ one of the largest coverage areas of any FM station on the West Coast.

KRUZ broadcasts an analog signal only; it does not air in digital HD Radio or carry RDS information.
