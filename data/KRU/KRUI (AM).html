KRUI (1490 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a News Talk Information format. Licensed to Ruidoso Downs, New Mexico, United States, the station is currently owned by MTD Radio Inc. and features programming from Citadel Media, Premiere Radio Networks and Westwood One.
