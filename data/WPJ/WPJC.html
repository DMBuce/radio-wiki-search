WPJC (88.3 FM) is an EWTN-affiliated Catholic talk radio station licensed to Pontiac, Illinois, United States. The station is owned by 2820 Communications, Inc., and simulcasts its sister flagship station Catholic Spirit Radio WSPI from Normal, IL.
