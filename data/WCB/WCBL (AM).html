WCBL is an Oldies formatted radio station broadcasting on 1290 AM. Licensed to Benton, Kentucky, United States, the station is currently owned by Jim Freeland, through licensee Freeland Broadcasting.

A control booth for WCBL was used in the filming of the 1980 Loretta Lynn biography movie Coal Miner's Daughter.
