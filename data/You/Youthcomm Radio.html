106.7 Youthcomm Radio is a youth community radio station, licensed by Ofcom, broadcasting to Worcester, England, on 106.7FM. The station officially launched on FM on Sunday 13 January 2008 after running online for seven years.

Youthcomm was established in 1996.

Youthcomm broadcasts from studios in St Johns in Worcester.

Shows include 'Owen Lowe In The Morning', and 'Chris Roberts In The Afternoon' every weekday.

The tagline for Youthcomm is 'Worcester's More Music Station'.
