KMED (1440 AM) is a News/Talk formatted radio station licensed to Medford, Oregon, United States, and serving the Medford-Ashland area. The station is owned by Bicoastal Media Licenses Vi, LLC.

KMED's programming is rebroadcast on 106.7 MHz FM by translator K294AS, Ashland.
