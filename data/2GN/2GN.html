2GN is an Australian radio station serving the Goulburn region, which opened in January 1932 on a frequency of 1390 kHz. It is now co-owned by the Capital Radio Network and Grant Broadcasters, broadcasting on 1368 kHz. The station carries a Classic Hits format and Forever Classic branding similar to its Canberra sister station, 2CA. The station's studios are shared with their FM counterpart, Eagle FM.

2GN has an FM repeater serving the township of Crookwell and re-transmitting 2GN on 106.1 MHz.
