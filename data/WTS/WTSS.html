WTSS is a commercial FM radio station in Buffalo, New York. It operates at 102.5 MHz and airs a hot adult contemporary radio format branded as Star 102.5. It is owned and operated by Entercom Communications. WTSS's transmitter is in Colden, New York on the WIVB-TV Tower, while its studios and offices are located on Corporate Parkway in Amherst, New York.
