KVMO (104.3 FM) is a radio station serving Northeast Missouri and parts of Western Illinois, including the Hannibal, MO/Quincy, IL Micropolitan area with a country music format currently licensed to Dennis Wallace, Court-Appointed Receiver (for Twenty-One Sound Communications, Inc. of St. Louis, Missouri), and broadcasts from a tower 8 miles north of Vandalia, MO. Up til The end of December 2018, KVMO was airing a format of adult album alternative rock, but in late January 2019 switched to a simulcast of country music station 99.9 KFAV Warrenton, MO. The former Adult Alternative Rock format from KVMO is now an online only station called 1Channel Radio which has all the same hosts and programming formerly heard on KVMO.
