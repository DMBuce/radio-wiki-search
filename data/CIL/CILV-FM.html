CILV-FM (88.5 FM, Live 88.5) is a radio station licensed to Ottawa, Ontario. Owned by Stingray Group, it broadcasts an alternative rock format. Its studios are located on Antares Drive in Nepean, while its transmitter is located in Greely, Ontario.
