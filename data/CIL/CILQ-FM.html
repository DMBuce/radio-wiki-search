CILQ-FM is a Canadian radio station, broadcasting at 107.1 FM in Toronto, Ontario. The station broadcasts a mainstream rock format branded as Q107. The station is owned by Corus Entertainment. CILQ's studios are located at the Corus Quay building on Dockside Drive at Toronto's Harbourfront neighbourhood, while its transmitter is located atop the CN Tower, with backup facilities located atop First Canadian Place. Q107 is also available through streaming audio, and simulcast on Bell Satellite TV channel 954, and Shaw Direct channel 864.
