CKLX-FM (91.9 MHz) is a French language Canadian radio station located in Montreal, Quebec. Owned and operated by RNC Media, it airs a sports radio format.

CKLX-FM's studios are located on West Laurier Avenue in the Le Plateau-Mont-Royal neighbourhood of Montreal. Its transmitter, located atop Mount Royal, uses a directional antenna with an average effective radiated power of 1,780 watts and a peak effective radiated power of 4,675 watts (Class B1). It also is heard nationally on Channel 978 on the Bell Satellite TV radio station line up.
