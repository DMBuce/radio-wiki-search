CKLF-FM is a Canadian radio station serving Brandon, Manitoba at 94.7 FM. The station uses its on-air brand name Star 94.7 with a hot adult contemporary format.

CKLF is owned and operated by Westman Radio Ltd., a subsidiary of Westman Communications Group. It's housed, along with CKLQ-FM, branded Q Country 91.5 FM, at 624 14th Street East, on Brandon's northeast side.
