KMXH (93.9 FM, "Mix 93") is a radio station broadcasting an Urban Adult Contemporary format. Licensed to Alexandria, Louisiana, United States, the station is currently owned by Urban Radio Broadcasting. Its studios are located at FCA Plaza in Alexandria, and its transmitter is located off I-49 south of downtown Alexandria.
