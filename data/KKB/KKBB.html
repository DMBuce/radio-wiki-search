KKBB (99.3 FM, "Groove 99-3") is a commercial radio station located in Bakersfield, California. The station is owned by Alpha Media Ltd. through licensee Alpha Media Licensee Ltd. KKBB airs a rhythmic oldies music format. The station was assigned the KKBB call letters by the Federal Communications Commission on 9 December 1994. KKBB's studios and transmitter are separately located in Oildale.
