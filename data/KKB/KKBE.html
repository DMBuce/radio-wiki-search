KKBE (910 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a Contemporary Christian format branded as "93.7 The Light ". Licensed to Roswell, New Mexico, United States, the station is currently owned by Noalmark Broadcasting Corporation.

The station (as KBIM) previously aired conservative talk programming, with hosts such as Jason Lewis, Rusty Humphries, Rush Limbaugh, Michael Savage, and Sean Hannity. On April 1, 2015 KBIM flipped to Rhythmic CHR and on April 21, 2015 the call letters were changed to KKBE. On June 1, 2018, KKBE switched to a Contemporary Christian format, after not gaining much market share, or advertising revenue.
