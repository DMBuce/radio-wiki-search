KNUI (550 AM) is a radio station that broadcasts a Country Music format. Licensed to Wailuku, Hawaii, United States, the station is owned by Pacific Radio Group, Inc.

KNUI was as a talk station named "The Talk of Maui." After 3 months of being silent, KNUI re-branded as K-Country on July 6, 2020. The new station has a playlist of mainstream country artists from different years along with Hawaiian country singers. Additionally, FM translator K291CZ was added to compete with KRYL.
