KNUE (101.5 FM; 101.5 KNUE) is a terrestrial American radio station, broadcasting a Country format. Licensed to Tyler, Texas, United States, the station serves the Tyler-Longview area. The station is currently owned by Townsquare Media and licensed to Townsquare Media Tyler License, LLC. KNUE's studios are located in south Tyler and its transmitter is located near Overton, Texas.

101.5 KNUE is the long-time home of Big D & Bubba in the morning, Mandee Montana, and Danny Merrell.
