KNUW is a radio station airing an Adult hits format licensed to Santa Clara, New Mexico, broadcasting on 95.1 MHz FM. The station serves the Silver City, New Mexico area and most of Grant County, New Mexico, and is owned by Duran-Hill, Inc.
