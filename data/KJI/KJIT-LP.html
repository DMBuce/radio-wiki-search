KJIT-LP (106.7 FM) is a radio station licensed to Bismarck, North Dakota, United States; the station serves the Bismarck area. The station is owned by He Will Provide Radio. It airs a religious radio format as an affiliate of Radio 74 Internationale.

The station was assigned the KJIT-LP call letters by the Federal Communications Commission on September 19, 2005.
