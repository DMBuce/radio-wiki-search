KJIM (1500 kHz) is a commercial AM radio station in Sherman, Texas. The station is owned by Bob Mark Allen Productions. KJIM airs an adult standards radio format, featuring programming from America's Best Music, a service of Westwood One. World and national news is heard every hour from CBS Radio News. Studios, offices and the transmitter are on Woodlawn Road in Denison, Texas.

Because AM 1500 is a clear-channel frequency reserved for WFED in Washington, D.C., and KSTP in St. Paul, Minnesota, KJIM is a daytimer and must sign-off at sunset. Programming is heard around the clock on FM translator K267CB at 101.3 MHz.
