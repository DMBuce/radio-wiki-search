WEXR (106.9 FM) is a radio station broadcasting in the Meridian, Mississippi, area.

WEXR is part of the Alert FM digital alert and messaging system for Lauderdale County first responders.

In September 2011 WKZB was donated to Meridian Community College.

On September 9, 2011 WKZB changed their call letters to WEXR and changed their format from classic hits to college radio, branded as "The Eagle".
