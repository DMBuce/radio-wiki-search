WTPM (92.9 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a Religious format. Licensed to Aguadilla, Puerto Rico, United States, it serves the Puerto Rico area. The station is currently owned by the Corporation of the Seventh-day Adventists of West Puerto Rico.
