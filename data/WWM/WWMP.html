WWMP (103.3 FM, "Rock 103.3") is a radio station broadcasting an mainstream rock format that is licensed to Waterbury, Vermont, United States. The station serves the Burlington-Plattsburgh area. The station is owned by Radio Broadcasting Services, Inc.

In 2011, WWMP was granted a U.S. Federal Communications Commission construction permit to move to a new transmitter site, decrease ERP to 1,050 watts, increase HAAT to 806 meters and change to a directional antenna.
