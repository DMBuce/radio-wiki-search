WYTJ is a non-commercial and non-profit Christian music radio station licensed to Linton, IN. It is owned by Bethel Baptist Church. WYTJ broadcasts at a frequency of 89.3 MHz with 1,400 watts of power using a non-directional antenna pattern. The Federal Communications Commission considers WYTJ to be a class A FM broadcasting facility. WYTJ stands for "Will You Trust Jesus?". WYTJ is also heard in Vincennes, Indiana through a translator, W201BO, broadcasting on 88.1 FM.
