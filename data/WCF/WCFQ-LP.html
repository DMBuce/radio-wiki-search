WCFQ-LP (104.9 FM) was a radio station licensed to Inverness, Florida, United States. The station was owned by Standing in the Gap Inc.

The Federal Communications Commission cancelled WCFQ-LP's license on February 4, 2020, due to the station failing to file an application for license renewal.
