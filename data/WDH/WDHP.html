WDHP (1620 AM) is a radio station licensed to serve Frederiksted, U.S. Virgin Islands. The station is owned by Reef Broadcasting, Inc. It airs a mix of news, talk, gospel music, and country music.

The station was assigned these call letters by the Federal Communications Commission on March 6, 1998.
