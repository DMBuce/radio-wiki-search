WNKN (105.9 FM) (formerly WPFB-FM) is a radio station broadcasting a classic country format. Licensed to Middletown, Ohio, United States, the station serves the Cincinnati and Dayton areas. The station is currently owned by Grant County Broadcasters, Inc.
