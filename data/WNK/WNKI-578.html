WNKI 578 (1610 AM ) is an emergency radio station based in Idyllwild, California. The station is operated by the Mile High Radio Club of Idyllwild. The main purpose of WNKI 578 is to broadcast emergency information such as road conditions, weather updates, and evacuation orders to the communities of Idyllwild, Fern Valley, Pine Cove, and parts of Mountain Center.
