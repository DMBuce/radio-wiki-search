CJAN-FM is a Canadian radio station, which airs at 99.3 FM in Asbestos, Quebec. The station, branded as FM 99.3, is a commercial broadcasting radio with local talk and variety music programming.

CJAN originally began broadcasting on the AM dial in 1972, until it moved to its current FM frequency in 2002.
