WPFR-FM is a Christian radio station licensed to Clinton, Indiana, transmitting on 93.7 MHz. The station serves the Terre Haute, Indiana area, and is owned by Word Power, Inc. The station began broadcasting in June 2000, and originally on 93.9 MHz. In 2011, the station's frequency was changed to 93.7 MHz.
