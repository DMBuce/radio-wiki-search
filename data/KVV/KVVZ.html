KVVZ (Latino Mix 100.7 FM) is a radio station licensed to San Rafael, California, United States, and it serves the San Francisco area. The station is currently owned by Univision Radio, through licensee Univision Radio Illinois, Inc. The station serves as the northern simulcast of its sister station KVVF Santa Clara. Its studios are in San Jose, and the KVVZ transmitter is in San Rafael.
