KVVR (97.9 FM) is a radio station broadcasting in an Adult Contemporary format. Licensed to Dutton, Montana, United States, the station serves the Great Falls area. The station is currently owned by Ccr-Great Falls Iv, LLC and features programming from Premiere Radio Networks.
