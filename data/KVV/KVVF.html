KVVF (Latino Mix 105.7 FM) is a radio station that broadcasts from Santa Clara, California, and is being simulcast in San Rafael, California on KVVZ (100.7 FM). It is owned by Univision Communications, with studios located at 1940 Zanker Road in San Jose. The KVVF transmitter is located near Mount Hamilton. It serves the San Francisco Bay Area with a Spanish CHR format. It is the San Jose affiliate for the Uforia Audio Network.

KVVF broadcasts in HD.
