KOLY (1300 AM) is a radio station licensed to serve Mobridge, South Dakota. The station is owned by James River Broadcasting. It airs a full-service soft adult contemporary music format with extensive news, agricultural reports, sports, and Paul Harvey talk programming.

All three Mobridge James River Broadcasting stations share studios at 118 3rd St. East, in Mobridge. The KOLY AM and FM transmitters and the 581 foot tower are east of town, on Highway 12.

The station was assigned these call letters by the Federal Communications Commission.
