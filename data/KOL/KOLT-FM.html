KOLT-FM (100.7 FM, "100.7 KOLT FM") is a radio station broadcasting a country music format. Licensed to Cheyenne, Wyoming, United States, the station serves the Ft. Collins-Greeley area. The station is currently owned by iHeartMedia, Inc., through licensee Citicasters Licenses, Inc.
