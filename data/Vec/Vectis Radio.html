Vectis Radio is an community radio station in Newport on the Isle of Wight. The station began transmitting exclusively online from 23 January 2010, and continued to broadcast this way until beginning FM broadcasting on 4 November 2017. Vectis Radio transmits on FM from Newport Golf Club.
