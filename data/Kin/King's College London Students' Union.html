King's College London Students' Union (KCLSU) is an independent charitable organisation existing solely to further the interests of its members (approximately 36,000 students at King's College London) and govern over the more than 300 student societies and activity groups at King's. KCLSU claims to be the oldest students' union in England.

The Strategic Plan that defines the mission of KCLSU is "together we are a union of students where individuals connect, have fun, build communities, share experiences and make change".
