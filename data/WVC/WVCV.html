WVCV is a broadcast radio station licensed to Orange, Virginia, serving Orange and Orange County, Virginia. WVCV is owned and operated by Piedmont Communications, Inc. and simulcasts the Country format of sister station 103.1 WJMA Culpeper. Prior to February 2016, it had aired a satellite-fed Adult Standards format.
