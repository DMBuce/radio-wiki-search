WVCB (1410 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a combination of Contemporary Christian and Gospel Music. WVCB transmits with a daytime power of 500 watts, reducing to 168 watts during nighttime. It has always held the callsign WVCB. WVCB is an affiliate of the Carolina Panthers Radio Network.
