WVCO (94.9 FM, "94.9 The Surf") is an American radio station in the Myrtle Beach, South Carolina, market. They play a mix of Blues/Carolina beach music/shag. Its studios are located on Ocean Boulevard in Myrtle Beach, and its transmitter is southeast of Loris.
