WXRD (103.9 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a classic rock format. Licensed to Crown Point, Indiana, United States, it serves Northwest Indiana and Chicago's south suburbs. The station is currently owned by Adams Radio Group, LLC, through licensee Adams Radio of Northern Indiana, LLC, and features programming from Jones Radio Network and Premiere Radio Networks.
