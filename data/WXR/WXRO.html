WXRO (95.3 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a hot adult contemporary music format. Licensed to Beaver Dam, Wisconsin, United States, the station is currently owned by Good Karma Brands. WXRO originally had the call letters WBEV-FM. It broadcasts NASCAR racing as part of the Motor Racing Network (MRO).

On July 12, 2019, WXRO changed their format from country to hot adult contemporary, branded as "95X".
