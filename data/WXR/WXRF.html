WXRF (1590 AM) is a radio station licensed to serve Guayama, Puerto Rico. The station is part of the WAPA Radio News Network and is owned and operated by Grupo AM Puerto Rico, and features programming from CNN en Español. It airs a Spanish-language News/Talk format. The station is shared with translator station W280FS 103.9 FM also in Guayama.
