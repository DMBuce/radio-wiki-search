WXRQ (1460 AM, "The Power Gospel") is a radio station broadcasting a Southern Gospel music format. Licensed to Mount Pleasant, Tennessee, United States, the station is currently owned by New Life Broadcasting, Inc. and features programming from USA Radio Network.
