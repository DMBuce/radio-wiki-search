KZZA (106.7 FM, "La Ranchera 106.7 FM/1540 AM") is a radio station broadcasting a Regional Mexican format. Licensed to the suburb of Muenster, Texas, United States, it serves the Dallas-Ft. Worth metropolitan area. It first began broadcasting in 1990 under the call sign KXGM at 106.5 FM. The station is currently owned by Estrella Media. Its transmitter is located in northernmost Wise County near Greenwood, Texas.
