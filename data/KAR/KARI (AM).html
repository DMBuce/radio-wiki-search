KARI (550 kHz, "Word Radio") is a commercial AM radio station, licensed to Blaine, Washington, and serving Vancouver, British Columbia, and Northwest Washington state. It broadcasts a Christian talk and teaching radio format and is owned by Multicultural Broadcasting.

The transmitter is on Tracy Lane in Blaine, only a few miles from the Canada–United States border. KARI broadcasts at 5,000 watts by day and 2,500 watts at night, using a directional antenna pointed at the Greater Vancouver radio market.

The station features programming from "Let the Bible Speak" with Ian Goligher, Focus on the Family, Grace to You, In Touch Ministries, Turning Point, Thru the Bible Radio, and many others.

KARI first signed on the air on February 12, 1960.
