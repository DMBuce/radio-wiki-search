Rother FM was an Independent Local Radio station for Rotherham, South Yorkshire, England. Rother FM was based in Doncaster. The Station began broadcasting in October 2006 from its studios at Aspen Court in Templeborough. It mainly covers the Rotherham Borough from its transmitter at Boston Castle, although it can easily be heard in neighbouring cities such as Sheffield. The station is owned and operated by Bauer Radio.
