DWTL (93.5 FM), broadcasting as Barangay FM 93.5, is a radio station owned and operated by GMA Network Inc. subsidiary Radio GMA. The station's studio and transmitter are located at GMA Complex, Claveria Rd., Brgy. Malued, Dagupan.
