WLGZ-FM (102.7 FM) is a radio station broadcasting an oldies/classic hits format. The station is licensed to Webster, New York, serving the Rochester, New York area, and is currently owned by DJRO Broadcasting LLC. Studios are in Rochester's east side, while the transmitter tower is in the city's west side.
