KNDY-FM 95.5 FM is commercial broadcast station in Marysville, Kansas that plays country music as well as local news, weather, and sports coverage. The station signed on the air July 23, 1974 and moved into a new broadcast studio with sister-station KNDY 1570 AM in July 2006.
