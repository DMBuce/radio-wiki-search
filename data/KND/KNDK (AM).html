KNDK (1080 AM) is a radio station broadcasting talk and country music programming serving Langdon, North Dakota. The station is currently owned by Simmons Broadcasting. All four Simmons Broadcasting stations share studios at 1403 Third Street in Langdon, ND. It serves as the only American affiliate station of the Winnipeg Jets of the National Hockey League.

1080 AM is a United States clear channel frequency; KOAN, KRLD, and WTIC share Class A status on this frequency.
