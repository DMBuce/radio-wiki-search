KICS (1550 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a sports format. Licensed to Hastings, Nebraska, United States, the station serves the Hastings-Grand Island-Kearney area. The station is currently owned by Platte River Radio, Inc. and features programming from ESPN Radio and Westwood One.

The majority of the programming is simulcast with sister station KXPN.
