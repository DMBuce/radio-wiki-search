KICT-FM, branded as T-95, is a radio station in Wichita, Kansas. It is an Active Rock Radio station operating on 95.1 FM. The station is owned by SummitMedia. Its studios are located with television station KWCH in Wichita and the transmitter is located outside Colwich, Kansas.
