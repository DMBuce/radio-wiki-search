KGLD (1330 AM, 104.9 FM; The Light) is a terrestrial American radio station, relayed by an FM translator, broadcasting a Gospel music format. Licensed to Tyler, Texas, United States, the station serves the Tyler-Longview area. The station is currently owned by Salt of the Earth Broadcasting.
