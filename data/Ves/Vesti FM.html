Vesti FM (Russian: Радиостанция «Вести ФМ») is a radio station based in Russia and owned by VGTRK. It has started broadcasting on February 5, 2008 at 6:00 am Moscow Time. The station is included in the first multiplex of digital television in Russia using DVB-T technology.
