DXMY (729 AM) RMN Cotabato is a radio station owned and operated by the Radio Mindanao Network. The station's studio is located in Esteros, Brgy. Rosary Heights 10, Cotabato City.

The station went off-air on November 5, 2020 due to the technical upgrades and the relocation of its studio and transmitter.
