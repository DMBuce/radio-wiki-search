DXMA (104.3 FM), also known as Hope Radio 104.3, is an FM radio station owned and operated by Adventist Media, the media arm of the Seventh-day Adventist Church. The station's studio is located at #5 Palm Dr., Brgy. Buhangin Proper (Bajada), Davao City and its transmitter is located at Shrine Hills, Matina, Davao City.

The station used to be The Edge Radio Davao of United Christian Broadcasters and Century Broadcasting Network, until AdventistMedia acquired the station in mid-2010s.
