DXMM (810 AM) was a radio station of UM Broadcasting Network in the Philippines. It was established in 1963 as the third AM station of UMBN after DXMC (now DXWT) and DXUM. The station was shut down during Martial Law.

Currently, the callsign is assigned to an AM station in Jolo, Sulu.
