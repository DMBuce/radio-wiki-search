DXMM (102.5 FM), broadcasting as 102.5 Brigada News FM, is a music and news FM radio station owned and operated by Brigada Mass Media Corporation in the Philippines. The station's studio is located at Suan Arcade, Mastersons Ave., Ilaya Carmen, Cagayan de Oro, while its transmitter is located at the 5th floor, CFI Bldg., Tiano-Yacapin St., Cagayan de Oro.
