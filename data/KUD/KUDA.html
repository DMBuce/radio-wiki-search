KUDA (88.7 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a variety format. Licensed to Shoshoni, Wyoming, United States, the station is owned by KUDA, Inc. KUDA broadcasts from a mountain north of Shoshoni, and serves the greater Fremont County/Hot Springs County region. It also broadcasts on a translator, K244EU (96.7 FM) located in Thermopolis, Wyoming. The station's RDS states it is "Wyoming's Hot Spot".
