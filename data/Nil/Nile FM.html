Nile FM is a privately owned English-language radio station based in Cairo, Egypt, that plays English-language music hits and is owned by Nile Radio Productions. Nile FM airs at 104.2 FM. Some of the station's most popular programs are The Morning Show hosted by Mark Somers and Sally Sampson, American Top 40 hosted by Ryan Seacrest, and the Nile FM Drive, presented by Rob Stevens who is also the station's program director.
