KDNO (101.7 FM) is a classic country radio station in Thermopolis, Wyoming. The station is owned by Jerry and Steve Edwards, through licensee Edwards Communications, LC and features programming from AP Radio. The majority owner of Edwards Communications, LC is also the owner of KFCW and KWYW. The call letters used to belong to what is now KDFO-FM in Delano, California from 1970–1997. KDNO covers most of Hot Springs County and reaches most of Fremont County and can be heard in Riverton and as far north as Worland.
