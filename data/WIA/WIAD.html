WIAD (94.7 FM, "94.7 The Drive") is a commercial FM radio station licensed to serve Bethesda, Maryland. The station is owned by Entercom through licensee Entercom License, LLC, and broadcasts a classic hits format, branded as "94.7 The Drive". WIAD's studios are located on Half Street SE near the Navy Yard in Southeast Washington, with transmitting facilities located off River Road in Bethesda.

WIAD broadcasts using HD Radio, using its HD2 subchannel to air "Channel Q," Entercom's Talk/EDM service for the LGBTQ community, while the sports programming of sister station WJFK-FM is simulcast on its HD3 subchannel.
