WCOH (1400 AM) is a radio station broadcasting as an affiliate of Fox Sports Radio. Until January 2009, WCOH played a Classic Country format. Licensed to Newnan, Georgia, United States, it serves the Atlanta area. WCOH broadcasts Newnan High School football and baseball. The station is currently owned by iHeartMedia, Inc. as part of the Newnan cluster with WMGP 98.1 and WGST 720, but operated separately from the Atlanta cluster.
