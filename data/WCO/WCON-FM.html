WCON-FM (99.3 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a classic country format. Licensed to Cornelia, Georgia, United States, the station is currently owned by Habersham Broadcasting Co. WCON-FM also broadcasts games of the Habersham Central High School "Raiders".

Its tower is located in White County, and its studios are located at 540 North Main Street in Cornelia.
