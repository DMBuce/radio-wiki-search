WCOX (1450 AM) was a radio station formerly licensed to serve Camden, Alabama. The station was owned by Down Home Broadcasting. It aired a Gospel music format.

This station was a daytime-only station broadcasting on 1540 kHz before going full-time on 1450 kHz.
