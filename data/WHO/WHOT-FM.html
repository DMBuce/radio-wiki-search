WHOT-FM (101.1 MHz, "Hot 101") is a commercial radio station in Youngstown, Ohio. It airs a Top 40 (CHR) radio format. It is one of seven radio stations in the Youngstown market owned by Cumulus Broadcasting. It carries syndicated shows from Zach Sang on weeknights and Carson Daly on Sunday mornings. The studios and transmitter are Simon Road at Mayport Avenue in Boardman, using a Youngstown address.
