WHOG-FM (95.7 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a Classic rock format. Licensed to Ormond-By-The-Sea, Florida, United States, the station serves the Daytona Beach area, from a transmitter site located in Holly Hill. The station is currently owned by Southern Stone Communications.

The station currently plays a mix of Classic Rock of the 1970s and 1980s from groups such as Led Zeppelin, Van Halen, and Aerosmith, as well as Classic Modern Rock from artists such as Pearl Jam, Soundgarden, Green Day, Nickelback, Creed, and Red Hot Chili Peppers.
