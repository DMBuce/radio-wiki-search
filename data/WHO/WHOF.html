WHOF (101.7 MHz) – branded Sunny 101.7 – is a commercial FM radio station licensed to North Canton, Ohio, serving the Canton metropolitan area. It broadcasts an adult contemporary radio format, switching to all-Christmas music for much of November and December. Owned by iHeartMedia, Inc., the station serves as the local network affiliate for syndicated Ellen K Weekend Show. The studios and offices are located in North Canton.

The station transmitter is off 22nd Street NW at Whipple Avenue in Meyers Lake. In addition to standard analog transmission, WHOF broadcasts over two HD Radio channels, and is available online via iHeartRadio. The HD2 digital subchannel airs a Country format under the brand 99.7 Canton's New Country, and is simulcast over Canton translator W259BW (99.7 FM).
