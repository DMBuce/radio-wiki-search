KIXL is a Catholic talk radio station in Del Valle, Texas, broadcasting to the Austin-Round Rock area of Texas on A.M. 970 kHz. KIXL is a Relevant Radio owned-and-operated station, owned by the Immaculate Heart Media.

KIXL was home to the Christian radio format for over thirty years, until the programming moved to KOKE-FM (then KLGO) on January 20, 2006. When the format aired on KIXL, it was cited by the National Religious Broadcasters twice with national recognition as Radio Station of the Year and Morning Show of the Year.
