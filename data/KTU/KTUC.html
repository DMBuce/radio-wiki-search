KTUC (1400 kHz) is a commercial AM radio station in Tucson, Arizona. The station is owned by Cumulus Media and airs an adult standards radio format, known as "Tucson's All Time Favorites." On Sunday mornings, it plays three hours of big band music. Most hours begin with co-owned Westwood One News. KTUC's studios and transmitter are on Nixon Low Way, off Roger Road.
