WJDZ (90.1 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a Contemporary Christian format. Licensed to Pastillo, Puerto Rico, the station serves the Puerto Rico area. The station is currently owned by Juan Esteban Díaz, through licensee; Siembra Fertil P.R.
