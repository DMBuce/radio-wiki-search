WJDK-FM (95.7 FM) is a radio station broadcasting an adult contemporary format. Licensed to Seneca, Illinois, the station serves Grundy County and Eastern LaSalle County, and is owned by Nelson Multimedia Inc. The station began broadcasting in 1998.
