Radio Jadran is a radio station in Montenegro. Its headquarters are in Herceg Novi. The founders of radio Jadran are Montenegrin water polo players Boris Zloković and Predrag Jokić

Schedule:

8:00 am Jadranov Đir - The morning program

12:30 pm Šugiganje - Talk Show

2:30 pm Spektar - News from Herceg Novi, Kotor, Tivat, Budva

Vijesti - World news

Frequency:

Herceg Novi and Tivat - 103.20 MHz

Budva - 101.70 MHz

URL: www.radiojadran.com
