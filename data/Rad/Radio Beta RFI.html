Radio Beta RFI was a Belgrade radio station that operated on 107.9 FM. The station ran a news/talk format and was the first full-time talk station in Belgrade. It was a joint venture between the French radio station Radio France Internationale and the renowned Belgrade news agency "Beta". The broadcasts originated from the studios located in BeograÄ‘anka.
