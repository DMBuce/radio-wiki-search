Radio 1010 is a Uruguayan Spanish-language AM radio station that broadcasts from Montevideo.

It currently has a sports format, with partisan football broadcasts of Nacional (Pasión tricolor) and Peñarol (Fútbol a lo Peñarol), and daily sports talk shows led by Jorge da Silveira (A fondo) and Julio Ríos (Las voces del fútbol).
