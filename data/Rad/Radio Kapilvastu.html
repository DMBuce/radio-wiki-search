Radio Kapilvastu is a first community radio of Kapilvastu District, Nepal. It broadcasts on 104.2 MHz by a 100 watt FM transmitter, and covers kapilvastu rupandehi Dang Arghakhachi and Uttar Pradesh.

Social information, music and health message broadcasting for social awareness .
