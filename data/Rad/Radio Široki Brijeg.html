Radio Široki Brijeg is a radio station, broadcasting from Široki Brijeg, Bosnia and Herzegovina on frequencies 92.7, 93.1 and 102.3 MHz. It serves the West Herzegovina Canton, Herzegovina-Neretva Canton and West Bosnia Canton.

It was founded on April 10, 1992. The station broadcasts from the Dom kulture (Cultural Home) at Gojko Šušak Square.
