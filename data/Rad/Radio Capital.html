Radio Capital is an Italian private radio station, was founded on May 1977 and is owned by the Gruppo Editoriale L'Espresso (ultimately by EXOR owned by the Agnelli-family) and began broadcasting in 1985.

Broadcast FTA on Hot Bird, on SKY Italia channel 713 and on Alice Home TV channel 775.
