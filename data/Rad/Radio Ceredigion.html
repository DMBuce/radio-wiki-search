Radio Ceredigion (Welsh pronunciation: [ˈradjɔ kɛrɛˈdɪɡjɔn]) was an Independent Local Radio station serving Ceredigion in Mid Wales. It was owned and operated by Nation Broadcasting and broadcast on 96.6, 97.4 and 103.3 FM from studios near the St Hilary transmitter in the Vale of Glamorgan.

The station played chart music from the 1980s to the present day, alongside local news, travel and community information.

On May 31, 2019, the station became a relay of Nation Radio Wales.
