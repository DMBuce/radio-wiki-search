Radio Bosanski Petrovac is a Bosnian local public radio station, broadcasting from Bosanski Petrovac, Bosnia and Herzegovina. As a municipal radio, this station broadcasts a variety of programs such as local news, talk shows and music (from 15:00 - 17:00 hours). Program is mainly produced in Bosnian language at one FM frequency (Bosanski Petrovac 90.9 MHz).
