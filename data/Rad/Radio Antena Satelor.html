Radio Antena Satelor (Radio Villages' Antenna) is the fifth state-owned radio station in Romania. It broadcasts seven days a week, 24 hours a day.

Radio Antena Satelor was heard for the first time regionally on December 25, 1991, with 5 hours of broadcast per day and coverage only in Bucharest, Dâmbovița and Argeș, following which on March 1, 2006 it will reach the national position. Radio Antena Satelor includes programs and programs for the public in rural areas, debating real problems of the Romanian village.
