Radio Verulam 92.6 FM is a Hertfordshire based non-profit community radio station run by Verulam Community Radio Ltd (VCRL).

The station is staffed by over 70 presentation, production and technical volunteers .

Until April 2010 Radio Verulam was based in Victoria Street, St Albans and has since moved to Hatfield Road. The station has a coverage area designated as a 5 kilometres (3 miles) radius from the transmitter site.
