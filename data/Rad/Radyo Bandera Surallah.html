Radyo Bandera 98.1 News FM is an FM radio station owned by Bandera News Philippines. Its studios and transmitter are located at Brgy. Dajay, Surallah.

It was formerly an affiliate of Sagay Broadcasting Corporation as Muews Radio from 2010 to 2018.
