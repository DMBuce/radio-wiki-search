Radio Light is a listener supported, English and iTaukei language Christian, radio station in Fiji. The station broadcasts on the 104.2Mhz to the cities of Suva, Navua and Nausori. The station also broadcasts on line.

It is operated by Evangelical Bible Mission Trust Board the organisation which also operates Radio Nayajiwan in Fiji. Radio Light was launched in November 26, 1996 with the mission to carry the message of The Christ in Fiji.
