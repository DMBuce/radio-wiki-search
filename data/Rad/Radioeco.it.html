Radioeco.it is the official web-radio station of the University of Pisa, Italy. The radio is fully run by University students, whose aim is to inform and entertain all the students of the University. The main purpose of this non-profit organization is to give voice to the University students of the Pisa area. The radio studios are located in the Faculty of Economics of Pisa, Pisa, and it is possible to listen to the radio via its website and through mobile devices, as the TuneIn app and Nokia Internet Radio. The radio broadcasts 24/7 and features several live programmes each day, from band interviews to politics and economics debates. The radio also runs a reporting and blogging activity, mainly concerning events and students' initiatives.
