Radio Foča or Радио Фоча is a Bosnian local public radio station, broadcasting from Foča, Bosnia and Herzegovina.

It was launched on 26 November 1976 by the municipal council of Foča. In Yugoslavia and in SR Bosnia and Herzegovina, it was part of part of local/municipal Radio Sarajevo network affiliate. This radio station broadcasts a variety of programs such as music, sport, local news and talk shows. Program is mainly produced in Serbian language.

Estimated number of potential listeners of Radio Foča is around 24,758. Radiostation is also available in municipalities of Bosnian-Podrinje Canton Goražde.
