Radio Kostajnica or Радио Костајница is a Bosnian local public radio station, broadcasting from Kostajnica/Bosanska Kostajnica, Bosnia and Herzegovina. It was launched in 1995 by JP Radio Kostajnica. This radio station broadcasts a variety of programs such as music, local news and talk shows.

Program is mainly produced in Serbian language from 08:00 to 16:00h. Estimated number of potential listeners of Radio Kostajnica is around 10,361. Radiostation is also available in municipalities of Bosanska Krajina and in neighboring Croatia.
