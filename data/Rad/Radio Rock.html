Radio Rock is a Finnish rock music radio station owned by Nelonen Media, a part of the media group Sanoma.

Radio Rock's broadcasting began on 1 January 2007, at 0.00, with the official spoken programs beginning on 15 January 2007. The first song that the station played was Metallica's "Master of Puppets".
