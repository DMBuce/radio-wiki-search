Radio Republike Srpske or Radio RS is entity level public radio channel operated by Radio Televizija Republike Srpske (RTRS). Its headquarters are located in Banja Luka.

This radio station broadcasts a variety of programs, including news, music, talk shows and sports. The program is broadcast 24 hours per day in the Serbian language.
