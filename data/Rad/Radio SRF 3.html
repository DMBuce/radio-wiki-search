Radio SRF 3 is the third radio station from Schweizer Radio und Fernsehen (SRF). The station was launched in 1983 (as DRS 3) in response to the licensing of private radio stations in Switzerland that was directed primarily for music to a younger audience, and was repositioned to a "generalist young-adults" station in 1999 following the launch of DRS Virus that same year.
