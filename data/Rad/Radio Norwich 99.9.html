Note: not to be confused with the Alan Partridge parody Radio Norwich.

Radio Norwich 99.9 was an Independent Local Radio station owned and operated by Bauer as part of the Greatest Hits Radio network. It broadcast to Norwich and the surrounding area. The station was merged with North Norfolk Radio and The Beach and became Greatest Hits Radio Norfolk and North Suffolk.
