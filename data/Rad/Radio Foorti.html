Radio Foorti is a Bangladeshi FM radio station. The station went live on September 22, 2006 and is currently available in Dhaka, Chittagong, Rajshahi, Khulna, Barisal, Mymensingh, Cox's Bazar, Bogra and Noakhali. It is the largest FM radio network in Bangladesh.
