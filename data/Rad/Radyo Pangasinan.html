Radyo Pangasinan (95.3 FM) is a commercial radio station owned and operated by Pangasinan Gulf Waves Network Corporation. The station's studio and transmitter are located at the 3rd floor, Jumel Bldg., A.B. Fernandez Ave., Dagupan. It broadcasts daily from 5:00 AM to 10:00 PM.
