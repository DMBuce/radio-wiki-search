LOHRO is an acronym for local radio station of the Hanseatic City of Rostock. LOHRO is a member of the German Federal Association of Free Radios (BFR). The radio station is maintained by two associations: Kulturnetzwerk e.V. acts as licence holder and foerderverein.LOHRO supports the radio station and organizes trainings. The radio station meets its expenses by project funds of several organizations, membership fees of associations, and sponsoring.
