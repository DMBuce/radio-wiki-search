WPDX is a Classic Country formatted broadcast radio station licensed to Clarksburg, West Virginia, serving the Clarksburg/Fairmont/Buckhannon area. WPDX is owned by West Virginia Radio Corporation and operated under their AJG Corporation licensee.

Because it shares the same frequency as "clear channel" station WSB in Atlanta, Georgia, WPDX operates only during the daytime hours.
