WPDQ (103.9 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a sports talk format, licensed to Drakesboro, Kentucky, United States. The station is currently owned by Nashville's Sportsradio, Incorporated and features programming from ABC Radio and CBS Sports Radio. This programming is a simulcast of Nashville-market AM radio station WNSR.
