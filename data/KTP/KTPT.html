KTPT, known as "97.9 The Breeze", is a radio station serving Rapid City, South Dakota and the surrounding region. The station airs a Contemporary Worship music format. 97.9 The Breeze is owned by Bethesda Christian Broadcasting.

KTPT signed onto the air on February 17, 2005 on 88.3 FM, originally with a Christian Hit Radio format and branded as "The Point."

In April 2007, KTPT switched dial positions with KLMP.

On January 4, 2016, KTPT flipped from Christian CHR to Contemporary Worship and re-branded itself as "97.9 The Breeze"
