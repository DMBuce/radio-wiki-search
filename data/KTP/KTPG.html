KTPG (99.3 FM) is a radio station licensed to serve the community of Paragould, Arkansas, and broadcasting to the Jonesboro, Arkansas market. The station is owned by George S. Flinn, Jr. It airs an adult contemporary music format.

The station was assigned the KTPG call letters by the Federal Communications Commission on June 1, 2012.
