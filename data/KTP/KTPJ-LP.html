KTPJ-LP (105.9 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a religious radio format as an affiliate of LifeTalk Radio. Licensed to Pueblo, Colorado, United States, it serves the Pueblo area. The station is currently owned by the Hope Radio Of Pueblo Corporation.
