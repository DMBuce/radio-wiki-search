KACT (1360 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a country music format. Licensed to Andrews, Texas, United States, the station is currently owned by Zia Broadcasting Company and features programming from ABC Radio .

KACT-AM now airs talk radio, local and regional sports.

KACT is one of the stations founded in the late 1940s by State Senator Marshall Formby.
