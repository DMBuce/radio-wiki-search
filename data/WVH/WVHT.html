WVHT (100.5 MHz, "Hot 100") is a commercial FM radio station licensed to Norfolk, Virginia. The station broadcasts a Top 40/CHR music format to the Hampton Roads area. WVHT is owned and operated by Max Media.

The studios and offices are on Greenwich Road in Virginia Beach. The transmitter is off Gammon Road, also in Virginia Beach.
