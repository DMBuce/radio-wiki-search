WCZQ (Hot 105.5) is an urban contemporary-leaning rhythmic contemporary station serving Champaign, Illinois. WCZQ broadcasts at 105.5 MHz with an ERP of 6 kW and is licensed to Monticello, Illinois. They are owned by Neuhoff Corp., through licensee Neuhoff Media Decatur, LLC. The station has been rhythmic since 2002 and previously targeted Monticello itself with a country music format.
