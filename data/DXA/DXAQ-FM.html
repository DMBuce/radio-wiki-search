95.9 Radyo Bisdak (DXAQ 95.9 MHz) is an FM station owned and operated by Times Broadcasting Network Corporation. Its studios and transmitter are located at JM Bldg., Bonifacio St., Dipolog City, Zamboanga del Norte.
