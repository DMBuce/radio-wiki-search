DXAQ (1404 AM) Kingdom Radio was the flagship radio station of Sonshine Media Network International. The station's studio was located along Philippine-Japan Friendship Highway, Catitipan , Davao City.

Kingdom Radio has been off the air since 2013, while some Kingdom Radio programs were absorbed by sister station DXRD Sonshine Radio, becoming ACQ-KBN's flagship station.
