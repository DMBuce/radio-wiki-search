DXAM (103.1 FM), on-air as 103.1 Sunny FM, is an radio station owned by Hypersonic Broadcasting Center and operated by Almont and Blue Waters Group of Companies. The station's studio is located at Block 14, Lot 3, Monti Vista Villas, Brgy. Villa Kananga, Butuan.

Last November 5, Sunny FM went off the air for operating without a business permit.
