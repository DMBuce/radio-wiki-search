DXAB (1296 AM) Radyo Patrol is a radio station of ABS-CBN Corporation in the Philippines. The station's studio is located at the ABS-CBN Broadcasting Complex, Broadcast Avenue, Shrine Hills, Matina, Davao City, while the transmitter is located at Km 4 McArthur Highway, Matina, Davao City. Some programs from DZMM Manila and DZMM TeleRadyo are also aired on this station when local programming is not available.
