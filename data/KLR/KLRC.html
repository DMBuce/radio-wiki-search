KLRC is a non-commercial Contemporary Christian music radio station based in Siloam Springs, Arkansas broadcasting on 90.9 FM in Northwest Arkansas and northeast Oklahoma.

It is also heard on 101.1 in Siloam Springs, AR, 99.1 FM in Bentonville, and at 103.5 FM in Springdale. KLRC has been named the Christian Music Broadcasters (CMB) Small Market Radio Station of the Year six times: in 2000, 2001, 2002, 2006, 2008, and 2016. The National Association of Broadcasters (NAB) named KLRC the Marconi Award winner for Religious Station of the Year in 2013.
