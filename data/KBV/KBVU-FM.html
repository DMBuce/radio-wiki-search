KBVU-FM (97.5 FM) is a commercial radio station licensed to serve the community of Alta, Iowa. KBVU-FM is owned by Buena Vista University and broadcasts from there as well in Storm Lake. KBVU broadcasts an alternative rock format and brands itself Northwest Iowa's Best Alternative, 97.5 KBVU the EDGE.

In November 1997, KBVU-FM started broadcasting from the KAYL tower located on the east side of Storm Lake.
