KLAA-FM (103.5 FM) is a country music radio station broadcasting in the Alexandria, Louisiana radio market. The station is licensed to the hamlet of Tioga, Louisiana, which is located northeast of Alexandria in Rapides Parish. It is branded as Louisiana's 10 In A Row Country with the slogan of "LA 103.5" and is owned by Stephens Media Group, through Stephens Media Group License of Alexandria, LLC. Its studios are located in Pineville and its transmitter is in Ball, Louisiana.
