KLAG (91.7 FM) is a radio station licensed to serve Alamogordo, New Mexico. The station is owned by Educational Media Foundation. It airs an adult contemporary Christian music format.

The station was assigned the KUPR call letters by the Federal Communications Commission on November 10, 1997. The station changed to the current KLAG call sign on May 13, 2013.
