WJZP-FM (107.9 FM) is a low-power broadcast radio station in Portland, Maine, United States. The station broadcasts a community-based jazz, classic soul and urban gospelformat. The station has been on the air since 2005.

This station features monthly concerts of Maine jazz artists. These concerts are supported by local businesses and community members.

WJZP owner and operator Dennis Ross spoke out at the 2007 Federal Communications Commission hearing on media ownership issues in Portland, ME.
