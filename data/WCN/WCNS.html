WCNS ("Lite Favorites 1480") is an AM radio station licensed to Latrobe, Pennsylvania, and operating on a frequency of 1480 kilohertz by authority of the Federal Communications Commission. WCNS broadcasts with a daytime power output of 500 watts, and a nighttime power of 1,000 watts. The station broadcasts non-directionally during the daytime, and uses a four-tower directional antenna pattern at night.
