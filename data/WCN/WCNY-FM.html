WCNY-FM (Classic FM) is a public radio station in Syracuse, New York that plays classical music and is a National Public Radio member station. Broadcasting on 91.3 MHz, the station is owned and operated by the Public Broadcasting Council of Central New York and shares studios with WCNY-TV on West Fayette Street in Syracuse's Near Westside neighborhood. The station broadcasts oldies from the 1950s and 1960s on its second HD Radio subchannel and jazz on a third channel.
