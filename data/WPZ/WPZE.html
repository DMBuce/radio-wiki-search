WPZE (102.5 MHz "Praise 102.5") is a commercial FM Christian radio station licensed to Mableton, Georgia, and serving Metro Georgia. It is owned by Urban One and airs an urban gospel radio format. Studios and offices are at Centennial Tower in Downtown Atlanta.

The effective radiated power (ERP) is 3,000 watts, making it a Class A FM station. The transmitter is in Southwest Atlanta, off Fairburn Road SW, near I-285. WPZE broadcasts using IBOC digital radio, an HD Radio system from iBiquity.
