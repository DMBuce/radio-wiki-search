WWFA (102.7 FM, 102.7 Kiss FM) is a CHR/Top 40 radio station licensed to serve St. Florian, Alabama and is operated by Southern Broadcasting Corporation. WWFA serves the Shoals area, Northwest Alabama, parts of northeast Mississippi, and parts of southern middle Tennessee with an equivalent 25,000 watts of power (Class C3).
