KQRT (La Tricolor 105.1 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a Regional Mexican format. Licensed to Las Vegas, Nevada, United States, the station serves the Las Vegas area. The station is currently owned by Entravision Holdings, LLC. Its studios are in the unincorporated community of Paradise in Clark County near McCarran International Airport and its transmitter is on the northwest edge of the Las Vegas Valley.

KQRT broadcasts one channel in the HD Radio format.
