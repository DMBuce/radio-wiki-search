DZGV (99.9 FM), broadcasting as GV 99.9 or GV Southern Luzon, is a music radio station owned and operated by GV Radios Network Corporation, a subsidiary of Apollo Broadcast Investors. The station's studio and transmitter are located at #53 Evangelista St., Batangas City, Philippines. It operates daily from 4:00 AM to 12:00 MN.

GV holds the distinction as the first and the most influential FM station in the province of Batangas. GVFM used to carry the Drive Radio brand from 2010 to 2017. The slogan of this radio is "Your Good Vibes!"
