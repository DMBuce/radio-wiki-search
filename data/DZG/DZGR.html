DZGR (891 AM) Bombo Radyo is a radio station owned and operated by Bombo Radyo Philippines through its licensee People's Broadcasting Service. The station's studio and transmitter are located at Bombo Radyo Broadcast Center, Taft St. Ext., Brgy. Centro 5, Tuguegarao.
