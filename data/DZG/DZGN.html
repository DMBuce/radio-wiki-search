DZGN (102.3 FM), broadcasting as 102.3 Spirit FM, is a radio station owned and operated by Good News Sorsogon Foundation, the media arm of the Roman Catholic Diocese of Sorsogon. Its studios & transmitter are located inside the Sts. Peter & Paul Cathedral compound, Maharlika Highway, Brgy. Pangpang, Sorsogon City.

It was a Top 40 station from its inception in November 22, 1984 until February 17, 2007, when it was relaunched as a mass-based station with a mix of music, religious & talk programming.
