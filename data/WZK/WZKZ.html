WZKZ (101.9 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a country music format. Licensed to Alfred, New York, United States. The station is currently owned by Sound Communications, LLC, with a sale to Standard Media Group pending approval. The station features programming from Dial Global's Mainstream Country network.

Since March 2018, WZKZ has been simulcast on AM radio station WOEN in Olean, New York.
