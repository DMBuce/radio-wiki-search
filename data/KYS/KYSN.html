KYSN (97.7 FM, "Kissin' 97.7") is a radio station broadcasting a country music format. Licensed to East Wenatchee, Washington, United States, the station serves the Wenatchee area. The station is currently owned by Cherry Creek Media and licensed to CCR-Wenatchee IV, LLC and features programming from Premiere Radio Networks and ABC Radio .
