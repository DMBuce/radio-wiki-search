WCCR-FM is an American radio station licensed to the borough of Clarion, Pennsylvania. WCCR-FM operates at the federally assigned frequency of 92.7 MHz (FM), with an effective radiated power of 3,000 watts. This station, along with its co-located AM sister WWCH, is owned by Clarion County Broadcasting.
