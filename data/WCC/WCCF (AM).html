WCCF (1580 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a news/talk format. Licensed to Punta Gorda, Florida, United States, the station is owned by iHeartMedia, Inc.. Carries Tampa Bay Buccaneers American football and Tampa Bay Rays baseball games along with Rush Limbaugh, and Coast To Coast AM.

1580 AM is a Canadian clear-channel frequency.
