RTÉ Radio Cork, also known as RTÉ Cork Local Radio and Cork 89FM, was a local radio station in the Republic of Ireland serving Cork City.

The FM signal spilled into Ballincollig and Carrigaline, but the rest of County Cork had to rely on the medium wave signal.

It was shut down in 2000.
