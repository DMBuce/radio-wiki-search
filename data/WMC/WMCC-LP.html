WMCC-LP is a Religious formatted broadcast radio station licensed to and serving Spencer, West Virginia. WMCC-LP is owned and operated by Radio 7 Company. WMCC-LP is a 3ABN Radio Network affiliate and radio ministry of Spencer Seventh-day Adventist Church.
