WYJJ (97.7 FM, "JJ 97.7") is a radio station broadcasting an urban adult contemporary format. Licensed to Trenton, Tennessee, United States, the station serves the Jackson, Tennessee area. The station is currently owned by Forever Media through Forever South Licenses, LLC.
