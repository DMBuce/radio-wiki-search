KPNP (1600 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a World Ethnic format. Licensed to Watertown, Minnesota, the station is currently owned by Self Retire, Inc. KPNP's studios are located on Brooklyn Boulevard in Minneapolis, while its transmitter is located near Minnetrista.
