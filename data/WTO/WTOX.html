WTOX is a regional Mexican formatted broadcast radio station licensed to Glen Allen, Virginia, serving Metro Richmond. WTOX is owned and operated by Mahan Janbakhsh's TBLC Holdings, LLC, through licensee TBLC Virginia Holdings, LLC.
