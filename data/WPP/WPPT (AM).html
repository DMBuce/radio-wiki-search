WPPT (1230 AM) was an American radio station formerly licensed to serve the community of Talladega, Alabama. The station, established in 1945, was last owned by Birmingham Christian Radio, Inc. Due to transmitter damage caused by a lightning strike, WNUZ was temporarily off the air from August 2008 through August 2009.
