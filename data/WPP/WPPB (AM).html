WPVD (1290 AM) is a radio station in Providence, Rhode Island. It is owned by Rhode Island Public Radio.

On November 23, 2018, the FCC granted a construction permit for W275DA, an FM translator station on 102.9 MHz, to simulcast the then-WRPA in the Providence area.
