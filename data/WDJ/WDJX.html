WDJX is a Top 40/CHR-formatted radio station located in Louisville, Kentucky. The station is licensed by the Federal Communications Commission (FCC) to broadcast on 99.7 FM with an effective radiated power (ERP) of 24 kW. The station's studios are located in downtown Louisville and the transmitter site is in New Albany, Indiana.
