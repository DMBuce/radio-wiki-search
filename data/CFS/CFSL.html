CFSL is an AM radio station licensed to Weyburn, Saskatchewan. Owned by Golden West Broadcasting, it broadcasts a country music format on 1190 AM; the station's range covers much of southeastern Saskatchewan. It shares studios with CKRC-FM and CHWY-FM at 305 Souris Avenue in downtown Weyburn.
