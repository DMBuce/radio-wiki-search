KJKK (100.3 MHz) is a commercial FM radio station in Dallas and serving the Dallas/Fort Worth Metroplex. It is owned by Entercom, and airs an adult hits radio format known as "JACK FM." The station's studios and offices are located along North Central Expressway in Uptown Dallas.

KJKK has an effective radiated power (ERP) of 97,000 watts (100,000 with beam tilt). The transmitter site is off Plateau Street in Cedar Hill, amid the towers for several Dallas-area TV and FM stations. KJKK broadcasts in the HD Radio hybrid format, with its HD2 subchannel airing Urban Contemporary music and its HD3 subchannel playing classic country music.
