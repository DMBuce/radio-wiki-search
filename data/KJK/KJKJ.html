KJKJ (107.5 FM, "KJ108") is an American commercial Rock radio station serving Grand Forks, North Dakota. It first began broadcasting in 1985. The station is currently owned by iHeartMedia, Inc. and the station's broadcast license is held by Citicasters Licenses, L.P.
