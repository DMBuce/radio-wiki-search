KQTY (1490 AM) was a radio station broadcasting a talk radio format. Licensed to Borger, Texas, United States, the station was last owned by Zia Broadcasting Company. The station featured hourly newscasts from CBS Radio. Show hosts included Doug Stephan, Hugh Hewitt, Dennis Prager, Dana Loesch and Michael Medved. On August 12, 2019, station management announced that it would cease operation on Friday, August 16, 2019 at 10 pm. Its license was surrendered to the Federal Communications Commission and cancelled on September 15, 2020.
