WZTU (94.9 FM, "Tú 94.9") is a radio station in the Miami-Ft. Lauderdale radio market. Owned and operated by iHeartMedia, its studios are located in iHeartMedia complex in Rivera Blvd in Miramar and the transmitter site is in Miami Gardens. The station plays a Spanish Top-40 format, mixed with some English top 40 songs.

WZTU is licensed by the FCC to broadcast in the HD Radio (hybrid) format.
