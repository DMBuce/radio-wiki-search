WZTA (1370 kHz) is a commercial AM radio station in Vero Beach, Florida. The station serves the Treasure Coast area and is owned by iHeartMedia, Inc. WZTA broadcasts an Oldies radio format, and is known on air as "Oldies 107.9 and 1370."

Programming on WZTA is also heard on FM translator W300BQ at 107.9 MHz.
