KXZT is an American radio station licensed by the Federal Communications Commission (FCC) to broadcast at 107.9 MHz from Newell, South Dakota, covering the Rapid City and Black Hills area. On April 14, 2017 it went on air as a simulcast tower for True Country 99.5/107.9. The tower is owned and operated by Bad Lands Broadcasting Company, Inc. KXZT serves the Northern Black Hills including Lead, Deadwood, Spearfish, Belle Fourche, and Sundance, WY.
