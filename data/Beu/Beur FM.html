Beur FM is a French local radio station catering mainly to France's Maghrebi communities including: Algerians, Moroccans and Tunisians.

Beur FM broadcasts in three languages: French (60% of the time) and Arabic, including Arab Maghrebi dialects (20% of the time) and Berber languages (20% of the time).
