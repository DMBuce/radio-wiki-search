KDKD (1280 AM) is a radio station broadcasting an Oldies format. Licensed to Clinton, Missouri, United States, the station is currently owned by Alpha Media and licensed to Alpha Media Licensee LLC. KDKD features programming from ABC Radio and Dial Global.
