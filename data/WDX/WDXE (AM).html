WDXE (1370 AM) is a classic country music formatted broadcast radio station licensed to Lawrenceburg, Tennessee, serving Lawrenceburg and Lawrence County, Tennessee. WDXE is owned and operated by Roger Wright, through licensee Radio 7 Media, LLC.
