KDWG (90.9 FM, "90.9 The Dawg") is a radio station licensed to serve Dillon, Montana, United States. The station is owned by The University of Montana Western. It airs a Variety format.

The station was assigned the KDWG call letters by the Federal Communications Commission on December 4, 2000.

The station now has a web app that you can find by googling KDWG or by using the playstore for android.
