KGIA-LP (92.9 FM) was a low-power FM radio station broadcasting a Christian radio format. Licensed to Grand Island, in the U.S. state of Nebraska, it served the Grand Island-Kearney area. The station was owned by Grand Island Adventist Educational Radio. It was affiliated with Radio 74 Internationale, an international religious broadcasting network.
