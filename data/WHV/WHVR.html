WHVR (1280 kHz) is a commercial AM radio station in Hanover, Pennsylvania, serving the York radio market. The station is owned by Forever Media, through licensee FM Radio Licenses, LLC. It airs an Adult Contemporary radio format, primarily playing songs from the 1980s through Today. Programming is also heard on FM translator W237EN at 95.3 MHz.

WHVR also carries Baltimore Orioles baseball games.
