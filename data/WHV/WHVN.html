WHVN (1240 AM) is an adult standards radio station licensed to Charlotte, North Carolina, United States. The station was owned by WHVN Inc. The station was simulcast onto WAVO at 1150 AM and was also heard on 104.3 FM W282BP.

WHVN was licensed by the U.S. Federal Communications Commission (FCC) for digital HD operation.

WHVN was scheduled to cease operations on March 31, 2020 but returned November 2.
