WFAE (90.7 FM) is a non-commercial public radio station in Charlotte, North Carolina. It is the flagship NPR news/talk station in the Charlotte region. The station's main studio is located at One University Place in the University City neighborhood of northeast Charlotte, and a satellite downtown Charlotte studio is located in Spirit Square on North College Street. WFAE's broadcast tower is located at (35°17′15.0″N 80°41′44.0″W).

WFAE broadcasts using HD Radio.
