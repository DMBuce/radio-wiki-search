WFAN-FM (101.9 FM), branded Sports Radio 66 and 101.9 FM or The Fan, is a commercial sports radio station licensed to New York City. The station is owned and operated by Entercom, and is simulcast with WFAN (660 AM). WFAN-FM's studios are located in the combined Entercom facility in the Hudson Square neighborhood of Manhattan and its transmitter is located at the Empire State Building. The station is also simulcast on WJFK-HD3 in Washington, D.C..
