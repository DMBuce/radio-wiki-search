WFAW (940 AM) is a radio station broadcasting an oldies format. WFAW previously aired news and talk and oldies music, and for many years featured a country music format. Licensed to Fort Atkinson, Wisconsin, United States, the station mainly serves the communities of Jefferson County. The station is owned by Magnum Media and features programming from ABC Radio, Jones Radio Network and Westwood One. Along with its sister station WKCH, WFAW is also a major broadcaster of UW-Whitewater Warhawks athletics.

On October 5, 2020 WFAW changed their format from news/talk to oldies, branded as "Oldies 940".
