WFKX (95.7 FM, "96 Kix") is a radio station broadcasting an urban adult contemporary music format. Licensed to Henderson, Tennessee, United States, the station is currently owned by Thomas Radio, LLC, Debtor-in-Possession and features programming from Citadel Media.
