WBEB (101.1 FM, "B101.1") is a commercial FM radio station licensed to serve Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. Owned by Entercom, the station broadcasts an adult contemporary format. The broadcast tower used by the station is in the Roxborough section of Philadelphia at (40°2′19.7″N 75°14′12.8″W), while its studios are co-located within Entercom's corporate headquarters in Center City, Philadelphia

In addition to a standard analog transmission, WBEB broadcasts over two HD Radio channels, and is available online via Radio.com.

Prior to 2018, WBEB was co-founded and owned by broadcaster Jerry Lee, and was recognized as one of the last independently owned and operated major market FM stations remaining in the United States. WBEB has been a top-ranking station in the Philadelphia Nielsen Audio ratings since the early 1990s; this dominance has been further demonstrated during the holiday season, where WBEB has historically seen the largest ratings gains among U.S. radio stations that switch to Christmas music.
