WBEN (930 kHz) is a commercial AM radio station in Buffalo, New York. It broadcasts a news/talk radio format and is owned by Entercom. The studios and offices are located on Corporate Parkway in Amherst, New York, along with its sister stations.

WBEN is powered at 5,000 watts. At night, it uses a directional antenna to protect other stations on 930 AM. The signal covers Buffalo and its suburbs, the Niagara Falls region and Western New York as well as part of Southern Ontario. WBEN's transmitter is located on South Parkway at Bush Road in Grand Island, New York. Programming is also heard on 98.5 WKSE's HD3 subchannel.
