4BH 882 is the on-air identifier (ACMA callsign: 4BH) of a radio station in Brisbane, the state capital of Queensland, Australia. It broadcasts on a frequency is 882 kHz in the AM radio band and is simulcasted in DAB+ on channel 9B. The station directs its programming for the baby boomer market. It is owned by Nine Entertainment Co.
