WXYK (105.9 FM, "105.9 The Monkey") is a Rhythmic CHR/Top 40 radio station based in Pascagoula-Gulfport–Biloxi, Mississippi. The station is owned by Telesouth Communications, Inc, and they broadcast with an ERP of 25 kW. The Monkey's signal can be heard as far as Grand Bay to Bay St Louis and far north as Wiggins and Lucedale.
