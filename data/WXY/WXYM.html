WXYM-FM (96.1 FM, "Mix 96.1 and 107.3") is a radio station broadcasting a hot adult contemporary music radio format. Licensed to Tomah, Wisconsin, United States, the station serves the greater La Crosse, Wisconsin, area. The station is currently owned by Magnum Broadcasting and features programming from AP Radio, Premiere Networks and Jones Radio Network. The station can also be heard on WKBH-HD3.
