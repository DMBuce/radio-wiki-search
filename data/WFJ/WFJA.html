WFJA (105.5 FM) is a classic hits formatted radio station licensed to Sanford, North Carolina, US. The station is currently owned by WWGP Broadcasting Corporation. According to the Sanford Herald Newspaper of February 6, 2019, WFJA and WWGP have been sold by Susan Feindel to Sandhills Broadcasting Group with principal owner Jon Hockaday as the buyer.
