Hellenic Radio is a Greek language radio station, broadcast from studios in Marrickville to Sydney and Melbourne, Australia. It is owned and operated by Costas Nikolis, who had previously produced a Greek language program on community radio station 2NBC.

The station was launched in 1996. In addition to local programs, the station also rebroadcasts shows from RIK Triton and Skai 100.3.
