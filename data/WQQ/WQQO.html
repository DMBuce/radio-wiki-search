WQQO (105.5 FM) – branded Q105 – is a commercial contemporary hit radio station licensed to serve Sylvania, Ohio. Owned by Cumulus Media, the station services the Toledo metropolitan area and much of surrounding Northwest Ohio. Both the WQQO studios and station transmitter reside in Toledo's Scott Park neighborhood. Besides a standard analog transmission, WQQO broadcasts over two HD Radio channels, and is available online; WQQO-HD2 carries an active rock format branded as "100.7 The Zone", which is relayed over low-power analog translator W264AK.
