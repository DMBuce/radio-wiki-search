WKHW (88.5 FM, "K-LOVE") is a radio station broadcasting a contemporary Christian music format. Licensed to Halifax, Pennsylvania, United States, the station is owned by Educational Media Foundation (EMF).

WKHW previously carried the call sign WLVU. It was swapped with the former WRQQ in Nashville, Tennessee when EMF purchased that station on July 15, 2012. From June 13, 2017 to September 1, 2017, the station used the WLVO call sign; the call sign was transferred from WFFY in San Carlos Park, Florida after EMF's sale of that station, and was then moved to a station in Providence, Rhode Island following its acquisition of the former WBRU.
