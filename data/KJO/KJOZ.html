KJOZ (880 AM, 102.5 FM; "Rumba 102.5") is an AM radio station, paired with an FM relay translator. Licensed to Conroe, Texas, KJOZ primarily serves the northern Houston, Texas metropolitan area. The station's broadcasts a Tropical music format.
