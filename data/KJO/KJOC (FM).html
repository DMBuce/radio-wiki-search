KJOC (93.5 MHz) is a commercial FM radio station licensed to Bettendorf, Iowa, and serving the Quad Cities radio market. It broadcasts an active rock radio format, known as "I-Rock 93.5." KJOC is owned by Townsquare Media, with studios and offices on Brady Street in Davenport, Iowa, along with co-owned 96.9 WXLP, 99.7 KBEA, 104.9 KIIK-FM and KBOB 1170 AM.

KJOC has an effective radiated power (ERP) of 6,000 watts. Its transmitter is located on 205th Street in rural LeClaire in Scott County.
