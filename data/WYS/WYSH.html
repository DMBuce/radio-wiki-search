WYSH (1380 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a classic country music format. Licensed to serve Clinton, Tennessee, United States, the station serves the Knoxville area. The station is currently owned by Clinton Broadcasters, Inc. and features programming from Dial Global and Motor Racing Network. WYSH's programming can also be heard on FM at 101.1 MHz over translator W266AM, which operates at 250 watts.
