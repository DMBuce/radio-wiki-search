WYSZ (89.3 FM) is a radio station licensed to Maumee, Ohio. It is the flagship for Yes! FM, a non-profit, Christian Hot CHR radio station in Toledo, Ohio. WYSZ's tower is located near the intersection of Nebraska Avenue and McCord Road in Springfield Township, west of US 23.
