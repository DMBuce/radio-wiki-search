Broadcasting System of Niigata Inc. (株式会社新潟放送, Kabushikigaisha Niigata hoso, BSN) is a broadcasting station affiliated with Japan News Network (JNN) in Niigata, Niigata. It broadcasts in Niigata Prefecture, Japan and was established on December 24, 1958.
