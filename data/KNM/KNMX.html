KNMX (540 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a Spanish Variety format. Licensed to Las Vegas, New Mexico, United States, the station serves the Santa Fe area. The station is currently owned by Sangre De Cristo Broadcasting Co, Inc. and features programming from CNN Radio.

540 AM is a Canadian and Mexican clear-channel frequency. KNMX must reduce nighttime power in order to prevent skywave interference to the dominant Class A stations on this frequency.
