Unilag FM is an educational focused radio station, in Lagos. It was established in 2004.

Unilag FM operates on 103.1 (MHz) frequency. The radio station exist to educate students and the general public and the radio station is the first Campus radio to receive a licence and the first to go on air in 2004, Lagos, Lagos State, Nigeria.
