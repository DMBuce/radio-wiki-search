WEBR (1440 AM) is a radio station that broadcasts an adult standards format. Licensed to Niagara Falls, New York, United States, the station serves the Niagara Falls area from studios in Kenmore. The station is currently owned by William Yuhnke, through licensee Kenmore Broadcasting Communications, Inc.
