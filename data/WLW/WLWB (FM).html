WLWB (103.9 FM, "K-LOVE") is a broadcast radio station licensed to Lawrenceburg, Tennessee, serving Lawrenceburg and Lawrence County, Tennessee. WLWB is owned and operated by Educational Media Foundation, and broadcasts EMF's contemporary Christian K-LOVE format.
