WELE (1380 AM) is a radio station currently broadcasting a News Talk Information format. Licensed to Ormond Beach, Florida, United States, the station serves the Daytona Beach area. The station is currently owned by Bethune Cookman University and features programming from Westwood One, ESPN Radio and CNN Radio.

Radio personalities include: Don Imus, Paul Carpenella, Dr. Joy Browne, Pavlina Osta, and Clark Howard.
