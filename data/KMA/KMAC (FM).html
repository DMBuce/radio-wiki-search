KMAC (90.3 FM) is a radio station licensed to Antlers, Oklahoma, United States. The station is currently owned by Oklahoma Catholic Broadcasting, Inc.

KMAC broadcasts a Catholic Religious format to the Antlers, Oklahoma, area.
