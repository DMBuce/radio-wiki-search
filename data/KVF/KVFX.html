KVFX, also known as VFX, is a Top 40 (CHR) radio station broadcasting in the Logan- Salt Lake City-Ogden, Utah metropolitan area. It is owned by the Sun Valley Radio, Inc. KVFX-FM also broadcasts via translator in Tremonton at 98.3 MHz. The Station has been branded "Utah's VFX". The station's studios are in Logan and the main transmitter (for 94.5 FM) is in Newton with a booster on 94.5 FM in Tremonton.
