KINN (1270 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a News Talk Information format. Licensed to Alamogordo, New Mexico, United States, the station is currently owned by Burt Broadcasting and features programming from ABC Radio, Premiere Radio Networks and Talk Radio Network.
