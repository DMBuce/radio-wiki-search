KBUK (104.9 FM) is a terrestrial American radio station broadcasting a Classic Country format, in simulcast with AM sister station KVLG. It is licensed to La Grange, Texas, United States. The station is owned by KBUK Radio, Inc. and features programming from ABC Radio.
