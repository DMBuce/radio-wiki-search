KSVB 94.1 FM is a low-power FM radio station licensed to serve the communities located within the Big Bear Valley of California, which includes the communities of Big Bear City, Big Bear Lake, Fawnskin, Sugarloaf, Bladwin Lake, Erwin Lake, Holcomb Valley, Bluff Lake, and surrounding areas.

KSVB airs a variety format consists of "oldies and obscurities" - not the top 100, but music from the past, the B Sides, one hit wonders, rarities, and new artists.

The station was assigned the KSVB-LP call letters by the Federal Communications Commission on March 3, 2014.

KSVB is owned by Bear Valley Seniors and Veterans Assistance, Inc.
