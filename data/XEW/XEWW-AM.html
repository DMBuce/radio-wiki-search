XEWW-AM (690 kHz) is a commercial AM radio station licensed to the Tijuana/Rosarito area of Baja California, Mexico. Its studios and offices are located in Burbank, California, United States and it is leased by H&H USA, whose owners are Vivian Huo and Julian Sant.

XEWW carries a Chinese-language Music radio format, pop music in Mandarin and Cantonese.
