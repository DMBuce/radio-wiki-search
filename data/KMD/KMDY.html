KMDY is a Christian radio station licensed to Keokuk, Iowa, broadcasting on 90.9Â MHz FM. KMDY serves the areas of Keokuk, Iowa, Fort Madison, Iowa, and Carthage, Illinois. The station is owned by Cornerstone Community Radio.

KMDY began broadcasting in 2001, and was owned by Moody Bible Institute, running 30 watts. In 2005 KMDY was purchased by Cornerstone Community Radio. In 2006, its power was increased to 7,700 watts.
