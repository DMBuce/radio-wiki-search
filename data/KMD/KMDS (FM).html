KMDS is a radio station broadcasting on a frequency of 107.1 MHz on the FM band. KMDS is currently owned by Sangre de Cristo Broadcasting. It features a Sports format known as 107.1 THE FAN.

The station broadcasts New Mexico State University sporting events.
