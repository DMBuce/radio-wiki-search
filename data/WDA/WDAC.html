WDAC (94.5 FM) is a commercial FM radio station licensed to serve Lancaster, Pennsylvania. The station is owned by WDAC Radio Company and broadcasts a Christian radio format featuring programming from Salem Communications.

WDAC uses HD Radio and broadcasts a Christian radio format with the slogan "HOPE Music for the Heart!" on its HD2 subchannel.
