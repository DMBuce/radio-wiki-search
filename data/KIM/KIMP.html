KIMP (960 AM, 104.3 FM; "La Super K") is a terrestrial American radio station, relayed by an FM translator, broadcasting a Regional Mexican format. Licensed to Mount Pleasant, Texas, United States, the station is owned by East Texas Broadcasting, Inc.
