WPEA (90.5 FM) is a non-commercial educational radio station licensed to serve Exeter, New Hampshire. The station is owned by the Trustees of Phillips Exeter Academy. It airs a Variety format. The WPEA studios are located in the Phelps Academy Center on the campus of the Phillips Exeter Academy.

WPEA is the oldest continuously operated high school radio station in the United States. The station was assigned the WPEA call letters by the Federal Communications Commission.
