WMPS (1210 AM) – branded as Sunny 103.1 – is a commercial adult standards radio station licensed to Bartlett, Tennessee. Owned by Flynn Broadcasting, the station serves the Memphis metropolitan area. In addition to a standard analog transmission, the station also simulcasts over Memphis low-power FM translator W276BH (103.1 FM) and Earle, Arkansas full-power station KHGA (103.9 FM), and streams online.
