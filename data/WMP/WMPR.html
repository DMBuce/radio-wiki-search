WMPR (90.1 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a Variety format. WMPR is a community station which specializes in gospel and blues but also features other forms of music as well as several community-oriented talk shows. Licensed to Jackson, Mississippi, United States, the station serves the Jackson MS area. The station is currently owned by J.C. Maxwell Broadcasting Group, Inc.

Its studios are located in Jackson, west of downtown, and the transmitter site is in Florence, Mississippi.
