WMPA (93.1 FM) is a radio station operating on 93.1 MHz. It is licensed to Ferrysburg, Michigan. It airs a classic rock music format. It is owned by WGHN, Incorporated, owner of WGHN-AM-FM in Grand Haven, Michigan.
