WMPO (1390 AM with translator W279CE 103.7 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a Sports radio format. Licensed to Middleport-Pomeroy, USA. The station is currently owned by Positive Radio Group, Inc. of Ohio and features programming from ABC Radio and ESPN Radio. According to http://wyvk.com/wmpo.html, the station now is FOX Sports. Joe Hill of Tucson AZ, began his radio career in 1977 at WMPO, under the air name "David Barnes" before moving on to WWJM Zanesville in 1978.
