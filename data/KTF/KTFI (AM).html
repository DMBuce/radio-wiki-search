KTFI (1270 AM, "Funny 1270") is a radio station broadcasting a comedy format. Licensed to Twin Falls, Idaho, United States, the station serves the Twin Falls area. The station is currently owned by Salt & Light Radio, Inc. and features programming from EWTN.
