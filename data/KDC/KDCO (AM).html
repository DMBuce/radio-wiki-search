KDCO (1340 kHz) is a commercial AM radio station licensed to Denver, Colorado. The station is owned by Gregory, James, and Jeffrey Merilatt, through licensee 4-K's LLLP, and airs a sports radio format, branded as "Mile High Sports." The station is also heard on an FM translator station, 104.7 K284CI in Denver.

KDCO has local sports hosts most of the day, while nights and weekends syndicated sports programming from SB Nation Radio is heard.
