KDCZ (107.7 FM, "Z Rock 107.7") is a radio station broadcasting a mainstream rock format. KDCZ is licensed to St. Charles, Minnesota, and serves the Rochester, Minnesota metropolitan area. The station is owned by Townsquare Media.
