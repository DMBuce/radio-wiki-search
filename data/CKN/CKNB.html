CKNB is a Canadian radio station broadcasting in Campbellton, New Brunswick at 950 kHz (AM). The station currently airs an adult contemporary format and is owned and operated by the Maritime Broadcasting System. The station has been on the air since December 27, 1939. CKNB's studios are located at 74 Water Street in Downtown Campbellton.

CKNB originally broadcast at 1210 AM until it moved to 1240 in 1941, and then to its current AM position at 950 in 1942.

CKNB can be heard over much of Atlantic Canada at night, using a good radio.
