CKNC-FM is a radio station in Simcoe, Ontario, broadcasting on 99.7 MHz with an oldies/classic hits format branded as Oldies 99.7. The station is owned by My Broadcasting Corporation, who first applied to operate a second station in Simcoe on June 9, 2017. The CRTC approved the application, and CKNC-FM signed on the air on September 1, 2017, with the first song being The Rolling Stones' hit Start Me Up.
