CKNX is a Canadian radio station, which broadcasts at 920 AM in Wingham, Ontario. The station broadcasts a classic country music & news format. The station can be most reliably heard within the boundaries of Grey County, Bruce County, Huron County, northern Perth County and northern Wellington County. However, it can be heard acceptably without much interference as far as Elora on a good radio receiver.

CKNX has been playing a country music format for a major part of its history.
