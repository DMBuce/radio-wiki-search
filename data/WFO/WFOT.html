WFOT 89.5 MHz "Annunciation Radio" is a Catholic radio station licensed to Lexington, Ohio and serving the Mansfield, Ashland and Mount Vernon areas of the northern Mid-Ohio region airing Catholic programming from EWTN Global Catholic Radio and Ave Maria Radio as a local repeater of originating station WNOC 89.7 MHz licensed in Bowling Green, Ohio with main studios located in Toledo, Ohio and locally serving the southeast corner of the Toledo Diocese and the Catholic parishes of the Ashland, Mansfield and Mount Vernon area

WFOT can be heard on a typical car radio along I-71 between Delaware and New London and online
