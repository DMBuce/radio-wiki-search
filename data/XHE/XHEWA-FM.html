XHEWA-FM/XEWA-AM is a Class A Mexican radio station that serves the San Luis Potosí area, with its transmitter in Soledad de Graciano Sánchez. XEWA broadcasts on 540 kHz, a Canadian and Mexican clear-channel frequency.
