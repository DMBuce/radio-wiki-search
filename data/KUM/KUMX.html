KUMX (106.7 FM) is an American radio station broadcasting an Adult Contemporary radio station. Licensed to North Fort Polk, Louisiana, United States, the station serves the area surrounding Fort Polk and Vernon parish and surrounding areas. The station is currently owned by West Central Broadcasting
