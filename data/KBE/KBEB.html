KBEB (92.5 FM, "92.5 The Breeze") is a commercial radio station that is licensed to Sacramento, California, United States and serves the Sacramento area. The station is owned by iHeartMedia and airs a soft adult contemporary format. The KBEB studios are located in North Sacramento near Arden Fair Mall, and its transmitter is near Rio Linda. KBEB broadcasts in HD Radio.
