WRSS (1410 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a Spanish News/Talk format. Licensed to San Sebastián, Puerto Rico, United States, it serves the Puerto Rico area. The station is currently owned by Angel Vera-Maury. Its station licensee is Radio Progreso, Inc.
