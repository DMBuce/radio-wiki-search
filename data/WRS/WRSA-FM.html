WRSA-FM (96.9 FM, "Mix 96.9") is an adult contemporary-formatted radio station serving the Huntsville, Alabama, area.

The broadcast transmitter and tower for WRSA-FM is located on Brindlee Mountain, and the station's studios are located at 8402 Memorial Parkway SW (US 231) in Huntsville.
