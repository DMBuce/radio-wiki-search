WRSO (810 AM), also known as Acción 97.9, is a radio station licensed to Orlo Vista, Florida, serving the Orlando area. The station is owned by Star Over Orlando and operated by iHeartMedia under a local marketing agreement. The station broadcasts Spanish-language news and talk. WRSO's programming is also heard on translator station W250CE (97.9 FM) in Kissimmee.
