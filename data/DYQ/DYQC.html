DYQC (106.7 FM), broadcasting as 1067 Home Radio, is a radio station owned and operated by Aliw Broadcasting Corporation. Its studios are located at the G/F Fortune Life Bldg., Osmeña Blvd. Cebu City, and its transmitter is located at the 3/F Ludo and Luym Bldg., Plaridel St., cor. Osmeña Blvd., Cebu City. It operates daily from 5:00 AM to 10:00 PM.
