DYQN (89.5 FM), broadcasting as 895 Home Radio Iloilo, is a radio station owned and operated by the Insular Broadcasting Systems Inc. Its studio and transmitter are located at Iloilo City. The main format of the station is a fusion of easy listening music and local produced content consisting of news and talk shows.

Temporarily, it operates from 6 am to 9 pm due to the Community Quarantine brought by the COVID-19 Pandemic.
