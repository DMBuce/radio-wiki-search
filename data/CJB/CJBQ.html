CJBQ is a full-service radio station in Belleville, Ontario, Canada. It is owned by Quinte Broadcasting along with Mix 97 and Rock 107. CJBQ broadcasts in C-QUAM AM Stereo with 10,000 watts from a site south of Belleville and Trenton in Prince Edward County. The antenna is a six-tower array with differing patterns day and night, to protect Class-A clear-channel station XEROK-AM in Ciudad Ju√°rez, Mexico, as well as neighbouring stations CKLW in Windsor and CJAD in Montreal. As of November 2020, CJBQ is the only remaining Canadian AM radio station operating between Ottawa and the Greater Toronto Area.

CJBQ's format includes country music, oldies and adult contemporary music. It is mostly locally programmed, with the exception of John Tesh's syndicated program that airs during evenings.CJBQ also carries live broadcasts of Belleville Senators hockey games.
