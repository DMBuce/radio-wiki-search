KLMS (1480 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a sports format. Licensed to Lincoln, Nebraska, United States, the station serves the Lincoln area. The station is currently owned by Alpha Media.

KLMS is a sports talk station and has been an affiliate of ESPN Radio since 2000. Some programming is also provided by Premiere Radio Networks (including The Jim Rome Show and Westwood One). On January 26, 2010, KLMS dropped its ESPN Radio format and flipped to an oldies format as simply KLMS 1480. In March 2010, KLMS returned to the sports format and brought back their ESPN affiliation.
