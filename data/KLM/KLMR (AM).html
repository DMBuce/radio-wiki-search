KLMR (920 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a news/talk format. Licensed to Lamar, Colorado, United States, the station is currently owned by Aaron Leiker through licensee 25-7 Media, Inc., and features programming from Citadel Broadcasting and Westwood One.

KLMR was originally on 1340 kHz. It moved to 920 kHz in 1955.

On June 11, 2018, KLMR changed their format from classic country to news/talk/sports.
