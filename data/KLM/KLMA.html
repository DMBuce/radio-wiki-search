KLMA (96.5 FM) is a radio station licensed to serve the community of Hobbs, New Mexico. The station is owned by Ojeda Broadcasting, Inc., and airs a Spanish variety format.

The station was assigned the KLMA call letters by the Federal Communications Commission on November 10, 1993.
