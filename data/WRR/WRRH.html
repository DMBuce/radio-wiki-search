WRRH (106.1 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a Contemporary Christian format. Licensed to Hormigueros, Puerto Rico, United States, it serves the Puerto Rico area. The station is currently owned by Renacer Broadcasters Corporation.

The call letter "WRRH" also refer to the radio station of the Ramapo-Indian Hills School District in Franklin Lakes-Oakland-Wyckoff, New Jersey until 1989. It was a part-time, student-run radio station at 88.7 FM.
