KTLF (90.5 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a Contemporary Worship format. During the Christmas season KTLF plays Christmas music that is mostly focused on Christ-centered songs and carols and some secular Christmas songs but all featuring Christian Music artists. Licensed to Colorado Springs, Colorado, United States, it serves the Colorado Springs area. The station is currently owned by Educational Communications Of Colorado Springs, Inc.
