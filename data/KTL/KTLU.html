KTLU (1580 AM, 103.9 FM) is a terrestrial American radio station, relayed by an FM translator, currently broadcasting a classic hits format, and using the branding "K-Hits". Licensed to Rusk, Texas, United States, the station serves the Tyler-Longview area. The station is currently owned by Paul Coates, through licensee Coates Consulting, LLC, and features programming from Citadel Broadcasting.
