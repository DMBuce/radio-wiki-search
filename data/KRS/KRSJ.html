KRSJ (100.5 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a country music format. Licensed to Durango, Colorado, United States, the station serves the Four Corners area. The station is currently owned by Four Corners Broadcasting, LLC and features programming from Fox News Radio and Westwood One.
