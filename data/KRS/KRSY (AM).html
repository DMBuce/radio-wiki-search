KRSY (1230 AM, "Alamo AM") is a radio station broadcasting a talk/personality format. Licensed to Alamogordo, New Mexico, United States, the station is currently owned by WP Broadcasting, LLC and features programming from Citadel Media, ESPN Radio and Premiere Radio Networks.
