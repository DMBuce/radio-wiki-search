KHRT (106.9 FM and 1320 AM) are two Christian radio stations located in Minot, North Dakota owned and operated by Faith Broadcasting. KHRT-FM concentrates on contemporary Christian music (CCM), while the AM station concentrates on a Southern gospel format. Both stations air satellite fed programming from Salem Communications during the overnights and weekends.

The FM station is also rebroadcast on several translators (low-powered rebroadcasters) outside of its main listening area. They no longer stream on the internet.

Though they are classified as commercial radio stations by the FCC, KHRT acts as a nonprofit organization using commercial advertisements as a way to fund, though it also takes donations.
