KHRO (1150 kHz) is a commercial AM radio station in El Paso, Texas. This station is ownered by Entravision Communications. Its studio facilities are located on North Mesa Street/Highway 20 in northwest El Paso. The transmitter is located east of downtown in Ascarate Park, near Texas State Highway Loop 375. KHRO simulcasts the radio format heard on sister station KOFX 92.3 FM.
