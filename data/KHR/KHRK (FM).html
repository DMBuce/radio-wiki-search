KHRK (97.7 FM) is a radio station licensed to serve the community of Hennessey, Oklahoma. The station is owned by Chisholm Trail Broadcasting Co. It airs a classic rock format.

The station was assigned the call sign KHEO by the Federal Communications Commission on August 17, 2018. The station changed its call sign to KHRK on October 19, 2018. KHRK programming is also heard on co-owned 99.7 KNAH's HD radio secondary channel.
