KHRI (90.7 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a Christian Worship format. Licensed to Hollister, California, United States, the station is the flagship station of the Air 1 Christian music radio network and is owned by Educational Media Foundation. On November 4, 2016 KHRI was granted a Federal Communications Commission construction permit to increase the effective radiated power to 6,000 watts.
