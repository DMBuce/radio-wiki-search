KQEP-LP is an FM radio station broadcasting on a frequency of 97.9 Megahertz. KQEP-LP has a Chinese format and is licensed to the city of Saint Paul, Minnesota. The station's 90 watt signal covers most of downtown Minneapolis and portions of St. Paul, Minnesota. It can also be heard as far north as Roseville.
