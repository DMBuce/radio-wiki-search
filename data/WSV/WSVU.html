WSVU (960 AM) – branded The Surf 92.5/101.7 – is a commercial classic rock radio station licensed to North Palm Beach, Florida. Owned by Vic Canales, the station serves West Palm Beach and surrounding areas. Besides a standard analog transmission, the station also simulcasts over low-power FM translators W223CJ (92.5 FM) in West Palm Beach and W269DS (101.7 FM) in North Palm Beach, and streams online. The WSVU studios are located in Palm Beach Gardens, while the station transmitter resides in West Palm Beach.
