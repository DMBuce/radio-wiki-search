Zenith Classic Rock is an independent regional radio station in Ireland broadcasting from Waterford. It is owned by Andy Linton and Andy Green of Total Broadcast Consultants, Ltd. and airs a Classic rock format, targeting an adult audience.

Zenith Classic Rock is one of Ireland's oldest internet stations. It has been airing on terrestrial radio since 2010 and DAB since 2013.
