XHQT-FM/XEQT-AM is a combo radio station in Veracruz, Veracruz. It broadcasts on 800 kHz and 106.9 MHz and is known as La Poderosa. The station is jointly owned by Grupo Radiorama (the Pérez family) and the Ferraez family of Avanradio.
