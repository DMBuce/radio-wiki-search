XEQY-AM/XHQY-FM is a radio station in Toluca, State of Mexico. Broadcasting on 1200 AM, a United States clear-channel frequency, and 103.7 FM, XEQY/XHQY carries the La Bestia Grupera format of its owner, Grupo Audiorama.
