smooth Chill (formerly Koffee) is a digital radio station broadcast by NOVA Entertainment in Sydney, Melbourne, Adelaide and Brisbane. It was launched on 16 April 2009 under the name Koffee, prior to the commencement of DAB+ broadcasts.

In September 2019 , Koffee was rebranded as smooth Chill to extend the smoothfm brand, and its slot on DAB+ radio was replaced by smooth Relax. smooth Chill is currently available for online streaming only.
