Smooth 95.3 (call sign: 2PTV) is a commercial radio station based in Sydney, New South Wales, Australia.

The station is owned by NOVA Entertainment along with sister station Nova 96.9 and currently plays easy listening songs from the late-1950s/early-1960s to the present with a 55+ year span.
