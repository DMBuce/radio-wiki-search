CHOK is a Canadian radio station, licensed to Sarnia, Ontario at 1070 kHz and owned by Blackburn Radio. The station broadcasts a country music format with local news, talk and sports. CHOK also has an FM translator, CHOK-1-FM, broadcasting at 103.9 MHz.
