KYRO is an American radio station in Troy, Missouri and serves the western suburbs of St. Louis. KYRO airs a News/Talk format and is owned by KYRO Group, LLC, and operated by Westplex Broadcasting, LLC.

The station is an affiliate of The Dave Ramsey Show
