KNJR-LP (107.9 MHz, "The Well") is a low-power FM radio station which is licensed to Thousand Oaks, California and serves the Conejo Valley area. Owned by Conejo Radio Ministries, the station airs a Christian Contemporary format. The station went silent for about 1 year starting on January 1, 2018. KOXC-LP in Oxnard, California had an extended coverage as a result of this radio silence. In late December 2018, the station returned to the air in the Conejo Valley.
