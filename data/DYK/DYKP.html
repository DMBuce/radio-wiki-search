DYKP (97.3 FM), on-air as 97.3 Boracay Beach Radio, is a radio station owned by Interactive Broadcast Media and operated under the Dream FM Network. The station's studios are located at the PhilFirst Building, Ayala Avenue, Makati City, and its transmitter is located at Station 3, Boracay.
