DYKI (89.9 FM), broadcasting as 89.9 MemoRieS FM, is a radio station owned and operated by Primax Broadcasting Network. Its studios and transmitter facilities are located at the Vacation Hotel Cebu compound, Juana Osmena St, Cebu City. This station operates daily from 5:00 AM to 10:00 PM.

DYKI is an affiliate station of the Radio Mindanao Network since 2017.
