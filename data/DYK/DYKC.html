DYKC (675 AM) Radyo Ronda is a radio station owned and operated by Radio Philippines Network and Nine Media Corporation. The station's studio is located at RPN Compound, M.L. Quezon St., Brgy. Maguikay, Mandaue, while its transmitter is located at Brgy. Umapad, Mandaue. It operates Mondays to Fridays from 3:30 AM to 11:00 PM, Saturdays from 3:30 AM to 9:15 PM, and Sundays from 3:30 AM to 9:00 PM.
