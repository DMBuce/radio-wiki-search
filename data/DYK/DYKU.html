DYKU, branded as 88.7 Brigada News FM Iloilo, is a music and news FM radio station owned and operated by Brigada Mass Media Corporation in the Philippines. The station's studio is located at Iloilo City and transmitter are located at Cabugao Norte, Pavia, Iloilo.
