DYKA (94.1 FM), broadcasting as 94.1 Spirit FM, is a radio station owned and operated by Kauswagan Broadcasting Corporation, the media arm of the Roman Catholic Diocese of San Jose de Antique. Its studio and transmitter are located at 3/F St.Joseph Center, Gen. Fullon St., San Jose de Buenavista, Antique.
