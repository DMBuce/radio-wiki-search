DYKS (91.9 FM), broadcasting as 91.9 Love Radio, is a commercial FM radio station owned and operated by Manila Broadcasting Company in the Philippines. The station's studio and transmitter is located at The Penthouse, Wilrose Building, Burgos-Locsin St., Bacolod.
