WYXL (97.3 MHz, "Lite Rock 97.3") is a radio station in Ithaca, New York that programs an adult contemporary format. The station has been owned by the Cayuga Radio Group subsidiary of Saga Communications since 2005. It is co-owned with WHCU, WQNY, WIII, and WNYY. It is known as "Lite Rock 97.3." It was known as just "Lite 97" for at least two decades.
