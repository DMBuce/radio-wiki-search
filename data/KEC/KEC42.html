KEC42 (sometimes referred to as Eugene All Hazards) is a NOAA Weather Radio station that serves the Eugene-Springfield metropolitan area and surrounding cities. It is programmed from the National Weather Service forecast office in Portland, Oregon with its transmitter located at Blanton Heights, near Eugene Airport. It broadcasts weather and hazard information for the following counties Lane County, Oregon, Linn County, Oregon, and Benton County, Oregon.
