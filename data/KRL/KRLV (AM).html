KRLV (920 kHz) is a commercial AM radio station in Las Vegas, Nevada, United States, serving the Las Vegas area. Owned by Lotus Communications, KRLV airs a all-sports radio format focused on the Las Vegas Raiders of the National Football League. The station's studios and offices are in the unincorporated community of Spring Valley in Clark County The transmitter is off Wild Jan Drive, northwest of downtown Las Vegas.
