WBOW was a radio station on 1230 AM in Terre Haute, Indiana, which broadcast between 1927 and 2001.

In the early 1990s, the WBOW intellectual unit moved to a new 640 AM facility. 1230 then became WBFX, WZZQ and WBUZ, closing down when its licensee, Contemporary Media, Inc., had all of its licenses cancelled in response to a Federal Communications Commission investigation.
