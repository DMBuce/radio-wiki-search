WBOM (1470 AM) is a radio station licensed to Meriden, Connecticut. broadcasting a talk radio format. WBOM is part of a three station simulcast, along with 610 WSNG, Torrington and the home base, 1360 WDRC, Hartford. The three stations use the slogan "The Talk of Connecticut" featuring a local morning show and syndicated programs the rest of the day, including Michael Savage. The station is owned by Red Wolf Broadcasting and features programming from Fox News Radio, Talk Radio Network and Westwood One. The station provides a blend of issue-oriented talk, news and information.
