WHEO is a Classic Hits and Oldies formatted broadcast radio station licensed to Stuart, Virginia, serving Stuart and Patrick County, Virginia. WHEO is owned and operated by Patrick Community Media, Inc.

The station is an affiliate of the syndicated Pink Floyd program "Floydian Slip."
