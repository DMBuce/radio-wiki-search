WHEZ-LP (95.9 FM, "Easy 95.9 Community Radio") is a low-power radio station broadcasting an easy listening music format. Licensed to Hartsville, South Carolina, United States, the station is currently owned by Lighthouse Gospel Network and features programming from USA Radio Network.
