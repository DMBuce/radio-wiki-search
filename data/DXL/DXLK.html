DXLK (103.9 FM), broadcasting as 103.9 Gold FM, is a radio station owned by Kalayaan Broadcasting System and operated by One Accord Mass Media Management in the Philippines. The station's studio is located in COMPHIL Compound, Purok Kauswagan, Brgy. Apopong, General Santos. It operates daily from 5:00am to 10:00pm.

This station was an affiliate of Ultimate Entertainment under the brand Mango Radio from 2010 to 2013, when Kalayaan Broadcasting System bought the station and renamed it to Gold FM.
