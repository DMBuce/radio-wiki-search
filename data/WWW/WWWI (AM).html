WWWI (1270 AM) is a radio station in Baxter, Minnesota airing a talk format and simulcasting KLKS 100.1 FM Pequot Lakes. Established in 1987 as WJJY, the station is owned by Jimmy D. Birkemeyer's R & J Broadcasting.

On September 16, 2016, Red Rock Radio announced that it would sell WWWI to R & J Broadcasting as part of an eight station deal; the sale was completed on December 21, 2016.
