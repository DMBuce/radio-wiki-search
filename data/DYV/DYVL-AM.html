DYVL (819 AM) Aksyon Radyo is a radio station owned and operated by Cebu Broadcasting Company, a subsidiary of Manila Broadcasting Company. Its studio, offices and transmitter are located along PNP Rd. cor. Maharlika Highway, Brgy. Campetic, Palo, Leyte.

DYVL was formerly located at Brgy. PHHC Seaside, Tacloban until 2013, when Typhoon Yolanda destroyed the station's offices and killed 2 of its reporters.
