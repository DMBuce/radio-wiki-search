DYVR (657 AM) RMN Roxas is a news/talk radio station owned by Radio Mindanao Network. The station's studio and transmitter are located at Brgy. Punta Tabuc, Roxas City. DYVR RMN Roxas operates daily from 4:00 AM to 9:30 PM, except Holy Week of each year where it signs-off at 9:30 PM of Holy Wednesday and lasts until 4:00 AM of Easter Sunday. The main format of the station is, news, public affairs, music, drama and public service.
