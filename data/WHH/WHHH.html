WHHH (96.3 MHz) is a commercial FM radio station in Indianapolis, Indiana, known as Hot 96.3. It airs a rhythmic contemporary radio format and is owned by Urban One, along with WTLC (AM), WTLC-FM, WNOW-FM and WDNI-CD. The radio and TV stations share studios and offices on Meridian Street in Downtown Indianapolis.

WHHH has an effective radiated power (ERP) of 3,300 watts, making it a Class A FM station. The transmitter tower is atop the residential high-rise Riley Towers II on North Alabama Street. WHHH broadcasts in the HD Radio hybrid format.
