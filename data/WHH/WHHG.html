WHHG (92.3 FM), known as "92.3 the Hog", is a classic rock formatted radio station based in Milan, Tennessee. The station is owned by Forever Media, through licensee Forever South Licenses, LLC. WHHG serves Jackson and West Tennessee with an ERP of 100,000 watts at 92.3 MHz. The station can be heard clearly throughout nearly all of West Tennessee, but can be intermittently picked up in Memphis, areas along the Tennessee River, southwestern Kentucky, extreme northern Mississippi, and even in portions of the Arkansas/Missouri delta along the Mississippi River. Previously, the station was "WYNU U92, Classic Hits for U", and before that it was (also with the WYNU calls) "Rock 92.3".
