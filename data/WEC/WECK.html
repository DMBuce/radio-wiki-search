WECK AM FM Radio (1230 kHz) is a commercial AM radio station licensed to Cheektowaga, New York and serving the Buffalo-Niagara Falls metropolitan area. The station airs an oldies music format. The new studios, offices and transmitter are located on Genesee Street in Buffalo's suburban Cheektowaga, which is WECK's official city of license, however the station is a metro Buffalo station. WECK programming is simulcast on three FM translator stations at 102.9, 100.1 and 100.5 MHz. The station is owned by Radio One Buffalo, LLC, a company set up by long–time Buffalo radio DJ, & radio marketing executive, Buddy Shula, former top revenue producer for Entercom Radio Buffalo
