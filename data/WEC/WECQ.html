WECQ (92.1 FM, "Q92") is a contemporary hit radio music formatted radio station licensed to Destin, Florida, United States, and serving the Fort Walton Beach area. Owned and operated by Community Broadcasters, LLC, the station broadcasts at 92.1 MHz with an ERP of 25 kW.
