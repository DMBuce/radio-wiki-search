DYXY (99.1 FM), on-air as 99.1 iFM RMN Tacloban, is a news/talk and music radio station owned and operated by Radio Mindanao Network in the Philippines. The station's studio and transmitter is located at #181 Goldtrade Bldg., P. Burgos St., Tacloban City.
