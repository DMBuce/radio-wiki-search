WKMZ-LP is a silent broadcast radio station licensed to Ruckersville, Virginia and serving Greene County, Virginia. WKMZ-LP is owned by Gateway Media, consisting of former Stu-Comm, Inc. board member Jon G. Hall, WNRN announcer John "Rusty" Cempre, and Robert M. Johnson. The station previously broadcast an oldies format, simulcasting the programming of WREN-LP 97.9 FM in Charlottesville.
