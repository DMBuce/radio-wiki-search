KPCP (88.3 FM) is a radio station licensed to serve the community of New Roads, Louisiana. The station is owned by Stop the Violence/Save the Children, Inc., and airs a variety format.

The station was assigned the KPCP call letters by the Federal Communications Commission on December 27, 2007.
