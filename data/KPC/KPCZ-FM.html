KPCZ-FM (106.7 FM) is a classic country radio station, branded as "Pure Country 106.7, Acadiana's Home of the Country Superstars". Licensed to Rayne, Louisiana, it broadcasts to the central Acadiana area, including Lafayette. The station is owned by Broadcast Partners Inc. Its studios are in Crowley and the transmitter is located north of Crowley.

The classic country format was introduced on October 6, 2014. KPCZ-FM had previously broadcast a Cajun music format since January 2012, which had in turn replaced an earlier classic country format under the station's previous KLEJ call sign.
