WTTC (1550 AM, "True Oldies") is an oldies radio station in Towanda, Pennsylvania, United States.

WTTC AM 1550 is simulcast with WTZN 1310 AM Troy, Pennsylvania. Both stations air Westwood One's Good Time Oldies format.
