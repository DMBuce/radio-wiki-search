WTHB-FM, also known as Praise 96.9, is a Christian radio station with an urban gospel format located in the Augusta, Georgia area. The station is licensed by the FCC to the city of Wrens, Georgia to broadcast on 96.9 FM with an ERP of 6.2Â kW. The station's studios (which are shared with its other sister stations) and AM simulcast transmitter are co-located at the aptly named intersection of Broadcast Drive and Radio Station Road in North Augusta, South Carolina, while the FM transmitter is southwest of Fort Gordon.

Praise 96.9 is home to the Yolanda Adams Morning Show.
