WTHU is an Adult Standards, Classic Hits, Classic Top 40, and Oldies formatted broadcast radio station licensed to Thurmont, Maryland, serving Northern Frederick County, Maryland. WTHU is owned and operated by Christian Radio Coalition, Inc.
