WCTS (1030 AM; "97.9 FM / 1030 AM, The Bible Station") is a radio station licensed to Maplewood, Minnesota, and serving the Twin Cities metropolitan area with a non-commercial fundamentalist Christian format. WCTS is owned by the Central Baptist Theological Seminary of Minneapolis, hence the call letters. Studios are located in Plymouth, while its transmitter is located in Woodbury.
