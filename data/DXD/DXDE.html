DXDE, branded as 100.7 One Radio News FM, is a radio station owned and operated by Rizal Memorial Colleges Broadcasting Corporation. Its studios and transmitters are located at Purok Bautista, Brgy. Mankilam, Tagum City, Davao del Norte.
