KPUS (104.5 FM, "Classic Rock 104.5") is a radio station broadcasting a classic rock music format. Licensed to Gregory, Texas, United States, the station serves the Corpus Christi area. The station is currently owned by John Bushman, through licensee ICA Radio, Ltd., and features programming from Westwood One. Its studios are located along South Padre Island Drive in Corpus Christi, and the transmitter is in Ingleside, Texas.
