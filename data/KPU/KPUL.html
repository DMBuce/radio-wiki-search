KPUL (101.7 MHz, "Pulse 101.7") is a Christian CHR (Top 40) formatted FM radio station. It is licensed to Winterset, Iowa, with studios located on 335th Street in Waukee. It is one of only a few independent stations in the Des Moines metropolitan area radio market.

The transmitter is off Quail Ridge Lane in Winterset. In Ames, the station can also be heard on 25 watt translator station K238AN at 95.5 MHz.
