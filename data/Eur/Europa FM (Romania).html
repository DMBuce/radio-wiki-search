Europa FM is a Romanian radio station, launched on 26 May 2000 by Lagardere Active Radio. It is appreciated for news and music selection and also for the best signal-coverage of Romania. Many important Romanian journalists are part of Europa FM team - Andreea Esca, Cristian Tudor Popescu, Cătălin Tolontan, Vlad Petreanu, Ovidiu Ioanițoaia and many more.

The morning show, called Deșteptarea, is one of the best radio shows in Romania
