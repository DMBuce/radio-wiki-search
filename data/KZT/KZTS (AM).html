KZTS (1380 kHz) is a commercial AM radio station licensed to North Little Rock, Arkansas, and serving the Little Rock radio market. The station is owned by Salem Media Group, and broadcasts a gospel music radio format.

Programming is simulcast on a translator station to allow Little Rock listeners to hear the station on FM. K288EZ broadcasts at 105.5 MHz.
