KZTL (93.5 FM) is a radio station licensed to Paxton, Nebraska, United States. The station airs the Big D & Bubba Morning Show; Farm News nearly every hour during the weekday and plays exclusively pop and bro country. The station serves the North Platte and Ogallala, Nebraska area in western Nebraska. The station is currently owned by Legacy Communications, LLC, from Grand Island, Ne.
