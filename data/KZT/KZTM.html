KZTM (102.9 FM) is a Regional Mexican radio station serving the Tacoma and Olympia, Washington area. Owned and operated by Bustos Media, through licensee Bustos Media Holdings, LLC, the station is licensed to Centralia, Washington. The transmitter site is in Capitol State Forest near Olympia, while its studios are located in Kent.
