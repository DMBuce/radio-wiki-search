WFCF (88.5 FM) is a radio station broadcasting an eclectic college radio format. Licensed to St. Augustine, Florida, United States. The station is owned by Flagler College. The F.C.C. originally issued the WFCF callsign on November 5, 1992. WFCF airs a college radio format, with multiple forms of music and speech.
