Echo of Moscow (Russian: Э́хо Москвы́, romanized: Ekho Moskvy) is a 24/7 commercial Russian radio station based in Moscow. It broadcasts in many Russian cities, some of the former Soviet republics (through partnerships with local radio stations), and via the Internet. The current editor-in-chief is Alexei Venediktov.
