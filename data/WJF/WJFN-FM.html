WJFN-FM is a news and conservative talk-formatted broadcast radio station licensed to Goochland, Virginia, serving Goochland and Goochland County, Virginia. WJFN-FM is owned by John Fredericks, through licensee MAGA Radio Network, LLC. Fredericks hosts the weekday morning show at the station, with other daily shows including syndicated talkers Michael Savage and Dennis Prager.
