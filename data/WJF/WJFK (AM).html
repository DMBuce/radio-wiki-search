WJFK (1580 AM; "El Zol Deportes") is a radio station broadcasting a Spanish-language sports talk format. Licensed to the suburb of Morningside, Maryland with studios in Southeast DC in the Navy Yard neighborhood, it serves the Washington, D.C. metropolitan area. The station is currently owned by Entercom.
