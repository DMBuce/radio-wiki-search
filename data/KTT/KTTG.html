KTTG (96.3 FM, ESPN Sportsradio 96.3) is a radio station broadcasting a Sports radio format. Licensed to Mena, Arkansas, United States, the station serves the Hot Springs area. The station is currently owned by Pearson Broadcasting of Mena and features programming from ESPN Radio.
