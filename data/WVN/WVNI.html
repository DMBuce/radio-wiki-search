WVNI (95.1 FM) is a radio station licensed to Nashville, Indiana and serving the Bloomington, Indiana area. The station is owned by Mid America Radio Group Inc. It airs a Christian contemporary music format. The station also broadcasts at 107.7 to downtown Bloomington and the Indiana University campus.
