WVNJ (1160 AM) is a religious formatted radio station licensed to Oakland, New Jersey and serving Bergen County, New Jersey and parts of the New York City area. Since November 2018 the station has been owned-and-operated by Immaculate Heart Media, Inc, whose Catholic programing is known as Relevant Radio.
