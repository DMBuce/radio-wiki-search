WVNZ is an Oldies formatted broadcast radio station licensed to Richmond, Virginia, serving Metro Richmond. WVNZ is owned and operated by Mahan Janbakhsh's TBLC Holdings, LLC, through licensee TBLC Virginia Holdings, LLC.
