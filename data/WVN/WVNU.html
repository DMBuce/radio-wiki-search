WVNU (97.5 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a Soft Adult Contemporary format. Licensed to Greenfield, Ohio, United States, the station is currently owned by Southern Ohio Broadcasting and features programming from CBS Radio and Westwood One. The main signal can be heard on a translator broadcasting at 107.9 FM primarily to Hillsboro, Ohio. WVNU is the only CBS Radio affiliate in South West Ohio that broadcasts CBS "News on the Hour" on a 24/7 basis.
