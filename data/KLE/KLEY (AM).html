KLEY (1130 AM) is a radio station broadcasting an adult contemporary format to the Wellington, Kansas, United States, area. The station is owned by Rocking M Media, LLC. 1130 AM is a clear-channel frequency shared by Canada and The United States.

The station is currently silent.
