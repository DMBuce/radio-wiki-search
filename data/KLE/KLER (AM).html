KLER (1300 AM) is a radio station in the western United States, broadcasting a country music format in Orofino, Idaho. The station is currently owned by Central Idaho Broadcasting and features programming from ABC Radio and Jones Radio Network.

KLER broadcasts Major League Baseball as an affiliate of the Seattle Mariners radio network.
