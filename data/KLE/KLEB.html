KLEB (1600 AM "The Rajun' Cajun") is a 5,000 watt daytime directional/ 250 watt nighttime directional radio station in Golden Meadow, Louisiana, broadcasting a mix of swamp pop and traditional cajun music. In September 2019, it began to simulcast on K274DE 102.7 MHz licensed to Golden Meadow but broadcasting from the KLRZ tower facilities in Larose, Louisiana which allows the station's listening audience to choose between AM or FM.
