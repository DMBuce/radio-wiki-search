KLEN (106.3 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a country music format. Licensed to Cheyenne, Wyoming, United States, the station serves the Cheyenne area. The station is currently owned by Townsquare Media. The station shares its logo and programming with my country 95.5 KWYY Casper Each day at noon, the station plays the Star Spangled Banner. In addition, during the noon hour, the station airs "The 90s at Noon", a show that features country music from the 1990s as well as taste of country network.
