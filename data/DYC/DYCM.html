DYCM (1152 AM) is a radio station owned and operated by Makati Broadcasting Company. The station's studio is located at the 2nd Floor, Dona Luisa Bldg., Fuente Osmena, Cebu City, and its transmitter is located at Brgy. Paknaan, Mandaue.
