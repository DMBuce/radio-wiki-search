DYCD (103.5 FM), broadcasting as 103.5 Retro Cebu, is a radio station owned and operated by Ditan Communications, a subsidiary of UM Broadcasting Network. The station's studio and offices are located at the Room 309, 3rd Floor, Doña Luisa Bldg., Fuente Osmeña, Cebu City, and the transmitter is located at Brgy. Kalunasan, Cebu City. Retro Cebu operates 24/7.
