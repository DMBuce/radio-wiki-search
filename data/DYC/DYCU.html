DYCU-FM (99.9 MHz), MOR 99.9 Puerto Princesa was an FM station of ABS-CBN Corporation and Palawan Broadcasting Corporation in Puerto Princesa City in Region 5 in Visayas. The station's studio and transmitter are located at Mabini St., Puerto Princesa City, Palawan.
