CJRW-FM is a Canadian radio station broadcasting at 102.1 FM in Summerside, Prince Edward Island owned & operated by the Maritime Broadcasting System. The station plays an adult contemporary format branded on-air as Spud 102. CJRW-FM is the only radio station in Prince Edward Island that is not being licensed to Charlottetown.
