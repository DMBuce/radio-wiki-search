WEZS (1350 AM) is a broadcast radio station licensed to Laconia, New Hampshire, serving the Laconia and Franklin, New Hampshire area. WEZS began an FM simulcast on translator W277DJ 103.3 in June 2018. WEZS is owned and operated by Gary W. Hammond.
