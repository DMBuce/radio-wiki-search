WEZV (105.9 FM) is a radio station broadcasting an adult contemporary format. Licensed to North Myrtle Beach, South Carolina, United States, the station serves the Myrtle Beach and Wilmington areas. The station is currently owned by Byrne Acquisition Group MB, LLC and is branded as Easy 105.9. Its studios are located in Myrtle Beach, South Carolina and its transmitter is located north of North Myrtle Beach.
