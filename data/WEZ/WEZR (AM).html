WEZR (1240 AM, "WIGY") is a radio station licensed to serve Lewiston, Maine. The station is owned by Bennett Radio Group. WEZR airs an adult contemporary music format. Its programming is also heard on translator station W288CW (105.5 FM) in Lewiston, WPNO (1450 AM) and W245CQ (96.9 FM) in South Paris, and WIGY (780 AM) and W252DS (98.3 FM) in Rumford. In addition to music, WEZR broadcast Lewiston–Auburn area high school sports.

The station was assigned the WEZR call letters by the Federal Communications Commission on January 18, 2007.
