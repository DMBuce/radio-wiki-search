KJZY (106.3 FM; "The Beat") is a radio station in Cazadero, California, and serving the Santa Rosa area. It airs a contemporary hit radio format. The station is currently owned by Redwood Empire Stereocasters. Jamtraxx Media operates the station under a LMA deal from Redwood, and carries its Spin FM network.
