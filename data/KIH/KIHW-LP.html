KIHW-LP (104.1 FM) is a radio station licensed to serve West Helena, Arkansas. The station is owned by Hope Radio. It airs a Christian radio format deriving a portion of its programming from Radio 74 Internationale.

The station was assigned the KIHW-LP call letters by the Federal Communications Commission on December 19, 2002.
