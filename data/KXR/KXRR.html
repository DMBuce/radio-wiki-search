KXRR (106.1 FM, "Rock 106") is a mainstream rock formatted radio station broadcasting in the Monroe, Louisiana, radio market and covering all of the "Ark-La-Miss". It is owned by Stephens Media Group, through licensee SMG-Monroe, LLC. The General Manager is Mike Downhour. The Operations Manager is Don Kellogg. KXRR Rock 106 is home of the Christmas for Kids Toy Drive.
