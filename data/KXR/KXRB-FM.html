KXRB-FM (100.1 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a classic country format. Licensed to Brandon, South Dakota, United States, the station serves the Sioux Falls area. The station is currently owned by Townsquare Media.

Its studios are located on Tennis Lane in Sioux Falls, while its transmitter is located near Rowena.
