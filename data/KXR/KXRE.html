KXRE (1490 kHz) is an AM radio station broadcasting a Public; Adult Album Alternative radio format simulcasting sister station, 102.3 FM KVOQ (FM), Denver. Licensed to Manitou Springs, Colorado, the station serves the Colorado Springs radio market. The station is currently owned by Colorado Public Radio, through licensee Public Broadcasting of Colorado, Inc.

KXRE is also heard on two FM translator stations: 180 watt 98.5 K253AH in Colorado Springs and 99 watt 102.1 K271CK in Manitou Springs.
