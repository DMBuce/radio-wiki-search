WSYW (810 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a Spanish Adult contemporary format, branded as Exitos 94.3. It is licensed to Indianapolis, Indiana, United States, and serves the Indianapolis area. The station is owned by Continental Broadcast Group, LLC.

810 AM is a United States clear-channel frequency, on which KGO in San Francisco and WGY share Class A status.
