WQGR-FM (93.7 FM) is a radio station broadcasting an oldies format. Licensed to North Madison, Ohio, U.S., it serves Lake County in Northeast Ohio as well as Cleveland, Ohio.

WQGR went on the air August 1, 2013. Its offices are located in Mentor.

On Monday December 10, 2018 at 6 a.m., "Cougar 93.7 FM" flipped formats from hot AC to oldies as "Gold 93.7 FM".
