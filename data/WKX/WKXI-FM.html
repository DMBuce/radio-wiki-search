WKXI-FM (107.5 FM, "Kixie 107") is an Urban Adult Contemporary music formatted radio station licensed to Magee, Mississippi and serving Jackson. The station is owned by Alpha Media, through licensee Alpha Media Licensee LLC, and is an affiliate of the Tom Joyner Morning Show. Along with five other sister stations, its studios are located in Ridgeland, a suburb of Jackson, while the transmitter tower is south of Pelahatchie.
