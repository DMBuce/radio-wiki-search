WXKB (103.9 MHz, "B1039") is a commercial FM radio station licensed to Cape Coral, Florida, broadcasting to Fort Myers and Southwest Florida. WXKB is owned by the Beasley Broadcast Group and airs a Top 40 music format branded as "B-103.9 All the Hits", sometimes called "The Killer B."

WXKB has studios and offices on South Tamiami Trail in Estero. The transmitter is off Carter Road, also in Estero. WXKB is simulcast on translator station W268AH in Bonita Springs at 101.5 MHz. and W299BJ in Naples at 107.7 MHz
