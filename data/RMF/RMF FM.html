RMF FM (Radio Muzyka Fakty FM, previously: Radio Małopolska Fun) is the first commercial radio station in Poland. RMF FM started broadcasting on 15 January 1990 in Kraków. The current director is Tadeusz Sołtys. The radio is wholly owned by the German Bauer Verlagsgruppe.
