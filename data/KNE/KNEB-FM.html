KNEB-FM (94.1 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a country music format. Licensed to Scottsbluff, Nebraska, United States, the station serves the Scottsbluff area. The station is owned by Nebraska Rural Radio Association and features programming from ABC Radio and Westwood One.
