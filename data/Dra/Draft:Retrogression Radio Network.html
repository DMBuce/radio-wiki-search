Retrogression Radio Network is a Online Radio Station Originating from Cary, North Carolina, United States. Retrogression Radio Plays a mix of commercial free hits, Some Rythmic Top 40 songs, Classic Rock, Country Music, and eclectic tunes from the 70's, 80's and 90's. Retrogression Radio Began broadcasting on January 1 2019 at 12:00 PM. Retrogression Radio has a worldwide audience of almost 200-400 people worldwide listing to this station. Retrogression Radio Is mostly automated on Weekdays, but a live DJ broadcasts from the Studio every Saturday-Sunday Morning from 7:00 AM-9:00 AM. Retrogression Radio has a audience from these countries, United States, Austria, United Kingdom, Italy, Japan, Poland, Germany, Slovenia, and Argentina. Every December, Retrogression Radio switches to a Christmas Music radio format through January 1.

Retrogression Radio can be heard on many different online radio apps. One is Online Radio Box, where listeners can listen to Retrogression Radio and see what songs have been played during the last 7 days. Another is Streema, where listeners can tune in to Retrogression Radio from wherever the listner is.
