WOW FM (call sign: 5WOW) is a community radio station based in Adelaide, Australia and located in Semaphore, South Australia. Established in 1998, and originally broadcasting on 100.7 MHz from Exeter, South Australia, before moving to their current location in Semaphore, and to their current frequency of 100.5 MHz.
