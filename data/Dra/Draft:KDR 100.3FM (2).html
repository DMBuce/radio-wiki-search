Karuguuza Development Radio (KDR 100.3FM) is a local Radio Station located at Kirembo Hill in Karuguuza, Kibaale Town Council, Kibaale District-Bunyoro region, P.O.Box 75, Karuguuza. Its owned and established by Hon Matia Kasaija

Karuguuza Development Radio broadcasts in different Languages including Runyoro, Rutooro, Runyankore, Rukiga, Lukonzo, English, Luganda, and Kinyarwanda.

KDR 100.3FM has daily programs including Zinduka, Omukozi Hamulimo Gwe, Manya Ensi Yaawe, Haizooba among others and its wide coverage makes it create a strong bond with listeners by delivering relevant content on the air waves and on social media.
