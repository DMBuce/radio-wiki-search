Mithilanchal FM is a popular radio station of Province No. 2 with coverage of Mithila (region). It is one of the private radio station owned by Mukesh Kumar Dubey.The motto of Mithilanchal FM is "Harek Dilak Dhadkan".It is one of the most listened radio station in Nepal.

Mithilanchal FM maintains its regular time-table of broadcasting which includes schedules of varous programmes along with news and songs of local as well as national languages. Live broadcast of Mithilanchal FM also can listened through its official website https://www.mithilanchalfm.com.np. Mithilanchal Dalan and Sapna Sapne Rahi gel are the popular programme of Mithilanchal FM.

Mithilanchal FM has set up in Janakpur, Nepal which run from 5 AM till 11 PM.

Mithilanchal FM had played significant role in delivering instant news with realtime reporting during Madhes Movement III.
