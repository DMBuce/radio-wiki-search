2CBD FM is a community radio station with studios in Glen Innes, NSW. The station transmits on 105.9 FM in Glen Innes and 91.1 FM Deepwater.

The station is operated on a not for profit basis, and is primarily staffed by volunteers.

The organisation behind 2CBD FM is Deepwater and Districts Community FM Radio Inc which was incorporated in NSW in April 1997.

In 2016 the station moved to new premises on Church Street in Glen Innes.

The station transmits to the wider Glen Innes and Deepwater areas, and also operates an online streaming service via their website.

The music format of 2CBD FM is varied, including easy listening, country, classic hits, jazz and others.

2CBD FM provides news and information of relevance to its local community.
