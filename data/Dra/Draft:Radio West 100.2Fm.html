Radio West 100.2 fm, Radio Best (Gamba mpurire) is an FM radio station based in Western Region, Uganda. It is having the large area of coverage across the country and worldwide. it was originally founded by Mr. Tumusiime R James in 1998,under a mission to bring media closer to the masses.

It is one of the Vision Group's radio stations. It is also among the oldest radio stations in the region starting in 1999. It is best known for its 'Ekinihiro' which happens every year celebrating the years of existence. The station has also managed to host both local and national celebrities in their Ekinihiro and fans have always managed to win prizes. it is also known for its promotion moment, Hangura na radio west where listeners are given a chance to call on radio and win prizes.
