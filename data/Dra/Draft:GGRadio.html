GGRadio is a music internet radio station that has been streaming 24/7 on its own website as well as platforms TuneIn and Streema since July 7, 2015. The station was founded and is still being run by Chris Verringer. According to the owner, the station is most popular with gamers and younger audiences. Rock music, EDM, and Pop music is generally played on the radio.
