This article is about the Network. For the National station see Greatest Hits Radio.

The Greatset Hits Radio Network (Previously, Magic Network and Bauer City 2) is a network of 18 Independent Local Radio Stations across Northern England, West Midlands and Scotland. In September 2020, the Network will be massively increased by the addition of 49 new stations across the UK.
