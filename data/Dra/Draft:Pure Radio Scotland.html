Pure Radio is a new radio station from DC Thomson featuring Robin Galloway, Paul Harper, Lynne Hoggan and Amber. The station's slogan is "Scotland's Best Music" and was started with the aim of provided an alternative to the established stations which "play a small number of songs on repeat". Pure Radio is known for its 'on the edge' Scottish patter and chatty presenting style.
