Simulator Radio, also known as SR, is an independently run online radio station created originally for the Euro Truck Simulator 2 and American Truck Simulator community. Since opening it's doors back in August 2014 it has expanded over multiple games - predominantly simulation games such as Farming Simulator but also other games, including the Grand Theft Auto V modded client FiveM.

Simulator Radio is run entirely by a large group of volunteers, from the Radio Administration down to Discord Moderation.

The station plays mostly Chart Music with occasional throwbacks from the 1980s, 1990s, 2000s and 2010s. It also has the ability to request songs on it's website, mobile app and discord server.

The station is fully funded by Patreon and PayPal donations from the community. Up until 2020 it also ran on-air community advertisements.
