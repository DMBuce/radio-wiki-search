WGL (1250 AM) is a radio station licensed to serve Fort Wayne, Indiana, and owned by Brian R. Walsh. The station is temporarily rebroadcasting WWFW.

One of the oldest surviving radio stations in the Fort Wayne metropolitan area, WGL temporarily suspended operations on March 22, 2020 due to a loss of advertising revenue related to the COVID-19 pandemic; prior to that, WGL carried a sports radio format and served as the market affiliate for Fox Sports Radio. The station's studios and transmitter site are located in Fort Wayne's Southeast Waynedale neighborhood in a facility also used by other Adams Radio Group stations in the market.

The station is now an all news format.
