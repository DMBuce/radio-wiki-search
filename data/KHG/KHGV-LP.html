KHGV-LP (99.7 FM) is a radio station licensed to serve the community of Houston, Texas. The station is owned by Garden Villas Community Association, Inc. It airs a variety radio format.

The station was assigned the KHGV-LP call letters by the Federal Communications Commission on February 5, 2015.
