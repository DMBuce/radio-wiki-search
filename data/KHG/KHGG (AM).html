KHGG (1580 AM) is a radio station broadcasting an all-sports format. Licensed to Van Buren, Arkansas, United States, it serves the Ft. Smith area. The station is currently owned by Pharis Broadcasting, Inc.

The station is an affiliate of the Dallas Cowboys radio network.

In October 2017 KHGG switched its FM simulcast from KHGG-FM 103.1 Waldron (which went silent) to FM translator K266BS 101.1 FM Van Buren.
