KDZR (1640 kHz "The Patriot") is a commercial AM radio station, licensed to Lake Oswego, Oregon, and serving the Portland metropolitan area. The station airs a conservative talk radio format and is owned by the Salem Media Group. KDZR's studios and offices are on SE Lake Road in Portland.

The transmitter is off SE Eastview Drive in Happy Valley, Oregon. KDZR is powered at 10,000 watts by day, but at night reduces power to 1,000 watts, to reduce interference to other stations on AM 1640. The station uses a non-directional antenna at all times.
