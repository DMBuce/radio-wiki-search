KHBZ (102.9 FM, "The Z") is a radio station broadcasting a country music format. Licensed to Harrison, Arkansas, United States, the station is currently owned by KHOZ, LLC. KHBZ FM used to be the call letters for 94.7 "The Buzz" in Oklahoma City, OK.
