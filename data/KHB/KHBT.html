KHBT (97.7 FM) is an American commercial radio station that serves the Humboldt, Iowa area. The station broadcasts an Adult Contemporary format. KHBT is licensed to Open Roads Media, LLC, of Humboldt, Iowa.

The transmitter and broadcast tower are located in northeast Humboldt along Iowa Highway 3 near Montana Avenue. According to the Antenna Structure Registration database, the tower is 91 m (299 ft) tall with the FM broadcast antenna mounted at the 87 m (285 ft) level. The calculated Height Above Average Terrain is 84 m (276 ft).

KHBT is a FOX News, Iowa State University sports and NASCAR affiliate. KHBT broadcasts Humboldt High School Wildcat sports, four daily newscasts Monday-Friday and is host of the Sam T. Blues Revue.
