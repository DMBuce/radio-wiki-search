KHBR-LP (96.7 FM) was a radio station broadcasting a religious format. Licensed to Decatur, Arkansas, United States, the station was owned by Heaven Bound, Inc.

The station's license was surrendered to the Federal Communications Commission on May 7, 2018, and cancelled on May 21, 2018.
