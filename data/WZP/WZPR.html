WZPR (92.3 FM) is a news/talk radio station licensed to Nags Head, North Carolina, serving the Outer Banks of North Carolina. The station is owned by Jam Media Solutions LLC.

The original WZPR was a country station in Meadville, Pennsylvania at 100.3 FM. That station dropped the call letters when it became known as "Froggy" in early 2000.
