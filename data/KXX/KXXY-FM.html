KXXY-FM (96.1 MHz, "96.1 KXY") is a commercial radio station in Oklahoma City and is owned by iHeartMedia, Inc.. It airs a country radio format. KXXY-FM is co-owned with another country station in Oklahoma City, KTST. KTST leans towards the youthful side of the country fanbase, while KXXY-FM's playlist includes current and recent hits, as well as some classic country titles. In its logo, the station omits one of the two Xs in its call sign, calling itself "KXY."

KXXY-FM's studios and offices are located at the 50 Penn Place Building on the northwest side of Oklahoma City. The transmitter is off NE 122nd Street in Oklahoma City, near the John Kilpatrick Turnpike.
