WGRK-FM (105.7 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a country music format. Licensed to Greensburg, Kentucky, United States, the station is currently owned by Terry Forcht, through licensee Tri-County Radio Broadcasting Corp., and features programming from ABC Radio and Jones Radio Network.
