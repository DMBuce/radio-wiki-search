WGRN (89.5 FM) is a radio station licensed to serve the community of Greenville, Illinois. The station is owned by Greenville College Educational Broadcasting Foundation, Inc, and airs a contemporary Christian format.

The station was assigned the WGRN call letters by the Federal Communications Commission on January 26, 1965.
