WGR (550 AM) – branded "WGR Sports Radio 550" – is a commercial sports radio station licensed to serve Buffalo, New York. It is the flagship station of both the Buffalo Bills Radio Network and the Sabres Radio Network. WGR airs a mostly local sports talk and play-by-play lineup, with ESPN Radio heard nights and weekends. Owned by Entercom, its studios and offices are located on Corporate Parkway in Amherst, while the station transmitter—utilized by it and co-owned WWKB—resides in Hamburg. In addition to a standard analog transmission, WGR is relayed over the second HD subchannel of WKSE, and is available online via Radio.com.

WGR is Western New York's oldest radio station, dating back to 1922. During the day, it can be heard across nearly all of Western New York, the Greater Toronto Area, and nearby Erie, Pennsylvania. While a single tower is used during the day, power is fed to a four tower array at night in a directional pattern to protect WKRC in Cincinnati, Ohio and WDEV in Waterbury, Vermont, concentrating the signal around Buffalo and Toronto.
