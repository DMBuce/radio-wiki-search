WGRM-FM (93.9 FM) is an American radio station licensed to serve the community of Greenwood, Mississippi, United States. The station, established in 1989 by Clay Ewing, is owned and operated by Christian Broadcasters of Greenwood, Inc. WGRM-FM broadcasts to the northwestern central region of Mississippi.
