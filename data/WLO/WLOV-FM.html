WLOV-FM (99.5 FM) is a radio station licensed to Daytona Beach Shores, Florida. The station broadcasts "Daytona's Greatest Hits".

Susan Hall bought WLOV-FM in an FCC auction for $416,000, including a "new entrant bidding credit". WLOV-FM signed on March 21, 2012, with no commercials or on-air personalities for its first 99 days. It will be part of the Black Crow group, which includes WHOG-FM.
