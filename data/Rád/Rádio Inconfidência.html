Rádio Inconfidência, commonly Inconfidência is a radio station broadcasting on AM 880 and 100.9 FM serving Belo Horizonte, Minas Gerais and the central portion of the state in Brazil. The station includes talk radio mainly during the mornings, Brazilian and foreign music, news and sports. The two stations broadcast separately and sometimes simulcast on two stations.
