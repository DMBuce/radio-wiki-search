Rádio Unifei is the radio station owned and operated by the Universidade Federal de Itajubá in the state of Minas Gerais, Brazil, broadcasting on 1570 kHz AM.

The radio station broadcasts mainly non-commercial advertising, Brazilian popular music, university and city news, rock n' roll and reggae music.

Currently, the station employs three full-time employees and five university pupils working as "undergraduate monitors".
