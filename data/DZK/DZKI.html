DZKI (1332 kHz) Radyo Ronda is an AM radio station, owned by Radio Philippines Network and Nine Media Corporation. The station's studio is located at the new RPN Regional Broadcast Center, Bonacua Bldg., Iriga-Baao Rd., Brgy. San Nicolas, Iriga, and its transmitter is located in Brgy. San Roque, Iriga City.

It serves the Rinconada area, the province of Camarines Sur and the Bicol Region with news and information that matters, and with unbiased reporting and fair commentaries. DZKI is the first radio station established in Iriga and one of the forerunners in radio broadcasting in Camarines Sur. It airs a few programs simulcast from DWIZ 882 in Manila, as well as Radyo Asenso program from the Radio Corporation of the Philippines.
