DZKX (103.1 FM), broadcasting as 103.1 The Edge Radio, is a Contemporary Christian music FM radio station owned by Christian Music Power, a broadcast franchisee of the United Christian Broadcasters in the Philippines. It is an affiliate station of The Edge Radio network. The station's studio and transmitter is located at Candelaria, Quezon.
