WNLN-LP (107.3 FM) is a radio station licensed to serve Niantic, Connecticut. The station is owned by New Haven Educational Radio Corp. It airs a Christian radio format.

The station was assigned the WNLN-LP call letters by the Federal Communications Commission on December 1, 2003.
