WGTN (1400 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a comedy format, which is licensed to Georgetown, South Carolina, United States. The station was first licensed October 4, 1949. The station is currently owned by R.J. Stalvey. It previously featured programming from America's Radio News Network and CBS News. It aired Fox Sports Radio on weekends.
