WGTJ is an AM Christian radio station broadcasting a Christian radio format on 1330 kHz with 1000 watts during daytime hours. WGTJ is simulcast on an FM translator W250CN at 97.9 MHz. The broadcast facility is licensed to Murrayville, Georgia, USA. The station is owned by Vision Communications, Inc.
