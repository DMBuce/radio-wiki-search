WGTF (89.5 FM) is a radio station licensed to serve Dothan, Alabama, United States. The station is owned by Dothan Community Educational Radio, Inc. It airs a Religious radio format including programming from the Bible Broadcasting Network.

The station was assigned the WGTF call letters by the Federal Communications Commission on May 7, 1987. WGTF signed on the air September 1988. WGTF is 19,000 watts serving the Wiregrass area of Southeast Alabama.
