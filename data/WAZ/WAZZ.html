WAZZ (1490 AM, "My Kiss Radio 93.5") is a top 40 radio station in Fayetteville, North Carolina, owned by Beasley Media Group, LLC., and is "Never More Than 60 Seconds Away From Music". Its studios and transmitter are located separately in Fayetteville.
