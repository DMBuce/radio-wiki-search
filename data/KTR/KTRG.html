KTRG 94.1 FM is a radio station licensed to Hooks, Texas, serving the Texarkana, Texas area. The station broadcasts a Sports radio format and is owned by Freed AM Corp. Studios are located along Summerhill Road in northwest Texarkana, and the transmitter is located northwest of Texarkana city limits in Bowie County.
