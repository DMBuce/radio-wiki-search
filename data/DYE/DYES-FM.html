DYES (102.7 FM), broadcasting as 102.7 Easy Rock, is a radio station owned and operated by Manila Broadcasting Company through its licensee Pacific Broadcasting System. The station's studio is located at Eggling Subdivision, Busay Hills, Cebu City.
