DYEZ (98.3 FM), broadcasting as 98.3 Love Radio, is a radio station owned and operated by Manila Broadcasting Company in the Philippines. Its studio, offices and transmitter are located at MBC Palawan Broadcast Resources, 4th Floor Ascendo Suite, Malvar Street, Brgy. Mandaragat, Puerto Princesa.
