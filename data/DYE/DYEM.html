DYEM (96.7 FM), broadcasting as 96.7 Bai Radio, is a radio station owned and operated by Negros Broadcasting & Publishing Corporation, a local media outlet of Ely Dejaresco which also owns The Negros Chronicle, the longest running weekly newspaper in the city and the province of Negros Oriental. Its studios and transmitter facilities are located at 106 EJ Blanco Dr., Brgy. Piapi, Dumaguete. This station operates from 3:00 AM to 11:00 PM.

DYEM is the pioneer FM radio station in Dumaguete. It was formerly affiliated with Ultrasonic Broadcasting System's Energy FM network from 2000 until 2013.
