DYEY, under the brand 93.5 Easy Rock Boracay, is an FM station owned by JME Broadcast Resources and operated by Cebu Broadcasting Company. The studio is located in Barangay Balabag, Boracay Island, Malay, Aklan, Philippines.

93.5 Easy Rock Boracay was started on May 18, 2009.
