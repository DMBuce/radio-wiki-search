DYEN (107.1 FM), broadcasting as Barangay FM 107.1, is a commercial FM radio station owned by RGMA Network Inc. (RGMA, a subsidiary of GMA Network Inc.) in the Philippines. The station's studio & transmitter are located at 3rd Floor, Door#10 of Centroplex Mall, Gonzaga Street, Bacolod.
