MOR 99.7 Española (DYEA-FM 99.7 MHz) was an FM station of ABS-CBN Corporation and Palawan Broadcasting Corporation in Sofronio Española, Palawan. The station had a shared coverage with another network-owned FM station in Puerto Princesa City in the other side of the province that reached in the southern part of the said mentioned area due to its location. Currently, the station is inactive.
