WIHR-LP (94.1 FM) is a low-power FM radio station broadcasting a Christian radio format, and is a 3ABN Radio affiliate. Licensed to Jamestown, New York, United States, it serves the Jamestown area. The station is currently owned by Advent Radio Ministries Corporation.
