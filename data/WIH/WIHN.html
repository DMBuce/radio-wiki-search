WIHN, branded Rock 96.7, is an FM rock radio station on the Normal, Illinois, frequency of 96.7 MHz and studios in Bloomington, Illinois, in the Bloomington-Normal area. It is owned by Elizabeth Neuhoff's Neuhoff Corp., through licensee Neuhoff Media Bloomington, LLC, and switched to its current rock format in early 1995 (as I-97) from its former adult contemporary format.
