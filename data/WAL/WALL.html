WALL (1340 AM) is a radio station licensed to Middletown, New York, that serves Orange County, New York. WALL is owned by Neversink Media Group through licensee Digital Radio Broadcasting, Inc., and broadcasts at 1340 kHz with 1,000 watts, daytime and nighttime, both nondirectional from a transmitter in Middletown. Its studios are in Port Jervis, NY and Middletown, NY.
