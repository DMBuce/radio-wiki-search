KSPK-FM (102.3 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a Country format. Licensed to Walsenburg, Colorado, United States, the station is currently owned by Mainstreet Broadcasting Company, Inc. and features its own programming. KSPK-FM is not affiliated with any mainstream broadcasting company, and touts itself as being one of the few locally owned and operated radio stations left in Southern Colorado. In addition to the main station, KSPK-FM is simulcast on KSBK 100.3FM in Alamosa and is carried on four translators:

  • K281CU 104.1 FM Del Norte, Colorado
  • K281BI 104.1 FM Trinidad, Colorado
  • K281BC 104.1 FM Alamosa, Colorado
  • K262AQ 100.3 FM Colorado Springs, Colorado
