KSPN (710 kHz) is a commercial AM radio station licensed to Los Angeles, California, and serving the Greater Los Angeles Area. The station airs an all-sports radio format. KSPN is owned by The Walt Disney Company and operated through ESPN Radio. The studios are located at the ESPN Los Angeles Studios at L.A. Live in Downtown Los Angeles. The KSPN broadcast license is held by ABC Radio Los Angeles Assets, LLC.

On weekdays, morning drive time is hosted by Keyshawn Johnson, Travis Rodgers and LZ Granderson. The nationally syndicated Stephen A. Smith Show is heard in late mornings. Mason & Ireland (Steve Mason and John Ireland) host early afternoons, followed by Jorge Sedano in PM drive time. Nights and weekends, most programming comes from the ESPN Radio Network. KSPN is one of two ESPN Radio affiliates in the Los Angeles radio market, the other being KLAA.

KSPN is licensed by the Federal Communications Commission to broadcast in the HD Radio (hybrid) format. The transmitter is on Burbank Boulevard at Bellaire Avenue.
