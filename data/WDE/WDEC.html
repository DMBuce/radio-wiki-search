WDEC-FM (94.7 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a Hot Adult Contemporary format. Licensed to Americus, Georgia, United States, the station serves the Albany, GA area. The station is currently owned by Sumter Broadcasting Co., Inc. and features programming from ABC Radio and Motor Racing Network.
