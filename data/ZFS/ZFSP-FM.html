ZFSP-FM, or Spin FM (94.9 FM), is a radio station in the Cayman Islands in the British West Indies. The station is owned by Paramount Media Services. It airs a dance music format. The station often features disc jockeys that also entertain in Cayman Islands nightclubs.

The station's license was issued on 15 December 2004.
