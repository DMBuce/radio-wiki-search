KRQX-FM (98.9 FM) is an American radio station licensed to serve the community of Hurricane, Utah. The station is owned by G. Craig Hanson, through licensee Redrock Broadcasting, Inc.

The station was assigned the KBZB call letters by the Federal Communications Commission on May 26, 1999. The station began regular broadcast operations in August 1999.
