KRQZ (91.5 MHz, "RadioU") is a non-commercial FM radio station that is licensed to Lompoc, California and serves the Santa Maria-Lompoc area. The station is owned by Spirit Communications, Inc. and broadcasts a youth-targeted Christian rock format, relaying the signal of WUFM in Columbus, Ohio via satellite.
