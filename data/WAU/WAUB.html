WAUB (1590 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a News Talk Information format. Licensed to Auburn, New York, United States, the station is part of the Finger Lakes Radio Group, and is a companion station to WGVA in Geneva, New York. WAUB features programming from ABC Radio and Westwood One.
