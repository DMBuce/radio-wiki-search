WAUC (AM 1310) and (FM 102.1) is a radio station broadcasting a Real Country format. Licensed to Wauchula, Florida, USA, the station serves the Florida Heartland, includes the Hardee, Desoto and Highlands County areas, along with listeners in nearby Polk County.

On August 22, 2019, WAUC began simulcasting on FM translator W271DH on frequency 102.1.
