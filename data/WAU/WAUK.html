WAUK (540 kHz) is a commercial AM radio station licensed to the Washington County community of Jackson, Wisconsin, and serving the Milwaukee metropolitan area. It is known on-air as 540 ESPN. Since June 2019, WAUK has mainly carried the national ESPN Radio schedule with some diversions for local content. The station's studios and offices are in the "Radio City" complex on East Capitol Drive in Milwaukee.

The station is owned by Beaver Dam-based Good Karma Brands (formerly Good Karma Broadcasting, LLC), headed by Craig Karmazin, son of legendary radio executive Mel Karmazin. Sister station WKTI 94.5 FM carries mainly local sports talk and WTMJ 620 AM/103.3 FM airs a news/talk and sports format. WAUK is among the few independent network affiliates of ESPN Radio whose webstream is carried on the network's mobile apps, and whose website is hosted on ESPN.com.
