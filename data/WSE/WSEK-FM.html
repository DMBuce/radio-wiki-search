WSEK-FM (93.9 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a country music format. Licensed to Burnside, Kentucky, it serves the south central region of the state, specifically the Somerset and Lake Cumberland areas. It can also be received in London and Corbin, and as far north as the Lexington area. The station is currently owned by iHeartMedia, Inc. and features programming from Premiere Radio Networks.
