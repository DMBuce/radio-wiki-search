WLHI (90.3 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a contemporary Christian music format. Licensed to Schnecksville, Pennsylvania, United States, the station serves the Allentown/Bethlehem area. The station is currently owned by Four Rivers Community Broadcasting Corporation.
