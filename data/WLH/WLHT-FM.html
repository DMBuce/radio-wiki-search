WLHT-FM (95.7 FM) is a hot adult contemporary music formatted radio station in Grand Rapids, Michigan owned by Townsquare Media. Until late April 2006, the station was the home of the husband-and-wife morning team of Dave & Geri.

WLHT-FM is also licensed for HD Radio operation, and features WNWZ on its HD-2 subchannel.
