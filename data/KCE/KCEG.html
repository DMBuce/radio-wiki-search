KCEG is a radio station airing a classic country/bluegrass format licensed to Fountain, Colorado, broadcasting on 780 kHz AM and 105.1 FM. The station serves the Colorado Springs, Colorado and Pueblo, Colorado areas, and is owned by Timothy C. Cutforth.
