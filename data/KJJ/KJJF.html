KJJF (88.9 MHz) is a non-commercial FM radio station in Harlingen, Texas. KHID (88.1 MHz) is also a non-commercial FM radio station in McAllen, Texas. Both stations are owned by the Roman Catholic Diocese of Brownsville and serve the Rio Grande Valley.

KJJF's transmitter is located on Fresnal Road in San Benito. KHID's transmitter is off West Monte Cristo Road in La Homa.

The two stations simulcast a mix of NPR news and talk programs, with some hours of classical music and jazz. Both stations are being bought by Immaculate Heart Media, Inc. for $1.25 million. The Catholic radio network "Relevant Radio" is expected to air on both stations when the purchase is finalized.
