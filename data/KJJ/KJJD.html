KJJD (1170 AM "La Ley") is a radio station broadcasting a Mexican regional format. Licensed to Windsor, Colorado, United States, it serves the Ft. Collins-Greeley area. The station is currently owned by Rodriguez-Gallegos Broadcasting Corporation. The station plays banda, cumbia, norteña, and ranchera music.
