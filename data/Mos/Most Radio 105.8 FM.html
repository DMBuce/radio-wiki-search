PM2FGI (105.8 FM), broadcasting as Most Radio 105.8 FM or simply 105.8, is an Indonesian radio station broadcasting from Jakarta. It is owned and operated by Mahaka Media under its radio arm Mahaka Radio Integra, and airs a Classic hits format.
