KLIQ (94.5 FM) is a radio station broadcasting an adult contemporary format. Licensed to Hastings, Nebraska, United States, the station serves the Grand Island-Kearney area. The station is currently owned by Platte River Radio, Inc. and features the Bob & Sheri show.
