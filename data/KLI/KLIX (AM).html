KLIX (1310 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a News Talk Information format serving the Twin Falls, Idaho, United States, area. The station is currently owned by Townsquare Media and licensed to Townsquare Media Twin Falls License, LLC. KLIX features programming from Fox News Radio and Premiere Radio Networks.

The station's skywave signal has been received in Salt Lake City, Utah, Bonners Ferry, Idaho and Green River, Wyoming.
