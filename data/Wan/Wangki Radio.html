Wangki Yupurnanupurru Radio, usually shortened to Wangki Radio , is a small Aboriginal community radio station based in Fitzroy Crossing, Western Australia.

Wangki Radio has a daily listenership of around 5000 people in various communities surrounding the Fitzroy Valley.

Wangki Radio airs national news from NIRS, regional current affairs programming produced locally or through the PAKAM Network, world news from DW, interviews, road reports, and weather information. Wangki Radio is also home to the Youth Media Hub run in coordination with SYN Media in Melbourne.
