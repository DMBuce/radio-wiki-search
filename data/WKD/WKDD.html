WKDD (98.1 FM) – branded 98.1 KDD – is a commercial hot adult contemporary radio station licensed to Munroe Falls, Ohio, serving both the Akron and Canton metro areas. Owned by iHeartMedia, Inc., the station serves as the local affiliate for American Top 40. The WKDD studios are located in the Canton suburb of North Canton, while the station transmitter resides in the Akron suburb of Cuyahoga Falls. Besides a standard analog transmission, WKDD broadcasts over two HD Radio channels, and streams online via iHeartRadio.
