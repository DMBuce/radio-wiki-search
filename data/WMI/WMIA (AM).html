WMIA (1070 AM) is a radio station licensed to serve Arecibo, Puerto Rico. The station is owned by Wifredo G. Blanco Pi and it is part of the WAPA Radio News Network. It airs a News/Talk format and features programming from CNN Radio. The station is shared with translator station W227DY 93.3 FM also in Arecibo.

The station was assigned the WMIA call letters by the Federal Communications Commission.
