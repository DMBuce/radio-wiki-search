WMIB (103.5 FM) is an Urban Contemporary radio station in South Florida, United States. It is licensed to Fort Lauderdale, Florida but it also serves Miami and the Miami metropolitan area. The station is owned by iHeartMedia and the broadcast license is held by Clear Channel Broadcast Licenses, Inc. Its studios are located in Miramar and the transmitter site is in Miami Gardens. WMIB competes with 99 Jamz. WMIB broadcasts with 100,000 watts effective radiated power and 1,007 feet height above average terrain from the Guy Gannett broadcasting tower in Miami. Other stations on the tower are WAXY-FM, WHYI-FM, WMXJ, WHQT-FM, WMIA-FM, WFLC, WFEZ, WEDR, and WMGE.

WMIB is licensed by the FCC to broadcast in the HD Radio (hybrid) format.
