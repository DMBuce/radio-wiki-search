WMIO (102.3 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a Rhythmic Top 40 format. Licensed to Cabo Rojo, Puerto Rico, United States, it serves the Puerto Rico area. The station is currently owned by Uno Radio Group and repeats the audio of WCMN-FM.
