National Radio 1 is an English speaking national online radio station operated by TDK Media Ltd in Cyprus, playing a mix from the 1980s, 1990s and today. The station is aimed at the English speaking residents and visitors to the island. National Radio 1 was launched on 26 April 2008 and operates 24 hours a day providing a program of music, entertaining features, and Cyprus and world news.
