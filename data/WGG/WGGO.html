WGGO is an AM radio station located in Salamanca, New York, United States. The station broadcasts at 1590Â kHz. Its license is held by Sound Communications (a partially owned affiliate of Waypoint Media), who has operated the station intermittently since 2017. Since 2019, it has been a repeater of Waypoint-owned WENI in Corning, New York, sharing most of its conservative talk lineup and its brand of "The Patriot" along with another Waypoint station, WSHY in Lafayette, Indiana.

WGGO's licensed 5,000-watt daytime signal, the strongest AM signal in southwestern New York, covers all of Cattaraugus County and much of Chautauqua, Allegany, and McKean Counties as well as the Southtowns of Erie County. Further north, an FCC oversight means that WRSB's signal begins interfering with WGGO's near Buffalo.
