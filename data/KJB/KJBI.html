KJBI (100.1 FM, "100.1 The Eagle") is a radio station licensed to serve Fort Pierre, South Dakota. The station is owned by and Ingstad Family Media and licensed to James River Broadcasting. It airs a Classic Hits music format.

All four Pierre James River Broadcasting stations share studios at 214 West Pleasant Drive, in Pierre.

The station was assigned the KJBI call letters by the Federal Communications Commission on August 2, 2007.
