DXPS (95.1 FM), broadcasting as MOR 95.1 For Life!, was a radio station owned and operated by the ABS-CBN Corporation. Its studios and transmitter are located at ABS-CBN Broadcast Center, Don E. Sero St., Rosary Heights V, Cotabato City.

On May 5, 2020, the station, along with the other My Only Radio stations, went off the air due to the cease and desist order of the National Telecommunications Commission.
