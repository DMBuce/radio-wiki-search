DXPA (103.1 FM) Radyo Serbisyo is a radio station owned and operated by Andres Bonifacio College Broadcasting System, Inc.. Its studios and transmitters are located at Purok 4, Brgy. Sta. Maria, Nabunturan, Davao de Oro.
