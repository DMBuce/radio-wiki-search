CJHD-FM is a Canadian radio station that broadcasts an active rock format, branded as The Rock at 93.3 FM in North Battleford, Saskatchewan, Canada. Its local sister stations are CJNB and CJCQ-FM. All three are located at 1711 100th Street in North Battleford.

The station is owned by the Jim Pattison Group, and was licensed in 2004.
