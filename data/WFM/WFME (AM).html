WFME (1560 AM) is a non-commercial educational, religious-formatted radio station licensed to New York City; it operates as a Class A clear-channel station broadcasting at the maximum power for AM radio, 50,000 watts The station is owned and operated by Family Radio, a Christian radio ministry based in Nashville, Tennessee, and broadcasts the Family Radio - East satellite feed from its transmitter located in Maspeth, Queens.
