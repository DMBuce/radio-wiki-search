KPGE (1340 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a country music format. Licensed to Page, Arizona, United States, the station is currently owned by Lake Powell Communications, Inc.

The station's skywave signal was reported in Salt Lake City on 2007-11-27 and audio proof of the station was recorded.
