KPGM (1500 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a SportsTalk format. Currently, KPGM is a WWLS The Sports Animal And Fox Sports Affiliate. The station is licensed to Pawhuska, Oklahoma, United States, and is currently owned by Potter Radio, LLC. In addition to being heard at 1500 AM and Bartlesvilleradio.com, KPGM simulcasts on 100.1 HD2 channel and on a translator at 99.1 MHz in Bartlesville.

The FCC first licensed this station to begin operations on April 6, 1964 using callsign KOSO.
