WXBP (90.3 FM) is a Catholic talk radio station licensed to serve Corinth, Maine, United States. The station is an affiliate of Relevant Radio and is owned by Immaculate Heart Media, Inc. The station signed on during the winter of 2011 as a simulcast of WXTP in Portland. The station is also heard in the Augusta area on WWTP and in the Brunswick/Bath area on WTBP.
