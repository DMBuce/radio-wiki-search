WODB-LP (90.9 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a Contemporary Christian format. Licensed to Caguas, Puerto Rico, the station serves the central Puerto Rico area. The station is currently owned by Jackeline Contes Santiago requested under Iglesia Refugio, Sanidad y Adoración, Inc.
