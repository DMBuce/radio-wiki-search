WODT is an all-news radio station based in New Orleans, Louisiana, an affiliate of the Black Information Network. The iHeartMedia, Inc. station broadcasts at 1280 kHz with 5,000 watts full-time. Its transmitter is located in New Orleans' Algiers district, and its studios are located downtown.
