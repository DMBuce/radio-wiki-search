WODY is a radio station licensed to Fieldale, Virginia, serving Martinsville and Henry County, Virginia. WODY is owned and operated by Positive Alternative Radio, Inc.

WODY broadcasts on the United States clear-channel frequency of 1160 AM. It formerly aired programming from ESPN Radio before being bought by Positive Alternative Radio, who turned it into a repeater of Southern gospel WXRI in Winston-Salem, North Carolina.
