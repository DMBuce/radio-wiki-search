WQBZ (106.3 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a mainstream rock format. Licensed to Fort Valley, Georgia, United States, the station serves the Macon area. The station is currently owned by iHeartMedia, Inc. and licensed to AMFM Radio Licenses, LLC.
