WQBU-FM (92.7 FM, Que Buena 92.7) is a radio station licensed to Garden City, New York and serving the western Long Island and New York City area. It broadcasts a Spanish language adult contemporary music format and is owned by Uforia Audio Network. The station's transmitter is located at the North Shore Towers in Floral Park, New York.
