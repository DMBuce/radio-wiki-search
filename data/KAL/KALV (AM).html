KALV (1430 AM) is a radio station licensed to serve Alva, Oklahoma, United States. The station, established in 1956, is currently owned by Blue Sky Media, LLC. It has a sister station, KRDR also in Alva.

KALV broadcasts a progressive country format of new and classic country with State news from the Oklahoma News Network and Agriculture News from First OK AG. Listeners of KALV will enjoy a family friendly variety of music and entertainment. Weekend shows include Red Steagall's Cowboy Corner at 8 Am Saturday Mornings and the Sunday Night KALV SOCK HOP 9 to midnight which features the greatest hits of the 1960s and 70s
