KIFS (107.5 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a CHR format. Licensed to Ashland, Oregon, United States, the station serves the Medford-Ashland area. The station is currently owned by Bicoastal Media Licenses Vi, LLC. The station is branded "107.5 Kiss-FM." Longtime market talent Gemineye returned to KISS-FM after an eight-month leave in August 2013 and resumed his role as morning man and program director. He is followed at 10:00AM by Mike Raines, and Kristina With A "K" at 2:00PM.
