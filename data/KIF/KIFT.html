KIFT (106.3 FM, "Lift 106") is a radio station broadcasting a top 40 music format. Licensed to Kremmling, Colorado, United States, the station is currently owned by Patricia MacDonald Garber and Peter Benedetti, through licensee AlwaysMountainTime, LLC.
