WJIT (1250 AM, Faro de Santidad) is a radio station broadcasting a Religious format. It is licensed to Sabana, Puerto Rico, United States, and is owned by Aurio A. Matos Barreto. The station shares with translator W267DD (101.3 FM) in Vega Baja.
