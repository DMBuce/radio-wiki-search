KIRV (1510 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a Christian radio format. It is licensed to Fresno, California, United States, and serves the Fresno area. The station is owned by Centro Cristiano Viva Abundante, Inc.

KIRV must sign off the air at sunset to protect the signal of KGA in Spokane, Washington, the Class A clear channel station on 1510 kHz.
