KJSK (900 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a news/talk format. Licensed to Columbus, Nebraska, United States, the station is currently owned by Alpha Media through licensee Digity 3E License, LLC, and features programming from CBS Radio, Salem Communications and Sporting News Radio.

The station also features religious and secular talk radio programs such as James Dobson, Woodrow Kroll, Chuck Swindoll and Michael Medved.
