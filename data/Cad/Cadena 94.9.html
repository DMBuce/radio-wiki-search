Cadena 94.9 (Spanish: 94.9 Network) is an Argentine radio station broadcasting in the 94.9 mHz from the city of Pilar, Buenos Aires. The station originally was called simply "FM 94.9", but the name was changed when it was bought by the local journalist Claudio Ponce de León. The station programming is almost entirely dedicated to the music (with a great emphasis on the 80s and 90s world pop hits), despite having a very small playlist that repeats itself once and again.
