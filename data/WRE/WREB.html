WREB is a radio station licensed to Greencastle, Indiana, broadcasting at 94.3 MHz in FM. Although primarily a music broadcaster, WREB serves as a source of local information, weather and sports broadcasting for Putnam County, Indiana. WREB began broadcasting on May 16, 1966.
