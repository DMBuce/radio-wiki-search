KKFN (104.3 MHz, "Sports Radio 104.3 The Fan") is a commercial FM radio station serving the Denver-Boulder market. Owned and operated by Bonneville International, KKFN airs a sports radio format. The station is licensed to Longmont, Colorado. Its studios are located in Greenwood Village, and the transmitter is in Lakewood on Green Mountain. KKFN runs local sports shows days and evenings, and carries ESPN Radio programming late nights and weekends.
