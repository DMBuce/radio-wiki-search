KBAY (94.5 FM, "Bay FM") is a classic hits radio station licensed to Gilroy, California, serving the San Francisco Bay Area and owned by Alpha Media, along with sister station KEZR. Its studios are located off US 101 and Hellyer Ave in South San Jose and the transmitter is on a hill in Santa Teresa County Park. The station competes with KUFX and KSAN.
