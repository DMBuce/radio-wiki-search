KRJC (95.3 FM, "All American Country") is a radio station licensed to serve Elko, Nevada. The station is owned by Elko Broadcasting Company. It airs a country music format.

The station was assigned the KRJC call letters by the Federal Communications Commission on February 17, 1981.
