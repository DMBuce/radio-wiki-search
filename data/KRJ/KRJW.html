KRJW (1240 AM, a.k.a. "1240 The Winner") is a radio station broadcasting a sports talk format. It is licensed to Altamont, Oregon, and serves Klamath Falls, Oregon, and the surrounding areas. The station is currently owned by Wynne Broadcasting, LLC, and licensed to Cove Road Publishing, LLC. It is currently affiliated with CBS Sports Radio. It is the Klamath Basin's home for the Portland Trail Blazers NBA basketball and University of Oregon Ducks football. It also broadcasts high school games featuring Mazama High School with program and sports director Randy Adams on the call.
