DXEF, branded as 100.7 Star FM General Santos, is an FM radio station owned and operated by Bombo Radyo Philippines in the Philippines. The station's studio is located at Amao Road Brgy Bula General Santos City. It operates daily from 4:00am to 12:00mn.
