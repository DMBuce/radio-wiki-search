DXES (801 AM) Bombo Radyo is a commercial radio station owned and operated by Bombo Radyo Philippines. The station's studio and transmitter are located at Bombo Radyo Broadcast Center, Amao Road, Brgy. Bula, General Santos City. The station operates daily from 4:00 AM to 10:00 PM.
