DXED Radyo Agila Tagum (1224 kHz Tagum) is an AM relay station of DZEC in the Philippines. The station's studio and Transmitter is located at Purok Maharlika, Lourdes Sinangcote Village II, Barangay Magumpo East, Tagum, Davao del Norte. The main format of the station is music and news. DXED broadcasts daily from 4am to 12 mn.
