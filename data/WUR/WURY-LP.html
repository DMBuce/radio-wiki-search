WURY-LP (97.1 FM) is a radio station licensed to serve Phenix City, Alabama. The station is owned by Talkfaith Radio. It airs a Religious radio format, with a focus on Seventh-day Adventist teachings and programs from the Three Angels Broadcasting Network.

The station was assigned the WURY-LP call letters by the Federal Communications Commission on August 20, 2003.
