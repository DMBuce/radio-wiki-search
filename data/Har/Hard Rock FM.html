PM2FPB (87.6 FM), on-air name Hard Rock FM, is a radio station in Jakarta, Indonesia. Hard Rock FM, along with its network, is owned by MRA Media under license of Hard Rock itself. Started broadcasting in 1996, Hard Rock FM claims itself as first lifestyle radio in Indonesia, especially Jakarta.
