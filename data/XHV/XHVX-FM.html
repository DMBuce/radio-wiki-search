XHVX-FM is a radio station on 89.7 FM in Comalcalco, Tabasco. The station is owned by Grupo VX, a business of the late radio entrepreneur and former federal deputy Moisés Félix Dagdug Lützow, and is known as "La Grande de Tabasco" with a news/talk format.
