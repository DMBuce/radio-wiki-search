DZVA (106.3 FM), on-air as Radyo Natin 106.3, is a low-power radio station in Calamba City owned and operated by Manila Broadcasting Company. Its studio is located at 3rd Floor, SQA Bldg., Crossing, Brgy. Uno, Calamba City, Laguna, and its transmitter is located at Brgy. Bagumbayan, Santa Cruz, Laguna.
