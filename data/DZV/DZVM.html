DZVM (104.1 FM), broadcasting as 104.1 Yes The Best, is a radio station owned and operated by Manila Broadcasting Company. Its studios and transmitter are located at 2nd Floor, Public Market, Brgy. Poblacion, Urdaneta, Pangasinan.
