DZVC (1224 AM) Radyo Pilipinas is a radio station owned and operated by the Philippine Broadcasting Service. Its studios & transmitter are located inside the Catanduanes State University campus, Brgy. Calatagan, Virac, Catanduanes.

URL: www.pbsradio.ph
