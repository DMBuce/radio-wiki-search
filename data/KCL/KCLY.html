KCLY (100.9 FM) is a radio station based out of Clay Center, Kansas. It has operated since 1978 under the ownership of Taylor Communications. KCLY broadcasts local programming, including news, sports and weather. KCLY's sister station is KFRM, also owned by Taylor Communications.
