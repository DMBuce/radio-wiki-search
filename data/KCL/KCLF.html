KCLF (1500 AM, Voice of Pointe Coupee) is a radio station broadcasting a Rhythmic Oldies music format. Licensed to New Roads, Louisiana, United States. The station is currently owned by New World Broadcasting Company.

On January 12, 2015 KCLF was granted a Federal Communications Commission construction permit to move to a new transmitter site, decrease day power to 850 watts and add night operation with 18 watts.
