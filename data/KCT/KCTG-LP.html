KCTG-LP (98.1 FM) was a radio station licensed to Turtle Lake, Montana, United States. The station was owned by Confederated Salish & Kootenai Tribes Disaster & Emergency Services.

The station's broadcast license was cancelled by the Federal Communications Commission on December 12, 2012, as the station had been silent for longer than twelve months.
