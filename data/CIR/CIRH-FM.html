CIRH-FM (98.3 FM) was an English-language radio station licensed by the CRTC in Vancouver, British Columbia, Canada. It was an urban talk/music radio station owned by, and doing business as, Roundhouse Radio. The station ceased operations on May 6, 2018, at 7:32 PM PDT, pending CRTC approval of the sale of the station to South Fraser Broadcasting Inc., who operate CISF-FM in Surrey, British Columbia.
