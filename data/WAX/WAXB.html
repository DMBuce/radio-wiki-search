WAXB (850 AM, "94.5 The Hawk") is a radio station licensed to Ridgefield, Connecticut, United States, one of three area stations owned by The Berkshire Broadcasting Corporation. The others are News/Talk 800 AM WLAD and Hot Adult Contemporary 98Q/98.3 FM WDAQ. WAXB airs a classic rock radio format for the Danbury region of Connecticut. The station uses the identification "94.5 The Hawk", which is the dial position of its FM translator 94.5 W233CF.

Because 850 AM is a clear channel frequency, WAXB must sign-off at night to avoid interfering with higher-class stations on that frequency. Programming continues via the FM translator throughout the overnight.
