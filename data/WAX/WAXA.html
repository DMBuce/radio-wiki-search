WAXA (1200 AM; "Maxima 97.3/95.7") is a radio station broadcasting a Spanish-language Tropical hits format. Licensed to Pine Island Center, Florida, United States, the station serves the Fort Myers area. The station is currently owned by Fort Myers Broadcasting Company.
