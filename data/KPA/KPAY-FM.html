KPAY-FM (93.9 FM) is a commercial radio station located in Chico, California. KPAY-FM airs a news/talk format.

On August 1, 2019, the then-KFMF changed their format from mainstream rock to a simulcast of news/talk-formatted KPAY 1290 AM Chico. The station changed its call sign to KPAY-FM on August 16, 2019.
