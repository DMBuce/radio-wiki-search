KMQA/KMEN (100.5 FM) is a simulcast radio station broadcasting a Regional Mexican format. The two stations are licensed to East Porterville and Mendota, California, United States. KMQA's coverage area includes Bakersfield and Tulare, while KMEN primarily covers Mendota. Both stations are owned by Lazer Broadcasting. KKDD of San Bernardino, California had the callsign KMEN (1962-1997).
