WZNL (94.3 FM, "The Breeze") is a radio station licensed to Norway, Michigan, United States, and serving the Norway, Iron Mountain, and Kingsford areas of Michigan's Upper Peninsula. The station broadcasts a soft adult contemporary music format. The station is currently owned by Northern Star Broadcasting, with a sale pending to Sovereign Communications.

In July 2020 WZNL changed their format from hot adult contemporary to soft adult contemporary, branded as "The Breeze".
