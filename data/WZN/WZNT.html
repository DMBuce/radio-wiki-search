WZNT (93.7 FM, "Zeta 93") is a radio station broadcasting a Spanish Tropical format. Licensed to San Juan, Puerto Rico, United States, it serves the Puerto Rico area. The station is currently owned by Spanish Broadcasting System Holding Company, Inc.
