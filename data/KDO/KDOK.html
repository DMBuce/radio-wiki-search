KDOK (1240 AM, "All Hit Radio K-DOK") is a Classic Hits formatted radio station licensed to Kilgore, Texas, United States; the station serves the Longview-Marshall area.

KDOK signed on the air in 1936 as KOCA, which stood for "Kilgore Oil Capital of America". It is the third oldest licensed broadcast station in East Texas, only surpassed in age by KYZS 1490 Tyler, Texas, which signed on in 1931, and KFRO 1370 Longview, Texas in 1935.
