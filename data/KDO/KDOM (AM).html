KDOM (1580 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a country music format. Licensed to Windom, Minnesota, United States. The station is currently owned by Carolyn and Doyle Becker, through licensee Riverfront Broadcasting of Minnesota, Inc.

1580 AM is a Canadian clear-channel frequency, on which CKDO is the dominant Class A station.
