KTYL-FM (93.1 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a Top 40 (CHR) format to the Tyler-Longview, Texas area. The station is owned by Townsquare Media and is licensed to Townsquare Media Tyler License, LLC. Studios are located on Brookside Drive in south Tyler, and the transmitter is near Overton in northwest Rusk County.
