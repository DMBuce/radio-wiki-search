KDYL (1060 kHz) is an AM broadcasting radio station. The station is owned by James Su, through licensee Mountain View Broadcast, LLC.

The station broadcasts with 10,000 watts by day. But because AM 1060 is a clear channel frequency, after sunset KDYL must reduce power to 149 watts so it does not interfere with Class A stations XEEP, located near Mexico City, and KYW in Philadelphia. The transmitter is located off Bacchus Highway near Lark, Utah.
