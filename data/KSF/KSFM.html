KSFM (102.5 FM) is a commercial radio station that is licensed to Woodland, California and serves the Sacramento area. The station is owned by Entercom and broadcasts a rhythmic contemporary format. KSFM's studios and offices are located at 5345 Madison Avenue in Sacramento, and its transmitter is in Woodland.

From 2006 to 2010, KSFM's slogan was "Sacramento's #1 Station for Hip-Hop and R&B". In 2010, the station changed its slogan to simply "102-5" with a broader mix of hip-hop, R&B, and rhythmic pop music. Entercom bills KSFM as a rhythmic top 40 station due to its multicultural listenership.

KSFM broadcasts in HD Radio.
