Capital XTRA (originally called Choice FM uk ) is a Global-owned radio station that broadcasts in London on 96.9 and www.choicefmuk.co.uk MHz FM; and nationally on DAB Digital Radio, Freesat, Sky, Virgin Media and online. It is a radio station that specialises in contemporary grime, hip hop and R&B music.
