WWJK (107.3 FM, "107.3 Planet Radio") is a mainstream rock formatted radio station located in Jacksonville, Florida. The station is owned by iHeartMedia. The station's studios are located on Central Parkway in Jacksonville's Southside section, and the transmitter is in the Arlington section.
