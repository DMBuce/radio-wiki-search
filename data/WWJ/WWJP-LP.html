WWJP-LP (101.7 FM, "Walking with Jesus Radio") is a radio station broadcasting a religious format. Licensed to Rice Lake, Wisconsin, United States, the station is currently owned by Meadow Creek Christian Radio Corporation and features programming from the Three Angels Broadcasting Network.
