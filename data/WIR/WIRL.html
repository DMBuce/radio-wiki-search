WIRL (1290 AM) is a radio station broadcasting an oldies music format. Licensed to Peoria, Illinois, United States, the station serves the Peoria area and broadcasts in AM stereo. The station is currently owned by Midwest Communications.

WIRL can also be heard in HD on sister station WPBG HD2. WIRL itself is not licensed to broadcast in HD.
