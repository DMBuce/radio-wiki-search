KSNO-FM (103.9 FM) is a radio station broadcasting an Album Adult Alternative format. Licensed to Snowmass Village, Colorado, United States, it serves the Aspen area. The station is owned by Cool Radio, LLC, Tom Dobrez CEO. KSNO was previously operated in brokered combo with KUUR by Marcos A. Rodriguez, its CEO.
