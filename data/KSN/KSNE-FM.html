KSNE-FM (106.5 FM) is a radio station broadcasting an adult contemporary music format. Licensed to Las Vegas, Nevada, United States, the station serves the Las Vegas area. The station is currently owned by Citicasters Licenses, a division of iHeartMedia, Inc.. Its studios are in Las Vegas a mile west of the Strip and its transmitter is on Black Mountain in Henderson.

KSNE broadcasts two channels in the HD Radio format.
