KSMR (92.5 FM) is a non-commercial educational radio station broadcasting a variety format. Licensed to Winona, Minnesota, United States, it serves the Saint Mary's University campus and entire Winona area. It first began broadcasting under the call sign KSMW. The station is currently owned by Saint Mary's University of Minnesota where it operates as the campus radio station.
