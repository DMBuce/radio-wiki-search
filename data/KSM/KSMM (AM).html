KSMM (1470 AM) is a radio station licensed to serve the community of Liberal, Kansas. The station is owned by Monte and Doris Miller, through licensee Rocking M Media, LLC, and airs an oldies format as a simulcast of sister station KAHE.

The station was assigned the call sign KLIB by the Federal Communications Commission on July 20, 1960. The station changed the call sign to KILS on March 1, 1985, to KYUU on October 4, 1988, when it was part of the "LS Network" of Kansas radio entrepreneur Larry Steckline. The call sign changed to KSMM on February 13, 2008.
