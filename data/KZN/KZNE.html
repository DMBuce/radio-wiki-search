KZNE (1150 AM) – branded The Zone 1150 AM – 93.7 FM – is a commercial sports radio station licensed to serve College Station, Texas. Owned by the Bryan Broadcasting Company, KZNE covers College Station, Bryan and much of the Brazos Valley. The KZNE studios are located in College Station, while the station transmitter resides in Bryan. In addition to a standard analog transmission, KZNE is simulcast over low-power FM translator K229DK (93.7 FM) College Station, and is available online.
