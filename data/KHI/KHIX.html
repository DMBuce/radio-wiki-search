KHIX (96.7 FM, "Mix 96.7") is a radio station licensed to serve Carlin, Nevada. The station is owned by Ruby Radio Corporation. It airs a Hot Adult Contemporary music format.

The station was assigned the KHIX call letters by the Federal Communications Commission on May 15, 1998.
