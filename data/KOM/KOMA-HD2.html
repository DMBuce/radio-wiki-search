KOMA-HD2 (92.9/92.5-HD2;FM) is the HD2 digital subchannel of radio station KOMA/Oklahoma City. Broadcasting an Alternative Rock format, the feed also simulcasts over translator K225BN (92.9 FM).

This K225BN translator was assigned its current call sign on April 24, 2013. KOMA-HD2 is relayed by a single FM translator to extend its broadcast area.
