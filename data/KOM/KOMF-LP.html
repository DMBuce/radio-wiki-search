K225BS (92.9 FM) is a radio station that broadcast local artist/bands from Denver, Colorado operated by the Rocky Mountain Public Broadcasting Network, Inc. under the branding of "Denver Open Media". The station carried mainly locally-originated content from local musical artists, and from a membership base of approximately 500 local individuals and organizations who used Denver Open Media equipment and facilities to produce and broadcast shows on radio and cable television. The station mainly served downtown Denver proper over-the-air.
