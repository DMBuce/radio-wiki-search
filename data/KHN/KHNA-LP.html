KHNA-LP (94.3 FM) is a low-power FM radio station broadcasting a Catholic Radio format. Licensed to Sheridan, Wyoming, US, the station is currently owned by Aperio Radio, Inc. The transmitter for the station is located in downtown Sheridan atop an office building.
