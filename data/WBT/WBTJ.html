WBTJ (106.5 FM) – branded as 106.5 The Beat – is a commercial mainstream urban radio station licensed to serve Richmond, Virginia. Owned by Entercom, the station services the Greater Richmond Region and the Petersburg area. The WBTJ studios are located just north of Richmond proper in unincorporated Henrico County, while the station transmitter resides in the Richmond suburb of Bon Air. Besides a standard analog transmission, WBTJ broadcasts over three HD Radio channels, and is available online via Radio.com.
