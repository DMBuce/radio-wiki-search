WBTZ (99.9 MHz "99.9 The Buzz") is a commercial FM radio station in Plattsburgh, New York, broadcasting to the Burlington, Vermont / Plattsburgh radio market, heard around the Champlain Valley and into Montreal. The station is owned by Hall Communications and airs an alternative rock radio format. On-air personalities include Pete Powers, Matt Grasso, Mays, Kat Vanish and Johnny Utah.

WBTZ is one of a handful of stations in New York State with an effective radiated power (ERP) of 100,000 watts as a Class C outlet. The transmitter is located off The Ninety Road in Morrisonville. The studios and offices are on Weaver Street in Winooski, Vermont.
