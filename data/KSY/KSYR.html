KSYR (92.1 FM) is a terrestrial American radio station, licensed to Benton, Louisiana and serving Shreveport/Bossier City. The station went silent on June 27, 2019.

Prior to going silent, the station was in trimulcast with KOYE and KCUL-FM as Regional Mexican formatted "La Invasora".
