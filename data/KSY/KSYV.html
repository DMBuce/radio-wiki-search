KSYV (96.7 MHz, "Mix 96.7") is a commercial FM radio station that is licensed to Solvang, California, United States and broadcasts to the Santa Maria-Lompoc, California area. The station is owned by Knight Broadcasting Inc. and airs an adult contemporary music format. KSYV features programming from AP Radio.
