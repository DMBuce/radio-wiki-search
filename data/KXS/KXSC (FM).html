KXSC (104.9 FM) is a radio station based in Sunnyvale, California, serving the San Francisco Bay Area. It is owned by the University of Southern California and airs a classical music format, simulcasting KDFC in San Francisco.

KXSC broadcasts in HD.

KXSC (AM) is, somewhat confusingly, also the call sign of the University of Southern California's student-run radio station, which airs a traditional college radio format and is broadcast online, as well as via 1560 AM in the Los Angeles area.
