KXSB (101.7 FM) is a radio station broadcasting Spanish language programs. Licensed to Big Bear Lake, California, United States, it serves San Bernardino County, California. The station is currently owned by Radio Lazer. Previously, this was a locally-oriented station called KTOT-FM in the early 1990s.
