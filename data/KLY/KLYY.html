KLYY (97.5 MHz, "José 97.5 y 107.1") is a commercial FM radio station located in Riverside, California, broadcasting to the Inland Empire , Greater Los Angeles and San Diego areas. KLYY airs a Spanish adult hits music format. It operates from studios in Los Angeles with the transmitter located in the San Bernardino National Forest.

KLYY broadcasts in HD Radio.
