KLYQ (1240 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a news/talk format. Licensed to Hamilton, Montana, United States, the station is currently owned by Townsquare Media and licensed to Townsquare Media Missoula License, LLC. KLYQ has studios at 320 North First Street in Hamilton, Montana.

The station was assigned the "KLYQ" call letters by the Federal Communications Commission.

The station previously aired oldies, but on February 2, 2017 became a simulcast of Missoula's KGVO, albeit with separate branding.
