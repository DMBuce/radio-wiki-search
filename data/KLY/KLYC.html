KLYC (1260 AM) is a radio station licensed to serve McMinnville, Oregon and surrounding areas such as the state capitol, Salem, United States. The station's broadcast license is held by Wesley Simkins, through licensee ProMedia Partners LLC.

KLYC Radio sports the theme of the "new AM Radio, Always Mobile" broadcasting reports from the street of news and community events. The station is also heavily involved in video streaming and podcasting.
