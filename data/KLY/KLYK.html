KLYK (94.5 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a Top 40-leaning hot adult contemporary music format that is focused on hit music from the 90s to now. Licensed to Kelso, Washington, United States, the station is currently owned by Bicoastal Media Licenses IV, LLC and features programming from Citadel Media. The station focuses on Today's Best Music with the slogan "Southwest Washington's Hit Music Channel."
