KLYT (88.3 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a Contemporary Christian music format. Licensed to Albuquerque, New Mexico, United States, the station serves the New Mexico area. The station is currently owned by Calvary Chapel of Albuquerque, Inc. The station is branded as "Star 88" and promotes itself as "Vertical Worship * Christian Hits * Fresh Indie". The station is heard via its main transmitter atop Sandia Peak, throughout New Mexico and portions of southern Colorado through a network of about 10 translators, and around the world via Internet streaming.
