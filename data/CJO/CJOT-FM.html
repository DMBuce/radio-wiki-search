CJOT-FM (99.7 MHz) is a commercial FM radio station licensed to Ottawa, Ontario. It broadcasts a classic hits radio format and is owned by Corus Entertainment. The station uses its on-air brand name Boom 99.7.

CJOT's studios and offices are located on Merivale Road in Nepean, Ontario along with sister station CKQB-FM. The transmitter is located off Stagecoach Road (Route 25) in Greely, southeast of Ottawa.
