CJOK-FM is a Canadian radio station serving the Fort McMurray, Alberta area broadcasting at 93.3 FM with a country music format branded on-air as Country 93.3. The station is owned by Rogers Sports & Media.

CJOK originally began broadcasting in 1973 at 1230 kHz. In the 1980s, the station received approval by the CRTC to move to 550 kHz, however, the move was never implemented. In 1994, CJOK added an FM rebroadcaster at 95.7 MHz on Tar Island. CJOK moved to the FM dial in 1997.
