CJOS-FM is a Canadian radio station, that broadcasts a classic hits format at 92.3 FM in Owen Sound, Ontario.

Owned by Larche Communications, the station was licensed in 2008. The CJOS call sign formerly belonged to a religious station in Caronport, Saskatchewan, which ceased broadcasting in 2006.

On June 9, 2010, CJOS received CRTC approval to decrease their effective radiated power from 20,000 watts to 9,400 watts.

On August 9, 2017, Bell Media announced that it would acquire CJOS. Bell Media received approval from the CRTC on February 14, 2018.
