KRBZ (96.5 MHz, "Alt 96.5") is an alternative rock station, licensed to Kansas City, Missouri and serving the Kansas City metropolitan area. The station is owned by Entercom Communications, with studios located in Mission, Kansas and a transmitter site in Kansas City's East Side.
