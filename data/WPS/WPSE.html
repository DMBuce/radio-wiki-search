The Station

Money Radio WP$E AM 1450/FM 107.1 is a commercially licensed radio station in Erie, Pennsylvania, and the market's only money-business news-talk radio station. It is owned by the Penn State Board of Trustees and operated by Penn State Behrend under the direction of a professional broadcast staff. In addition to being an around-the-clock, one-stop source for global and financial news, Money Radio WPSE also is the home for Bloomberg Radio, the Dave Ramsey Show, Jim Bohannon, Gordon Deal, Ric Edelman, Motley Fool Money, CBS News Radio, FOX News Radio, commentaries by regional business leaders, Cleveland Browns football, Penn State football, NFL primetime and postseason football including the Super Bowl, High School football and basketball, and Penn State Behrend sports.

Audience

Our core listeners are business owners, executives, community leaders, and investors seeking information about the day's breaking business news, financial information, and advice. WP$E's audience covers a broad span of adults above the age of 25 who support Erie's economy and its entrepreneurial spirit.
