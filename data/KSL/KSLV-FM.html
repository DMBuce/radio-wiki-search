KSLV-FM (96.5 FM, The Mix 96.5) is a radio station broadcasting an adult contemporary music format. It is licensed to Del Norte, Colorado, United States. The station is currently owned by San Luis Valley Broadcasting and features programming from AP Radio and Dial Global.
