KSLC (90.3 FM) is a College radio station at Linfield College in McMinnville, Oregon. It is broadcast over the air at 90.3 MHz and on the internet using the Live365 player. It is housed in Renshaw Hall on the Linfield College campus.

KSLC broadcasts in HD.

On April 2, 2020 at 7 am, KSLC switched to a simulcast of classical-formatted KQAC 89.9 FM Portland and to ICAN-children's arts on its HD2 subchannel.
