KSLU (90.9 FM) is a radio station broadcasting an adult album alternative (AAA) format. Licensed to Hammond, Louisiana, United States, the station serves the Lake Ponchartrain area. The station is currently owned by Southeastern Louisiana University and features programming from Public Radio International.
