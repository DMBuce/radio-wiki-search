KSLO-FM 105.3 is a Hispanic radio station licensed to Simmesport, Louisiana, serving the fringe area between the Lafayette, Alexandria, and Baton Rouge, Louisiana radio markets. KSLO-FM simulcasts the programming of KSLO AM 1230 in Opelousas, Louisiana and KFXZ 1520 AM in Lafayette, LA. KSLO-FM is owned by Delta Media Corporation along with KLWB, KLWB-FM, KXKW-LD, and KXKW-LP.

KSLO's studios are located on Evangeline Thruway in Carencro, and its transmitter is located in Avoyelles Parish, Louisiana. KSLO's audio feed was simulcast on KBCA's fourth subchannel in Alexandria until late 2019.
