KSLT (107.1 MHz) known as "Power 107-1 KSLT" is a radio station licensed to Spearfish, South Dakota serving Rapid City, South Dakota and the surrounding region with a Christian contemporary format. The station is currently owned by Bethesda Christian Broadcasting.

On October 24, 2011 KSLT moved from 107.3 FM to 107.1 FM.
