Barangay FM 97.5 Palawan (97.5 MHz) is a music FM radio station of GMA Network Inc. in the Philippines with its subsidiary RGMA (Radio GMA Network, Inc.). Its studios and transmitter are located at Solid Road, Brgy. San Manuel, Puerto Princesa City, Palawan. The station airs hits that are current pop, OPM and Hot AC in genre.
