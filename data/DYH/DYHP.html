DYHP (612 kHz AM), locally known as RMN Cebu, is a commercial news/talk, drama and music radio station owned and operated by the Radio Mindanao Network, broadcasting in Cebu City, Philippines. Its studios and offices are located at the RMN Broadcast Center, G/F Capitol Central Hotel and Suites (formerly The Professional Group Center), N. Escario St., cor. F. Ramos Ext., Capitol Site, Cebu City; while its transmitter site, modern facilities and 10,000-watt transmitter are located at White Road, Brgy. Inayawan, Cebu City. It also maintain its production center studios at Room 302, 3/F Jose R. Martinez Bldg., Osmeña Blvd., Cebu City for the most supplying drama programming to all cebuano-speaking from the other RMN stations in Visayas and Mindanao. DYHP was founded in 1963 as a music and news radio station and it is the first radio station in Cebu.

This station operates on Mondays to Saturdays from 3:00 a.m. to 12:00 m.n. and on Sundays from 3:30 a.m. to 11:00 p.m. On truncated hours, during the Paschal Triduum of Holy Week; the station's regular programming is pre-empted to give way by some special programs of religious songs and dramas in the Season of Lent every Maundy Thursday and annually during Siete Palabras days every Good Friday from 12:00 n.n. – 3:00 p.m.. During Black Saturday, however, DYHP is off the air and resumes broadcast operations at 3:30 a.m. of Easter Sunday.
