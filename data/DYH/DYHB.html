DYHB (747Â kHz Bacolod) Radyo Mo Nationwide Bacolod (formally called Radyo Agong and RMN Bacolod) is an AM station of Radio Mindanao Network in the Philippines. The station's studio is located in RMN Broadcast Center, 17th Lacson St., while the Transmitter is located at Sitio Sibucao, Barangay Banago, Bacolod. The main format of the station is drama, news, public service, personality programs and entertainment. It serves the general Bacolod Metropolitan Area and the rest of the (now-defunct) Negros Island Region.
