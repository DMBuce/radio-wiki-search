WGAC (580 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a news radio format. Licensed to Augusta, Georgia, United States, the station is currently owned by Beasley Broadcast Group, Inc., through licensee Beasley Media Group Licenses, LLC. WGAC broadcasts games of the Augusta GreenJackets, the local minor league team.
