France Bleu Toulouse is a public radio station by the France Bleu network for the Toulouse area. The station broadcasts in the Haute-Garonne department on 90.5 MHz from Toulouse, the station is part of Radio France. France Bleu Toulouse is based in the old Toulousian studios of Le Mouv' after the station moved its main location to the capital Paris.

France Bleu Toulouse started broadcasting on 23 February 2011 at 6 AM after a 2-month delay (it was originally planned to start broadcasting 14 December 2010), after a dispute between local independent radio stations and Radio France for using the 90.5 FM frequency.
