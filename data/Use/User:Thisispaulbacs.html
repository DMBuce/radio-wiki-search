Kabayaning Michaelinian FM (89.5Â MHz) is an educational campus radio FM station owned and operated by Saint Michael College of Caraga of the Diocese of Butuan. Its studio is located at the Ground Floor of Magbayanihan Building along Atupan Street, Nasipit, Agusan del Norte with its transmitter located within the Magbayanihan Building's fourth floor. Kabayaning Michaelinian 89.5 FM serves as the community radio station of Saint Michael College of Caraga delivering the good news through the Magbayanihan and Michaelinian spirit to Nasipitnons and Agusanons. Kabayaning Michaelinian FM airs every Monday to Sunday from 5:00 AM to 11:00 PM.
