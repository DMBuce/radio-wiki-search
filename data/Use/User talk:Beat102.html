Beat 102 Norfolk is an independent regional radio station in UK licensed by the MercyMedia Ltd. covering counties Norfolk, Suffolk and Essex in the United Kingdom. It began broadcasting on July 1st, 2010 from studios at The Broadcast Centre, Norfolk, Cromer, becoming the first station to operate under the licence.

The station is a Hip-Hop|R&B station aimed at listeners aged between 15 and 40.
