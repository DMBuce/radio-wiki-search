KZYR (97.7 FM, "The Zephyr") is a radio station broadcasting an Alternative Rock music format. Licensed to Avon, Colorado, United States, the station is currently owned by Gary Schwedt and James Schubert, through licensee Rocky Mountain Radio Group LLC.
