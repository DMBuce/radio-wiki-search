KZYS-LP is a low-power broadcast radio station licensed to Saint Cloud, Minnesota, serving Saint Cloud and Sauk Rapids in Minnesota. The station signed on the air in August 2015, from a tower shared with KVEX-LP on the campus of St. Cloud State University. The station airs an ethnic radio format, primarily Music of Somalia.
