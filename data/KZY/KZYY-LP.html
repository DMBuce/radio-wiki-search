KZYY-LP is a Spanish Religious formatted broadcast radio station licensed to Tyler, Texas. KZYY-LP serves the northern half, within the immediate City Limits of Tyler. KZYY-LP is owned and operated by Ministerio de Dios Pentecostal.

KZYY-LP's studio is located at 5763 Linwood Street, at the Iglesia de Dios Pentecostal, in the northeast section of Tyler.
