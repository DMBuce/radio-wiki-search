WRJC-FM (92.1 FM, "NOW 92ONE FM") is a radio station broadcasting a Hot Adult Contemporary format. Licensed to Mauston, Wisconsin, United States, the station serves the Wisconsin Dells area. The station is currently owned by Murphy's Law Media Group, LLC and features programming from CBS Radio.
