The Ogun State Broadcasting Organization also known by its acronym OGBC is the public-service broadcaster of Ogun State. The organization is headquartered at Abeokuta, the capital of Ogun State southwestern Nigeria and transmits on a frequency of 90.5FM and 92.80. It was established on February 2, 1977 as a government owned radio station.
