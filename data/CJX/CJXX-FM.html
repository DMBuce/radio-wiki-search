CJXX-FM is a Canadian radio station, broadcasting at 93.1 FM in Grande Prairie, Alberta. Owned by the Jim Pattison Broadcast Group, the station is branded as Big Country 93.1 and broadcasts a country music format.

The station originally began broadcasting on 1430 kHz (AM) on December 16, 1979, received approval to move to 840 AM in 1991 and then to its current FM frequency in November 2000.
