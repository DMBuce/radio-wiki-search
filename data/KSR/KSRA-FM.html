KSRA-FM (92.7 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a Country music/adult music hybrid format. Licensed to Salmon, Idaho, United States. The station is currently owned by Salmon River Communications. It features programming from Cumulus Media Networks.

Studios for KSRA AM-FM are located at 315 Riverfront in Salmon. Both transmitters are at the joint transmitter site, northeast of town, off North St. Charles Road.
