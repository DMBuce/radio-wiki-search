KSRV-FM (96.1 MHz "Bob FM 96.1") is a commercialFM radio station licensed to Ontario, Oregon, and broadcasting to the Boise metropolitan area. KSRV-FM is owned by the Impact Radio Group and airs an adult hits radio format. The studios and offices are on East Franklin Road in Nampa, Idaho.

KSRV-FM has an effective radiated power (ERP) of 47,000 watts. The transmitter is off Shafer Butte Road in Horseshoe Bend, Idaho, amid the towers for other Boise-area FM and TV stations. The signal covers a large section of Western Idaho and a small section of Eastern Oregon.
