KUUB (94.5 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a sports format. Licensed to Sun Valley, Nevada, United States, it serves the Central and Northern Nevada area. The station is currently owned and operated by Lotus Communications. Its studios are located on Plumb Lane in South Reno and its transmitter is located on Red Hill in North Reno.
