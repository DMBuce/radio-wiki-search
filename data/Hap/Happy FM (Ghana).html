Happy FM (Ghana) is a privately owned radio station in Accra, the capital of Ghana, broadcasting on 98.9 MHz from Accra, Ghana. It began official transmission on September 2003 and is owned and run by the Global Media Alliance. "The Best Ever Sports Station in Ghana Broadcasting Sports News."
