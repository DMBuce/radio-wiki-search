KBZT (94.9 FM, "Alt 94.9") is a commercial radio station licensed to San Diego, California. Owned by Entercom, the station broadcasts an alternative rock format. Its studios are located in San Diego's Stonecrest area, and the transmitter is located in La Jolla.

KBZT broadcasts in HD Radio; it carries two additional formats on digital subchannels, including the reggae Bob Radio, and dance music Glow.
