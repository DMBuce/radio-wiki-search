CHVI-FM (Spirit FM) is a Canadian radio station, which broadcasts a low-power Christian radio format on the frequency of 88.7 FM/MHz in Campbell River, British Columbia, with an effective radiated power of 26 watts. The manager of Spirit FM and president of Total Change Ministries is Terry Somerville. The music director is Trever Wickens.

Owned by Total Change Christian Ministries, the station received CRTC approval on October 15, 2010, and began broadcasting on April 8, 2011.
