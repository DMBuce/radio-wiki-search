CHVN-FM (95.1 FM) is a radio station licensed to Winnipeg, Manitoba, broadcasting a contemporary Christian music format. The station is currently owned by Golden West Broadcasting.

CHVN's studios are located on St. Mary's Road in south Winnipeg, while its transmitter is located near Springstein.
