WSIR (1490 AM) is a radio station broadcasting an Urban Contemporary format. Licensed to Winter Haven, Florida, United States, the station serves the Winter Haven area. The station is currently owned by Anscombe Broadcasting Group, Ltd. and features programming from ABC Radio .
