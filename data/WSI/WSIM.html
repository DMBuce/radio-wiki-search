WSIM (93.7 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a hot adult contemporary format. Licensed to Lamar, South Carolina, United States, the station serves the Florence area. The station is currently owned by Community Broadcasters, LLC and features programming from ABC Radio . The programming was also simulcast on WDKD, which now airs W246BX, or "97.1 Frank FM", the HD-2 channel.
