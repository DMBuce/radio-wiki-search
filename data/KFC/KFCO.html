KFCO (107.1 FM, "Hot 107.1") is a commercial Top 40 (CHR) radio station located in Bennett, Colorado, broadcasting to the Denver, Colorado area. Its studios are in Aurora and the transmitter is northeast of Leader in rural Adams County.
