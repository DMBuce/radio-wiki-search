WHBC-FM (94.1 FM) is a commercial FM radio station in Canton, Ohio, United States, broadcasting at 94.1 MHz with a Hot AC format. The station was established in 1948 as the sister station to WHBC, and it has been commonly owned with the AM station ever since.

The station back in the 1980s was Beautiful Music using reel to reel tapes. The station pushes about 50,000 watts of power, and can be heard as far north as Cleveland, OH and down south as far as Wheeling, WV and as far east as Beaver County PA.

By 2007, the station gradually morphed from adult contemporary to modern adult contemporary and in later years moved to a traditional adult top 40 format. Mediabase reports the station as a hot adult contemporary.
