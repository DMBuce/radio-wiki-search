WHBT-FM is a classic hip hop formatted broadcast radio station licensed to Moyock, North Carolina, serving Hampton Roads and Northeastern North Carolina. WHBT-FM is owned and operated by iHeartMedia.

WHBT-FM broadcasts in the HD Radio (hybrid) format.
