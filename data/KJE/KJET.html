KJET (105.7 MHz) is a commercial FM radio station licensed to Raymond, Washington, and serving Willapa Bay and Grays Harbor. The station airs a Hot Adult Contemporary radio format and is owned by Jodesha Broadcasting, Inc. Most programming on KJET is from Westwood One's Hot AC service, with some local segments. KJET uses a booster station on the same frequency (105.7) to widen its broadcast area to Grays Harbor County and can also be heard on two FM translator stations.
