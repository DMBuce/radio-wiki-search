Deutschlandfunk (DLF, German: [ˈdɔɪ̯t͡ʃ.lantˌfʊŋk] (listen)) is a public-broadcasting radio station in Germany, concentrating on news and current affairs. It is one of the four national radio channels produced by Deutschlandradio.
