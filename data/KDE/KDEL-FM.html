KDEL-FM (100.9 FM) is a radio station licensed to serve Arkadelphia, Arkansas, United States. The station is owned by Monte Spearman and Gentry Todd Spearman, through licensee High Plains Radio Network, LLC.

The station was assigned the KDEL-FM call sign by the Federal Communications Commission on January 31, 1979.
