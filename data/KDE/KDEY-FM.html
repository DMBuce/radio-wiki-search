KDEY-FM (93.5 FM, "93.5 KDAY") is a commercial radio station that is licensed to Ontario, California and serves the Inland Empire area. The station is owned by Meruello Radio Holdings Ltd. and broadcasts a classic hip-hop format. The KDEY-FM transmitter is located on Heaven Mountain to the north of Ontario. KDEY-FM simulcasts with co-channel station KDAY in Redondo Beach to fill coverage gaps in KDAY's eastern coverage area.
