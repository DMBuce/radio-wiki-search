WHCE is a non-commercial educational Contemporary Hit Radio formatted broadcast radio station licensed by the FCC to serve Highland Springs, Virginia, located on the East Side of Metro Richmond. WHCE is owned and operated by Henrico County Public Schools.

As of 2009, the station was operated by students from the Highland Springs Technical Center.
