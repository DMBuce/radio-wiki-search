WDYZ (660 kHz) is a commercial AM radio station licensed to Altamonte Springs, Florida, and serving Greater Orlando. The station is owned by JVC Media and airs a hot talk radio format. The studios and offices are in Maitland, Florida.

WDYZ's transmitter is off Charles Street in Longwood. The station uses a three-tower array. It is powered with 3,500 watts by day. But because AM 660 is a clear channel frequency reserved for WFAN in New York City, WDYZ must reduce power to 1,000 watts at night. The station uses a directional antenna at all times. Programming is also heard on a 250-watt FM translator, W288CJ at 105.5 MHz in Oviedo.
