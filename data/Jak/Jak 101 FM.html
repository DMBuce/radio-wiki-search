PM2FGG (101.0 FM), on-air name Jak 101 FM or Jak FM, is an adult contemporary radio station in Jakarta, Indonesia. In 2015, Jak FM held 6% radio market share. The radio company, PT Suara Irama Indah, was the first commercial FM radio station in the country.
