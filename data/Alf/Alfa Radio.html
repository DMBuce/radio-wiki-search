Alfa Radio (Macedonian: Алфа Радио) was a private radio station broadcasting in the Municipality of Radoviš, North Macedonia, with its headquarters located in the city of Radoviš. It started broadcasting on 1 May 2002, and since then it is one of the most listened radios in Radoviš. Alfa Radio is broadcasting on 98.3 FM in the Radoviš municipality and live on the internet.

The radio stopped its program on 7 October 2010.
