KDSH-LP (107.3 FM) is a radio station licensed to Borger, Texas, United States. The station is currently owned by Living River Ministries. In January 2010, the station has obtained a broadcast license from the FCC to operate at 67 watts of effective radiated power. KDSH-LP broadcasts a religious radio format.
