KHSA-LP (107.3 FM) is a radio station licensed to serve Hot Springs, Arkansas, in the United States. The station is owned by Maranatha Broadcasting Ministry Inc. It airs a Christian radio format deriving a portion of its programming from Radio 74 Internationale.

The station was assigned the KHSA-LP call letters by the Federal Communications Commission on September 25, 2006.
