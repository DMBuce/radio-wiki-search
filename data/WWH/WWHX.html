WWHX is an FM radio station on 100.7 MHz at Normal, Illinois. It received its license on January 10, 2006, and began its current Rhythmic Top 40 format with the name Hot 100.7 on April 1, 2018. The station is a Neuhoff Media through licensee Neuhoff Media Bloomington, LLC.
