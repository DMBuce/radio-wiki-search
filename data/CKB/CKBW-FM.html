CKBW-FM is a Canadian radio station broadcasting at 98.1 FM in Bridgewater, Nova Scotia. The station also operates in Liverpool, Nova Scotia at 94.5 FM and in Shelburne, Nova Scotia at 93.1 FM. The station plays a classic hits format branded as "70s 80s 90s CKBW" with the current slogan Turn Up The Feel Good. CKBW is owned & operated by Acadia Broadcasting which also owns sister station CJHK-FM.
