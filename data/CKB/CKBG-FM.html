CKBG-FM was a Canadian commercial radio station, which operated at 107.3 FM in Middle Musquodoboit, Nova Scotia. The station is called "CKBG 107.3 FM Blue Garage Classic Mix", and offers news and sports, a mix of rock, classic rock, oldies and country music.

Owned by Blue Garage Broadcasting, the station received CRTC approval on March 2, 2010.
