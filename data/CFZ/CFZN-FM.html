CFZN-FM is a Canadian radio station with an active rock format broadcasting at 93.5 FM in Haliburton, Ontario and is owned by the Vista Broadcast Group. It was founded by the Haliburton Broadcasting Group. The station uses an on-air branding as Moose FM.

CFZN began broadcasting in 2006 after it was given approval by the Canadian Radio-television and Telecommunications Commission on October 13, 2005.

On April 23, 2012 Vista Broadcast Group, which owns a number of radio stations in western Canada, announced a deal to acquire Haliburton Broadcasting, in cooperation with Westerkirk Capital. The transaction was approved by the CRTC on October 19, 2012.
