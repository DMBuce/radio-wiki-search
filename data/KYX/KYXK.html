KYXK (106.9 FM, "KIX 106.9") is a radio station licensed to serve Gurdon, Arkansas, United States. The station is owned by Monte Spearman and Gentry Todd Spearman, through licensee High Plains Radio Network, LLC. It airs a Sports format known as Fox Sports Arkansas.

The station was assigned the KYXK call letters by the Federal Communications Commission on February 8, 1993.
