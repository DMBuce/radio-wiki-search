KYXY (96.5 MHz) is a commercial FM radio station owned by Entercom. KYXY is licensed to San Diego, California with its studios in the city's Stonecrest area. Its transmitter site is in La Jolla, on the KGTV Tower which it shares with KGTV and several other San Diego FM stations. KYXY airs an English-language mainstream adult contemporary radio format.

KyXy 96.5 broadcasts in HD Radio. The programming on the station’s HD2 channel is FlashBack ALT.

KYXY was also the San Diego Radio Disney affiliate (on HD Radio) until June 2018.
