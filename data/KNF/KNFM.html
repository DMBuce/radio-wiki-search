KNFM (92.3 FM), branded as "Lonestar 92", is a country music-formatted radio station that serves the Midland–Odessa metropolitan area. The station broadcasts on FM frequency 92.3 and is under ownership of Townsquare Media.

KNFM broadcasts with 100,000 Watts from the top of the KPEJ-TV Fox 24 tower near Gardendale and has center of radiation of 985 feet making it the largest FM signal in the Permian Basin.

KNFM carries High School Football games live, rotating between the Midland High Bulldogs and Midland Lee Rebels weekly with Newstalk 550 KCRS.
