WFYL (1180 AM) is a daytime-only radio station broadcasting a News Talk Information format. Licensed to King of Prussia, Pennsylvania, United States, the station is currently owned by Alan and Susan Loch, through licensee Trinity Associates Broadcasting, LLC. Its Valcom whip antenna and transmitter are located in Jeffersonville in the Jeffersonville Golf Club.

As a daytime only station, its frequency is occupied by WHAM from Rochester, New York in the evenings.
