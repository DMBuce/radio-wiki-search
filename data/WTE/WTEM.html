WTEM (980 AM) – branded The Team 980 – is a commercial sports radio station licensed to serve Washington, D.C. Owned by Urban One and operated by Entercom pending an outright sale, the station services the Washington metropolitan area as a co-flagship station for the Washington Football Team (shared with Cumulus Media-owned WMAL-FM and WSBN), by virtue of previously being owned by Redskins owner Daniel Snyder's Red Zebra Broadcasting. WTEM is also the market affiliate for the Baltimore Orioles Radio Network, the Maryland Terrapins and Fox Sports Radio, and the radio home of Steve Czaban, Brian Mitchell and Rick Walker.
