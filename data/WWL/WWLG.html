WWLG (102.5 FM) – branded as La Grande 102.5 – is a commercial Regional Mexican radio station licensed to Baltimore, Ohio. Owned by the WHIZ Media Group of Zanesville and operated by Urban One via a long-term local marketing agreement, the station serves much of the Columbus metropolitan area. The WWLG studios are located in Downtown Columbus, while the station transmitter resides off of Pickerington Road in Carroll. In addition to a standard analog transmission, WWLG is available online.
