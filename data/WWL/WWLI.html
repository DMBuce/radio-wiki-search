WWLI (105.1 FM) is an adult contemporary radio station in Providence, Rhode Island owned Cumulus Media. Its transmitter is located in Johnston, Rhode Island, while its studios are located in East Providence. It broadcasts a Hot AC-leaning adult contemporary format.
