KRWB is a classic rock formatted radio station, licensed to Roseau, Minnesota. It's owned and operated by Border Broadcasting, along with sister station KKWQ. They share studios at 113 Lake St NW, in Warroad. Their two tower transmitter site is on County Road 28, east of Roseau.
