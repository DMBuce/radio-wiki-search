WAAJ (89.7 FM) is a radio station licensed to Benton, Kentucky, United States. The station airs a Christian Contemporary format and is owned by Heartland Ministries, Inc.

The station originally signed on in November 1996 with a Christian Contemporary format. In 2006 it switched formats to Bluegrass/Folk music. Then in October 2014, it went back to its original roots as a Christian Contemporary format.
