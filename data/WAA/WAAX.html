WAAX (570 AM, "News-Talk AM 570: The Big WAAX") is a radio station broadcasting a News/Talk format. Licensed to Gadsden, Alabama, United States, the station is currently owned by iHeartMedia, Inc.. Overnight, the station broadcasts the syndicated Coast to Coast AM hosted by George Noory.

Morning talk show host Dave Mack is a frequent guest/commentator on Nancy Grace's weeknight talk show on HLN.
