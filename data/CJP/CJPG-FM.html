CJPG-FM is a Canadian radio station being licensed to Portage la Prairie, Manitoba, serving the Central Plains region of Manitoba broadcasting at 96.5 FM with a hot adult contemporary format branded as "Mix 96.5". The station is currently owned by Golden West Broadcasting, and is located at 2390 Sissons Drive, along with sisterstations CFRY and CHPO-FM. While the Transmitter is located by Meseyton Construction Ltd. West of Portage La Prairie
