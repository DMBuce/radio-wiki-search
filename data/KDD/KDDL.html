KDDL (94.3 FM, "Cattle Country 94.3") is an American radio station licensed to serve Chino Valley, Arizona. The station is owned by Arizona's Hometown Radio Group and licensed to Prescott Valley Broadcasting Co. Inc. It airs a country music format.

The station was assigned the "KDDL" call sign by the Federal Communications Commission on October 15, 2007.
