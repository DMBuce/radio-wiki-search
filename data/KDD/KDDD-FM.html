KDDD-FM (95.3 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a classic country format. Licensed to Dumas, Texas, United States, the station serves the Dumas area. The station is currently owned by Highway 64 Radio, and features programming from ABC Radio .

In June 2006, KDDD-FM made a major update to their website. The new site includes local news stories and sports stories for the northern Texas Panhandle.
