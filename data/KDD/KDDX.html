KDDX (101.1 FM, "X Rock") is a radio station licensed to serve Spearfish, South Dakota. The station is owned by Riverfront Broadcasting, LLC. It airs an active rock music format.

The station was assigned the KDDX call letters by the Federal Communications Commission on April 28, 1995.
