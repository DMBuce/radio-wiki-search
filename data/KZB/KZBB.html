KZBB (97.9 FM, "B-98") is a commercial radio station in Poteau, Oklahoma, broadcasting to the Fort Smith, Arkansas, area. KZBB airs a hot adult contemporary music format. Before it became B-98, it was known as The Zebra 97.9, and played rock music. Formerly, KZBB is formerly known as B-98 airing a Top-40 station in the 1980's and 1990's before switching to The Zebra.
