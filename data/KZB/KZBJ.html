KZBJ (89.5 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a Christian CHR format. Licensed to Bay City, Texas, United States. The station is currently owned by the KSBJ Educational Foundation. KZBJ broadcasts in HD radio, with studios located in Humble. KZBJ's transmission site is located northeast of Palacios in extreme southwestern Matagorda County.
