KYBC (1600 AM) is a radio station licensed to serve Cottonwood, Arizona, United States. The station is owned by Yavapai Broadcasting Corporation. Yavapai Broadcasting is owned by W. Grant Hafley. It airs an Adult Standards music format.

The station was assigned the KYBC call letters by the Federal Communications Commission on May 24, 1996.

KYBC also operates on FM translator K242BZ at 93 watts serving the Verde Valley area.
