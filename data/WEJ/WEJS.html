WEJS (1600 AM) was a talk radio station in Williamsport, Pennsylvania, United States branded as NewsTalk 104.1 & 1600. WEJS' first air date was August 1, 1979. The station went silent on May 16, 2020.

WEJS programmed a mix of general-interest and conservative talk radio from Salem Radio Network and Westwood One, alongside sports talk from Fox Sports Radio. The station also carried high school sports, Pennsylvania College of Technology athletics, and national sports from Sports USA Radio Network.
