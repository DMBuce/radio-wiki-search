WHKZ (1440 AM) – branded Relevant Radio – is a non-commercial Catholic radio station licensed to Warren, Ohio. Owned by Immaculate Heart Media, Inc., the station serves both the Sharon, Pennsylvania, and Youngstown, Ohio market. WHKZ does not broadcast any local programming, functioning as a repeater for the Relevant Radio network. The WHKZ transmitter resides on Calson-Salt Springs Road in Warren, where WHKZ operates at a continuous power of 5,000 watts. The directional antenna pattern uses two towers during the day, and six towers at night.
