WHKC is an American non-profit religious FM radio station licensed by the Federal Communications Commission (FCC) to serve the community of Columbus, Ohio.

According to FCC filings,[1], Robert Casagrande - the President of Christian Broadcasting Services died 2007-10-13. His widow has replaced him in that position and the FCC has been notified of additional changes in the composition of the board of directors of the corporation.
