Radio "Astana" is a news and music station that started broadcasting from Astana, Kazakhstan on January 19, 1999 at 101.4 FM. On October 1, 2012, the station started broadcasting from new media center "Qazmediaortalygy" in Astana. The radio station is a part of "Kazakhstan" corporation.
