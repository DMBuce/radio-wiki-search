KNKR-LP (96.1 FM) is a radio station licensed to serve the community of Hawi, Hawaii. The station is owned by Kohala Radio and airs a variety radio format.

The station was assigned the KNKR-LP call letters by the Federal Communications Commission on March 10, 2014.
