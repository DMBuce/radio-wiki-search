KNKL (88.1 MHz) is a contemporary Christian music radio station in Tremonton, Utah. The station is owned and operated by the Educational Media Foundation (EMF) and broadcasts EMF's K-Love programming.

For nearly 50 years, the KNKL license was KWCR-FM, which was the radio station of Weber State University in Ogden. In 2012, a demolition of a dormitory that housed the station's transmitter and antenna forced the university to find a new location. However, the university failed to find a suitable site and KWCR became an online-only radio station in 2015. The university sold the license two years later to EMF, which moved the signal north in 2019 to cover Logan and northern Utah.
