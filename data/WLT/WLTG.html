WLTG (1430 AM/101.7 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a rhythmic contemporary format. WLTG also broadcasts the games played by the Tampa Bay Rays. Licensed to Upper Grand Lagoon, Florida, United States, the station serves the Panama City area. The station is currently owned by Hour Group Broadcasting, Inc.
