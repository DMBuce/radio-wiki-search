WLTR is a noncommercial Classical/News/Talk formatted radio station located in Columbia, South Carolina, United States. The station features both classical music as well as news and other programs from National Public Radio. The station is the flagship of the statewide "Classical NPR News network" from South Carolina Public Radio.

From its sign-on and sign-off in 1976 until 2001, it was the overall flagship of the South Carolina Educational Radio Network. In 2001, however, sister stations WRJA-FM in Sumter, WJWJ-FM in Beaufort and WHMC-FM in Conway broke off to carry an expanded schedule of NPR news programming.
