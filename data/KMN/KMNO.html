KMNO (91.7 FM) is a radio station licensed to serve the community of Wailuku, Hawaii. The station is owned by Maui Media Initiative, Inc. It airs a variety radio format.

The station was assigned the KMNO call letters by the Federal Communications Commission on August 1, 2012.
