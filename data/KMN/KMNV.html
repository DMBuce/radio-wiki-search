KMNV (1400 AM) is a radio station in the Twin Cities area of Minnesota that airs a Spanish-language music and entertainment format. KMNV's studios were formerly located on 27th Avenue South and East Lake Street in Minneapolis; the station currently originates out of the studios of KFAI (90.3) on Riverside Avenue in Minneapolis. Its transmitter is located near the Mississippi River in St. Paul.
