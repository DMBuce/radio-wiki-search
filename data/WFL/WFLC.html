WFLC (97.3 MHz, "Hits 97.3") is a radio station in Miami, Florida. Owned by Cox Media Group, it broadcasts a contemporary hit radio (CHR) format. WFLC's studios and offices are located on North 29th Avenue in Hollywood. The transmitter site is off NW 210 Street in Miami Gardens.

WFLC broadcasts in the HD Radio format. The HD-2 channel carries all-1980's Hits.
