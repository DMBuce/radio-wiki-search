WFLE (1060 AM) was a radio station broadcasting a country music format. Licensed to Flemingsburg, Kentucky, United States, the station was last owned by Dreamcatcher Communications, Inc.

Dreamcatcher Communications surrendered the station's license to the Federal Communications Commission for cancellation on May 27, 2020, and the FCC cancelled the license on May 28.
