WFLM (104.5 FM) is a radio station broadcasting an urban adult contemporary format. Licensed to White City, Florida, United States, the station serves the Vero Beach area. The station is owned by Midway Broadcasting Company. On August 27, 2013, the station applied for a frequency change from 104.7 to 104.5 MHz and an upgrade from C3 at 17.5 kW to C2 at 50 kW. The license for this change was issued on February 17, 2016.
