WFLK is a classic hits music formatted radio station in Auburn, New York. They are on the radio at 99.3 FM throughout the Finger Lakes.

Their format is classic hits. DJ's include Bobby Mac in the morning and Ken Paradise in the afternoon. News by Steve Penstone. Program director is Paul Szmal. They can be heard in 13 counties in Upstate NY. The station can be heard on their website at http://flradiogroup.com/index.php/stations/classic-hits-99-3-wflk/

On September 1, 2016 WLLW and its classic rock format moved to 101.7 FM Geneva, NY, replacing country-formatted WFLK. The 99.3 FM frequency picked up the WFLK calls and switched to a classic hits format, branded as "Classic Hits 99.3" at 5 pm on September 2.
