WPTX is an Adult Standards formatted broadcast radio station licensed to Lexington Park, Maryland, serving Southern Maryland and the Northern Neck.

WPTX is Maryland's first and only AM expanded band radio station. WPTX is owned and operated by Somar Communications, Inc.
