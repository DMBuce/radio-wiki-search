WPTW "The Voice of the Upper Miami Valley, Ohio" (for Piqua, Troy and West Milton) is a commercial AM radio station in Piqua, Ohio, United States, on 1570 kHz with a power output of 250 watts. It is owned by Muzzy Broadcasting Group. ABC Radio Network news and local news from hall of famer Terry Lafferty. Its music format is a mix of 1970s', 1980s' and pinch of 1990s' classic hits. Local high school sports coverage remains a tradition at the station in addition to Ohio State Buckeyes football.
