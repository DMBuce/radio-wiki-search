KKHH (95.7 MHz "95.7 The Spot") is a commercial FM radio station in Houston, Texas. It is owned by Entercom and airs an adult hits radio format. The studios and offices are located in the Greenway Plaza district of Houston.

KKHH has an effective radiated power (ERP) of 100,000 watts, the maximum for non-grandfathered FM stations. The transmitter is off Farm-to-Market Road near Fort Bend Parkway in Southwest Houston. KKHH broadcasts in the HD Radio hybrid format. The HD-2 subchannel carries Channel Q, an Entercom LGBTQ talk and EDM service. The HD-3 subchannel simulcasts the sports radio programming heard on AM 650 KIKK.
