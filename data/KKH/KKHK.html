KKHK (95.5 FM, "Bob FM") is a commercial radio station in Carmel, California, broadcasting to the Santa Cruz-Monterey-Salinas, California area on 95.5 FM. Owned by Stephens Media, it broadcasts a variety hits format. Its studios are in Monterey while its transmitter is located east of the city.
