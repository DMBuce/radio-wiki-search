WAYR-FM (90.7 MHz) is a non-profit radio station licensed to Brunswick, Georgia, USA, and serving the Georgia coast and northern suburbs of Jacksonville, Florida. The station broadcasts a Christian contemporary music radio format, and is owned by Good Tidings Trust, Inc. WAYR-FM holds periodic fundraisers on the air and asks for listener support.

The studios and offices are on Russell Road in Green Cove Springs, Florida, along with the co-owned AM 550 WAYR, which broadcasts a Christian talk and teaching format. WAYR-FM's transmitter is off Rose Drive in Brunswick.
