WAYO-LP is a radio station based in Rochester, New York, broadcasting at 104.3 MHz on the FM band.

WAYO is a free-form, low-power station in Rochester, NY providing diverse and idiosyncratic arts and cultural programming. WAYO is an affiliate of the Multi-Use Community Cultural Center (MuCCC).

WAYO is a non-commercial radio station broadcasting to the City of Rochester and environs that provides expansive musical and cultural expression. Programming will explore the unique possibilities of radio by emphasizing creative freedom, human personality, and diversity of voices. The station will actively pursue engagement with creators and cultural organizations.

Online broadcasting began on November 30, 2015. The launch of FM radio broadcasting began on January 4, 2016.
