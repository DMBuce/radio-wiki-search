WMNP (99.3 FM, "Mixx 99.3") is an American radio station licensed to serve the community of Block Island, Rhode Island. The station is owned by 3G Broadcasting, Inc. Its studio is located in Newport, Rhode Island. WMNP broadcasts a rhythmic adult CHR music format that targets southern Rhode Island, Southeastern Connecticut and the South Coast of Massachusetts.
