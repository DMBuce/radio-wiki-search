KHKU (94.3 MHz FM) is a Mainstream AC station licensed to Hanapepe, Hawaii, serving Kauai. The studio and office facility is located at 4357 Rice Street, Suite 201, in downtown Lihu'e, the county seat of Kaua'i County. The station is owned by Kauai Broadcast Partners LLC, whose managing member is Larry Fuss. Syndicated programming includes Open House Party with Kannon and Intelligence of Your Life with John Tesh. The call-sign KHKU is derived from the Hawaiian word "Haku", meaning "Star."
