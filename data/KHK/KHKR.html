KHKR (1210 AM) is an American radio station simulcast with a format known as 97.7 FM ESPN. Licensed to Washington, Utah, United States, the station is owned by Cherry Creek Radio.

KHKR has been granted an FCC construction permit to diplex at the KDXU site and decrease night power to 231 watts.
