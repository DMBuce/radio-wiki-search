KHKK (104.1 MHz) and KDJK (103.9 MHz), known as The Hawk, are a pair of commercial FM radio stations in the Central Valley of California. They are owned by Cumulus Media and they simulcast a classic rock radio format. KHKK is licensed to Modesto and KDJK is licensed to Mariposa. The Hawk carries the syndicated Bob & Tom morning show and during the football season, The Hawk airs Las Vegas Raiders games.

The Hawk's studios and offices are on Transworld Drive in Stockton. KHKK's transmitter is off Corral Hollow Road in Tracy, California. The one for KDJK is on Morrisey Lane in Mariposa.
