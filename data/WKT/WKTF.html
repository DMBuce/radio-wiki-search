WKTF (1550 AM) is a Christian radio station broadcasting a Southern gospel format. It is licensed to Vienna, Georgia, United States. The station is currently owned by Len Radio Broadcasting of Vienna, Georgia, LLC and features programming from Salem Communications' "Singing News Radio" satellite feed.

After several extended periods of silence over the years, Hammett Financial Management took over operation of WKTF on June 1, 2019 under a time brokerage agreement, pending consummation of the purchase of the station from Len Radio.
