WKTS (90.1 FM, The Bridge) is a radio station broadcasting a contemporary Christian music format. Licensed to Kingston, Tennessee, United States, the station is currently owned by Foothills Broadcasting, Inc. Call letters originated in the late 1950s as WKTS-AM, Sheboygan, Wisconsin, with the KT representing the Kettle Morraine area of East Central Wisconsin.
