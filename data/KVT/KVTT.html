KVTT (1110 AM) is an American radio station licensed to Mineral Wells, Texas. It is owned by Saumil and Poorvesh Thakkar, through licensee Decatur Media Land, LLC, and is broadcasting a South Asian full service format to the Dallas-Fort Worth Metroplex. Because KVTT uses the same frequency as Class A station KFAB in Omaha, Nebraska; it broadcasts only during the daytime and critical hours. Studios are located in Richardson along east Belt Line Road, and the transmitter is southwest of Alvord in Wise County.
