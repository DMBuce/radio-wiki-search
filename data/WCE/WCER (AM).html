WCER was an commercial radio station that was licensed to Canton, Ohio at 900 AM, serving the Canton-Massillon Metropolitan Area. The station broadcast from 1947 to 2011, ceasing operations when the owners voluntarily allowed their license to expire, and the Federal Communications Commission (FCC) cancelled it.
