KAOX (105.1 FM) is a Top-40 (CHR) formatted radio station licensed to Shelley, Idaho. Known as "Now 105.1", it advertises itself as "Idaho's only non-stop hit music channel", although it does play short commercials of itself and its sponsors. Dusty Bee and The Morning Show air from 6:00 am to 10:00 am each morning.
