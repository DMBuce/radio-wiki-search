KAOI-FM (95.1 FM, "iFM") is an American radio station licensed to serve the community of Wailuku, Hawaii, United States. The station, established in 1974, broadcasts a rock adult contemporary music format with a hybrid of classic rock and adult alternative. The station is currently owned by Visionary Related Entertainment, LLC.
