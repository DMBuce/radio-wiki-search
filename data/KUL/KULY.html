KULY (1420 AM) and KHGN (106.7 FM) are radio stations broadcasting a classic hits format. Licensed to Ulysses, Kansas, United States, the stations are currently owned by Mark Yearout, through licensee Southwind Broadcasting, LLC, and feature programming from ABC Radio.
