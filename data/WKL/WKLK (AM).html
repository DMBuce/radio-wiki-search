WKLK (1230 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a Nostalgia/Adult Standards format. Licensed to Cloquet, Minnesota, United States, the station serves the Duluth area. The station is owned by the Fond du Lac Band of Lake Superior Chippewa, and features programming from AP Radio and Westwood One.
