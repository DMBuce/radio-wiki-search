WKLB-FM (102.5 FM, "Country 102.5") is a country radio station licensed to Waltham, Massachusetts and serving Greater Boston. WKLB's studios are located in Dorchester. The transmitter is located in Newton, Massachusetts on a tower shared with WBUR-FM and several TV stations serving Boston and beyond.
