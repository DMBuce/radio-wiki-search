WCIM (91.5 FM, "Family Life Network") is a radio station broadcasting a contemporary Christian music format. Licensed to Shenandoah, Pennsylvania, United States, the station is currently owned by Family Life Ministries, Inc. and features programming from Salem Radio Network.
