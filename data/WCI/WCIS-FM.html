WCIS-FM is a Contemporary Christian radio station serving the Central New York Region. The station broadcasts at 105.1 MHz with an ERP of 33 kW and is licensed to DeRuyter, New York; it is currently owned and operated by the Family Life Network, a regional Christian broadcaster active in upstate New York and Northern Pennsylvania.
