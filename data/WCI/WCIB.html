WCIB (101.9 FM), known as "Cool 102", is a classic rock-leaning classic hits radio station licensed to Falmouth, Massachusetts, playing music from the 1960s through the 1980s. It is owned by iHeartMedia, Inc.. It serves the Cape Cod and New Bedford-Fall River markets, and had significant listenership in the Providence, Rhode Island market prior to a 2014 signal change.
