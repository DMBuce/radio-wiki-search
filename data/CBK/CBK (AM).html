CBK (540 kHz) is a Canadian public radio station, licensed to Watrous, Saskatchewan. It broadcasts the CBC Radio One network to most of southern Saskatchewan. Its studios are located at the CBC's broadcast centre at 2440 Broad Street in Regina, with an additional bureau in Saskatoon on 4th Avenue South. The Regina facility also houses CBK-FM and CBKT-DT.
