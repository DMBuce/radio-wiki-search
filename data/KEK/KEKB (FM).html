KEKB is a radio station serving Grand Junction, Colorado and its vicinity with a country music format. This station broadcasts on FM frequency 99.9 MHz and is under ownership of Townsquare Media, through licensee Townsquare Media Grand Junction License, LLC. The station began broadcasting May 24, 1984.
