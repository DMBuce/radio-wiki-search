WHUD (100.7 WHUD) is an adult contemporary FM radio station licensed to Peekskill, New York. The station is owned by Pamal Broadcasting and broadcasts on 100.7 MHz at 50 kW ERP from a tower site in Philipstown, New York, and has studios on Route 52 in Beacon, New York with other radio stations.
