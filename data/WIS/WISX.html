WISX (106.1 FM, "106.1 The Breeze") is a commercial soft adult contemporary FM radio station licensed to serve Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. Owned by iHeartMedia, Inc., the station serves as the Philadelphia affiliate for Delilah. The WISX studios are located in the neighboring community of Bala Cynwyd, while the station transmitter resides in nearby Wyndmoor. Besides a standard analog transmission, WISX broadcasts using two HD Radio channels, and is available online via iHeartRadio.
