KCCN-FM "FM100" is a Hawaiian Contemporary hits station. The SummitMedia outlet broadcasts at 100.3 MHz with ERP of 100 kW. The station's studios are located in Downtown Honolulu and its transmitter is located near Akupu. It also transmits on Oceanic Time Warner Cable digital channel 852 for the entire state of Hawaii.
