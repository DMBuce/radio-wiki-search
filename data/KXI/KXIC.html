KXIC (800 AM) is a radio station licensed to serve Iowa City, Iowa and the greater Johnson County area. The station is owned by iHeartMedia and licensed to Citicasters Licenses, Inc. It airs a News/Talk radio format.

The station was assigned these call letters by the Federal Communications Commission.
