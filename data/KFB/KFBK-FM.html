KFBK-FM (93.1 MHz) is a commercial radio station licensed to Pollock Pines, California and serving the Sacramento metropolitan area. It simulcasts KFBK 1530 kHz. KFBK-AM-FM air a news-talk radio format and are owned by iHeartMedia, Inc. The studios and offices are on River Park Drive in North Sacramento, near the Arden Fair Mall.

KFBK-FM has an effective radiated power (ERP) of 20,500 watts. The transmitter is on Grizzley Flat Road at Mehwald Lane in Sweeneys Crossing, an unincorporated community in El Dorado County. KFBK-FM broadcasts in the HD Radio hybrid format. The HD-2 subchannel carries the Smooth Jazz service from iHeartRadio.
