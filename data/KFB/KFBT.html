KFBT (103.7 FM) is a commercial radio station that is licensed to Hanford, California, United States and serves the Visalia-Tulare-Hanford and Fresno area. The station is owned by iHeartMedia, Inc. and broadcasts a rhythmic adult contemporary format. The KFBT studios are located on Shaw Avenue in North Fresno, and the transmitter tower is south of Fresno near Caruthers.

KFBT broadcasts in HD radio.
