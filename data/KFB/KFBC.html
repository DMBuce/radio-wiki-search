KFBC (1240 AM) is a commercial radio station that broadcasts a sports format. Licensed to Cheyenne, Wyoming, United States, it serves southeastern Wyoming, far western Nebraska and northern Colorado. The station is owned and operated by Montgomery Broadcasting LLC.

KFBC carries CBS Sports Radio programming. It is also Cheyenne's home for University of Wyoming football and basketball, the Denver Broncos, and the Colorado Rockies. The station also broadcasts via translator K248CZ 97.5 MHz, also licensed to Cheyenne.
