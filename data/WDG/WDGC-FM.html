WDGC-FM is a silent high school radio station located in Downers Grove, Illinois. The station runs commercial-free 24-hours a day and is shared between Downers Grove North and Downers Grove South high schools, with the transmitter residing at Downers Grove North. When student DJs are not on the air, a computer program that plays songs on shuffle and inserts station identifications and public service announcements is turned on.
