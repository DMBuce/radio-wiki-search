XHSD-FM/XESD-AM is a combo radio station in Silao, Guanajuato. XHSD/XESD broadcasts on 1530 kHz and 99.3 MHz and carries a pop format known as Arroba FM. 1530 kHz is a United States clear-channel frequency.
