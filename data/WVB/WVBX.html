WVBX (99.3 MHz "Live 99.3") is a commercial FM radio station licensed to Spotsylvania, Virginia and serving the Metro Fredericksburg area. WVBX is owned and operated by Alpha Media LLC, through licensee Alpha Media Licensee LLC. It airs a Contemporary Hit Radio format.
