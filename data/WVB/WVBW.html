WVBW (92.9 MHz) is a commercial FM radio station licensed to Suffolk, Virginia, and serving Hampton Roads. WVBW is owned and operated by Max Media. It airs an adult contemporary radio format using the branding "92-9 The Wave."

Studios and offices are on Greenwich Road in Virginia Beach, Virginia. The transmitter is off Greenbrook Drive in Suffolk.
