WILL is the call sign assigned to three public broadcasting stations owned by the University of Illinois at Urbana–Champaign and licensed to Urbana, Illinois, United States: NPR member stations WILL (580 kHz) and WILL-FM (90.9 MHz), and PBS member station WILL-TV (VHF digital channel 9, virtual channel 12). These stations are the main components of Illinois Public Media, and operate out of Campbell Hall for Public Telecommunication on the university campus.
