WILO 1570 AM and 96.9 FM is a radio station licensed to Frankfort, Indiana. The station broadcasts a classic hits format, local news, sports, weather, and agricultural information. WILO AM/FM also features a local, live talk show weekday mornings from 8:30 to 9:30. WILO maintains Clinton County Daily News.com, Boone County Daily News.com and a LIVE video stream channel for community events at www.hoosierlandtv.com. WILO AM/FM is owned by Kaspar Broadcasting Co., Inc. WSHW Shine 99 is a sister station to WILO, also owned by Kaspar Broadcasting Co., Inc.
