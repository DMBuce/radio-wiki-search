KETX (1440 AM, 102.3 FM) is a terrestrial American AM radio station, relayed by an FM translator, broadcasting a classic rock format. Licensed to Livingston, Texas, United States, the station is currently owned by Ken Luck, who also serves as the licensee. The purchase from Telcom Supply was consummated on October 2, 2019.
