KBNW (1340 AM) signed on the air August 29, 2008, broadcasting a News Talk Information format. Licensed to Bend, Oregon, United States, the station is owned by Summit Broadcasting Group, LLC (operated under a local marketing agreement by Horizon Broadcasting Group, LLC) and features programming from ABC Radio, Premiere Radio Networks, Talk Radio Networks and Westwood One, and is known as "News Radio KBNW".
