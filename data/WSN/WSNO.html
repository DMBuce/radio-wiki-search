WSNO (1450 AM; "105.7 The Beat") is a radio station licensed to serve Barre, Vermont. Established in 1959, the station is owned by Jeffrey Shapiro's Great Eastern Radio. The station broadcasts a top 40 format. WSNO's programming is also heard on translator W289CH (105.7 FM)

The station has been assigned the WSNO call letters by the Federal Communications Commission.
