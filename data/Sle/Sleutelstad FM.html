Sleutelstad FM is a Dutch local radio channel in Leiden, Netherlands. Sleutelstad FM plays music from the 70's till the music from now. The programming also involves a mix of local and regional information and music. Every hour at Sleutelstad FM you can also hear music from musicians, singers and bands from Leiden and the surrounding area.
