KVIS (910 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a Southern Gospel format. Licensed to Miami, Oklahoma, United States, the station serves the Joplin, Missouri, area. The station is currently owned by Northeast Oklahoma Broadcast Network, Inc., and features programming from Salem Communications.
