KVIP Radio is a Christian radio station based in Redding, California. It is heard on both the FM band at 98.1 MHz and on the AM band at 540 kHz and on a network of translators in Northern California and mostly Southern Oregon.

KVIP is also heard on 89.3 MHz KNDZ in McKinleyville, California, 90.7 MHz KMWR in Brookings, Oregon, and on 29 FM translators covering parts of Northern California, Oregon, Nevada, and Arizona.
