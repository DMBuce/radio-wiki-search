WMUV is a commercial FM Christian radio station that broadcasts to the Jacksonville, Florida area on 100.7 MHz. The station is licensed in Brunswick, Georgia to Chesapeake-Portsmouth Broadcasting Corporation and is owned by Nancy Epperson. It is branded as The Promise and broadcasts Christian adult contemporary music. Studios are on Bonneval Road in the Southside district of Jacksonville, and the transmitter tower is in Kingsland, Georgia.
