WMUU-LP (102.9 FM) is a radio station. Licensed to Madison, Wisconsin, United States, the station serves the Madison, Wisconsin area.

On August 6, 2019, an application was filed with the Federal Communications Commission to transfer the WMUU license to Cow-Power Media Production Company; the station will continue in perpetuity to broadcast First Unitarian Society of Madison services.
