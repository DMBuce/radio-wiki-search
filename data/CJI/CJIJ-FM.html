CJIJ-FM (branded as C99 FM) is a Canadian radio station, broadcasting in FM stereo at a frequency of 99.9 MHz from Membertou, a First Nations community near Sydney, Nova Scotia. CJIJ-FM plays a variety of rock music.

The station received CRTC approval in 2002 and went on the air in 2003.
