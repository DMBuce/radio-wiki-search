KBKK (105.5 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a Classic Country format. Licensed to Ball, Louisiana, United States, the station serves the Alexandria area. The station is currently owned by Stephens Media Group, through licensee Stephens Media Group License of Alexandria, LLC, and features programming from CNN Radio. Its studios are located in Pineville and its transmitter is in Ball, Louisiana.
