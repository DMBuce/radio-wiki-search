Bombo Radyo Naga (DZNG-AM 1044 kHz Naga City), (Originally DZDR-AM) is an AM commercial radio station owned and managed by Newsounds Broadcasting Network, Inc., under Bombo Radyo Philippines. The station's studio and transmitter are located at Bombo Radyo Broadcast Center, Diversion Road, Tabuco Drive, Naga City, Philippines. Bombo Radyo Naga provides news, public service, drama and music for the audience of Naga City, Bicol Region and through the whole world via www.bomboradyo.com. Bombo Radyo Naga airs every day from 4:00 AM to 10:00 PM.
