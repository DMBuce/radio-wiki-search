DZNJ (98.9 FM), broadcasting as 98.9 Radyo Kaamigo, is a radio station owned and operated by the National Nutrition Council under the Nutriskwela Community Radio Network. Its studios & transmitter are located at the Administration Bldg., Catanduanes State University-Panganiban Campus, Brgy. Calatagan, Panganiban, Catanduanes. "Kaamigo" stands for friend in Bicolano.
