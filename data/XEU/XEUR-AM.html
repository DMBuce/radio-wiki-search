XEUR-AM is a radio station licensed to and located in Mexico City. Broadcasting on 1530 kHz, XEUR is owned by Grupo Radiorama and carries a pop music format known as Éxtasis Digital. 1530 kHz is a United States clear-channel frequency. The station's transmitter is located in the borough of Iztacalco.
