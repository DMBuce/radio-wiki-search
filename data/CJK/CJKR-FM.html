CJKR-FM is a Canadian radio station broadcasting at 97.5 FM in Winnipeg, Manitoba with an active rock format under its on-air brand name Power 97. The station is owned and operated by Corus Entertainment which also owns sister stations CJOB and CJGV-FM. The studios & offices are located at 201 Portage Avenue in Winnipeg, while its transmitter is located on Brady Road in south Winnipeg.

CJKR is the most powerful FM radio station in Canada, operating with 310,000 watts. Most FM stations in Canada and the United States run 100,000 watts or less. Because CKJR-FM is one of the oldest FM stations in Canada, it was grandfathered with a much higher power. (In Winnipeg, CBW-FM also operates with an unusually high power, 160,000 watts. Power 97 is simulcast on Shaw Direct channel 860.
