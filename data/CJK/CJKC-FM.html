CJKC-FM is a Canadian radio station, broadcasting at 103.1 FM in Kamloops, British Columbia. The station broadcasts a country music format branded as New Country 103.1.

The station received approval in 2005 and was launched in 2006 by local broadcaster NL Broadcasting, the owner of CHNL and CKRV. It was acquired by Newcap Radio in 2017.
