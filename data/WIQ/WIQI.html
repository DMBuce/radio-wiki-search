WIQI (95.9 FM) is a radio station in Watseka, Illinois that is owned by Smash Hit Media and is operated under a local marketing agreement. The station currently carries a classic hits format covering Watseka along with Iroquois County and Kankakee with an output of 6000 watts ERP.
