WEKJ-LP (107.9 FM) was a radio station licensed to Chassahowitzka, Florida, United States. The station was owned by Christian Radio Network.

The Federal Communications Commission cancelled WEKJ-LP's license on February 4, 2020, due to the station failing to file an application for license renewal.
