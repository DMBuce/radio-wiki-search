DXYK 99.7 (Campus Radio Butuan) is a defunct FM radio station owned by GMA Network Inc. in the Philippines with its subsidiary RGMA. Since it was inactive in 2007 (along with Campus Radio 97.1 Laoag, Campus Radio 97.5 Tacloban and Campus Radio 97.1 Zamboanga), the station's studios and transmitter are located at San Francisco Street, Butuan City; the former address of the city where radio station is located.
