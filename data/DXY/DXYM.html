DXYM (89.5 FM), broadcasting as 89.5 Brigada News FM, is the flagship station of Brigada Mass Media Corporation. Its studio is located in 2nd floor, Brigada Complex, NLSA Road, Brgy. San Isidro, General Santos. This is a 24-hour operating station, except Mondays, where it goes off the air from 12:00 midnight until 4:00 am. During Holy Week of every year, however, where it goes off the air starting Good Friday at 3:00 PM, until it resumes broadcasting at 4:00 AM of Easter Sunday.
