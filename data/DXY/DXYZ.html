DXYZ (963 AM) Sonshine Radio is a radio station owned and operated by Sonshine Media Network International. The station's studio is located at the 4th floor, SM City Mindpro, La Purisima St., Zamboanga City, while its transmitter are located at No. 80 Castillo St. cor. Pasonanca Ave., Zamboanga City.
