DXYP (102.7 FM), broadcasting as Mango Radio, is a radio station owned by RT Broadcast Specialist Philippines Incorporated. The station's current studio is located at Km. 5 Buhangin Road (Beside Honda Motorcycles), Davao City, Philippines.

DXYP has been operating in Davao since 2016, and features a simulcast of its programs on DXKZ, its Zamboanga City counterpart.
