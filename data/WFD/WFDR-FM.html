WFDR-FM (94.5 FM, "Streetz 94.5") is a radio station broadcasting a Hip-Hop music format. Licensed to Woodbury, Georgia, United States. The station is currently owned by Steve Hegwood of Edgewater Broadcasting, and was previously owned by Ploener Radio Group, LLC. Prior to its Hip-Hop format, the station was branded as "Mountain Country 94.5" with "Today's Country Favorites & Legendary Treasures!" as its slogan. The station was purchased by Edgewater mainly to remove any issues regarding W233BF in Atlanta, which shares WFDR's frequency and simulcasts the same programming mainly meant for the larger market.
