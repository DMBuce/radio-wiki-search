WFDR AM 1370 is a radio station broadcasting a Classic Country format. It is licensed to serve Manchester, Georgia, United States, south-southwest of metro Atlanta. The station is currently owned by Ploener Radio Group, LLC.

The stations' call letters WFDR are a reference to former U.S. President Franklin D. Roosevelt, who had his Little White House vacation home in nearby Warm Springs, Georgia.

As of September 12, 2016, this station is being heard on translator W247CJ 97.3 FM. (Info taken from Radio-Locator)
