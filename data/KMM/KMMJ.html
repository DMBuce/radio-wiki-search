KMMJ AM 750 is a radio station broadcasting a Christian talk and information format. Licensed to Grand Island, Nebraska, United States, the station serves the Grand Island, Hastings, Kearney area. The station is currently owned by The Praise Network, Inc. and features Christian Talk programming. Prior to the switch to religious programming, the station broadcast a news/information format.
