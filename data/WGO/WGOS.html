WGOS (1070 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a news talk format. Licensed to High Point, North Carolina, United States, it serves the Piedmont Triad area. The station is currently owned by Iglesia Nueva Vida, a religious broadcaster. WGOS operates during the day only.
