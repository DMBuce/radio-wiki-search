WGON-LP (103.7 FM) is a radio station licensed to serve the community of Slidell, Louisiana. The station is owned by New Beginnings Church of Slidell, Inc. It airs a religious format.

The station was assigned the WGON-LP call letters by the Federal Communications Commission on February 22, 2002.
