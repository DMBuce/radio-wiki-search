WROD (1340 AM) is a radio station licensed to Daytona Beach, Florida and broadcasting a classic rock format. The station serves the greater Daytona Beach area. As of June 24, 2015, WROD is owned by Miracle Media LLC

The station flipped from oldies to classic rock at midnight on January 1, 2015.
