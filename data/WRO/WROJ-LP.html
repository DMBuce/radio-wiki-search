WROJ-LP is a Contemporary Christian formatted low-power broadcast radio station licensed to Saint Cloud, Minnesota, serving Saint Cloud and Sauk Rapids in Minnesota. The station signed on the air in late July 2015, from a tower shared with KNSI and KCML on St. Cloud's southeast side.
