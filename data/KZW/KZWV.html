KZWV (101.9 The Wave) is a radio station airing an Adult Contemporary format licensed to Eldon, Missouri, broadcasting on 101.9 MHz FM. The station serves the areas of Osage Beach, Missouri (as well as all the communities surrounding the Lake of the Ozarks) and Jefferson City, Missouri. KZWV's studio is located on Osage Beach Road in Osage Beach, with transmitting facilities just south of Eldon off of U.S. Route 54.
