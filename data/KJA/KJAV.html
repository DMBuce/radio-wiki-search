KJAV (104.9 FM, "Ultra 104.9") is a radio station licensed to serve Alamo, Texas, United States. The station is owned by Bi-Media, LLC, through licensee Bi-Media Licensee, LLC. The principals of Bi-Media are the Bichara family, which also owns the NRM Comunicaciones cluster in Monterrey, Mexico.

On January 18, 2016, KJAV dropped the Jack FM branding and rebranded as Ultra 104.9.
