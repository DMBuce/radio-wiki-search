KJAT-LP (97.3 FM) is a radio station licensed to serve Sulphur Springs, Arkansas. The station is owned by Stateline Christian Radio Group, Inc. It airs a Christian radio format deriving a portion of its programming from Radio 74 Internationale.

The station was assigned the KJAT-LP call letters by the Federal Communications Commission on September 26, 2005.
