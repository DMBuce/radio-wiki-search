KYFS (90.9 MHz) is a non-commercial FM radio station in San Antonio, Texas. It is a Bible Broadcasting Network (BBN) owned-and-operated station, airing a Christian talk and teaching radio format.

Some of the national religious leaders heard on KYFS include Chuck Swindoll, Joni Eareckson Tada and Adrian Rogers.
