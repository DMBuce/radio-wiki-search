KYFO-FM (95.5 FM) is a radio station in Ogden, Utah, United States. The station serves Ogden and Salt Lake City with Christian programming from the Bible Broadcasting Network. The primary transmitter site is located west of Ogden; a 7-watt booster for the main signal and a translator at 91.3 FM are located on Ensign Peak, improving reception in Salt Lake City itself.
