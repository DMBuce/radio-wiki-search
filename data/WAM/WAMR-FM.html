WAMR-FM (107.5 MHz) is a commercial FM radio station in Miami, Florida, United States. It airs a Spanish AC & Tropical radio format aimed at Spanish-speaking adults. The station is owned by Univision Radio with the license held by Univision Radio Illinois, Inc. The station also broadcasts on HD radio.

The transmitter is off NW 207th Street in Miami. The call sign WAMR refers to the Spanish word amor, which means "love".
