WAMB (1130 AM) is a radio station broadcasting an adult standards format. Licensed to Brazil, Indiana, United States, the station serves the Terre Haute area. It first began broadcasting in 1982 under the call sign WWCM. The station is currently owned by David Crooks, through licensee DLC Media.

1130 AM is a clear-channel frequency shared by Canada and The United States.
