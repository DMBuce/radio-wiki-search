WAMJ (107.5 MHz, "Majic 107.5/97.5") is a commercial FM radio station licensed to Roswell, Georgia, and serving Metro Atlanta. It airs an urban adult contemporary radio format, simulcast with sister station 97.5 WUMJ in Fayetteville. The station is currently owned by Radio One, via licensee Radio One Licenses, LLC. The studios and offices are located inside the Centennial Tower building in downtown Atlanta.

WAMJ's transmitter is on Goshen Springs Road in the Gwinnett Village section of Norcross, Georgia, near Interstate 85. It has an effective radiated power (ERP) of 18,000 watts horizontal and 33,000 watts vertical, broadcasting from a tower at 185 meters (607 feet) in height above average terrain (HAAT). That gives WAMJ a good signal in Atlanta and its northern suburbs, but may not be received well south of Atlanta. Those listeners can hear the same programming on 97.5 WUMJ, with its tower in Tyrone, Georgia.

WAMJ broadcasts in HD Radio hybrid format. Its HD2 subchannel carries urban oldies music, known as "Classix 102.9." It is rebroadcast on 125 watt FM translator W275BK at 102.9 MHz in Decatur.
