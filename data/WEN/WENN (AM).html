WENN (1320 kHz) is a commercial AM radio station licensed to Birmingham, Alabama. It simulcasts the urban adult contemporary radio format heard on co-owned FM 98.7 WBHK. It is owned by SummitMedia, which also owns six other Birmingham stations, and all share studios and offices in the Cahaba neighborhood in southeast Birmingham. It is not related to the fictional radio WENN in the American Movie Classics sitcom Remember WENN which ran from 1996 to 1998.

By day, WENN broadcasts at 5,000 watts. But to avoid interfering with other stations on AM 1320, it reduces power to 111 watts at night. The transmitter is in Hopper City on the city's northside.
