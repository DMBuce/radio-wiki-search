CFBC (930 kHz) is a commercial Canadian radio station in Saint John, New Brunswick. The station plays a country music radio format and is owned & operated by the Maritime Broadcasting System.

CFBC broadcasts with a power output of 2,000 watts daytime and 150 watts nighttime non-directional as of January 2020. CFBC previously broadcast at a maximum 50,000 watts using a 4-tower directional array to protect other stations on AM 930. They applied for and received approval in January 2020 to lower their power, with the licensee stating the change was required to address system failures, repairs, the ever-increasing costs of operating an AM radio station, and a steady decline in the stationâ€™s revenues. CFBC's transmitter, a former four-tower array, is located off Sand Cove Road in Saint John.
