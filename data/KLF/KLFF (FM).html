KLFF (89.3 MHz, "Family Life Radio 89.3") is a non-commercial FM radio station that is licensed to and serves San Luis Obispo, California. The station is owned by Family Life Broadcasting and broadcasts the Contemporary Christian music format of the nationally syndicated Family Life Radio network.
