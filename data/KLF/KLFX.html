KLFX (107.3 FM) is a radio station broadcasting an Active Rock format. Licensed to Nolanville, Texas, United States, the station serves the Killeen-Temple area. The station is currently owned by iHeartMedia, Inc. and features programming from Premiere Radio Networks. The station's studios are located in Harker Heights, and its transmitter is located in Nolanville.
