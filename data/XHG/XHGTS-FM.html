XHGTS-FM 107.3 (branded as Me Gusta 107.3) is an International Contemporary Music format FM radio station that serves the Nuevo Laredo, Tamaulipas, Mexico and Laredo, Texas, United States border area. XHGTS mostly airs Spanish and English language pop and rock hits.
