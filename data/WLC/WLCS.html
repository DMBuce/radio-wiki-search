WLCS (98.3 FM, "Classic Hits 98.3") is a radio station broadcasting a classic hits format. Licensed to North Muskegon, Michigan, it first began broadcasting under the WFMM call sign.

WLCS is a Cumulus Media station, airing local on-air hosts and broadcasts plus the network's Greatest Mojo ("Classic Rock & Roll") satellite feed. 98.3 WLCS is home to classic hits from three decades. Hear from artists such as the Beatles, Fleetwood Mac, The Monkees, The Eagles, The Beach Boys, Chicago, Foreigner, and many of Motown's best. Program Schedule
