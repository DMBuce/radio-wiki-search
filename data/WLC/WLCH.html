WLCH (91.3 FM, "Radio Centro") is a non-commercial public FM radio station licensed to serve Lancaster, Pennsylvania. The station is owned by the Spanish American Civic Association For Equality, Inc., and broadcasts a variety format. Its broadcast tower is located in Manheim Township north of Lancaster at (40°04′13.6″N 76°17′18.5″W).

WLCH is one of 33 owned and operated Hispanic Public Radio stations in the United States.
