KSCU (103.3 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a Variety format. Licensed to Santa Clara, California, the station serves the San Francisco Bay Area. The station is currently owned by Santa Clara University. The station plays independent music, varying from indie rock, punk, jazz, blues, electronic, and others. KSCU has been a radio station (with one name or another) for over 50 years.
