KSCJ (1360 AM, "Talk Radio 1360") is a radio station licensed to serve Sioux City, Iowa. The station is owned by Powell Broadcasting and licensed to KSUX/KSCJ Radio Broadcasting Co. It airs a News/Talk radio format.

The station was assigned these call letters by the Federal Communications Commission.
