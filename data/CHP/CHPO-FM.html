CHPO-FM is a radio station which broadcasts a country music format on the frequency of 93.1 MHz FM in Portage la Prairie, Manitoba, Canada. The station is owned by Golden West Broadcasting, and is located at 2390 Sissons Drive, along with CFRY and CJPG-FM.
