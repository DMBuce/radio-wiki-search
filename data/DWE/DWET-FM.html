DWET (106.7 FM), on-air as 106.7 Energy FM, is the flagship radio station of Ultrasonic Broadcasting System in the Philippines. Its studios, offices and transmitter are located at Unit D-1, 15th floor, Strata 2000 Building, F. Ortigas Jr. Avenue, Ortigas Center, Pasig City. This station operates 24/7.
