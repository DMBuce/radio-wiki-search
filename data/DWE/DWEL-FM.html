DWEL (95.5 FM), broadcasting as MOR 95.5 For Life!, was a radio station owned and operated by ABS-CBN Corporation. Its studio is located at 2/F CAP Building, Brgy. 9, Balintawak St., cor. F.R. Castro Ave., Laoag City while its transmitter is located in San Nicolas, Ilocos Norte.
