WUBL (94.9 MHz) is a commercial FM radio station known as 94-9 The Bull. It is owned by iHeartMedia and it plays a country music radio format. The studios and offices are located at the Peachtree Palisades Building in the Brookwood Hills district of Atlanta. It has local DJs in the daytime and at night it carries two syndicated Premiere Networks country music programs, The Bobby Bones Show and CMT Radio Live with Cody Alan. The station's radio transmitter is located just northeast of Atlanta near Druid Hills Road in North Druid Hills, with several other stations.
