WUBR (910 AM) is a radio station licensed to serve Baton Rouge, Louisiana, United States. The station is owned by Red Peach LLC. It used to air a Gospel music format but as of November 7, 2007 the station is listed as "licensed and silent" in the FCC database.

The station was assigned the WUBR call letters by the Federal Communications Commission on March 21, 2006.
