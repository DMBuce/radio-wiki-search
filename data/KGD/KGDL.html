KGDL is a radio station on 92.1 FM in Trent, Texas. It is owned by José Antonio Aguilar, through licensee Radio Trent LLC, and carries the La Mejor Regional Mexican format from MVS Radio.

KGDL also broadcasts on an FM translator at 105.9 mHz which is owned by Armida A. Saille.
