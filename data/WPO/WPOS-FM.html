WPOS-FM (102.3 FM) is a 501(c)3 non-profit listener-supported & commercial radio station broadcasting Contemporary Christian music and a teaching programs format. WPOS-FM is licensed to Holland, Ohio, United States, and is currently owned by The Maumee Valley Broadcasting Association.

The station began broadcasting in 1966. Studios and transmitter are located at 7112 Angola Rd. Holland, Ohio 43528.

The station is an affiliate of the Moody Radio network and Salem Radio Network for the Toledo area.
