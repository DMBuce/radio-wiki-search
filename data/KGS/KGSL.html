KGSL (95.3 FM, "KG-95.3") is a radio station broadcasting a hot adult contemporary format. Licensed to Winona, Minnesota, United States, the station features programming from Jones Radio Network.

It is owned by Leighton Broadcasting, through licensee Leighton Radio Holdings, Inc., and is located at 752 Bluffview Circle, with its other sister stations.
