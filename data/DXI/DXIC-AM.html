DXIC (711 AM) RMN Iligan is a radio station owned and operated by the Radio Mindanao Network. The station's studio is located at the 4th Floor, Mejia Bldg., Roxas cor. Aguinaldo St., Iligan. DXIC is the pioneer (and only) AM station in Iligan.
