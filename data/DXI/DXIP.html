DXIP (900 kHz Davao City) El Nuevo Bantay Radyo is an AM station owned by Southern Broadcasting Network and affiliated with Bantay Radyo. The station's studio is located along 3rd Floor, Lachmi Shopping Mall, Bolton in Davao City. The main format of the station is music, public service and news. This station is inactive since 2009.
