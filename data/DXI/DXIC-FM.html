95.3 Hope Radio (DXIC 95.3 MHz) is an FM station owned and operated by Adventist Media. Its studios are located at Salvany St. cor. Mabuhay Rd., General Santos, and its transmitter is located at Alabel, Sarangani. The frequency is formerly owned by Matutum Broadcasting Network.
