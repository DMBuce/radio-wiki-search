DXIQ (106.3 FM), broadcasting as 106.3 Love Radio, is a radio station owned and operated by Manila Broadcasting Company. Its studios and transmitter are located at the 4th Floor, SG & G Bldg. Fortich St., Malaybalay, Bukidnon.
