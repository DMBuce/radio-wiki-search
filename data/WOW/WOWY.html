WOWY is a classic hits music formatted radio station broadcasting from University Park, Pennsylvania featuring the hits of the 1950s, 1960s, 1970s and 1980s.

The owner is Southern Belle LLC, which also owns WBHV, WFEQ and WZWW.

The current lineup is The Real Deal Bob Steele 6-10am, Roni Lynn 10am-2pm, Roger Corey 2-7pm and Kaptain Kranch 7-Midnight.

On June 28, 2019, WOWY began simulcasting on WHUN 1150 AM and WHUN-FM 103.5 Huntingdon and W249DD 97.7 FM Huntingdon and rebranded as "97.1 97.7 103.5 WOWY".
