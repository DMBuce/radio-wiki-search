WOWD-LP is a Variety formatted broadcast radio station licensed to Takoma Park, Maryland, serving Takoma Park, College Park and Hyattsville in Maryland, along with parts of Northeast and Northwest Washington, D.C. WOWD-LP is owned and operated by Historic Takoma, Inc.
