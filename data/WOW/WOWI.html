WOWI (102.9 FM) – branded 103 Jamz – is a commercial urban contemporary radio station licensed to Norfolk, Virginia. Owned by iHeartMedia, Inc., the station services Hampton Roads and Northeastern North Carolina, and is the local affiliate for The Breakfast Club. The WOWI studios are located in Norfolk, while the transmitter is in nearby Chesapeake. Besides a standard analog transmission, WOWI broadcasts over two HD Radio digital subchannels, and is available online via iHeartRadio.
