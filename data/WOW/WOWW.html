WOWW (1430 kHz) is a commercial AM radio station licensed to Germantown, Tennessee, and serving the Memphis metropolitan area. WOWW carries a classic hits radio format and calls itself "Guess FM." It is owned and operated by Flinn Broadcasting Corporation. The studios and offices are in Southaven, Mississippi.

WOWW is powered at 2,500 watts. At night, to protect other stations on 1430 AM, WOWW uses a directional antenna with a six-tower array. The transmitter is off Orgill Road in Bartlett, Tennessee. Programming is also heard on two FM translators: 220 watt W249BN at 97.7 MHz in Memphis and 250 watt W261CE at 100.1 MHz in Southaven, Mississippi.
