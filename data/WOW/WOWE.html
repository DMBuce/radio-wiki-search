WOWE (98.9 FM), branded "98.9 Jamz" is a radio station licensed to Vassar, Michigan. The station features an urban AC format targeting the Flint and Saginaw markets. Its studios are in Flint and its transmitter is in Millington, Michigan. The station now features both Urban AC music and African-American-oriented talk shows, including the John Mason morning show, syndicated from WGPR in Detroit. Sunday programming consists of gospel music and smooth jazz music (via satellite from Broadcast Architecture's Smooth Jazz Network).
