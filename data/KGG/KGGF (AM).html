KGGF (690 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a News Talk Information format. Licensed to Coffeyville, Kansas, United States, the station serves Southeast Kansas, as well as Wichita, Kansas, Tulsa, Oklahoma, and Joplin, Missouri. The station is currently owned by Sek Media, LLC, and features programming from Fox News Radio.
