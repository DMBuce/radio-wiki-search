KWXM (102.3 FM, "Sold Gold & Blues") is an American radio station broadcasting urban oldies music format. Licensed to Simsboro, Louisiana, United States, the station serves Ruston, Claiborne Parish, and surrounding areas. The station is currently owned by William W. Brown, through licensee North Louisiana Broadcasting, Inc.
