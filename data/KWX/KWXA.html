KWXA (1400 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a classic rock format. Licensed to serve Winnemucca, Nevada, United States, the station is currently owned by Isaac Diaz through licensee Fundacion Manitas Llenas, Inc., and features programming from ABC Radio.
