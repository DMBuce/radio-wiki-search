KKUS (104.1 FM; 104-1 The Ranch) is a radio station broadcasting a Classic Country format. Licensed to Tyler, Texas, United States, the station serves the Tyler-Longview area. The station is currently owned by Alpha Media LLC, through licensee Alpha Media Licensee LLC, and features programming from Fox News Radio. Its studios are located on Broadway Avenue in downtown Tyler, and its transmitter is located near Hideaway, Texas.
