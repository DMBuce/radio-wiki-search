WCPN (90.3 FM) – branded "90.3 WCPN" – is a non-commercial educational radio station licensed to Cleveland, Ohio. Owned by Ideastream, the station serves Greater Cleveland and much of surrounding Northeast Ohio. The WCPN studios are located at Playhouse Square in Downtown Cleveland, with a transmitter site in the Cleveland suburb of Parma. Broadcasting a mix of public radio and jazz, WCPN is the Cleveland affiliate for National Public Radio (NPR), Public Radio International, and the BBC World Service. In addition to its standard analog signal, WCPN broadcasts over two HD Radio channels, and is available online.

WCPN traditionally dates its start to September 8, 1984, when it began regular operation under its current broadcast license and call sign. However, other accounts trace its history to the station it supplanted, WBOE, which in 1938 was the first formally recognized educational radio station in the United States under the auspices of the Cleveland Board of Education, and in 1941 became the first educational station operating on the FM band.
