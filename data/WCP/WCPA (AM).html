WCPA is an American radio station, licensed to Clearfield, Pennsylvania, the seat of government for Clearfield County. WCPA is owned and operated by Kristin Cantrell, through licensee Southern Belle, LLC. WCPA broadcasts at the assigned frequency of 900 kHz with a power output of 2,500 watts day; and 500 watts night.
