WCPT (820 AM) is a commercial progressive talk radio station licensed to Willow Springs, Illinois. Owned by the Newsweb Corporation, the station serves the Chicago metropolitan area. The studios and daytime transmitter are located in the Jefferson Park neighborhood on Chicago's Northwest Side, while the nighttime transmitter resides in Joliet.

WCPT is the Chicago affiliate for The Stephanie Miller Show, Democracy Now!, The David Pakman Show, the Thom Hartmann Program and The Rick Ungar Show; and is the radio home of Santita Jackson and Joan Esposito.
