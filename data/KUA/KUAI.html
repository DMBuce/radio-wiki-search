KUAI (570 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a "Real Country" 70s 80s 90s early 2000s format. Licensed to Eleele in the U.S. state of Hawaii, the station serves the Kauai area. The station is currently owned by Pacific Radio Group, Inc.
