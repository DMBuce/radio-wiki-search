KUAD-FM (99.1 FM, "New Country 99.1") is a country music-formatted radio station licensed to Windsor, Colorado, United States. The station serves the Ft. Collins-Greeley and Cheyenne areas. The station is currently owned by Townsquare Media.

On February 4, 2019, KUAD-FM rebranded from "K99" to "New Country 99.1".
