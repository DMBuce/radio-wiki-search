WTNK is a radio station broadcasting a classic hits format. It operates on 1090 kHz in the AM broadcast band with 1000 watts during the day and 2 watts at night. WTNK utilizes a translator on 93.5 MHz with 250 watts ERP. Licensed to Hartsville, Tennessee, United States, the station is currently owned by Steve Sudbury and Louis M. Anzek, through licensee Fun Media Group of Tennessee, LLC. The station's studios and transmitters are located on Marlene Street.

The station featured a country music format until December 2015.
