WTNN (97.5 FM) is an American radio station broadcasting a country music format. Licensed to serve the community of Bristol, Vermont, US, the station broadcasts to the Burlington-Plattsburgh area. The station is currently owned by Impact Radio. Brian Ram serves as WTNN's program director.
