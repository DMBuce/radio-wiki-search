WTNQ (104.9 FM, "Q104.9") is a radio station broadcasting a Christmas music format, having switched over from classic country in November 2020 and before that from oldies in June 2020. Licensed to La Follette, Tennessee, United States, the station is currently owned Momentum Broadcasting LLC. But is operated by Loud Media under LMA.
