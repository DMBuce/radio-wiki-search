WTNT-FM (94.9 FM) is a country radio station broadcasting in Tallahassee, Florida. Under ownership of iHeartMedia, Inc., its studios and transmitter are located separately in the northside of Tallahassee. The weekday on air lineup includes The Bobby Bones Show (6:00 am–10:00 am), Billy Greenwood (10:00 am–3:00 pm), Jason Taylor (3:00 pm–7:00 pm), and Tige & Daniel (7:00 pm-midnight).
