KWPS-FM (99.7 FM, "ESPN WPS") is a radio station broadcasting a sports format. Licensed to Caddo Valley, Arkansas, United States, it serves the Arkadelphia, Arkansas area. The station is currently owned by Monte Spearman and Gentry Todd Spearman through licensee High Plains Radio Network, LLC.
