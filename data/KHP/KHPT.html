KHPT (106.9 FM, "The Eagle @ 106-9") is a classic rock radio station licensed to Conroe, Texas, which simulcasts on KGLK to reach the parts of the Houston metro where 107.5 is weak. It is owned by Cox Media Group and is part of the Houston cluster that includes KGLK, KKBQ, and KTHT. It is headquartered in Suite 2300 at 3 Post Oak Central in the Uptown district in Houston, Texas, United States. KHPT's transmitter is located in Splendora, Texas, once shared with KSBJ.
