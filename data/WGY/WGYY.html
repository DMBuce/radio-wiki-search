WGYY/WGYI, branded as Froggy 100.3 and 98.5, are country music formatted radio stations in Crawford County, Pennsylvania. Both stations simulcast the same programming. They share a market with Willie 95.1 in Grove City, Pennsylvania, but the station is unaffiliated.
