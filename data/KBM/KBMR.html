KBMR (1130 AM), known as "Country 1130", is a radio station located in Bismarck, North Dakota, owned by iHeartMedia, Inc.. The station runs a classic country format. KBMR also produces KBMY's morning newscast, Good Morning West Dakota.

iHeartMedia, Inc. also owns KFYR 550 (News/Talk), KXMR 710 (Sports), KYYY 92.9 (Adult Contemporary), KQDY 94.5 (Country), and KSSS 101.5 (Classic rock) in the Bismarck-Mandan area.

KBMR signed on as KBMK on 1350 kHz in 1958. It moved to 1130 kHz in 1971.
