KBMX (107.7 FM, MIX 108) is a Top 40 (CHR) radio station serving Duluth, Minnesota, and Superior, Wisconsin. It is owned by Townsquare Media.

The studios and offices are with its three other sister stations at 14 E. Central Entrance, on the west side of Duluth.
