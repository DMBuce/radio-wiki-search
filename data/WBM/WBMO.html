WBMO (106.3 MHz, "Power 107.5 & 106.3") is a commercial FM radio station licensed to London, Ohio, and serving the Columbus metropolitan area. It airs an urban contemporary radio format and is owned by Radio One. WBMO and sister station 107.5 WCKX simulcast their programming. The two stations carry the syndicated Rickey Smiley Morning Show weekdays from 6 to 10 a.m.

The studios and offices are on East First Avenue, just north of downtown. The transmitter is off London-Lockbourne Road in London.
