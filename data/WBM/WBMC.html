WBMC (960 AM, The Information Station) is a radio station broadcasting a news talk information format. Licensed to McMinnville, Tennessee, United States, the station is currently owned by Peg Broadcasting, LLC and features programming from Citadel Broadcasting, Dial Global, and Premiere Radio Networks.
