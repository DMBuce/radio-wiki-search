DXWK (101.5 FM), broadcasting as 101.5 Love Radio, is a music FM radio station owned and operated by Manila Broadcasting Company. Its studios and transmitter is located at 3rd floor, RD Plaza Bldg., Pendatun Ave., Brgy. Dadiangas West, General Santos, Philippines.
