WERL (950 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a Conservative Talk radio format. Licensed to Eagle River, Wisconsin, United States. The station is currently owned by Heartland Comm. License, LLC and features programming from ABC Radio.

WERL dropped its longtime Adult Standards format on July 1, 2010, in favor of the current "Freedom Talk" format.
