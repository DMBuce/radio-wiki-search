WERR (104.1 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a Contemporary Christian format. Licensed to Vega Alta, Puerto Rico, it serves the Puerto Rico area. The station is branded as 104.1 FM Redentor and is currently owned by WERR Radio Redentor, Inc. The station is the first Christian radio station in Puerto Rico.

The station is relayed through booster stations, WERR-FM1 in Caguas and WERR-FM2 in Yauco, and WERR-FM3 in Mayaguez three of them operating at 104.1 FM.
