KKPR-FM (98.9 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a classic hits format. Licensed to Kearney, Nebraska, United States, the station serves the Grand Island-Kearney area. The station is currently owned by Platte River Radio, Inc.

The station launched in June 1959 as KHOL-FM.
