KTWS (98.3 FM, "The Twins") is a commercial radio station broadcasting a classic rock music format to Bend in the U.S. state of Oregon. It has filed an application for a U.S. Federal Communications Commission construction permit to move to 98.5 MHz and increase ERP to 50,000 watts.
