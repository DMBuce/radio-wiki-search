KTWV (94.7 FM, ("94.7 The Wave") is a commercial FM radio station that is licensed to Los Angeles, California and broadcasts to the Greater Los Angeles area. The station is owned by Entercom and airs an urban adult contemporary radio format. KTWV has studios on Wilshire Boulevard in the Miracle Mile district of Los Angeles, and its transmitter (shared with its former sister station KTTV) is located on Mount Wilson.

KTWV is known for pioneering the smooth jazz radio format in the late 1980s.

KTWV broadcasts in HD Radio.
