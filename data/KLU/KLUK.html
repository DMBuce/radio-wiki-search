KLUK (97.9 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a classic rock format. Licensed to Needles, California, United States, it serves the entire "Tri-State" area including Lake Havasu, Kingman, Laughlin/Bullhead City and Needles, California. The station is currently owned by Cameron Broadcasting, Inc. and features TnT Radio Empire and Star in The Afternoon. Lucky 98 FM is the only live and local classic rock station in the area.
