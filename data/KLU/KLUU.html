KLUU (103.5 FM) is a Contemporary Christian station broadcasting EMF's K-Love network licensed to Wahiawa, Hawaii and serves the Honolulu radio market. Currently operating on 100 Kw horizontal ERP and 86.49 vertical ERP, the station may be operating on an auxiliary power of 85 KW ERP.
