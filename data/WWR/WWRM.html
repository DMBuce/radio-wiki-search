WWRM (94.9 FM) – branded Magic 94.9 – is a commercial hot adult contemporary radio station licensed to Tampa, Florida. Owned by Cox Media Group, the station serves the greater Tampa Bay area. The WWRM studios are located in St. Petersburg, while the station transmitter resides in the Tampa-St. Petersburg suburb of Riverview. Besides a standard analog transmission, WWRM broadcasts over one HD Radio channels, and is available online.
