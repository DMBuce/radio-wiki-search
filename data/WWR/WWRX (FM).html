WWRX ("Jammin' 107.7") is a Rhythmic Contemporary music formatted radio station licensed to Bradford, Rhode Island, and serving the New London, Connecticut, area. The station is owned by Red Wolf Broadcasting and broadcasts at 107.7 MHz with an ERP of 1.05 kW.
