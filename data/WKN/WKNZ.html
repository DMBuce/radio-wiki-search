WKNZ (88.7 FM) is a non-commercial educational broadcast radio station licensed to Harrington, Delaware, United States. WKNZ is owned and operated by The Bridge of Hope, Inc. WKNZ programming can also be heard on WNKZ-FM to the south of Salisbury, Maryland and on 100.1 (W261AE) in Dover, Delaware and on 94.3 (W232DJ) in Ocean City, Maryland.
