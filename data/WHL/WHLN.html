WHLN (1410 AM) is a radio station broadcasting an adult contemporary format. Licensed to Harlan, Kentucky, United States, the station is owned by Eastern Broadcasting Company, Inc., as of August 30, 2019. It carries programming from ABC Radio and AP Radio, as well as from the Kentucky Sports Network.
