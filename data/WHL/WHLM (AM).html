WHLM (930 AM, "NEWSRADIO WHLM") is a radio station broadcasting an adult contemporary format. Licensed to Bloomsburg, Pennsylvania, United States, the station is currently owned by Columbia Broadcasting Company (not a part of CBS Radio) but still features national news from CBS Radio Network. The station is news intensive with local and national news at the top and bottom of every hour during the day.

The Columbia Broadcasting Company also owns WBWX, and a second company called Columbia FM Inc. owns Classic Rock 103.5. The studios for all 3 radio stations are located in Bloomsburg, Pennsylvania.
