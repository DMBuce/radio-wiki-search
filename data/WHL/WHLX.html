WHLX is an American radio station, licensed to Marine City, Michigan at 1590 kHz, with a power output of 1,000 watts day, 102 watts night. Its programming is also simulcasted on FM Translator W224DT, licensed to Port Huron, Michigan at 92.7 mHz, with an effective radiated power of 125 watts.

The station currently broadcasts an Adult album alternative/Americana format branded as 92.7 WHLX The Hills.
