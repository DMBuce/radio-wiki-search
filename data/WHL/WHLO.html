WHLO (640 kHz) is a commercial AM radio station licensed to Akron, Ohio, serving both the Akron and Canton metro areas. Owned by iHeartMedia, the station broadcasts a talk radio format. The studios and offices are in North Canton.

WHLO is powered at 5,000 watts by day. But because 640 AM is a clear channel frequency, WHLO must reduce power at night to 500 watts. A directional antenna is used at all times. The transmitter is on Minor Road at South Cleveland Massillon Road in Copley. In addition to a standard analog transmission, WHLO streams online via iHeartRadio. Although WHLO itself does not broadcast in HD Radio, the station does simulcast over the HD2 digital subchannels of sister stations 98.1 WKDD and 106.9 WRQK-FM.
