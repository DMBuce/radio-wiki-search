WJTL (90.3 FM) is a non commercial radio station broadcasting a Contemporary Christian format. Licensed in Lancaster, Pennsylvania, United States, the station serves the Lancaster, Harrisburg, York, and Lebanon, PA. The station is currently owned by Creative Ministries, Inc. Their current Program Director and Station Managers are John Shirk and Fred McNaughton. The station started in August 28, 1984, and switched to a 24-hour stereo format in 1985. WJTL was Lancaster, PA's first Christian Contemporary. WJTL's transmitter is located on Prospect Rd. in Ironville, PA.
