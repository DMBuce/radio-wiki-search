WJTI (1460 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a Regional Mexican format playing a mix of salsa, mariachi and merengue. Licensed to West Allis, Wisconsin, United States, the station serves the Milwaukee area. The station is owned by El Sol Broadcasting, LLC. The station's signal is translated by W250BN in West Allis, which broadcasts on 97.9 FM and forms the station's current branding.
