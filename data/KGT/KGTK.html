KGTK (920 AM, "MegaTalk 920") is a radio station licensed to serve Olympia, Washington, United States. The station is owned by KITZ Radio, Inc., consisting of the gun rights group Second Amendment Foundation and its affiliate Citizens Committee for the Right to Keep and Bear Arms.

KGTK broadcasts a business/talk radio format.

The station was assigned the KGTK call sign by the Federal Communications Commission on March 30, 2004.
