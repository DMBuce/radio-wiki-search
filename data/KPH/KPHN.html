KPHN (1360 AM) is a Religious formatted broadcast radio station licensed to El Dorado, Kansas, serving the Wichita Metropolitan Statistical Area. The station is owned by the Catholic Radio Network, Inc. The KPHN broadcast license is held by Kansas City Catholic Network, Inc.
