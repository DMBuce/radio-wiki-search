KPHX (1480 kHz) is a commercial AM radio station in Phoenix, Arizona. It is owned by Vic Micheal's Cedar Cove Broadcasting. KPHX carries a Spanish language Christian talk and teaching radio format. It is also heard on FM translator 92.9 K225CT in Paradise Valley, Arizona.

Using a directional antenna, KPHX broadcasts at 5,000 watts by day and 500 watts directional at night. The station has a construction permit with the Federal Communications Commission (FCC) to reduce its power, broadcasting with 950 watts daytime and 320 watts nighttime. A two-tower array would be used. This information was found on the FCC link on May 31, 2019.
