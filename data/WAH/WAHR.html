WAHR (99.1 FM, "Star 99.1") is an American mainstream adult contemporary music-formatted radio station licensed to serve Huntsville, Alabama. The station is owned by the Black Crow Media Group and the broadcast license is held by BCA Radio, LLC. The signal covers most of northern Alabama and southern central Tennessee. WAHR is one of the top-rated radio stations in the Huntsville market. Its studios are located off University Drive (U.S. 72) in Huntsville, and its transmitter is located north of the city.
