WYGS (91.1 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a Southern Gospel format. Licensed to Columbus, Indiana, United States, it serves the Cincinnati area. The station is currently owned by Good Shepherd Radio, Inc..

WYGS is also heard on WKRY 88.1 in Versailles, Indiana, WAUZ 89.1 in Greensburg, Indiana, WHMO 91.1 in Madison, Indiana, W233AO 94.5 in Salem, Indiana, and W248AF 97.5 in Batesville, Indiana.
