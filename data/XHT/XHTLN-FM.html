XHTLN-FM (branded as Imagen Radio Nuevo Laredo) is an FM radio station that serves the Laredo, Texas, United States and Nuevo Laredo, Tamaulipas, Mexico border area. XHTLN is owned by Grupo Imagen and airs Imagen Radio programs.
