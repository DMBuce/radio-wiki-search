CIYM-FM is a Canadian radio station that broadcasts an oldies format at 100.9 FM in Brighton, Ontario.

Owned by My Broadcasting Corporation, the station was authorized on May 15, 2009 and began broadcasting later in the same year originally with an adult contemporary music format branded as 100.9 myFM.

On Friday May 20, 2016, The station switched to oldies branded as Oldies 100.9.
