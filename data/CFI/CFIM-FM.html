CFIM-FM is a french language community radio station that operates at 92.7 FM in Cap-aux-Meules, Quebec, Canada.

The station signed on in 1981 and is currently owned by Diffusion Communications des Iles Inc.

The station is a member of the Association des radiodiffuseurs communautaires du Québec.
