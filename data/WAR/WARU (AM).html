WARU (1600 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a Soft Adult Contemporary ("Lite Rock") format in simulcast with 101.9 WARU-FM. The station is licensed to Peru, Indiana, United States. The station is currently owned by Dream Weaver Marketing, LLC .
