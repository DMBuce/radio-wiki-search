WARU-FM (101.9 FM) is a radio station licensed to Roann, Indiana, United States, broadcasting a Soft Adult Contemporary ("Lite Rock") format. The station is currently owned by Dream Weaver Marketing, LLC and features programming from the MiBash Sports Radio Network and Westwood One. The station simulcasts its programming with WARU (AM) (1600 kHz). Both frequencies are operated through the same facility in Peru, Indiana.
