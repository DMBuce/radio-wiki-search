WARB (700 AM) is a radio station licensed to serve Dothan, Alabama, United States. The station, which began licensed operation in 1996, is currently owned by Alarado Media, LLC. The station is licensed to broadcast only during daylight hours.

700 AM is United States clear-channel frequency, on which WLW in Cincinnati, Ohio is the dominant Class A station. WARB must leave the air during the period from sunset to sunrise in order to prevent interference to the nighttime skywave signal of WLW.
