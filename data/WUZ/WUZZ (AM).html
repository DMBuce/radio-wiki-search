WUZZ (1280 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a sports radio format as a Fox Sports Radio affiliate. Licensed to New Castle, Pennsylvania, United States, it serves the Pittsburgh area. The station is currently owned by Forever Broadcasting, LLC.
