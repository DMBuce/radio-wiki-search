KVLY (107.9 FM) is a radio station broadcasting an adult contemporary format. Licensed to Edinburg, Texas, United States, the station serves the Rio Grande Valley area. The station is currently owned by Entravision. It shares a studio with its sister stations, while its transmitter is located near Scissors, Texas.
