KPWR (105.9 FM, Power 106) is a commercial radio station in Los Angeles, California, broadcasting to the Greater Los Angeles area on an analog signal and in HD Radio. KPWR airs an Rhythmic Contemporary format. It is owned and operated by the Meruelo Group, which acquired KPWR from Emmis Communications for $82.75 million in May 2017, officially taking ownership on August 1, 2017, pairing it with KDAY, KDEY-FM, and KWHY-TV.

KPWR's studios are based in the Los Angeles suburb of Burbank, and the transmitter is at Mount Wilson, shared with KCAL-TV and KRTH.
