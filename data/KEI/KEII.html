KEII is a commercial radio station in Blackfoot, Idaho, broadcasting to the Idaho Falls-Pocatello, Idaho area on 690 AM; it airs an variety hits format branded as Cannonball 101.

KEII is a division of Riverbend Communications LLC., which is owned by Frank L. VanderSloot.

The station's previous call letters, first used in 1951, were KBLI, which was changed on October 21, 2014.

On September 10, 2018, KEII and simulcaster KEIR 1260 Idaho Falls (under new KNBL calls) changed its format from religious to variety hits, branded as "Cannonball 101".
