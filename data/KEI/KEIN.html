KEIN (1310 AM) is an adult standards/middle-of-the-road formatted radio station licensed to Great Falls, Montana, United States and serving the Great Falls area. The station is currently owned by Wayne Ferree & Creed Evans, through licensee Tiger Butte Broadcasting, Inc., and features programming from Westwood One. It was first licensed as KFBB in July 1922, and is Montana's oldest radio station.
