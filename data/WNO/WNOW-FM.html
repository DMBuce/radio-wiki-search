WNOW-FM is a commercial Top 40 (CHR) licensed to Speedway, Indiana, serving the Indianapolis Metro area. WNOW is owned and operated by Urban One alongside sister stations WTLC, WTLC-AM, WHHH and TV station WDNI-CD. All four stations and TV outlet share studios on Meridian Street in downtown Indianapolis with its transmitter located on the city's east side.

Specialty programming includes the Friday Vibe and the Saturday Vibe with various house DJs including DJ Orion, DJ Cool Hand Lex, and DJ Indiana Jones.

WNOW-FM is licensed to broadcast in the HD Radio (hybrid) format.
