WNOE-FM (101.1 FM) is a country music station based in New Orleans, Louisiana. The iHeartMedia outlet broadcasts at 101.1 MHz with an ERP of 100 kW. Its current slogan is "New Orleans Country Station." Its transmitter is located in New Orleans' East Area, and its studios are located downtown.
