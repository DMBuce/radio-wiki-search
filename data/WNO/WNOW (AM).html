WNOW (1030 AM) "Activa" is a radio station broadcasting a Regional Mexican format licensed to Mint Hill, North Carolina, United States. The AM frequency is currently owned by Mahan Janbakhsh's TBLC Holdings, LLC, through licensee TBLC Charlotte Stations, LLC.

The AM frequency is a daytime-only operation, as it operates on the same frequency as clear-channel WBZ in Boston.
