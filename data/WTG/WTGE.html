WTGE (100.7 MHz, "100.7 The Tiger") is a commercial FM radio station in Baton Rouge, Louisiana. The station airs a country music radio format and is owned by Guaranty Broadcasting Company of Baton Rouge, LLC. Along with four sister stations, its studios and offices are at the Guaranty Group building on Government Street east of downtown Baton Rouge.

WTGE's transmitter is located near the Mississippi River in Baton Rouge, south of Louisiana State University. The station broadcasts in the HD radio digital format.

In 2003, WTGE began airing the syndicated Big D and Bubba Show in morning drive time.
