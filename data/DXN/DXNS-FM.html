DXNS (102.3 FM), on-air as 102.3 Bee FM, is an FM radio station owned and operated by Northern Mindanao Broadcasting System, and is one of the affiliates of the Magic Nationwide network. The station's studio and transmitter are located at 4th Flr., L.T. & Sons Bldg., Montilla Blvd., Butuan.
