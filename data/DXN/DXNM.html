99.3 Radyo Kahiusa is an FM radio station owned by the National Nutrition Council in partnership with the Municipal Government of Tupi. Its studios and transmitter are located at the Multi-Agency Bldg., Municipal Hall Compound, National Highway, Tupi, South Cotabato. "Kahiusa" stands for unity in B'laan.
