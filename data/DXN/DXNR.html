103.3 Radyo Katribu is an FM radio station owned by the National Nutrition Council under the Nutriskwela Community Radio Network in partnership with Municipal Government of Tboli. Its studios and transmitter are located at the Municipal Information Office, LGU Complex, Poblacion, T'Boli, South Cotabato. "Katribu" stands for Community in T'Boli.
