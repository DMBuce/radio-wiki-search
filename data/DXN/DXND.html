DXND (90.1 FM) is a radio station owned and operated by GMA Network subsidiary RGMA (Radio GMA), serving as a relay station of DZBB in Manila. The station's transmitter is located at the 3rd floor Kimberly Bldg., A. Bonifacio cor. I. Emilia Ave., Iligan.
