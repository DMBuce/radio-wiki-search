DXNY (107.9 FM), broadcasting as 107.9 Win Radio, is a radio station owned by Progressive Broadcasting Corporation and operated by ZimZam Management, Inc. The station's studio & transmitter are located at Pryce Plaza Hotel, Carmen Hills, Cagayan de Oro.
