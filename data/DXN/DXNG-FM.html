97.7 Radyo Kabakhawan, is an FM radio station owned and operated by National Nutrition Council. The station's studio and transmitter is located at G/F, Administration Bldg., Surigao State College-Del Carmen Campus, Brgy. San Jose, Del Carmen, Surigao del Norte. "Bakhaw" stands for mangrove in Surigaonon.
