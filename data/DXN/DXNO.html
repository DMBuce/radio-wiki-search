DXNO (97.5 FM), broadcasting as 97.5 Radyo Komunidad, is an FM radio station owned by the National Nutrition Council under the Nutriskwela Community Radio Network, in partnership with the City Government of Isabela and the United Evangelical Church of Isabela. Its studios and transmitter are located at the 3rd Floor, Tan Bldg., Valderosa St., Isabela, Basilan.
