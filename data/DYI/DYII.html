DYII (92.7 FM), broadcasting as Bee 92.7, is a radio station owned and operated by Groove Deejayz Entertainment Solutions, and is one of the affiliates of the Magic Nationwide network in the Philippines. The studio and transmitter are located along Maria Clara St., Tagbilaran.

The station was launched in 2017 as Magic 92.7, before it was later relaunched under its current brand a year later.
