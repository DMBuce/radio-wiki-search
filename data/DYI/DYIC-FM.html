DYIC-FM, branded as 95.1 iFM Iloilo, is an FM radio station owned and operated by Radio Mindanao Network in the Philippines. The station's studios is located at St. Anne Bldg., Luna Street, La Paz, Iloilo City and its transmitter is located at Coastal Road, Brgy. Hinactacan, La Paz, Iloilo City. iFM Iloilo operates 24 hours a day with Hot AC, adult contemporary, top 40 and OPM format, catering to the masses.
