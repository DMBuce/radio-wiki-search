KNTR (980 AM, "Sports 980") is a radio station licensed to serve Lake Havasu City, Arizona, United States. The station is owned by Steven M. Greeley. It airs a sports format.

The station was assigned the KNTR call letters by the Federal Communications Commission on November 30, 1999.
