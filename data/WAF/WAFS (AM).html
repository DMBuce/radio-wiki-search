WAFS (AM 1190) is radio station licensed to Atlanta, Georgia that broadcasts Catholic talk programming, as an affiliate of Relevant Radio. The station transmits on a frequency of 1190 kHz, and is classified as a Class D AM broadcast station according to the Federal Communications Commission, with 25,000 watts of power during the daytime and 2,300 watts during critical hours using a non-directional antenna. WAFS also operates pre-sunrise (PSRA) with 350 watts and post-sunset (PSSA) with power levels from 54 to 150 watts. (WNIV, AM 970 kHz, broadcasts from the same tower as WAFS using an antenna diplexing system.)
