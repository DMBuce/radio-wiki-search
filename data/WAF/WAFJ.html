WAFJ (88.3 FM) is a Christian contemporary radio station serving the Augusta, Georgia-Aiken, South Carolina, area owned by Radio Training Network (RTN). WAFJ was mostly a simulcast of WLFJ Greenville, South Carolina at the beginning but has since become an independent station of the Radio Training Network. The station is listener supported and depends on contributions for operating funding; it does not sell paid advertising.
