WDNS (93.3 FM) is a classic rock-formatted radio station that is licensed to, located in, and serving Bowling Green, Kentucky. The station is owned by the Daily News Broadcasting Company, the broadcasting arm of the Bowling Green Daily News, which also owns News-Talk formatted station WKCT.
