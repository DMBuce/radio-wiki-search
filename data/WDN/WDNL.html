WDNL 102.1 FM is a radio station broadcasting an Adult Contemporary format. Licensed to Danville, Illinois, the station serves East-Central Illinois and West-Central Indiana, and is owned by Neuhoff Corp., through licensee Neuhoff Media Danville, LLC.
