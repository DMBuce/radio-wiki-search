WDND (1620 kHz) was an AM radio station serving South Bend, Indiana. The station was owned by Artistic Media Partners, Inc. Its license was cancelled on April 15, 2019.

WDND's transmitter was in South Bend, located off Locust Road, near Lincoln Highway (U.S. Route 20 - U.S. Route 31). The studios and offices were on South Michigan Street, also in South Bend.
