WNBL (107.3 MHz) is a commercial FM radio station licensed to South Bristol, New York, and serving the Rochester metropolitan area. It is owned by iHeartMedia and airs a country music radio format, known as 107.3 The Bull. WNBL is a Class A station, operating at 650 watts, compared to its country rival, 92.5 WBEE-FM, which is powered at 50,000 watts. WNBL's transmitter is in Canandaigua, New York, near the Bristol Mountain Ski Resort. The studios and offices are on Chestnut Street in Rochester, at Five Star Bank Plaza.

Monday through Saturday mornings, WNBL runs The Bobby Bones Show, based in Nashville, from Premiere Networks, a subsidiary of iHeartMedia. The station also carries disc jockeys from other iHeart country stations around the U.S., using voice-tracking to sound local to WNBL.
