WNBM (103.9 FM, branded as "Radio 103.9") is a radio station licensed to Bronxville, New York. The station features an urban adult contemporary format and is owned by Cumulus Media. WNBM's transmitter is located on the campus of Montefiore Medical Center in the Bronx, with its antenna atop the Montefiore II dormitory tower.
