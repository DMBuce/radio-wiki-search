WMLP (1380 AM) is a commercial AM radio station licensed to serve Milton, Pennsylvania. The station is owned by Michael Radio Company, LLC, who acquired it from Sunbury Broadcasting Corporation on February 20, 2020, and broadcasts a conservative talk radio format.
