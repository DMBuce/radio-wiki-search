WMLZ-LP (107.9) is a low-power High School radio station broadcasting an adult hits format. (1955 to 2000) The station is licensed to Bedford Public Schools in Temperance, Michigan. It first began broadcasting in 2002 under the name of Blend 107.9, where it played a mix of 1980's music and current hits. The format was then changed in 2006 to incorporate a wide time range of Classic Rock, and the station changed its name to "Z108, Bedford's Home of Classic Rock and Sports".
