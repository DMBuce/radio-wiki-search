WMLY-LP (93.1 FM) is a low power radio station broadcasting a religious radio format as an affiliate of LifeTalk Radio. Licensed September 23, 2003, to operate in Marshall, in the U.S. state of Michigan, it first began broadcasting in 2004. It is licensed to Marshall Christian Radio in Marshall. Its tower height is 99 feet (30 m); its antenna type is a Nicom BKG77 non-directional, no beam tilt.
