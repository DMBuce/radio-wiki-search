KGRZ (1450 AM, "SportsTalk 1450") is a radio station licensed to serve Missoula, Montana, United States. The station is owned by Cherry Creek Radio and licensed to CCR-Missoula IV, LLC. It airs a Sports radio format.

The station was assigned the KGRZ call letters by the Federal Communications Commission.
