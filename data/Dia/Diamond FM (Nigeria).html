DIAMOND 101.1 FM is the community radio station of the University of Ibadan in Nigeria. The Station was officially opened on 30 October 2008 by the Executive governor of Niger State, Dr. Mu'azu Babangida Alliu, as part of activities marking the 60th Anniversary of the University.

The station is received by listeners in the city of Ibadan and nearby towns and cities. The station adopted the tag "Promoting Values and Excellence" and it is located in the Media Centre of the University of Ibadan.
