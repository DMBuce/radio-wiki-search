KCHE (1440 AM) is a commercial radio station licensed to serve the community of Cherokee, Iowa. The station primarily broadcasts a country format. KCHE is owned by Simon Fuller, through licensee Better Broadcasting Incorporated. It was first licensed on March 10, 1953.

Former owners include Sioux Valley Broadcasting Company, Inc, Cherokee Broadcasting Company, and J & J Broadcasting Corporation.
