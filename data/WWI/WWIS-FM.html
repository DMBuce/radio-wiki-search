WWIS-FM (99.7 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a Classic Country format. It is licensed to Black River Falls, Wisconsin, United States. The station is currently owned by WWIS Radio, Inc., and features programming from CBS Radio, Jones Radio Network and Westwood One.
