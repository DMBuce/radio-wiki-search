WWIS (1260 AM) is a radio station broadcasting an Adult Contemporary/Full-Service music format. Licensed to Black River Falls, Wisconsin, United States. The station is currently owned by WWIS Radio, Inc. and features programming from CBS Radio.

In 2007, WWIS music director Brian Brawner was recognized as the Adult Contemporary Music Director of the Year by the New Music Awards.
