KJLV is a non-commercial radio station licensed to Los Altos, California, broadcasting on 97.7 FM MHz, owned by the Educational Media Foundation. Its transmitter is located near Cupertino, California. It is the San Jose affiliate for the K-Love religious network. No local programming originates from the station, and it carries network programming full-time. KJLV's transmitter is located near Stevens Creek Reservoir.

KJLV broadcasts in HD Radio.
