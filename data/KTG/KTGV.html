KTGV (106.3 FM, "106.3 The Groove") is a radio station licensed to Oracle, Arizona, and serving the Tucson, Arizona area. Owned by Bustos Media, it broadcasts a rhythmic oldies format. Its studios are co-located with television station KGUN-TV in Tucson, and its transmitter site is located in the city's northwest side.
