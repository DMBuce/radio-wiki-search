KTGR (1580 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a sports-talk format, based on the ESPN Radio network. Licensed to Columbia, Missouri, United States, the station serves the Columbia, Missouri, area. The station is currently owned by Zimmer Radio Group of Mid-Missouri.
