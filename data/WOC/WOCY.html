WOCY (Cross Country 106.5, 106.5 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a country format. It is licensed to Carrabelle, Florida, United States, and broadcasts from Port Saint Joe, Florida. The station is owned by Rev. Dr. R.B. Holmes, Jr., through licensee Live Communications, Inc. The station used to be owned by 3G Broadcasting Inc.

Prior to the current country format, it has broadcast as sports, Top 40 Hitz 106, and Oyster Country.
