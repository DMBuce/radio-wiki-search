WOCQ (103.9 FM) is an American radio station in the Delmarva region of Maryland. The station is licensed to Berlin, Maryland, although its studios and main offices are located in the larger nearby city of Salisbury. The station broadcasts a Mainstream Top 40 format branded as OC104.

Its transmitter tower stands 348 feet (106.1 meters) and is located in Whaleyville.
