WOCC (1550 AM) is a radio station broadcasting an oldies music format. Licensed to Corydon, Indiana, United States, the station serves the Louisville, Kentucky, area. The station is currently owned by Sunny Stephens. Its former owner was Richard Lee Brabandt.
