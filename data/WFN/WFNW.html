WFNW (1380 AM, "Radio Fe De Excelencia") is a radio station licensed to serve Naugatuck, Connecticut. The station is owned by Candido Dias Carrelo. It airs a Portuguese contemporary format.

The station has been assigned the WFNW call letters by the Federal Communications Commission since February 13, 1989.
