WFNM (89.1 FM) is a non-commercial college FM radio station licensed to serve Lancaster, Pennsylvania. The station is owned by Franklin & Marshall College and broadcasts a variety format.

The station Advisor is Dan T. Lewis, Director of Facilities and Operations at Franklin & Marshall College. Since January 1998, Dan has hosted the "Bessie Smith Green Tea Show" Mondays 5-7 AM. The station's shows are hosted by students, faculty & professional staff DJs. The station broadcasts an eclectic mix, with DJs having talk shows, playing music, or a mix of both.

In 2018, WFNM was ranked #19 on The Princeton Review's Top 20 Best College Radio Stations.
