WFNX (95.3 MHz; "Oldies 95.3") is a commercial FM radio station licensed to Grand Marais, Minnesota, United States, and serves the North Shore region. The station airs and oldies format, and is part of a simulcast with AM 1450 WHRY in Hurley, Wisconsin. WFNX broadcasts with an ERP of 63,000 watts.
