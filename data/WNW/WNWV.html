WNWV (107.3 FM) – branded as jenY 107.3 – is a commercial alternative rock radio station licensed to Elyria, Ohio. Owned by Rubber City Radio Group, Inc., the station serves Greater Cleveland and much of surrounding Northeast Ohio. The WNWV studios are located in the Cleveland suburb of Independence, while the station transmitter resides off of South Island Road in Grafton. In addition to a standard analog transmission, WNWV broadcasts over a single HD Radio channel, and is available online.
