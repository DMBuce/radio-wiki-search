WNWZ (1410 AM, "Magic 104.9") is a radio station broadcasting an Urban Contemporary format, licensed to Grand Rapids, Michigan.

The station is simulcast on FM translator W285FO (104.9), licensed to Grand Rapids, and the HD2 subchannel of Hot adult contemporary sister station WLHT-FM 95.7.
