WNWC (1190 AM "Faith 1190 WNWC" or "Faith Radio") is a radio station in Sun Prairie, Wisconsin, that is owned and operated by University of Northwestern – St. Paul. It is a non-profit, listener-supported radio station relying on donations from the local community throughout the year. It broadcasts on 1190 AM and an FM translator at 104.7 FM, covering Madison, Wisconsin and surrounding areas from a transmitter located south of Sun Prairie.
