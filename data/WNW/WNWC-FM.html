WNWC-FM (102.5 FM, "Life 102.5") is an American radio station broadcasting a contemporary Christian music format. Licensed to Madison, Wisconsin, United States, "Life 102.5" has an AM sister station, "Faith 1190" (WNWC), that broadcasts programs on Christian faith. Both stations are currently owned by University of Northwestern – St. Paul.

WNWC transmits from a tower in Elver Park on Madison's Southwest Side.
