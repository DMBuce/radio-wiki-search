CKHZ-FM (103.5 FM, Hot Country 103.5) is a radio station in Halifax, Nova Scotia. Owned by Evanov Radio Group, it broadcasts a country music format. CKHZ's studios are located on Main Street in Dartmouth, while its transmitter is located on Washmill Lake Drive in Clayton Park.

Based on the current format, CKHZ-FM competes with CHFX-FM.
