Beijing Xinwen Guangbo (Chinese: 北京新闻广播) is also known in English as "Beijing News Station" or "Beijing News Radio". The radio station broadcasts on AM 828 kHz and FM 100.6 MHz in Beijing, China. The station is part of the Beijing Ren Min Guangbo Dian Tai group.
