Beijing Cheng Shi Guan Li Guangbo (Chinese: 北京城市管理广播) is the "Beijing Public Service Radio" station. It generally broadcasts local news about events within Beijing. The station broadcasts on FM 107.3 in Beijing, China and is one of the stations in the Beijing Ren Min Guangbo Dian Tai group.
