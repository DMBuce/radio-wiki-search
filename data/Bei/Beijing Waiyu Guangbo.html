The Beijing Waiyu Guangbo (Chinese: 北京外语广播), also known as the Beijing Foreign Broadcast, is a radio station which broadcasts on FM92.3 AM774. The radio station is part of the Beijing Ren Min Guangbo Dian Tai group.

There are many languages represented on this radio station including Japanese and English with the commercials, announcements and station identification done in Mandarin Chinese.
