WORO (92.5 FM) is a radio station that broadcasts in a Spanish-language easy listening radio format. It is owned by the Catholic Church. Licensed to Corozal, Puerto Rico, it serves the central-eastern Puerto Rico area. The station is owned by Grupo RTC, under its licensee Catholic, Apostolic, & Roman Church in Puerto Rico.
