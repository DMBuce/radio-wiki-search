DYBE (106.3 FM), on-air as Magic 106.3, is a radio station owned and operated by Quest Broadcasting Inc. Its studio is located at 4th Floor, VA Metroplaza, Araneta St., Bacolod. It broadcasts weekdays 6am - 12mn & weekends 6am - 1am.

Formerly branded as Killerbee 106.3 from 1993 - 2013, this station, along with the other Killerbee stations, underwent nationwide reformatting, as they were relaunched under the Magic moniker (adopted from its parent station) by April 29, 2013.
