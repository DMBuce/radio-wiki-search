DYBU (97.9 FM), on-air as 97.9 Love Radio, is a radio station owned and operated by Manila Broadcasting Company. Its studio is located at the Room 303, 3/F Doña Luisa Bldg., Fuente Osmeña, Cebu City, and the transmitter is located at Legacy Village, Brgy. Kalunasan, Cebu City. This station operates 24/7.

Established in 1950s, DYBU is the pioneer music station in Cebu City entertaining in mix of Cebuano and Filipino.
