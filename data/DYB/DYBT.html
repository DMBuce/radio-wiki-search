DYBT (105.9 FM) - broadcasting as Monster Radio BT 105.9 - is a radio station owned and operated by Audiovisual Communicators, Inc. The studios and transmitter station are located at West Entrance, G/F East Aurora Tower, #3 President Quezon (F. Cabahug) St., Kasambagan, Mabolo, Cebu City. It operates daily from 5:00 AM to 1:00 AM.
