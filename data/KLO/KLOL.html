KLOL (101.1 MHz "Mega 101") is a commercial FM radio station in Houston, Texas. It is owned by Entercom and airs a Spanish-language Latin pop radio format. KLOL serves as the Spanish-language flagship station for the Houston Texans football team.

The studios and offices are in Greenway Plaza in Houston. The transmitter is located off McHard Road in Missouri City, Texas.
