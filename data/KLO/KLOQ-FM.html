KLOQ-FM (98.7 FM, "Radio Lobo 98.7") is a radio station broadcasting a Regional Mexican format. Licensed to Winton, California, United States, it serves the Merced, California, area. The station is currently operated by Stephens Media via a LMA deal from Mapleton Communications, with a sale to Stephens pending regulatory approval. Its studios are in Merced and its transmitter is located west of Merced.
