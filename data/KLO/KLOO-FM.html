KLOO-FM (106.3 FM) is a commercial radio station licensed to serve Corvallis, Oregon, United States. The station is owned by Bicoastal Media and the broadcast license is held by Bicoastal Media Licenses V, LLC.

KLOO-FM broadcasts a classic rock music format to the Eugene-Springfield, Oregon, area. The station is an affiliate of the syndicated Pink Floyd program "Floydian Slip."

The station was reassigned the KLOO-FM call sign by the Federal Communications Commission on January 8, 1997.
