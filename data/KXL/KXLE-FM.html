KXLE-FM is a radio station located in Ellensburg, Washington, United States, operating on a frequency of 95.3 MHz with an effective radiated power of 51,000 watts. As of 2019, the programming format of the station is Country music. This format has mostly been the same for the past 45 years. The transmitter tower for the station is located on Lookout Mountain, east of Cle Elum [1]. The station can be heard as far as Snoqualmie Pass and the central Columbia Basin.
