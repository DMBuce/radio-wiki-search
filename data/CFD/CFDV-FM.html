CFDV-FM, branded as 106.7 The Drive, is a Canadian radio station, broadcasting an active rock format at 106.7 FM in Red Deer, Alberta. Owned by the Jim Pattison Group, the station was licensed by the Canadian Radio-television and Telecommunications Commission (CRTC) in 2004 and was launched on November 6, 2005. Currently CFDV-FM airs an active rock format with a mixture of alternative and classic rock.
