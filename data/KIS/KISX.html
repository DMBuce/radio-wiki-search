KISX (107.3 FM; 107.3 Kiss FM) is a radio station licensed to Whitehouse, Texas, and broadcasts in Tyler with an Urban Adult Contemporary format. It is owned by Townsquare Media. Its studios are located in south Tyler and its transmitter is located southeast of Tyler in Smith County.
