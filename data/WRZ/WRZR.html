WRZR (94.5 FM, "94.5 The Razor") is a radio station broadcasting a classic rock format. Licensed to Loogootee, Indiana, United States, the station is currently owned by Hembree Communications, Inc. and features programming from Westwood One.

WRZR went on the air in Southwestern Indiana on Pearl Harbor Day, December 7, 1984 as WKMD FM Radio 94.3, owned by Community Broadcasting Services of Loogootee, Donna Harris, President. The station changed frequencies on August 13, 1994 to 94.1 FM. Hembree Communications was formed and purchased WKMD in August 1997.

Larry Hembree is the principal owner, with Alan Williams as a minority stockholder. The call letters were changed to WRZR effective January 1, 1998. The station changed frequencies to 94.5 FM August 19, 1998. The station is operated by Dubois County Broadcasting Inc., Paul Knies, President, through an LMA with Hembree Communications.

WRZR is noted for its outstanding local sports coverage, including North Daviess Cougar Football; the Trent Fine Coaches Show with North Daviess High School Football Coach Trent Fine, high school girls' volleyball tournament coverage of Barr-Reeve, Loogootee, North Daviess, Shoals, Vincennes Rivet and Washington Catholic; high school boys' basketball coverage including every Loogootee High School boys' basketball game plus coverage of Barr-Reeve, North Daviess, Shoals, Vincennes Rivet and Washington Catholic; Inside Local Basketball, a weekly coaches' show held during basketball season featuring Loogootee Lions Coach Ryan Haywood, Barr-Reeve Vikings Coach Josh Thompson and North Daviess Coach Brent Dalrymple; Boys' and Girls' IHSAA Tournament Coverage of high school baseball including Barr-Reeve, Loogootee, North Daviess, Shoals, Vincennes Rivet and Washington Catholic; and IHSAA Tournament Coverage of high school girls' softball including Barr-Reeve, Loogootee, North Daviess, Shoals, Vincennes Rivet and Washington Catholic.

WRZR's sports on-air talent includes Greg Bateman (PBP Volleyball, Basketball, Softball, Baseball, Special Programs), Bruce Lukens (PBP Football), Alan Williams (Com Volleyball, Basketball, Special Programs), John Stoll (PBP/Com Volleyball, Basketball, Softball, Baseball, Special Programs), Josh Swartzentruber (Com Volleyball, Basketball, Softball, Baseball, Special Programs), Larry Hembree (Special Programs), Mike Wagoner (Com Volleyball, Basketball, Softball, Baseball, Special Programs), Jim Lilly (Com Football, Special Programs), Dave Smith (PBP/Com Basketball, Softball, Baseball), and Randy Wagler (Com Basketball, Softball, Baseball, Special Programs).

WRZR also carries Indiana Sports Talk from Network Indiana, the state's largest sports talk show held Friday and Saturday nights from August through May. WRZR is an affiliate of the Indiana Hoosiers Network and Indianapolis Colts Radio Network, airing Indiana football and basketball games and respective coaches' shows and Indianapolis Colts gameday coverage. WRZR is a Westwood One Sports affiliate and carries the (NFL) AFC & NFC Championships, (NFL) Super Bowl and the (NCAA) Final Four.
