KWTU is a classical music radio station in Tulsa, Oklahoma. Most of the station's classical programming comes from American Public Media's Classical 24, a 24-hour national service. KWTU is owned and operated by The University of Tulsa which also owns NPR station KWGS. KWTU is a Class C2 FM radio station.

KWTU is licensed by the U.S. Federal Communications Commission to broadcast in the HD (hybrid) format.
