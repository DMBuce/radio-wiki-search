WTCX (96.1 FM, "95-1 & 96-1 The Rock") is a radio station broadcasting a mainstream rock music format. Licensed to Ripon, Wisconsin, United States, the station is currently owned by Radio Plus, Inc. and features programming from CBS Radio Network, Dial Global, and Premiere Radio Networks.

WTCX was originally on 95.9 MHz. before moving to 96.1, and was co-owned with AM 1600, WCWC (now WRPN) with studios in Ripon, Wisconsin.
