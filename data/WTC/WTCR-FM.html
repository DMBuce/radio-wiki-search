WTCR-FM (103.3 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a Country format. Licensed to Huntington, West Virginia, United States, it serves the Huntington WV area. The station is currently owned by iHeartMedia. From 1993 to 2017, the morning show was hosted by radio personalities Clint McElroy and Judy Eaton.
