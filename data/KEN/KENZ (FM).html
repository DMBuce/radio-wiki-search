KENZ (94.9 FM, "Power 94.9 / 101.9") is a top 40/CHR radio station broadcasting to the Salt Lake City and Provo-Orem metropolitan areas. The station is licensed to Provo. It is owned and operated by Cumulus Media. The station's studios are located in South Salt Lake (behind the I-15/I-80 interchange) and its transmitter site is located on Lake Mountain.
