WVMP (101.5 FM, "The Mountain") is an adult album alternative formatted broadcast radio station licensed to Vinton, Virginia, serving Metro Roanoke, Virginia. WVMP is owned by Todd P. Robinson's WVJT, LLC. It's an affiliate of the syndicated Pink Floyd program "Floydian Slip."
