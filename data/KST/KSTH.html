KSTH (92.3 FM) is a radio station licensed to Holyoke, Colorado, United States. The station is owned by Armada Media - Mccook. The station has obtained a construction permit from the U.S. Federal Communications Commission (FCC) to move to 92.1 MHz and increase ERP to 100,000 watts.
