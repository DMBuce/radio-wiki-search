WPNN (790 AM) is a radio station serving the Pensacola, Florida market with a talk format. This station operates on AM frequency 790 kHz and is under ownership of Miracle Radio.

WPNN previously carried an all-news format, largely provided by HLN, though it also simulcasted news from WEAR-TV on weeknights.

In February 2016 the station acquired a move-in translator from Carrabelle, Florida as part of the AM Revitalization project approved by the FCC. It began transmitting on 103.7 MHz (W279CY) in early April 2016. (Taken from Alabama Broadcast Media Page)
