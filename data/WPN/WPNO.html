WPNO (1450 AM; "WIGY") is a radio station licensed to serve South Paris, Maine. The station is owned by Bennett Radio Group. WPNO is airs a hot adult contemporary format simulcast with WEZR (1240 AM and 105.5 FM) in Lewiston and WIGY (780 AM and 98.3 FM) in Rumford. WPNO also operates translator station W245CQ (96.9 FM) in South Paris. WPNO went on the air in 1955 as WKTQ, and was assigned its present call letters on September 26, 2019.
