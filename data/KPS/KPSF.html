KPSF (1200 AM, "Pure Radio") and 100.9 FM is a radio station licensed to Cathedral City, California. It airs a Christian radio format. The station is owned by CRC Broadcasting. KPSF signed on March 5, 2012. The station shares its transmitter site with sister station KXPS.

On July 11, 2019, KPSF changed their format from business talk to Christian radio, branded as "Pure Radio".
