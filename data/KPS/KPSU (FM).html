KPSU (91.7 FM, "The Xtreme") is a non-profit educational radio station licensed to serve Goodwell, Oklahoma. KPSU-FM is owned by Oklahoma Panhandle State University.

The station was assigned the call letters KPSU by the Federal Communications Commission.
