KPSZ (940 AM, "Hope 940") is a Christian radio station serving the Des Moines, Iowa, area. The station's studios are located on Locust Street in Des Moines along with Saga Communications' other Des Moines stations (KRNT, KSTZ, KIOA, KOEZ and KAZR), while its 8-tower transmitter array is located near Hartford.
