West Sound is an independent local radio station in Ayrshire. Based in Clyde 1's studios in Clydebank. with its AM transmitter in Symington, South Ayrshire and its DAB transmitter in Darvel, East Ayrshire, the station forms part of the Greatest Hits Radio network in Scotland and northern England.
