WMEQ (880 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a News Talk Information format. Licensed to Menomonie, Wisconsin, United States, the station serves the Eau Claire area. The station is currently owned by iHeartMedia, Inc. and features programming from Fox News Radio, Premiere Radio Networks and Westwood One.
