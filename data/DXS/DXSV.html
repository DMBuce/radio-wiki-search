96.7 Muews Radio is an FM station owned by Sagay Broadcasting Corporation. The station's studio is located at 2/F, La Castill Bldg., Magsaysay St., Brgy. Zone 1, Digos City, while its transmitter is located at Brgy. Soong, Digos City.
