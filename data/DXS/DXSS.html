DXSS (97.9 FM), broadcasting as Mom's Radio 97.9, is a music and talk radio station owned by Southern Broadcasting Network. Its studios and transmitter are located in SBN Compound, Along Shrine Hills, Matina, Davao City. It is considered to be the pioneer FM radio station in Davao City.
