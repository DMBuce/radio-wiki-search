97.7 Charm Radio (DXSZ 97.7 MHz) is an FM station owned and operated by Polytechnic Foundation of Cotabato and Asia. Its studios and transmitter are located at Brgy. Ramon Magsaysay, Sindangan, Zamboanga del Norte, Philippines.
