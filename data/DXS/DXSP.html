DXSP (96.1 FM), on-air as 96.1 One FM, is a radio station owned and operated by Radio Corporation of the Philippines. The station's studio and transmitter is located at 4/F Yuipco Bldg., Navarro St., Brgy. Taft, Surigao City. The station operates daily from 4:00 AM to 12:00 MN.
