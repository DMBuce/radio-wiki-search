KHOS-FM (92.1 FM, "Today's Traditional Country (TM)") is a radio station broadcasting a traditional country music format. Licensed to Sonora, Texas, United States, radio station KHOS-FM serves the greater San Angelo area. The station is currently owned by TENN-Vol, Corp. and has programming from All AG Network, Down On The Farm Network, Today's Traditional Country (TM) Radio Network, and Texas State Network.
