KGBI-FM (100.7 MHz) is a non-commercial FM radio station broadcasting a Contemporary Christian radio format. Licensed to Omaha, Nebraska, United States, the station serves the Omaha and Lincoln, Nebraska radio markets. The station is owned and operated by the University of Northwestern - St. Paul.

KGBI's studios are located on Burt Street, near North 120th Street and Dodge Road, in West Omaha, while its transmitter is located at the Omaha master antenna farm at North 72nd Street and Crown Point Avenue.
