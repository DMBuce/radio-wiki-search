DWXT (96.1 FM), broadcasting as 96.1 One FM, is a radio station owned and operated by Radio Corporation of the Philippines, serving as the flagship station of the One FM network. The station's studios and transmitter are located at RCP Broadcasting Center, Mcarthur Highway, Brgy. San Nicolas, Tarlac City. It operates daily from 5:00 AM to 10:00 PM.
