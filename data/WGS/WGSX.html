WGSX (104.3 FM) is a radio station licensed to Lynn Haven, Florida, United States. The station serves the Panama City area. The station is owned by Horizon Broadcasting Company, Inc.

Previously licensed to Bayamon, Puerto Rico from April, 1980 to August 1995.
