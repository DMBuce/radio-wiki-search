WGSW is a commercial FM Broadcast Radio Station licensed in the US to Americus, Georgia. WGSW is part of a group of South Georgia radio stations playing classic hits, and is owned by Victor M. Vickers.

In popular culture, the call letters were used for a fictional New York television station (Channel 8) in Ghostbusters (2016).
