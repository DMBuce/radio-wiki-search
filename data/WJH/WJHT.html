WJHT (92.1 FM, "Hot 92.1") is a radio station licensed to serve Johnstown, Pennsylvania. The station is owned by Forever Broadcasting, LLC. It airs a Top 40 (CHR) music format. This station was assigned the WJHT call letters by the Federal Communications Commission on July 13, 2006.

There is no relation with WWKL ("Hot 106.7") in Harrisburg, Pennsylvania. However, the station is related to WWOT ("Hot 100.1") in Altoona, Pennsylvania, as they are also owned by Forever Broadcasting.
