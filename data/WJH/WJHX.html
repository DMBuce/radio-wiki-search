WJHX (620 AM) was an American radio station licensed to serve the community of Lexington, Alabama. The station was last owned by Bar Broadcasting, Inc.

This station, WIXI, and WZGX comprised the "La Jefa" radio network that covers central Alabama, north Alabama, and central Tennessee. Before it fell silent in July 2013, WJHX broadcast a Regional Mexican music format and Spanish language programming.
