WRAD is an oldies formatted broadcast radio station licensed to Radford, Virginia, serving Pulaski and Montgomery counties in Virginia. WRAD used to be owned and operated by Cumulus Media, Inc. On September 6, 2018, Cumulus Media announced it will sell its Blacksburg cluster to Monticello Media. The sale was approved December 1, 2018.
