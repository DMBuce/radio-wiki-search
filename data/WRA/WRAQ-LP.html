WRAQ-LP (92.7 FM) is a radio station licensed to serve the community of Angelica, New York. The station is owned by Angelica Community Radio Inc. It airs a variety radio format.

The station was assigned the WRAQ-LP call letters by the Federal Communications Commission on February 26, 2014.
