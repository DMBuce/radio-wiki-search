WRAX is an adult standards-formatted broadcast radio station licensed to Bedford, Pennsylvania, serving Bedford and Bedford County, Pennsylvania. WRAX is owned and operated by Cessna Communications, Inc.

Known as WAYC from 1974 to 1993 and again from 2009 to 2020, the station adopted the call letters WRAX on September 15, 2020.
