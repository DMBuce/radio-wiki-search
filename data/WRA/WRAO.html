WRAO is a Christian radio station licensed to Wisconsin Rapids, Wisconsin, broadcasting on 91.7 MHz FM. WRAO is an affiliate of Three Angels Broadcasting Network and is owned by Wisconsin Rapids Seventh-Day Adventist Church.
