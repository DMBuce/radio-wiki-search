KNSG (107.5 FM) is a radio station broadcasting a sports format serving Marshall, Minnesota. The station is currently owned by Linder Radio Group.

On October 16, 2017, KARZ changed their call letters to KNSG.

On October 30, 2017, KNSG changed their format from mainstream rock to sports, branded as "The Fan". (info taken from northpine.com)
