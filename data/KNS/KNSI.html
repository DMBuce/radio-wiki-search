KNSI (1450 AM/99.3 FM) is a commercial radio station based in St. Cloud, Minnesota. It is owned by Leighton Broadcasting, which also owns KCLD-FM, KZPK and KCML.

KNSI is also carried on HD Radio, via KZPK-HD2.
