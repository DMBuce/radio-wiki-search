KNSU (91.5 FM) is a campus radio station licensed to Nicholls State University in Thibodaux, Louisiana. The station mainly programs alternative rock aimed at a college aged crowd, however the station also plays hip hop music on shows aired on the station. The station also broadcasts Nicholls Colonels athletic events.
