KNSS (1330 AM, "News Talk 98.7 and 1330") is a commercial AM radio station in Wichita, Kansas. It carries a talk radio format and is owned by Entercom Communications. The station simulcasts with co-owned KNSS-FM 98.7 MHz. The studios and offices are on East Douglas Avenue in Wichita.

KNSS is powered at 5,000 watts. At night, to protect other stations on 1330 AM, it uses a directional antenna. The transmitter is off North Rock Road in the Rockhurst neighborhood of Wichita.
