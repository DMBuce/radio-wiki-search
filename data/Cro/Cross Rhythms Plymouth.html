Cross Rhythms Plymouth is a UK Community Radio station broadcasting to Plymouth, Devon. The station is operated under a franchise agreement with Cross Rhythms in Stoke-on-Trent (who also own Cross Rhythms City Radio), but has separate ownership as required by Ofcom regulations.

The station first went on air on 29 March 2007.
