KQNM (1550 kHz) is an AM radio station in Albuquerque, New Mexico. KQNM is owned by Immaculate Heart Media, Inc. and airs a Catholic radio ministry featuring network talk programs and broadcasts of the mass.

KQNM broadcasts at 10,000 watts by day. But because AM 1550 is a clear-channel frequency reserved for Mexico and Canada, the station must drastically reduce power at night to 27 watts. The transmitter tower is in the city's Old Town district northwest of downtown. KQNM programming is simulcast on FM translator K255AU 98.9 MHz licensed to nearby Corrales, New Mexico.
