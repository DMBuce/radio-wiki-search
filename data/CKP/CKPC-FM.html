CKPC-FM (92.1 FM, Jewel 92) is a radio station in Brantford, Ontario. Owned by Evanov Radio Group, it broadcasts a soft adult contemporary format, billed as Lite Favourites. With an 80,000 watt signal, the station serves a very large area, including the Waterloo Region, Hamilton and the Regional Municipality of Niagara markets.
