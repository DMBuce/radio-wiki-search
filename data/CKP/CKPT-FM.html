CKPT-FM (99.7 MHz) is a Canadian radio station in Peterborough, Ontario. It is owned by Bell Media and airs a hot adult contemporary radio format. The studios and offices are on George Street North in Peterborough. The transmitter is off Greencrest Drive.

CKPT-FM has local DJs on weekdays in the daytime. Weeknights, the syndicated "On-Air with Ryan Seacrest" is heard.
