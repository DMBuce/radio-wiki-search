CKPW-FM (107.1 FM, Play 107.1) is a radio station in Edmonton, Alberta. Owned by Harvard Broadcasting, it broadcasts a rhythmic classic hits format focusing on music from the 1990s and 2000s. Its studios are located on the Calgary Trail, while its transmitter is located in Acheson.
