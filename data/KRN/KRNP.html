KRNP (100.7 FM) is a classic rock formatted broadcast radio station licensed to Sutherland, Nebraska, serving the North Platte/Ogallala area. KRNP is owned and operated by Legacy Communications, LLC.

On October 20, 2020, KRNP changed their format from active rock to classic rock, branded as "Flat Rock 100.7".
