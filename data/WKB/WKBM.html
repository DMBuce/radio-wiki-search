WKBM (930 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a Catholic format. Licensed to Sandwich, Illinois, United States, the station serves the Chicago area. The station is currently owned by Starboard Media Foundation. The station is one of three owned-and-operated Relevant Radio affiliates in the Chicago area.
