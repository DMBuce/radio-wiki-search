KLZK-FM (107.7 FM) is a radio station licensed to Idalou, Texas and serving the Lubbock, Texas area. KLZK is owned by Ramar Communications Inc. Its studios and transmitter are co-located in south Lubbock (where TV partner KJTV-TV is based).
