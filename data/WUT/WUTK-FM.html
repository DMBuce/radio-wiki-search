WUTK-FM is a variety formatted non-commercial, non-profit, broadcast radio station licensed to Knoxville, Tennessee and serving Metro Knoxville. WUTK is owned and operated by the University of Tennessee; however, WUTK’s daily operations are not funded by the university. WUTK-FM signed on in February 1982 from studios located in P-103 of Andy Holt Tower with an antenna on the roof generating 128.7 watts. WUTK now broadcasts with over 1000 watts, and streams worldwide at WUTKRadio.com [1] and on the Tune In and RadioFX apps.
