WUTQ-FM (100.7 MHz) is a commercial FM radio station licensed to Utica, New York. The station serves the Utica-Rome region of New York State. The station is currently owned by Roser Communications Network and it airs a talk radio format.

The studios and offices are on Leland Avenue in Utica. The transmitter is on Higby Road in New Hartford.
