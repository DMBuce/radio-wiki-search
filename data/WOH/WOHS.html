WOHS (1390 kHz) is an AM radio station broadcasting an oldies format, featuring beach music. Licensed to Shelby, North Carolina, United States, the station is currently owned by HRN Broadcasting, Inc. but is leased by KTC Broadcasting. Programming is simulcast on an FM translator station W268CU at 101.5 MHz.
