Sobi FM Ilorin is an indigenous radio station based in the city of Ilorin, Kwara State, Nigeria. The radio station broadcasts on a frequency of "101.9 Mega Hertz". The radio station was established on 10 July 2017. Sobi FM Ilorin is located on top of Sobi Hill, about 390 meters above sea level.
