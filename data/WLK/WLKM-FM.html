WLKM-FM is a radio station owned by Impact Radio and licensed to Three Rivers, Michigan. The featured music is Adult Contemporary. The format switched from Classic Hits on June 17, 2012, when the Dial Global Classic Hits format the station had been carrying was discontinued. Programming now comes from Dial Global's AC format. Prior to it being an Adult Contemporary formatted station, WLKM is formerly a Top-40 station with an affiliate of The Rockin' America Top 30 Countdown in the 1980's.
