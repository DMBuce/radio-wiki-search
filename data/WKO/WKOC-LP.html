WKOC-LP (103.1 FM) was a low power FM radio station formerly licensed to serve Ider, Alabama, United States. The station was owned by Ider Educational Broadcasting.

It aired a Christian radio format. The station received a portion of its programming as part of the Three Angels Broadcasting Network.
