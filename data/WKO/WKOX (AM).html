WKOX (1430 kHz, "1430 The Light") is a commercial AM radio station owned by the Delmarva Educational Association. It broadcasts gospel music. The station is licensed to Everett, Massachusetts and targets Boston and its suburbs. It broadcasts from radio studios in Medford. The transmitter site is also in Medford at a separate location.
