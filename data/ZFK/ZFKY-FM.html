Rooster 101 (101.9 FM) is a radio station in the Cayman Islands in the British West Indies. The station is owned by Hurley's Entertainment Corporation. It airs a country music format. Hurley's Entertainment Corporation Limited was founded in 1992 by Randy L. Merren.

It began broadcasting as ZFKY, issued under the British call sign system. The station's current license was issued on 11 December 2003.
