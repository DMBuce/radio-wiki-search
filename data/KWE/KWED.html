KWED (1580 AM) is a radio station broadcasting a country music format, licensed to Seguin, Texas. It is currently owned by Seguin Media Group, Ltd. and features programming from Premiere Radio Networks and Westwood One.

Until 1987, KWED was simulcast on 105.3 FM. After that, it became AM only. KWED-FM changed call letters to KSMG and format then billed as Magic 105.3, the first FM oldies format in San Antonio.

1580 AM is a Canadian clear-channel frequency.
