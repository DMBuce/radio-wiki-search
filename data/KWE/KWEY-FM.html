KWEY-FM 95.5 FM is a radio station licensed to Clinton, Oklahoma. The station broadcasts a country music format and is owned by Wright Broadcasting Systems, Inc. KWEY also airs a sports radio format on its HD2 channel and on its 1590 AM signal.
