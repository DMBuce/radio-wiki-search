WTDY-FM (96.5 FM, "96.5 TDY") is a commercial FM radio station in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. The station is owned by Entercom and broadcasts a contemporary hit radio format. WTDY's studios are co-located with located within Entercom's corporate headquarters in Center City, Philadelphia while its transmitter is located off Domino Lane in the Roxborough section of Philadelphia, a site where other local FM and TV towers are located.
