KVCE (92.7 FM) is a radio station licensed to the community of Slaton, Texas, United States, and serving the greater Lubbock, Texas, area. The station is owned by VCY America, Inc. It airs a Christian radio format.

The station was originally assigned the KJAK call letters by the Federal Communications Commission; it is a Class C1 station.
