DYLS (97.1 FM), broadcasting as MOR 97.1 Lupig Sila!, was a radio station owned and operated by ABS-CBN Corporation. The station's studio is located at the ABS-CBN Broadcasting Complex, North Road, Jagobiao, Mandaue. Its transmitter is located at Babag 2, Brgy. Busay Hills, Cebu City. It operated 24/7.

On May 5, 2020, the station, along with the other My Only Radio stations, went off the air due to the cease and desist order of the National Telecommunications Commission. It currently operates as an online platform.
