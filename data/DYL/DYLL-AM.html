DYLL (Pronounced as DY-double-L; 585 AM) Radyo Pilipinas is a station owned and operated by Philippine Broadcasting Service in the Philippines. Its studio is located at TRB Bldg, Mapa Street, Iloilo City, while its AM transmitter is located at Molo, Iloilo City.
