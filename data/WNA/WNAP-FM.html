WNAP-FM (88.1 FM) is a radio station licensed to serve Morristown, Indiana, United States. The station is owned by New Beginnings Movement, Inc. The studios are in an 1880s home on Wood Street in Greenfield, Indiana.

WNAP-FM broadcasts a contemporary Christian music format.
