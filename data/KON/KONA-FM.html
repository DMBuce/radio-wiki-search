KONA-FM (105.3 FM) is a radio station broadcasting an adult contemporary format. Licensed to Kennewick, Washington, United States, the station serves the Tri-Cities area. The station is currently owned by Ccr-Tri Cities Iv, LLC and features programming from ABC Radio and Premiere Radio Networks.
