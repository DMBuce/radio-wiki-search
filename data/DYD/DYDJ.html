107.7 Energy FM is a community FM radio owned by Ultrasonic Broadcasting System. Its studios are located at 3rd floor, ACP Center Bldg., Acevedo St. cor. Roxas Ave., Brgy. Poblacion, Kalibo.

It started operations in 2010 as Bay Radio. It was affiliated with Baycomms. In 2013, when Brigada bought Baycomms, the station became affiliated with Energy FM. It initially simulcasted most programs from its flagship Manila station until a few years ago, when it started producing its own programs.
