DYDD (1260 AM) El Nuevo Bantay Radyo was an AM station owned by Sarraga Integrated and Management Corporation. The station's studio is located is Crown Bldg. 6th road, Recl. Area, Cebu City. The main format of the station is music, public service and news. It broadcasts from 4:00 AM to 11:00 PM.

On August 1, 2015, Bantay Radyo went off the air due to management issues.
