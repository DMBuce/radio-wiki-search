DYDC (104.7 FM) is a low-power college radio station owned and operated by the Visayas State University. Its studios are located at the 2nd Floor, ADE Bldg., Visayas State University, Brgy. Pangasugan, Baybay. DYDC operates Weekdays from 5 am to 6 pm.

The station serves as a training ground for community broadcasting and Development communication students of the Visayas State University.
