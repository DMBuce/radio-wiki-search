DYDR (100.7 FM), on-air as 100.7 Kaugop Radio, is a radio station owned and operated by the Philippine Collective Media Corporation. Its studio and transmitter are located at Remedios Trinidad Romualdez Hospital Compound, Calanipawan Road, Brgy. 86, Tacloban.
