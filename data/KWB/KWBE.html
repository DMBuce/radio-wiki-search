KWBE (1450 AM, "Beatrice Radio 1450") is a radio station broadcasting a talk radio format. Licensed to Beatrice, Nebraska, United States, the station is currently owned by Mike Flood through Flood Communications of Beatrice, LLC, and features programming from Citadel Media and Westwood One.
