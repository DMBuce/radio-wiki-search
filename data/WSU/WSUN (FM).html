WSUN (97.1 MHz) is a commercial FM radio station, licensed to Holiday, Florida, and serving the Tampa Bay Area. Owned by CXR Radio, a divestiture trust for Cox Media Group, it airs an alternative rock radio format, calling itself "97X." Its studios and offices are located in St. Petersburg, Florida. The transmitter site is off Dartmouth Drive in Holiday.
