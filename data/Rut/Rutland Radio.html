Rutland Radio was an Independent Local Radio station which served Rutland and Stamford, Lincolnshire, broadcast on 107.2 FM in Rutland and 97.4 FM across Stamford. It started broadcasting on 13 December 1998 from Knights Yard, Gaol Street in Oakham.

The station was replaced by Greatest Hits Radio in September 2020, forming part of its Midlands region.
