DXKD (1053 AM) Radyo Ronda is a radio station owned and operated by Radio Philippines Network. The station studios are located at the 2nd floor of Juliana Building, Gonzales St. corner Lopez Jaena St., Biasong, Dipolog. It is the pioneer AM radio station in the province. The station also airs a handful of programs from DWIZ 882 Manila. It operates daily from 4:00 AM to 8:00 PM.
