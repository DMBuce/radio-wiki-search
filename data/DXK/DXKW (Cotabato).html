94.1 GNN FM (DXKW 94.1 MHz) is an FM station owned and operated by Kalayaan Broadcasting System. Its studios and transmitter are located at Sorilla Medical and Maternity Clinic and Hospital, National Highway, Brgy. Sibsib, Tulunan, Cotabato.
