DXKT (1071Â kHz Davao City) Radyo Ronda is an AM station of Radio Philippines Network and Nine Media Corporation in the Philippines. The station's studio is located at RPN Compound, Marfori Heights, Circumferential Road, Davao City, and the transmitter is located at Bolton Isla, Davao City. DXKT operates daily from 5:00 AM to 11:00 PM. It airs a few programs simulcast from DWIZ 882 in Manila.
