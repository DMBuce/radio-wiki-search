Gold FM 98.3 is an FM station owned and operated by Kalayaan Broadcasting System in the Philippines. The station's studio is located along Bonifacio St., Tagum City.

It was formerly known as X 88.7 from 2008 to 2012. Since then, it is revolutionized as 98.3 & changed to its current brand to cater and further suit the taste of the listening public.
