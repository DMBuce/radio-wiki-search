DXKX (91.5 FM), also known as 91.5 Brigada News FM, is a news and music station owned by Primax Broadcasting Network and operated by Brigada Mass Media Corporation in the Philippines. The station's studio is located at the 3rd floor of Gabucan Building, Ulas, Brgy. Talomo, Davao City with transmitter located at Broadcast Ave., Shrine Hills, Matina, Davao City.
