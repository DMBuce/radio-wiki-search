DXKR (639 AM) RMN Koronadal is a news/talk station owned and operated by Radio Mindanao Network. Its studio and transmitter are located along Gen. Santos Dr., Koronadal. Established in 1978, the radio station was very popular in Koronadal, South Cotabato entertaining in Hiligaynon (Ilonggo) dialect in all of its news & public affairs programs.
